Backlit keyboards

If you notice closely, the white KBC PBT key caps has “red dots” on them.
The glow caps on the other hand, allow the light to “diffuse” and spread out evenly throughout the top half/* of the keycap, giving a eerie feel. Well, in short, it gives off a very big dot under clear lighting conditions and gives off no dots during dark conditions.
The caps glows slightly after the LED is switched off. Still visible to the naked eye within 2-3 meters of view.
The two photos are taken from my Trigger at brightness level 1 (it has 5 levels of lighting). Given such low brightness, the glow cap is able to propagate to a brightness clearly under normal lightning conditions which the KBC white caps doesn’t do. Not that this is the differentiating point, just saying.
The photos do not do justice to them as I’m not good and photography and I’m not sure if it will convince you. Black legends will definitely be well received than engraved/blanks.
/*Since the LED is located at the top of every cherry MX switch.
Non-backlit keyboards
I have nothing much to say about these on non-backlit keyboards as I’m a fan of backlit keyboards.
They look normal on them, but giving it an additional glow effect would be awesome.
They can easily replace black on white keycaps, in my opinion.
The black on “white/greenish tint” can easily be distinguished even in the dark like other normal white keycaps do.
