Introducing
Origins Base Kit
Inspired by TA Typewriter design and based off the Italian Keyboard Layout, the Origins Base Kit removes the pastel modifiers and bright alphas for a warmer base colour and more saturated UV-printed accent sublegends. These sublegends are based off the Italian Keyboard Main Layout, with some modifications for better coherency. The Novelties and Nom Kit are compatible with this set.

GMK UV-Printing is often referred to as padprinting, but has a different raised texture and is far more durable than padprinting processes. Due to the textural differences from the base plastic however some users don’t enjoy using it, so by having most of the printing done outside the commonly used alphabetical keys, it aims to reduce the impact when typing and prolong the longetivity of the accent sublegends.
