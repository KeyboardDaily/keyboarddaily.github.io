FAQ
**/* What colors will you be using?*Before you guys check the kits, I wanted to mention the colors Ill be using here:
White: WS2
Black: RAL 000 15 00
Why not CR just like GMK Monochrome was? Simply because I got informed that it was impossible. I will aim for the darkest black possible (and I do think RAL 000 15 00 is quite nice).
/ Why should I buy Monochrome and not Refined?
Refined is a really good set, I’ve said it countless times and will say it again because it’s true. That said, Monochrome and Refined have differences worth noting:
۞ Different whites (WS2)
۞ Difference in kitting:
※ Wider compatibility in Monochrome’s modifiers kit
※ Icon Ergo kit
※ Stand alone ISO UK, North, South
۞ Wider choices in Alphas
۞ Different font (which I feel is the bigger differentiating point - GMK vs KAT font)
۞ Different icons (which ties up to the resemblance (or lack thereof) to GMK)
As a lot of things in our hobby, it’s all a matter of preference and whether you pick this or Refined next month, you are making a wise choice
