[Key Features]

  1. Premium-quality and affordable 65% keyboard
  2. PCB: Refined based on R1 PCB. Purple color, Type-C（Support C-C and C-A), QMK, ESD protection, Added reset button
  3. Mounting Method: Top mount; with two noise and vibration reduction pads
  4. No switch leakage views
  5. Carbon fiber plate or Aluminum plate
  6. Manufacturer: gray studio and factory
  7. Keyboard Design: Airpotter & oldcat
  8. PCB Design: DEMO Studio

Each board may not be 100% perfect but we will try our best to do it 100% perfectly. A special note regarding the PCB, every PCB we produce in this round will go through 2 rounds of QC, but there is always a slim chance the QC fails (<1%). A word of wisdom for all first time GB friends, always test the PCB before you solder the board. If there are any issues or dead keys (note the Fn key will not show up) with the PCB upon arrival of the board, please email me and request a free replacement (with proof showing dead keys with a tweezer). Do not solder before tweezer testing the PCB. Once soldered we, unfortunately, cannot provide free replacements.
