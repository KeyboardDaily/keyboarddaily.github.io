2. M A T E R I A L S A N D C O L O R S

The soft imitation leather is a very soft fabric and feels especially nice in combination with the padded/cushioned parts of the keyboard bag.

The smooth imitation leather feels sturdier than the other leather types. It has a smoother surface and feels a little heftier.

The nappa imitation leather is somewhere in the middle between soft and smooth. It feels a bit sturdier than the soft but rougher than the smooth imitation leather.

For our Premium keyboard bags we use soft imitation leather. Its distinctive look and feel make it a very pleasing fabric to the eyes and skin.

The vintage imitation leather is a very smooth fabric. Its unique patterns give the keyboard bags a very unique and vintage look and feel.

For the Adventure and Split keyboard bags and the Utility bag we use a fabric called dry oil skin. It is a high quality and slightly waxed fabric, which feels nice and is water repellent. It holds its form very well and frequent use gives it a beautiful look.

The canvas or inner lining of our bags is made out of sturdy cotton canvas, rub resistant and thus very durable. You have the options between uni colored and patterned styles.


For the cord of the Utility bag you can choose between imitation suede cords which come in various colors and real leather cords that comes in four different colors.
