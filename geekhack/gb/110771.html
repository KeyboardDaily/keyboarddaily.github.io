Background:
Hey guys, this TT from Owlab (OWL-LAB). Please allow me to elaborate a bit about Voice65%. This keyboard is our first project outside of China and it originated from my good old days in my band - OWL. For an introverted guy like me, music was and still is an important way to express my feelings and attitude. Keyboard as a hobby, is quite similar. It is also an essential tool for us to express ourselves and make your voice heard, especially during such a hard time. Hence, we decided to create a combination of both.
After countless all-nighters, me and my designer friend Nomi eventually concretized the idea with a knob mimicking the mixer (as well as the design on the weight) and a LED strip resembles the neon lights in those clubhouses our band used to perform at.
With those features and a package of various optional/interchangeable parts, we hope this project can help you getting into this hobby and more importantly, find your preference and your own voice.
Highlights:

  • An in-stock sale with one-month ETA

  • A gasket-mounted 65% with spring-leaf plates

  • A programmable scroll and LED strips thru VIA configurator or QMK (to abide the GPL license, the source code will be released after the sale). [Here’s a PCB guide if you are new to VIA]

  • An all-in-one package with lots of extras aiming to help our community experience different typing/acoustic profiles with control variates method. Standoffs/*, two different plate materials, PORON PCB foam/silicone PCB pad are all optional or interchangeable in builds.   • Thocky  PC plate without standoffs/* but with PORON® case foam

  • Muted  PC plate with silicone PCB pad and PORON® case foam

  • Specifications/:**

  • PCB*: supports QMK + VIA and equipped with ESD Protection

  • Materials:* 6063 Aluminum (Case & Scroll), Polycarbonate (PC plate) and Brass (Weight), PORON® (Gaskets, PORON® PCB foam and PORON® case foam), Silicone (Mute Pad, Spacebar dampener and light strip diffuser)

  • Structure:*

  • Gasket-mounted with leaf spring plate

  • The top case is the upper half of the sandwich that also hosts a silicone diffuser

  • The BLACK middle case is the lower half that comes with pre-assembled scroll and daughterboards of USB-C header, scroll and LED strip

  • The bottom case with a matching color of the top 1/3 accommodates the brass weight

  • Typing Angle:* 6 degrees

  • Size:* 339 x 118.65 mm, front height 17mm, back height 27.8mm

  • Total Weight:* 1.4 kg (built w/ alu plate, 1.3kg built w/ PC plate)
