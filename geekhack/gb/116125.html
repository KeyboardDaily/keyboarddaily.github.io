EARLY BIRD SALE (COMPLETED)
pssst. Hey!
Are you looking for a keyboard that has bright, polished bezels and rims?
Are you looking for a keyboard that has that sleek, polished/PVD stainless steel weight?
Are you into knobs?
Or that keyboard you are hoping to find for under 250USD?
If YES was an answer to any of the above, look no further than this line here, because the Loki65 isn’t it.

No doubt, looks pretty standard right? Nothing special. We’ve received a few comments on how it “looks run-of-the-mill”; how it looks ‘just like 50 other keyboards out there’. The Loki65 does not strive to get Brownie points for posh aesthetics. The Loki65 is not here to grab your attention with a low price tag.
But hey, what the Loki65 delivers to you, is:
-Function and Form
-Versatility
-More freedom to customisation
This will be a short post, but to summarise:
Form and Function
Right off the bat, the Loki65 keyboard encompasses the ability to have its plate mounted in 3 different styles. Top-mount, gasket-mount, burger-mount.

Yes, fellow keyboard enthusiasts, those are actual leaf-springs, and they actually work properly! )
Full CNC Polycarbonate. Not Injection-molded.
Stainless steel inserts. We wish that metal inserts in Polycaronate boards will become a norm as well, especially in IM ones, so we can say goodbye to thread-shearing.
5-degree typing angle because we believe it helps to reduce that stress on your wrists during ‘long-haul typing’.
Versatility
3 mounting-styles.
An array of plate options; PC, Aluminium, Brass, FR4, Copper.
Badge options; 3 designs, multiple options for material and colour.
More freedom to customisation
Different mounting-styles, many options for plates and badges, all in one single board.
Underglow RGB for the extra splash of colour.

  • The sale will be of a raffle-format. Winners will be notified through email and/or Discord.

  • There will only be 49 Early Bird kits produced for this run.

  • Each Early Bird kit will consist of:
    1 set of cases (Frosted or Ink Black PC top and bottom cases) with a sandblasted finish,
    1 Runes copper badge with a sandblasted and clear-coating finish,
    1 serialised copper weight featuring the exclusive Raven logo with a sandblasted and clear-coating finish,
    1 PCB of your choice (Hot-swap or soldered),
    1 plate with material (and colour, for aluminium plates) of choice, and
    1 set of Poron case foam sheets, Poron gasket strips, silicone O-rings, screws and rubber feet.
    More

  • You will only be able to buy additional plates, badges and PCBs. If you choose to buy aluminium plates and/or badges, you will be able to customise the anodisation colour for them; so, get creative!

  • The additional plates will be limited to a maximum of 1 per type of material, not including the plate chosen in the Early Bird kit.

  • The additional badges will be limited to a maximum of 1 per type of material+design combination.

  • Estimated time of delivery: 4 to 6 months upon sale closure.

  • The winners will be contacted directly and have their PayPal invoices sent. Payment will only be made through PayPal. We choose to transact with PayPal to protect the buyers interests. Winners will have 48 hours to make payment upon receiving the invoice. Not doing so will result in their Early Bird kits being re-raffled immediately.
