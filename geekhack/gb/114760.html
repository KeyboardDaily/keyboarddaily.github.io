ePBT Kavala

GB October 1 - November 1, 2021
Kavala (pronounced Kah-vaa-lah) is a town in Greece, and my moms hometown. I was inspired by a beautiful picture of a sunset my uncle took while on a walk. ePBT Kavala is a pbt dyesub set that is designed to capture how the last sunlight of the day plays amongst the clouds, creating a mesmerizing gradient of color from the open sky to the horizon.
