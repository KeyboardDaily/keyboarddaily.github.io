//Original text of OP// I have not updated anything aside from the current order list in a while. Some information may be outdated.
Form 1 has been finalized. You may update your order by PM’ing me.
This is a reboot of Changdrew’s ill fated group buy that has been delayed for far too long. I am well aware of the large number of rustled jimmies surrounding that, but with this, I hope we can move on. It is my understanding that the vast majority of people did actually get all their money back, which is good. To combat any question over how serious I am about getting this done, I have already purchased 2000 Row 5 (Function row) 1x WFJ PBT keys from SP. (Picture below) As previously decided upon, we get to use PBT that matches the previous sets I and other members have run, and get to put designs on them that we wouldn’t normally thanks to Imsto. I have already discussed with him how this group buy will work, and once we get the majority of orders in I will be mailing the keys to him for production, and he’ll print em up and ship em back to me for distribution.
Side note/mini rant about thick PBTs and why we aren’t just having Imsto do everything. Thickness of the keys has nothing to do with the durability, and has no appreciable effect other than the sound. These will not wear out, and I’ve stood all ~200 lbs of my weight on one and it hasn’t deformed in the slightest. These are top of the line PBT keys. I prefer the shape and sound of them, others don’t. At the end of the day, Imsto doesn’t have WFJ, so that’s that.
Prices will remain the same as before, $3 per key , with some extra price breaks for key sets. I have elected to offer an extremely large assortment of key designs, so please order them to make it all worthwhile!
