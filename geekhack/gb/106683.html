Specs:

  • 5x12 ortholinear layout

  • hotswap - full grid, MIT (1, 2u spacebar, and 2, 2u spacebar options)

  • top mount with no seams (PCB and plate slide up through the bottom similar to the Neuron)

  • bottom plate also curves up into the back (similar to Keycult No. 2 rev.1 TKL)

  • LED acrylic diffusers on front and sides for LED underglow

  • 2 degree angle - mostly so the pen rail can be more functional (angle helps keep pen on the board

  • QMK compatible and open source PCB
