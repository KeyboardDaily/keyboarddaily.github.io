Welcome to PBT Amalfi
Running from August 14th to the 28th. Shipping in Feburary 2021


A collaboration between myself and Stef, inspired by his Italian roots
For those of you who follow me on social, you know this set has been months in the making, and it is finally here: bring a taste of Southern Italy to your desk with Amalfi. Inspired by food, pottery, and the sunshine, Amalfi invokes the spirit of vintage Italian travel.
