Group Buy has been Cancelled.


Specifications:
 Top Mount
 660 Layout
 7 degree board angle
 QMK and VIA support
 MX and PCB mount stabs
 ARM STM32F072CBT6 mcu
 All aluminum construction
 Designed in collaboration with Emil, ai03, and OneCreativeMind.
DeltaBlade Info:
 Color options:
 E-White Top/Weight and Black ano Bottom/Plate
 Black ano Top/Weight and Silver ano Bottom/Plate
 Silver anoTop/Weight and Teal ano Bottom/Plate
 Black ano Top/Weight and Teal ano Bottom/Plate
 E-White Top/Weight and Teal ano Bottom/Plate
 Pricing:
 Board: $480
 Extra PCBs and extra plates as well as carbon fiber plates will be available.
 Quantity:
 MOQ of 80
Timeline:
 Group buy opens May 3rd at Noon CDT and closes on May 31st at midnight CDT
 My goal is to have these in customers hands by Q1 2022, but subject to change.
Where to buy:
 The GB will go live on www.Viktus.Design at the above time. The site is currently under construction and planned to open up right before the buy.
 I am accepting US and international orders and all will ship from me here in Texas.
 For international customers I am trying to setup DHL shipping as an option but it might not make it in time so USPS Priority Mail International might be the only thing setup.
Layout Support:

 ISO enter and left shift are supported on the PCB but not the included plate, I will make the plate files available later on after the buy to allow you to get custom ones made.
