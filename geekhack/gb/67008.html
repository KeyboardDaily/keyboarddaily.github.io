Hey Hackers!
2014 has been a blast of a year with all the /#GOLD plated goodness! Going to start off the New Year by introducing a 60% kit GB with a super-fast turnaround so you can start assembling early next year!
Everyone loves gold. I love gold plated springs (and I’m sure everyone else does), but deep down inside I wanted something more special. So I thought, why not gold plate stabilizer wires? Has no one really never thought of that?
As some people may have noticed on the forums the past couple days, some pictures of gold plated stab wires have shown up. This was a custom commission I requested through SPRiT, and the results are simply phenomenal! SPRiT has agreed to only produce 60 sets of gold plated SS stabs for this Simply 60 GB.
No MOQ this time. PCBs are already produced with factory pre-soldered diodes & resistors. Firmware has already been flashed & updated!
Only 60 spots will be open for this GB. PCB/Plate/Stabs are not to be sold separately.
What’s included in this kit:
60% SPRiT PCB (White/Black) - Factory pre-soldered diodes & resistors. Firmware already flashed & updated!
60% 5mm V1.1 Acrylic Plate - V1.1 is a revision of the original 5mm Acrylic plate. I’ve requested for FULL switchtop removal, which includes the ESC position.
1 set of 18k Gold Plated SS PCB mount Stabilizers (4x 2u Gold stabs, 1x 6.25u Gold stab).
M2 screws to bolt PCB to the case of your choice
1x Green Tea & 1x Strawberry Kit Kat for all participants. Spreading some (late) holiday cheer
Optional:
7u Gold SS Spacebar wire
65x Cherry Switches (Blue/Brown/Red/Black)
~100x Gold Plated Springs
70x LEDs
130x Individually removed standard length SIP Sockets
Shipping schedule: Shipping will start around second week of January.
