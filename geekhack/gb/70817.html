Ok guys and gals, here we go. This is the official GB for the Alps DCS Infinity mods kit.

Colors for the kit are NN (black)/BX (Teal blue) and are done in double shot abs with a DCS profile so that it matches the majority of existing alps caps. Font for the caps will be Cherry.

The 2 logos for the kit are being generously donated by Nubbinator, njbair and jdcarpe. An ultra huge thanks to you guys for the suggestions and donating to get these done. I sincerely hope that they will be used again in the future for many more custom Alps keycap sets.
Nubbinator: 1.25x “Alps” mountain logo. njbair: 1x “Alps” mountain logo.
