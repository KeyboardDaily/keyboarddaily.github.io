Heyo, whats up guys.
Glad to be here to introduce another GB, which collaborate with my favorite Artisans author GAF. GAF released this sketch about 3 yrs ago which you can find it out in his Instagram, xD. So, thats the beginning of this Groupbuy.
Frankly speaking, its more exciting than running my own Sisi R2 GB. I have ever thought that my dream can come true. This is really surprise and a gift to myself. I wanna see more derivate products from Artisans during these few years, which can decorate my desk more and more abundant. I hope Artisans is not only on the keyboards, but also be filled with my life. I wanna be with them wherever I can be. Its my pleasure to let them be one part of my life. In my opinion, Id like to put all of them in a room and build a small world. Something like many Sneaker heads who will have their own Sneakers Wall, thats my dream in the future. And this is one of the reasons why I wanna release this GB. I tried so hard and many times. But in fact, its really hard to make everything come true. Although experienced a lot of bad process, factory rejection, proofing failure, bad 3D drawing, size error, color error and so on. But all the hard work and the bad process are worth it when you see it come true. Obviously, I enjoy this experience, I hope you so.
