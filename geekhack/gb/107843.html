GMK Dolch R5
Welcome to the Dolch R5 group-buy, a revisited original-color Dolch keyset with classic Cherry icon-text modifiers and an R5 bottom row (also known as Row A) It preserves the original Dolch keycap colorway (CP white instead of WS1 for the legends), and also features a custom color that is close to the turquoise originally used in the old Dolch logo. See below for more information on all the features of this set.
Features

  • Cherry profile, ABS plastic, thick doubleshot keycaps manufactured by GMK.
  • Alphas: CC light grey base, CP white legends.
  • Modifiers: N9 dark grey base, CP white legends.
  • Accents: P3 red base, CP white legends; custom color-matched Dolch turquoise base (RAL 180 60 50), CP white legends.
  • OG Cherry-style Icon-Text modifiers.
  • Complete R5 bottom row support!
  • Support for most common layouts, as well as ISO-UK.
    Other sets that use the CP (white legends) color code, that you may use to mix-and-match: Olivia, Honeywell, Ursa, Barebones Stormtrooper, Bingsu.
    For N9 (dark grey modifier color): Skidata, Terminal, Wasabi, Nines, Q:01, etc…

Base - covers most layouts, including ISO-UK - (60%, 65%, 75%, TKL, 96-key, 1800/CP and fullsize)

Extension - extends coverage for other layouts (660, 88-key, 60% with arrows, Alice/split, standalone numpad), alternative legend keys, novelties, and includes extra keys so that you can have two modifier sets to dress up two keyboards (e.g. TKL/1800 + 60/65%) by reusing modifiers. You will not have enough for two full-size keyboards or two TKLs.

Hangul - further extend Dolch usage, now with Korean language sublegends – if you purchase ALL 3 kits, you may equip two keyboards fully with Dolch (e.g. TKL/1800 + 60/65).
