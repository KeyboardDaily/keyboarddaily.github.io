Hello everyone! I’ve designed some Abstract deskmats that are live on the following sites:

  • US/Canada - Auramech: bit.ly/2C0qaiE
  • EU - Mykeyboard: bit.ly/2YMwIui
  • Both vendors will be shipping out globally!
    I’ve designed this abstract art with not really any inspiration behind it, some say it looks like power puff girls, others say it looks like a dog. So I let your imagination to decide what it is! I’ve had an earlier IC on these deskmats, and we’ve decided we will run the 4 most voted colors of the deskmats.
    IC Link: https://geekhack.org/index.php?action=post;msg=2905176;topic=106413.0

The following colors we will run are:

  • Shades of Grey
  • Orange Sunset
  • Magical Purple
  • Wavy Blue
