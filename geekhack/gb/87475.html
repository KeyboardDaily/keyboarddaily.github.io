Hi Geekhack,
Welcome to the Australian HHKB group buy
Here’s how it will work:
1:You send me your order for any HHKB from PFU Fujitsu’s website.
2:I place the order with PFU Fujitsu
3:I get order shipped to a shipping proxy in batches of 3 Keyboards, 1 week apart (to make sure that you don’t have to pay tax and duty from customs)
4:Proxy receives order and ships to me (in Melbourne)
5:Once I receive order from proxy, I ship via AusPost regular mail //OR// if you you are local, you can pick up from me or I can deliver.
6:You receive your brand new HHKB
How will this work?
I will be ordering directly from PFU Fujitsu and shipping to Australia using http://tenso.com/en,
The keyboards are shipped in batches of 3, which costs roughly ¥5000 YEN (¥1666 per person($19.00 AUD)).
When the boards are in Australia, the AusPost shipping cost is roughly $15-20 Aud bringing the total cost up to $34-39 AUD.
What boards will be available?
The complete selection of boards can be found here: http://www.pfu.fujitsu.com/direct/hhkb/
But here’s a basic list of what boards will be available and their prices respectively:
HHKB Pro 2 English. Black or White. Blank or Printed caps ¥24,840 YEN = $290 AUD
HHKB Pro 2 Japan. Black or white. Printed caps ¥24,840 YEN = $290 AUD
HHKB Pro 2 Type-S English. White. Blank or Printed caps ¥29,700 YEN = $347 AUD
HHKB Pro 2 Type-S Japan. White. Printed caps ¥29,700 YEN = $347 AUD
HHKB Pro 2 Bluetooth English. Black. Blank or Printed caps ¥29,700 YEN = $347 AUD
HHKB Pro 2 Bluetooth Japan. Black. Printed caps ¥29,700 YEN = $347 AUD
How many spots are available?
There are 9 Spots available (3 Boxes). There is already 1 spot reserved for myself (meaning that 8 spots are still available)
How do I order?
You can send me your order via Geekhack PM’s.
