Updates
Update 5-27-20: The set came in on Monday. Upas wanted to send me first out of all everyone so I can review before anyone did, but research took too much of my time, and I did not have the chance to open the package and took some good pictures for you until today. I failed Upas …
Final Verdict
The set came out really, really nice. It is exactly what I hope it would be, even better. The contrast is great, the dark purple is muted, the lilac is such a cool touch. The black has a tint of gray into it (this is a custom black and not the CR stock black from GMK). Its hard to capture the differences in person, but if you have First Love and GMK White on Black, you will see what I meant. Its really nice to go in a professional office setting, not as flashy like some of the other sets. Upas and I spent weeks and weeks sending so many color chips back and forth, then forward everything to Rama and all (didn’t have to, but I really want to make sure the aluminum cap will be the same, or at least very close to the color chip) just to make everything right, but we did, and I’m happy. I’m excited to see everyone taking beautiful photos - if you post pictures on Instagram, tag me at @otani07. Nothing wrong to simp. Our first love is always a part of us to simp about every now and then . I still talk to her every now and then, and I think I might actually fly over to maybe take her out on dinner or something after this hahaha.
If you bought a set during GB, really, thank you for helping me making it happen! Can’t do it without your help. I know we didn’t do so hot during GB, especially during November where we had 9009 to compete against, but we made it through, and I’m glad.
Pretty Photos
GMK First Love (with Spacebar Kit, and Novelties) + Purple TGR-910 and Purple TGR-Alice
Its hard to capture the purple shade of these two boards, but they are purple. First few iterations of TGR’s purple weren’t as lilac as the one from later one (Purple Jane V2 for instance). I’ll show some pictures down here. I love both shades of purple, and I think either one will go very nice with First Love. Black will look awesome too. I just haven’t gotten my V2 CE just yet. I will see if I can find some times this weekend to put the keycaps on my current purple V2. Might do it some times this weekend, and post some more pretty photos!

Update 11-19-19: I will be mailing out these sample chips to RAMA this week so they can get a perfect match on the anodized aluminum RAMA caps. I wasn’t quite happy with the color mismatching between the color of the set and the color of the aluminum RAMA caps on some of the past run set, so I am set to make a difference this time. Cheers!
Update 11-17-19: Final color samples approved after final round! See below for detail.
Color Samples
Dark Purple


I received the sample on the left about a month ago or so from R1 sample. Although it looks nice under direct sunlight, I wasn’t quite happy with how it looks under regular room lighting. Almost a bit too dark if you will. I requested another round, and it came in beautifully. The new dark purple looks exactly like what I’ve always wanted - a very rich, dark blue-ish purple. Doesn’t look as pale as the purple from R1. I am very, very happy with how it turns out!
Lilac Purple

Was already satisfied with the R1 sample, so nothing much to say. I’ve requested a lighter tone for R2 just to see, and to my opinion, it looks much worse. The final lilac purple I approve is more saturated, and has a lot more character into it. The other looks quite plain and pale in my opinion.
Black

So as mentioned, I tried a gray RAL. Looks a whole lot like the faded, lack-of-character dark gray from a cheap aliexpress pbt set. The final dark gray/black I decided to go with is very crisp and bold. This is the same dark gray/black from Red Samurai. It is very, very nice!
Final Verdict

These are the colors that I have approved for production. Both I and the lead vendor (also a very good friend )) Upas are very excited to see how everything turns out. I am very happy with the overall result. They look exactly like what I was hoping they would be. I hope you will be receiving your set soon! Cheers everyone!
