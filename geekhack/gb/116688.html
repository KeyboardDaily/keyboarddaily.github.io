Inspiration:
I would like to think that the multifaceted nature of a person is like a conglomeration of souls filled
with different ideas and emotions popping in and out which makes a person, a person. It is a weird ideology
but I would like to think it fits in the narrative of the design. As a clueless teen, thrown out to college without
a clue in the world, Ive been struggling in coping up with all the changes and challenges that have been happening
left and right and have felt extremely small than I already do. With all these emotions building and accumulating
inside with nowhere to go, it bursts and fills the air with different colors, creating art with the mess it leaves behind.
(p.s. it’s not a nut mat)
