FAQ:
When will it be open until?
It will be open until Saturday, December 16th, 2017.
What is the MOQ?
The MOQ is 50. Both assembled keyboards and unassembled kits with or without switches count towards this number.
Do you ship internationally?
Yes. As long as I can ship it to you, then we have no issues.
How long until I get my keyboard?
I plan to start shipping in 2-2.5 months.
Your site is a .ca is it priced in CAD?
Sadly not. It is in USD for the stability and simplicity of ordering.
Where are your switches from?
They are from my buddy /u/legit309 on reddit over at http://www.switchsource.ca/
Are you trustworthy?
I’ve run a large group buy for let’s split kits, and 2 smaller orthodox prototype runs if that answers your question.
Can I have a refund?
As long as the group buy is still open for orders, yes. After that, however, yes.
I will be away from December 22nd until December 31st, and not much will happen then, other than manufacturing and possibly stuff shipping out to me. During this time I will try my best to answer any questions, and should have internet access for much of this time.
