Since so many of us love getting our doubleshots from SP, I thought to myself there must be decent demand for some blank keys which fit the same profile.
So here I am running an interest check on this. Keep in mind that by replying interested (and in how many sets) you aren’t committing to buying anything.
The eventual group buy also won’t happen unless we get at least 50 sets. The price of the base kit at 50 sets will be ~$40. If we have the interest I plan on having the base kit, the 10 keypad add-on kit and the Tsangan add-on kit available to order.
If you have any suggestions for changes, please also voice them in this thread. I guess this would include stuff like wanting a stepped caps lock, 0/00 keys for the 10 keypad add-on, etc.
One last thing: I live in Australia so if this does happen, I don’t mind organizing everything but it would probably be best for everyone if we had a US proxy.
Let the interest check begin!
