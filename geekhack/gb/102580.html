Kits
Prices

With Cream* (base kit, MOQ 250) because who doesn’t like a touch of cream in their coffee? **$125* MOQ MET

“Wait a second…do I see two TAB keys in that base kit?”
Why yes, you do! Since there is an option for the Black kit to be purchased in addition to the With Cream kit, you would be able to cover two full keyboards (up to TKL and 65% with different bottom rows) using the caps you would receive - with the exception that you’d be missing a second TAB key. Without adding cost, we were able to add in a second TAB key to add value to those who purchase both the With Cream and Black kits. <3
Sugar* (numpad/ISO, MOQ 100) for those in the world who like a little sweet in their life. **$48*

Black*(dark alphas, MOQ 100) for anyone who doesn’t want none of that crap in their cup. **$66*

Extra Shots*(spacebars, MOQ 100) to give it a little extra kick. **$23* MOQ MET

Special Blends*(40s/ortho, MOQ 100) to cover different palates. **$52*

Quick note: the current renders don’t show the presence of scooped homing keys for both the base and dark kit. All F & J alphas will have homing - both bar and scooped will be in the kits. I am working to update the renders to show this correctly.
