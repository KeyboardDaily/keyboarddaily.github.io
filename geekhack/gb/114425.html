La Baguette
Back in 2018, I decided to create my first custom keyboard ever. The idea behind it was pretty simple. Loving the HHKB and the FC660C as well as being French, I was like “ Hey maybe we have something cool there ?“.
This project wasn’t my most accomplished one but for the time it was decent, accoustic and finish were nice but this board deserved better.

F.A.Q
Q : Will you add more layouts ?
A : No, this might be hard to hear for some people but that’s in my opinion how the keyboard should be used and looks the best, it is a design choice that I assume.
Q : Will you add more colors ?
A : For this round we won’t add more colors but if the demand is there, we will for sure offer more in a future Group Buy ! We tried to have a good color selection to please as many people as possible.
Q : Can I order badges of different colors ? Do they only come in brass ?
A : Yes they only come in brass for this round, but that may change in a near future if demand is there
Q : Will you add more plates material ?
A : For this round no, but I plan on making the plate files open.
Q : Will KBDfans will declare full value while shipping ?
A : Yes
Q : What shipping service will be used ?
A : Fedex
Q : Is there anything you can do to use a 6.25 or a 7 U ?
A : I wanted to but due to the nature of blockers, I need them to be aligned / symetrical, and it’s not possible with a non 6u spacebar without having bigger bezels.
