KAM Little Pilot
This keyset is inspired by visual identity elements of Wes Anderson’s Isle of Dogs, and named after the nickname of the film’s main character.
Little Pilot is the second ever KAM profile keyset, and after multiple rounds of samples, it’s time for the group buy!
About KAM
KAM is a uniform keycap profile from Keyreative, the makers of KAT.
It features thick PBT plastic with a glossy finish and dye-sublimated legends.
