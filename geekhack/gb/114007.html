When I got into this hobby two years ago, I wouldn’t have imagined my life and wallet would have suffered so much. After spending a ‘good amount’ of money on my first board -an ALT with Cherry Browns-
I came to the conclusion this was just the beginning. First things first, so I decided trying out different switches. Started with some Cherry Reds, some Kaihl switches, and later on more ‘popular’ switches like
Gateron Silent Inks, Zealios, Healios - etcetera. I know now linears are my thing. However, soon after joining the Mechanical Keyboard addicts on Reddit, I saw people with their so-called ‘endgames’, which were so much
nicer than mine! Started learning to solder with cheaper boards, grabbed a Discipline, a Mysterium, a KBD8X and now I’m stuck with my (hotswap!) Maja with lubed Healios. Quite the upgrade since my last rubber dome,
which was a Ergo 4000.
Now, because of Covid, I - like many others - have been looking for other hobbys, since a lot of people unfortunately had more free time on their hands. So, getting into mechanical keyboards was one thing, but I recently
tried to start leatherworking too. After a fair amount of practice, I decided to try and make something more useful for me personally. I had a black HyperX deskmat laying on my desk, which was good, but it started suffering
from severe discoloration. I thought to myself: “Leather, deskmats…why not make a leather deskmat?”. And so I did. My first try turned out to be pretty nice, if I may say so myself, and now I’m confident enough to share
with you my first couple of handmade deskmats!
[TL;DR]: I love mechanical keyboards, but couldn’t find any deskpad of my taste. So I made my own, and now I’m confident enough to share with you the nicest, customizable handmade deskmat ever.
I am not a very good photographer. Also please keep in mind most pictures are just parts of the deskmat, as I am not able to stitch every combination just now.

TYPE_ACE DESKMATS
Do you consider yourself a ‘minimalist’ like me? Or maybe you just like having your desk clean at all times, organized, and not full of funky colors? Not a fan of all those hectic prints all over your desk?
TYPE_ACE deskmats are full-sized deskmats (900mm x 400mm), with a nice combination of leather and woolfelt. Your keyboard and typing sounds will be dampened, and your mouse will have its greatest time ever, gliding over that piece of
3 mm leather. Each deskmat has a layer of cork rubber so that it doesn’t slide all over your desk.
TYPE_ACE deskmats are customizable to your liking! Do you want a brown leather with a light grey woolfelt, to compliment your desk full of plants and other natural items? Or are you more of an ‘industrial-style’
black-and-white person? Pick the colors you like! Each deskmat will then be finished stitched with your favourite colour. Or maybe you want a yellow stitching to match it with your keycaps/keyboard; everything is possible!
TYPE_ACE deskmats are popular sized (900mm x 400mm x 5mm) deskmats which consist of:

  • 300mm x 400mm mouse pad, made of 3mm top grain leather in two colours: black or brown;
  • 600mm x 400mm 3mm thick, water repellent, 100% wool felt in two colours: light grey or dark gray, both naturally mixed colours;
  • a 2mm layer of cork rubber to prevent slipping and sliding;
  • stitched with a color to your liking. All colours of the rainbow, but also including white, black and brown.
