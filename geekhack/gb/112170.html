**Welcome to the group buy for the MB-44!
**


Photo Album: https://imgur.com/a/hjTMvT4
Typing test and build stream: https://www.twitch.tv/videos/975409245?t=01h51m38s
Timeline
Starts at 11am CDT on Friday, April 9th.
First come first serve.
Closes on April 30th or until 200 units are sold.
Expecting 3 months turnaround from close of group buy.
Color Options and Details:
• Blue-Grey
• Purple
• Green
• Black
• 40% keyboard with 65%-esque blocker
• 7 degree typing angle
• ESD protection
• Optional rotary encoder support
• Powered by QMK
Layout

Vendor: Aeternus
The base kit will be $250 USD plus applicable taxes and shipping and will include:
• Anodized aluminum case in blue-grey, green, purple or black
• FR4 switch plate
• PCB mainboard and daughterboard
• Hardware, bumpons, JST cable.
The following add-ons will be available for purchase through Aeternus:
• Plate foam ($10 USD)
• Polycarbonate plate ($25 USD)
• Extra mainboard PCB ($35 USD)
• EC11 rotary encoder ($3 USD)
For other plate materials, P3D will be offering MB-44 plates in POM and PETG! These will be available for purchase on https://p3dstore.com/
If those materials don’t quite tickle your fancy, the plate file will be made available after the close of group buy.
Thank you for looking!
