Following on from the successful UKKeycaps Group Buy /#1 for Tai Hao PBT ISO-UK/ANSI Keycaps in Orange and Black:
What?
This is UKKeycaps’ group buy /#2 for Tai Hao PBT Range in ISO-UK + ANSI Keys + Miami Blanks
/* Material = PBT
/* Profile = OEM
/* Legends = Doubleshot OR Blank
/* Layout = Full ISO layout w/ ANSI keys. Non-standard layout keys as optional extra
/* Colours

  • Miami (Light Blue & Pink)
  • Miami Blank (Light Blue & Pink)
  • Bumblebee (Yellow & Black)
  • Blood Red (Black & Red)

Layout?
The keysets will be a full ISO-UK layout with ANSI keys additional, so can be used on either keyboard layout
Non-standard keys are available in a pack as an optional extra, these packs include:
/* 1x 7u spacebar
/* 1x stepped caps lock (off-centre stem)
/* 1x 1.75u shift
/* 2/* 1.5u control
/* 2/* 1.5u Alt
/* 3/* 1u blank bottom row keys
The Miami blank keyset includes the non-standard keys in the price as these are linked the MOQ of the main blank keysets
Where?
This is a UK based group-buy with the UK in mind, shipping is free to the UK and available at a fair price to the EU, Russia, the US, Australia etc.
When/how long?
The group-buy will run for 4 weeks and will end on the 28th of September at 11:30pm. The lead time will be around 1.5 months, hopefully less.
How much?
The price for one keyset (full ISO layout + ANSI keys + ring keypuller + wire keypuller + bonus keys) is £34 - This will rise to £38 after 2 weeks
Non-standard key packs will be £4.50 - This will rise to £6 after 2 weeks
Blank Miami keysets at £38.50 as they include the non-standard keyset - This will rise to £44 after 2 weeks
Shipping is free to UK buyers, shipping to all other countries is £10,
What is the MOQ / What if MOQ is not reached?
The MOQ for the double-shot PBT keycaps is linked between colour types, for all the double-shot keycaps the MOQ will be 60. So for example this could be 30 Miami’s, 20 Bumblebees and 10 Blood Red’s.
The MOQ for blanks is seperate to the doubleshots and will be 30
As the double-shot and blank keysets are independant of each other one can go ahead if the other does not reach MOQ.
If MOQ is not reached you will be issued a full refund the date the GB ends
How can I get updates on the group-buy??
Follow the UKKeycaps facebook page! Or you could just keep an eye on this thread
