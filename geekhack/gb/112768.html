What comes in each kit
-Top and Bottom Cases(same color)
-PCB(C type)
-Aluminum plate OR PVD Brass plate(additional cost)
-Polycarbonate plate
-PVD Brass Weight
-Bumpons, screws, keyboard bag with foam
Specifications
-6061 Aluminum
-10 Degree Typing Angle
-Sandwich Mount with 2mm Plate
-Top Case: Winkey or Winkeyless
-Front Height: 20.5mm
-Total Weight: About 6lb and 3oz/ 2.8kg Unbuilt(Case, Brass plate, Brass weight)
-Plate Materials: 1) PVD Brass 2) Aluminum 3) Polycarbonate
-Plate Layout: Ansi with Universal Bottom Row
-PCB: QMK/ VIA compatible PCB(USB-C type) made by Mechlovin’. Also compatible with USB mini-b a.87-layout PCBs and USB-C type PCB with the connector on the upper side
-Brass Weight Bar and Brass Plate: PVD Coating to a Mirror Finish
Anodization Edition
-Top & Bottom Case: Burgundy, Blue Gray, Silver, Black
-Aluminum plate(Black, Silver, Red), +$20 for PVD Brass plate(Gold, Silver, Black, Rose Gold)
-Polycarbonate plate

-PCB: Mechlovin’ PCB(USB-C type)
-Custom Carrying Case, Case Foam
-569 USD
Powder Coating Edition
