Hello Everyone!

Welcome to the first keyboard group buy from the team at KeebsForAll, The Freebird60!

A Step in the Right Direction for Healthy Keyboard Competition…
Conceptualized by Jerry, one of the original co-founders at KeebsForAll,
the product is built around the preface that it be accessible, affordable, and easily customizable
for ease of access and ease of entry into the custom mechanical keyboard hobby, something we always,
and continue, to strive for as a company to this day.
Pricing and What comes in the Kit

At an attractive price-point of $135, this kit comes with…
1. An E-Coated Aluminum Case in Navy, Olive, White, or Black with only 4 Inner Posts for added flexibility
2. The Freebird60 Hotswap PCB with options for both an orthodox 60% layout or a 60% with arrow keys
3. Your Preference of Black FR4, White POM, or Aluminum Plate (Anodized the same color as the case)
4. Silicone Case Dampener, Silicone Strip Feet, Rubber O-Rings, and Mounting Hardware
5. Durock V2 Screw-in Stabilizers
6. Carrying Case with cutout foam
7. USB-C Cable
Case only option will also be purchasable at $70. Includes /#4 & /#6
