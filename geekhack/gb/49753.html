Swiss Studio is a new fround studio in China focusig on making dyesub keycaps. Their ways of using dyesub bring us many possibilities. This time, the thing we’re trying to push is color. You can choose any color you want for the dyesub fonts. And next time, hopefully we’ll surprise you by presenting another new type of dyesub products.
These keycaps are PBT (regular, not thick) and OEM profile. The spacebar has 5 stems and is compatible with Filco, Ducky, Das, Poker, Leopold FC200R, etc.
We’re providing three options for different uses:

  1. 61-Keyset (for 60% keyboards such as Poker)
  2. 87-Keyset (for 80% keyboards such as Filco Tenkeyless)
  3. Numpad Set (17 keys included)
    4 additional keycaps will be included in each 61-keyset and 87-keyset.

    As for other layouts like ISO, I’m sorry but we don’t have the mold to make those keycaps yet. We’re only able to provide ANSI layout at this time.
    We also have two different fonts for this group buy:

Font /#1 Swiss Special
Font /#2 Arial Rounded (Similar to Cherry replica font)
Available colors of fonts: any colors. Yes that’s right, any colors. The base color is white and we don’t have other base color available yet.
You can choose any color you want from http://www.ralcolor.com/ and we’ll try our best to make the actually colors accurate.
Deadline: November 30th. The deadline doesn’t mean when the production starts, it only means when I’m going to stop taking orders. I’ll split up the orders into 3 batches and send them to Swiss Studio every 15 days. So the sooner you order, the faster you get. After sending orders to Swiss Studio, it will roughly take them a week to produce the product and be ready to ship. Yeah, ONE week. They have a few people to work on it.

How to order:
Please choose the color(s) you want from http://www.ralcolor.com/ and then paste the RAL/# and RAL-RGB/# into the order form.
e.g. if you want Lemon yellow, you should type in RAL 1012, 199-180-070 as your color choice.
Here’s the order format: Keyset, Font/#, RAL/#, RAL-RGB/#.
Examples:
61-keyset, font/#1, RAL 1006, 228-160-016
87-keyset, font/#2, RAL 3024, 248-000-000
