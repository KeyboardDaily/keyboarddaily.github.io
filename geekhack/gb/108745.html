[Monochrome Deskmat Series]
Hello everyone! During this pandemic, my inspiration and passion for designing has grown so much on me, that I won’t stop bringing out designs! This time I wanted to do something simple, that was easy liked, and something that would fit all colors, therefore I went with a monochrome color scheme, and decided to do some abstract art!
This GB has now started and will be going on until October 7th! We will be offering you guys 3 different monochrome deskmats, the first being a Liquify design, second being a Wavy design, and the third being a Domino design, specifically made for the head of TKC (The Key Dot Company)
[Vendors & Prices]

About the deskmats:
Stitched edges
3mm thick
High quality printing process
900 x 400 x 3mm[/size]

[Prototypes]
Prototypes has been made for the Liquify variant and the Wavy variant since those were deskmats we were set on to launch. The third Domino design was mainly a side project, but we decided to smack it in with the other deskmats in the Monochrome series! These deskmats are not 100% accurate, they were mainly made to see how the design prints!
