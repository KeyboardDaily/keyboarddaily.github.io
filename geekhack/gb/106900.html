  • Main Features*

  • A Modern, Sleek, and Classy Keyboard : PCB, Case, and Firmware Fully Designed By: Garret G. - a.k.a. The_Royal

  • Compatible, Comprehensive Layout Options : Designed for ease of transition and ease of keycap outfitting.

  • Very Functional In Design : Southpaw Numpad/Open-Arrows/Split-Space gets the job done with ease, no matter the case.

  • USB Type-C : The Modern Standard

  • RGB LED “CAPSLOCK” Indicator : Fully addressable and fully customizable

  • Fully Powered by QMK : The Firmware that makes Sub-60 a viable daily driver (VIA compatibility will also available)

  • 5° Typing Angle : The best mix of angled and flat.

  • Accented Sandwich-Layered Design : Easy Assembly, good weight distribution.

  • 1.5mm Brass Plate : Allows for a solid even-stiffness and sound.

  • Brass Accent Nugget: Machined with our star of the show, Clarabelle

  • An Artfully Designed PCB : Simple, clean, and prairie picturesque.

  • Full ISO & ANSI Layout Configurations : Because I just love slappin-the-heck outta some ISO “Enter”.
