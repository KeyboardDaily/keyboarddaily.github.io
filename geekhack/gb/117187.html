Hi GeekHack, we are WinsCable, a new craft studio that focuses on making premium custom keyboard cables.
After a few successful GBs in China, we would like to introduce one of our proud products, Black-Gold cable, to the world.
Making the coil is not hard, making the coil consistent and durable is not that difficult as well. However, we spent months finding the best technique and process to make the coil durable, consistent and perfect. As you see, the decorating gold line will still be inline across the coil.
In the meantime, getting a perfect cable to match up our fancy premium keyboard/keycaps should not cost a fortune. We are also trying to balance high-end quality and budget-friendly prices. Therefore, we are aiming to put the overall cost under 70 USD including shipping.
