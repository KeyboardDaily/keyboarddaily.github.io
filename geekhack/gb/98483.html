Please refer to the Interest Check here: https://geekhack.org/index.php?topic=97582.0
This oriental looking board [TaiChi] is heavily inspired by the philosophy of Taichi, a Chinese cosmological term for the “Supreme Ultimate” state of undifferentiated absolute and infinite potential, the oneness before duality, from which Yin and Yang originate, can be compared with the old 無極. The word 太極 comes from I Ching: “易有太極，是生兩儀，兩儀生四象，四象生八卦，八卦定吉凶，吉凶生大業。
