We started working on this project more than a year ago, and have been able to resume the work and finished the design recently. Weve run a total of three rounds of prototypes to make sure everything works as they should.
There aren’t many Jetfires and Black Birds out there as it looks unlikely that Duck will make more of these (which is a shame because we think they’re two of the greatest full-sizers ever) , so this is meant to be a one-time thing. Please consider joining the GB and help us spread the news.
**WHATS AVAILABLE & SOME NOTES
**

  • PCBs and FR4 Plates that are compatible with the Duck Jetfire and Duck Black Bird custom keyboards. These are cross-compatible with the original Duck parts.
  • We wanted to offer more plate material options, but our access to local CNC partners is limited as the country is struggling with the pandemic, so instead, we will offer both full and half FR4 Plates.
  • The Mechlovin Olly PCBs use USB-C instead of Micro USB (on the original Jetfire PCB) and Mini-USB (on the Black Bird one), and have been tested to work on the cases.
  • We increased the number of underglow LEDs on both PCBs and rearranged the distribution, which helps improve the lighting brightness and consistency. People who’ve used the Jetfire would know there’s a dark spot on the front-facing LEDs and it is much improved with the Olly PCB. This issue does not apply to the Black Bird because its front-facing acrylic diffuser goes all the way inside and the LEDs shine directly towards it.

Some photos (please note that these are prototypes. Final product colours will be matte black).
