Product Details:
The DeskDaily keyboard bag is a custom molded eva hard case with custom cut foam inserts designed to fit keyboards as large as TKL/Alice keyboards and as small as 40% keyboards. (60% and smaller boards can fit with a mouse)
It can be used to safely carry your keyboards while travelling or simply to store your keyboards at home.
Features:

  • Interior pocket for extra plates, pcbs, cable or other accessories (depending on the thickness of your board, it can also fit a wrist rest)
  • Two interior straps that will secure your keyboard in place
  • Carrying handle
  • Includes 20 removable foam pieces with various sizes
  • Side foam: 2pcs 25mm, 2pcs 20mm, 2 pcs 15mm, 2pcs 10mm, 2pcs 5mm
  • Top/bottom foam: 2pcs 20mm, 2pcs 15mm, 2 pcs 10mm, 2 pcs 7mm, 2 pcs 5mm
  • Outer Dimensions: 410/*220mm
  • Inner Foam Dimensions: 360/*150mm (Maximum Keyboard Holding Area)
