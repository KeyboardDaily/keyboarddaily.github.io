What’s changed since IC begin?

  1. Removed badge on the HHKB case due to popular demand
  2. Substitute Black finish with Beige (all you beige gang is just too strong)
  3. Altered Beige color after receiving prototype, the production unit will be a close match to PANTONE 11-0105 TCX rather than the yellow hue with a greenish tint (Comparison album link click here)
  4. RETAINED all plate mounting points (Poron pads from the gasket conversion kit will need to stick onto the protruding plate points)
    Without the spacebar and sides’ mounting points, the plate’s bottom and sides sections do not have enough support during typing which resulting in a very soft or sinking feeling near the spacebar, enter, backspace, L&R shift.
  • IF you want to build it with top mont and want a softer typing feel - skip screws near the space bar and left and right side when installing your plate
  • IF you want to use the gasket conversion kit, I strongly recommend using all 8 mounting points and install Poron pads on all of them.
