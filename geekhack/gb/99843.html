**JER MINI screws and weight issues solutions
Problem 1: The JER mini weight has M3.5x6mm holes with thread inserts of M2.5x5mm holes. Removing the weight screws may cause the thread inserts to fall off in sucha a case it could be very hard to reinstall the weight.
Solution: Remove the thread Inserts. Purchase the M3.5x5mm phillips screws and lock the weight with a screwdriver. If you have not take the weight out of the keyboard, please avoid doing so. If you already did it, buy some M3.5x5mm screws in case you have any issue with the weight mounting.
Problem 2: The weight material is sandblasted on the surface of the brass, and the process property makes it easy to stick fingerprints and is not resistant to dirt.
Solution: Please avoid contact with oil. If any surface problem, please wash it with detergent that has good grease resistance, such as washing powder or laundry detergent. Then air-dry or blow-dry the weight after washing. Please do not wipe it after washing.
