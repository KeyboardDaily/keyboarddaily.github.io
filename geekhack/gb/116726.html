1. Details

  • 65%
  • Isolation gasket mount with leaf spring plate
  • 6.5° typing angle
  • 18mm front height (excluding front chamfer)
  • Footprint = 340mm wide x 136.5mm deep
  • Weight is between 1.8kg and 2.5kg depending on configuration
  • Arrow cluster
  • EC11 Rotary encoder
  • ANSI and ISO plate options (6.25u only)
  • Solder and Hotswap PCBs
  • USB-C daughter board
  • Aluminium and Polycarbonate Case Options
  • Various Weight Options
  • Custom Knob
  • Expected Q4 Production Completion, with Q1 2023 Delivery
    What’s included?
  • Top, bottom and weight
  • PC plate as standard (ANSI/ISO)
  • Knob matching chosen edition
  • Hotswap/solder PCB
  • Daughterboard and encoder
  • Necessary screws
  • Gaskets, feet and silicon plate alignment pins
  • Hard carry case
  • Chapter 1 comic

7. FAQ
Q: What layouts are available?
A: Only 6.25u bottom row. Normal and Split backspace. Stepped and normal Caps. ANSI and ISO
Q: What about hotswap PCB?
A: Exactly the same! Some switches on the hotswap PCB will be north facing to allow for extra layout options.
Q: Will there be a PC bottom?
A: no.
Q: Will there be RGB?
A: No
Q: Does a Rama knob fit on Chapter 1?
A: Yes! The encoder is slightly shorter than standard EC11, but the Rama knob still works perfectly.
Q: Lead time?
A: Estimated delivery of Q1 2023
