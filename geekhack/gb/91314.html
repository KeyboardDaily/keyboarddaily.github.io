Sorry for the lack of updates on Geekhack, everyone. I am getting much more active and will be more diligent about updating here. If all else fails the product pages on our site are updated consistently when new things brew up, so you can always check there or email us at orders@thekey.company
Update 02/28/18
I got word from GMK that production of the kits are completed. They are being sorted as I write this. I requested a photo, as soon as I get it I will post here. We are currently waiting for them to finish sorting, and waiting for the banderoles to be printed. We are on track for delivery in Mid March.
Update 1/11/18
So here is the latest. Payment was sent to GMK back on 10/23/2017
We got a sample of the red color from GMK and it wasn’t quite right. We requested a new color sample back in November and still have not received it. GMK tells me the plastics company is slow to send a new color sample. I will update as soon as we have the new sample so everyone is in the know. The good news: The beige and black were spot on.
So needless to say, this is going to delay the manufacturing date by at least a few months. I HATE these types of things and try VERY hard to give people reasonable timelines so as to not disappoint. I will keep you all in the loop as more transpires. [/b]
Hey all. First off, we wanted to give a huge thanks to everyone who has participated in the LedZep GB thus far. It’s bringing back the excitement we felt during the >Terminal_ buy, and we really enjoy the interactions with everyone!
It’s the home stretch! Less than 2 weeks to go on /u/thesiscamper’s Led Zeppelin-inspired brainchild. For those who are new around these mechanical parts, we’ll start from the top: LedZep will be manufactured with GMK’s legendary double-shot ABS process (the finest ABS in all the land), with proper profile coverage for most layouts. The keyset’s theme is inspired by the cover of Led Zeppelin’s Mothership album, as are the LedZep accessories. The GB will be ending 09/26/17 at midnight PST, so time is running out. Just like >Terminal_ we’ll be fulfilling Led Zep like there’s no tomorrow, to minimize the wait once received from GMK.
Oh, and the GMK LedZep Alternative Spacebar Kit has been added! You pay no additional shipping in addition to GMK LedZep Base! However, please not that we will not process any spacebar-only orders - if you’ve already participated in the GMK LedZep GB you are good. =)
This time around, don’t forget that we have proxies in both the EU and Asia, as well as safer expedited shipping internationally via DHL to improve on transit times vs. >Terminal_!
