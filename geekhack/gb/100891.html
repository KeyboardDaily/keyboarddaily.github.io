【GB】Jet Fuel33, a qmk powered 30％ alps and mx compatible usb-c keyboard.

May 25, 2019 GB, 键盘 本文总阅读量次

类型: GB
作者: Gabbzan
发布时间: 2019-05-25 17:12:28
更新时间: 2019-05-25 17:16:54

Hello!
the idea for this board came when i realized it would probably be the optimal portable layout, but there were not any available options that had alps support or usb type c.
the pcbs will be in white with an ENIG finish, and the case will be lasercut in clear acrylic.
Album with some sexy pics of the prototype: https://imgur.com/a/hQIVrib
The features:
-QMK firmware
-Usb type c
-Alps and MX support
-Only smd components
-ESD protection, a fuse and reverse current protection
The layout files can be found on my github: https://github.com/Gabbzan/Jet-fuel33/blob/master/README.md
This GB will be limited to 10 kits, and one kit per person, and will run for one week or until all kits are sold.
