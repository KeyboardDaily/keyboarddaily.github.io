IG: https://www.instagram.com/prestonwalzdesignstudio

I’ve been working closely with Jae from Prototypist to develop this collaboration piece for many months now, it’s been a pleasure and after much anticipation.. I’m very happy to finally reveal the finished product. Introducing the J-01 x DCS Mountaineering LE Bundle.
What’s Included?

  • 1x DCS Mountaineering x J-01 Unit
  • 1x DCS Mountaineering Base Camp (Core Kit)
  • 1x DCS Mountaineering Flag Set (Macro)
    (description) What’s being offered is a bundle that includes a limited edition J-01 x DCS Mountaineering and 2 DCS Mountaineering kits to complete the look: Base Camp [Core Kit] + Flag Set [Macro]

J-01 x DCS Mountaineering Keyboard Unit Specifications:

  • Featuring a keyboard enclosure comprised of anodized aluminum accented with a mirror polished stainless steel midpiece and pen rail.
  • Mountaineering hooks design engraved into top frame, white enamel fill.
  • Gasket isolation mount. Provides a cushioned and isolated key-press feeling.
  • Carbon Fiber switch-plate
  • 8 key macro column
  • Limited to 10 units worldwide.

Sale Details:
10 bundled J-01 x DCS Mountaineering units will be offered exclusively through https://prototypist.net/ through a FCFS GB format sale on the 15th of April. The estimated fulfillment date is Q4 2022, more details are TBA, announcements will come leading up to April 15th.
DCS Mountaineering will be going live on April 15th, here’s a link to the Geekhack GB thread: https://geekhack.org/index.php?topic=116804.0
