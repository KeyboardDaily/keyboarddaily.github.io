Status (2-23-2014):
We will use Cherry replica legends from Signature Plastics. We are going to work with ctrl alt store and take orders again plus some new options will be added. Sorry we couldn’t make things work with GMK, getting them to color match seemed difficult and time consuming, but in the future we might be able to make something work. The order/payment/shipping infrastructure of ctrl alt store will be best for all who participate within and without the USA. Plus I don’t want to wait anymore and I want everyone to get Toxic keys in their hands as soon as possible.
I think Signature Plastics and MassDrop will both be good places for group buys in the future, I just don’t want to throw in too many changes for this group buy compared to other community efforts of the past.
Information:
The colors will be GX for the dark grey color, and what I will call “TOX” for now (Probably going to be VDA at SP), which is a custom brighter green color we have custom ordered for this set. These will be ABS double shots.

Pricing:
The prices you see under most items is the base Signature Plastics price which is expired and I need a new quote for each set. If we are able to do most of the keys with GMK then prices will be even higher still, but entirely worth it. Prices are there for a rough calculation of what each item will cost. There are still four $45 new legend fees to take into account for the beaker and bubbles keys that are part of the base, modifiers, and winkeyless kits. Additionally for our time, Batmann and I will get some free kits that will also be baked into the prices. Essentially the price tiers will be about 10-15% more expensive depending on what actual price tiers we hit and if we are able to go through GMK.
