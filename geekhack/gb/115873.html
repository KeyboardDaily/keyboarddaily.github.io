Paragon - A 75% Gasket Mount with a Rotary Encoder
by r00dy, jr2417, and Keebwerk. Please join our Discord to stay up to date with updates. Keebwerk has provided a site with some renders and information (the same information can be found in this post as well).

Design
Our intent was to design a seamless, substantial, and symmetrical 75% with a rotary encoder. The weight pays homage to our roots with the San Francisco skyline. After 1.5 years, we’re happy to bring this to the groupbuy stage.


Case Specifications

  • Typing Angle: 7 degrees

  • Mounting Style: Poron and Silicone Gasket Mount, Plate and Case Foam included

  • Colors: E-White, Black, Deep Navy, Gunmetal, Purple

  • Plate Options: 1.5 mm Aluminum, 1.5 mm PC

  • PCB Options: Hotswap, Soldering, designed by jtallbean

  • Weight and Knob Options: Brass with a Gold or Silver PVD

  • PCB Options*

  • The Hotswap PCB supports the following:

  • Normal and Stepped Caps Lock

  • Normal and Split Backspace

  • Bottom Row: 1.25u 1.25u 1.25u 6.25u 1.5u 1.5u

  • Bottom Row: 1.5u 1.5u 7u 1.5u 1.5u

  • The Soldering PCB supports the following:

  • Normal and Stepped Caps Lock

  • Normal and Split Backspace

  • Split Spacebar Bottom Row: 1.25u 1.25u 1.25u 2.25u 1.25u 2.75u 1.5u 1.5u

  • Bottom Row: 1.25u 1.25u 1.25u 6.25u 1.5u 1.5u
