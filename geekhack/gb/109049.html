Hi Everyone.
UPDATE: Check it out - Going to anodize and cerakote tomorrow!

So Excited! Our first Group Buy for the bronze & aluminum spacebars is Live! https://mtbkeys.com/
The Group Buy is closed & was a success!
We just want to thank everyone who supported us! We have a lot of work to do now and have already started to produce.
Our Group Buy was started on “Mechmarket” October 9th - ( https://www.reddit.com/r/mechmarket/comments/j863fd/gb_spacebars_bronzes_aluminum/ ) and will be running through to October 23rd, 2020
Production time: Should be about 3 to 4 weeks, so we will start shipping product out November 23, 2020.
Here is what is available to order:
Two profiles, Cherry & OEM.

6.25u & 7u
Centered Stems Only
Polished - Anodized Aluminum
Brushed - Anodized Aluminum
Polished - Aluminum - Full Gloss Cerakote Clear
Brushed - Aluminum - Full Gloss Cerakote Clear
Polished - Aluminum - No Coating
Brushed - Aluminum - No Coating
Polished - Bronze - Full Gloss Cerakote Clear (mandatory due to lead content in material)
Brushed - Bronze - Full Gloss Cerakote Clear (mandatory due to lead content in material)
Front and back picture for product reference.

Colors for anodizing - See Color Swatch Chart below(these are for reference only, there are some pictures on our website of spacebars with polished & brushed anodized finishes for reference also)

Packaging: The spacebars will come in a sealed plastic sleeve in a black Cigar style box.

Springs: We will be including 3 different weight springs depending on aluminum or bronze (74g, 89g, 98g & 150g) so you can test what you like.
Sound Test: Here is a sound test sample (thanks to discord member Tim Raines) for our aluminum spacebars https://discord.com/channels/674799233911488514/750891394725314630/767100712101478410 or here on Imgur https://imgur.com/gallery/jPjlpbk
Unfortunately, we won’t have the bronze spacebar sound test before the GB ends… Sorry!
We welcome you to join our Group Buy on our website: https://mtbkeys.com/
And you can follow us on our discord channel for updates!
https://discord.com/invite/fUyWvek
Thanks so much!
