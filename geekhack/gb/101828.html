Nov 27 2019 -
All PCBs flashed and tested
All units qced and ano matched - we have enough units to ship all of GB units, extras will be around after everyone has received their iS0s
As for extras, colors are not yet confirmed - this will depend on which units can be reworked, and which will have to be scrapped
The hard cases shipped to us were amazing, with a snug fitting foam to keep your iS0 safe, but they forgot to print my damn logo on them
As a result, we have ordered another batch of cases, with printing this time in order to save time shipping back n forth
Bumpons are also still in transit, as the bumpons delivered had a rounded bottom instead of a flat bottom, and that won’t work for iS0
tldr; everything checked, good to go, waiting for bumpons and hard cases - units are ready to ship pending those and will go out in December. Once all GB units are received, information about extras will be available
