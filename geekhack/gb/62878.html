I found that quite many want NerD TKL Ver2.0 PCB for KMAC and Multi-layout Polycarb Plate for it.

Here are the specs:

  1. PCB : $60

    Based on NerD TKL Ver2.0
    Standard TKL PCB Designed by GON
    Optional Wireless Conecction(USB Combo) available!!! => But KMAC housing will not have enough space for the battery. Just skip this.
    USB Full NKRO & 6KRO Support(Toggle)
    1000Hz(1ms) Polling Rate
    Fancy LED Mods: Supports In-Switch Backlight Full LED, On-PCB Backlight LED, and Side LED
    Easy Key Remapping /w NerDy GUI
    Easy and Powerful Hardware MACRO(One Macro can include 1~2000 Key Inputs. User can save 2000 inputs at “one” key, or smaller chunks of inputs at “70” keys.) User can adjust each delay between key inputs.
    Supports almost all Keylayouts including ISO!!!
    Multimedia Keys Supported
    Controller SMD Parts Soldered and Firmware Flashed before shipping
    (Diodes, Resistors, SMD LEDs… should be bought separately if you need them.)
  2. 6T White PCB based on NerD Controller
    NerDy GUI Software Offered
    /#/# NerD TKL Ver2.0 for KMAC doesn’t have mounting hole on the PCB, so you need a plate.
    If you want assemble NerD TKL PCB for KMAC with original KMAC Plate, you should make a little mod to two points with a file.


    Above are the squared holes for spacebar stabilizer plastic parts. You have to file the interfering locations. Spacebar Wire and the plate edge will contact. Flip the Plate and file a bit as shown in the pictures.
  3. Plate : $45

    Above is not the plate for KMAC. It’s NS NerD Crystal TKL housing. But the appearance will be quite similar as this one.
    I will make the plate /w 2T Polycarbonate + diffuse finish for maximize the effect of On-PCB Backlight LEDs.
    => Due to manufacturing cost, the Plate will be changed to 1.5T Diffusing Polycarbornate.
    To maximize compatibility, the plate will be the same as that of KMAC1.
    So it should be fastened on the housing bottom(Even though you have KMAC1.2 or KMAC2, you can’t fasten your plate on the top part.)
