I am coordinating a group buy for silver metallic Phantom stickers. Shadovved is handling the backend (invoicing, production and shipping) and I’m in charge of the frontend (communication, orders and being awesome, ( to quote Tym).

Details
The size of the sticker will be 6cm x 0.9cm and made out of reflective silver metallic material with 3M adhesive. If you’re unfamilar with what the silver metallic stickers look like, they will be made of the same material as his earlier Cherry & GeekHack stickers. In the image below, the black color will be silver outline of the sticker.

Special thanks to 7bit for providing the original image from Deskthority Wiki and of course to HaveANiceDay for creating such a fantastic logo and releasing it as open source, so that this sticker could be made.
