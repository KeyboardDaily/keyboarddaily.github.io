【GB】the Moon - Custom Keyboard - CANCELLED, we will come back

IC- Democratic Republic of MOON: https://geekhack.org/index.php?topic=90037
I have to say I feel very bad for this unfortunate event drama. First of all, I’d like to thank Limitz for digging all this up. I can’t imaging what would it be if we are now half way through production. That will definitely be a disaster. I feel sorry for all the people involved, your passion is wasted, ours also. However I’d like to clarify a few things:
-I confirmed that Eve came up with this design by himself and here are some initial design drafts ( http://imgur.com/a/ci1v1);;)
-After the initial design, Eve made the CAD file and was looking for Logos and weight design, so he hired a guy who specializes in this (no really when we look back now)
-Guy denied our initial design ( http://imgur.com/a/1eUpY) and provided the OC design and current weight design, also the following logo as we requested. We felt they were better design so they were adopted. And now we all know the rest
For those who are not familiar with China, Google shut down it’s Chinese search engine in 2010, therefore a lot of obvious information to us is not so obvious to who lives in China. It’s a shame that we didn’t realize this earlier. After further dig up I surprisingly found that lots of design companies are doing shady stuff. I found at least three companies are using the weight design and claiming to be theirs, one of which is what we used and paid for: 1)http://www.xmggsjgs.com/?mod=pad&act=view&id=35 2)http://www.yanqingidea.com/html/2016/sjzx_0802/2821.html 3) http://www.nipic.com/show/8833124.html At this point I have to say I’m shocked and very angry. However as a GB runner, I did not look deeper in this as I trusted EVE so much (I still do), and started this GB. For this I blame myself.
At this point, we understand that there’s no way we continue to run this GB without a new design. We have no intention to copy other people’s work. With the regret I can’t emphasize enough, I confirm we will put this GB on hold for now. Please accept our sincere apology. But we will come back soon with 100% original design. We might be even asking for your help for the logo design(for rewards), so everyone gets to express what they like!

Introducing the Moon custom keyboard. After 8 weeks of interest check, we are very confident to bring you this gorgeous custom keyboard. EVE, who owns the CNC factory and has been in charge of multiple custom keyboards production, designed, manufactured the unique keyboard with beautiful curves!
You can find Interest Check thread here: https://geekhack.org/index.php?topic=88478.0
the Moon:

Alu plate, stripe insert, weight;
