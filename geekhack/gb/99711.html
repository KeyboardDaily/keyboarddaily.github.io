Specifications:

  1. Made with 6063 aluminum for case body, brass for plate and weight.
  2. Original Gasket Mounting Design. (To read more about the gasket mount: https://goo.gl/LAHjJ6)
  3. Typing angle is 5 degrees.
  4. Nett weight of each keyboard case is approximately 2.1KG.
  5. Premium materials will be used for the packaging of each keyboard. Boxes will include all case accessories inside eg: 16 pcs of rubber dampers, screws in M3 sizing and rubber feet.
