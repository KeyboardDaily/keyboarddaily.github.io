Design:

  • Sandwich mount with standoffs
  • FR4 plate and base
  • Optional acrylic middle layers

PCB:

  • Not factory assembled
  • All through-hole components
  • Uses pro micro
  • Supports 5-pin switches and PCB-mount stabs

  • Split space support
  • Arrow blocker
