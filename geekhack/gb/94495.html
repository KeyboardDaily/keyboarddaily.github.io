Welcome to the official Group Buy for the Hyper 7 inspired H7 (173%) PCB.
The buy will run for 7 days or until 30 orders have been met - due to the size we have to hand solder these and any more will likely cause timescales to overrun. If we still have significant demand we will run a Round 3 as well using the R2 design.
The interest check showed a number of people would be interested in a CNC case design - so this is continuing with Xondat in parallel but no further news on that front at the moment.
I’ll also share the files for the wooden floating case design once the GB concludes - I have dimensions for the R1 PCB but as things are changing for R2 I’ll need to re-draft once I have the first PCBs on hand.
Changes from R1:

  • Moved to three rows on the top daughter-board to closer match the original vision.
  • Re-aligned mount points
  • Some slight routing changes to accommodate ‘Function’ and ‘Mode’ LEDs for those two layers
  • PCBs will be Blue in colour
    Pricing as follows:
    PCB production, components & soldering (still requires switches and LEDs) - £120
    Optional Steel laser cut plate with Mounting points (2 piece - one for the main board and one for the daughter board) - £100
    ///*/*Packaging is included, but shipping will be requested once production has been completed and all boards are on hand.
    Production is likely to take 4 - 6 weeks, and on this thread there will be a link to the plan and sequence of events which I’ll update each week.
