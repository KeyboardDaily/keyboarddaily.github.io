    Going to keep this super transparent, here’s the math:
    My upfront fixed cost will be test print (I’ll first make 4 small screens to try and print with, to see if the overprinting effect works and to get the colours right). Second fixed cost is the actual set of 4 screens and ink/paper costs. This works out to be around $376 (500SGD).
