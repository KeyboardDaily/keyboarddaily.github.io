The Dolch Pac keyboard has been an iconic keyboard in the history. Now it comes back with a fresh modern look and more features than ever.
Meet Dolch.CR.
Designed by Cary (CR), the co-designer of ALF X3 Renova and ALF DC60, the Dolch.CR is a 65% customized mechanical keyboard that perfectly integrates the retro-style of the legendary Dolch Pac keyboard with the modern elements of colors, lights, badges, weights, and others. For those 65% + retro lovers, this is for sure an end game board!
[Pictures all based on actual prototypes, 5th iteration]




Key Features:

  1. Customizable badge (in place of the Dolch Badge), underglow 4 LEDs for visual effects with PC badge.
  2. Dolch Badge’s 3D model design file will be shared.
  3. All surface edges rounded.
  4. 7-degree slope
  5. Options for PC weight and Brass Weight
  6. Integrated 4mm plate perfectly cut for MX switches
  7. No switch leakage views
  8. Default QMK PCB with USB-C, south-facing, highly-compatible with multiple layouts
  9. Optional hot-swap PCB with SMD RGB LEDs, south-facing, in either WK or WKL layout
  10. Aluminum 6061
  11. Most popular color options of Classic Black, Gunpowder Grey or Pearl White
  12. Manufactured by ALF, designed and fulfilled by Cary.
