Features

  • Acrylic sandwich structure, with fully isolated, gasket mounted switch plate. Switch plate is supported by 6 silicone and 6 EVA gaskets and completely hidden from sides of the case.
  • Laser cut acrylic case plates
  • Switch plate options: Carbon Fiber, Brass
  • 5.3 or 6.9 degree typing angle, adjustable via using 3 or 4 layers of feet
  • 18 pre-set case color schemes and more to come..
  • Fully customizable case color. 20 colors for each of 11 acrylic layers, total over 409,600,000,000,000 possible combinations. And more to come..
  • Countersink holes only. All mounting screws will be flush with case surface

    Hotswap PCB
  • QMK programmable
  • North facing switch mount
  • Per-switch SMD RGB light
  • RGB blacklight
  • Center mounted USB-C port

  • ISO, 7U spacebar, split BS compatible
  • South facing switch mount
  • Extra backlight LEDs
