I got some updates from Bob at Signature Plastics. He said there were “dozens” of emails about the site not loading and people missing out and here is the conclusion -
“ Thank you all for participating in Booper’s Deep Space group buy. It really is a great looking keyset and I’m sure everyone will be pleased with it.
Here’s a quick update on where we are with this group buy.
As many of you experienced, our site was overloaded at the 11th hour with people trying to make last minute purchases. As a result, many customers were not able to complete their transactions. I sincerely apologize for this. Our marketing firm is working on moving the PMK site to a more robust platform which, hopefully, will eliminate the many issues people have had to deal with on the current platform.
There were also a number of customers that cancelled their order, or tried to cancel their order, because it didn’t look like one or more of the child deals were going to tip. What we have decided to do, with Booper’s permission, is to produce extra quantities of the keysets and put them up for sale on our Key Shop. We are also going to produce some quantity of all the child deals, (except for the Polaris Num Pad Adders and the 7u and 6.5u space bars) and have these available on our Key Shop as well.
We tried to force tip the child deals after the group buy had closed but were unsuccessful. So if you wanted to purchase a child deal that didn’t tip, you will need to purchase it through our Key Shop at the end of the production run. We won’t be processing pre-orders for these either. You will need to wait until they are available. Also, we will not be charging shipping for the Key Shop child deals as long as you participated in the original group buy.
Our lead time is currently running about 8 weeks so we hope to be producing late January and shipping early February.
Thanks everyone!“ -Bob
If anyone has questions or concerns, please let me know! I’ll be happy to address any and all issues I can.
