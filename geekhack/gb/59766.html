This is a group buy for a Black zippered hoodie.
We will be going with a hoodie by Independent Trading Company ( IND4000Z)
“Men’s zip hood made with premium heavyweight fabric, generous spec and quality construction. Fleece lined hood, heavy gauge round drawcord with nickel eyelets, /#5 nickel kissing zipper, 1x1 ribbing at cuffs and waistband.” Construction is 80% cotton and 20% poly, so there will be some shrinkage. Of note though is that the fit on these is oversize, so you should be good with your standard size - it will be a loose fit.
The design:
I am going to keep this simple and to the point. I will be doing the hoodies in black with a logo on the front left and back.
