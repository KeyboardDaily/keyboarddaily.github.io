CASE SPECS

  • Case: 6063 Alu
  • Colors: Black, Rose Gold, Wine Red, Navy
  • Layout: FRLTKL OR WKLFRLTKL
  • Typing angle: 7°
  • Width: 362.23mm
  • Height: 20.53mm (front), 34.7mm (back)
  • Weight: Approximately 1.519kg, depending on configuration
