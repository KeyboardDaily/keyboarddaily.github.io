Update: January 21st, 2020
Okay I know everyone has been wondering about Fro.Yo so here is where we are at.
After Fro.Yo finished there were some communication issues and finalization of everything took much longer than I initially expected. This is the first time I have ever ran a big buy within my single-man business and storefront and I was worried about doing everything absolutely correct for obvious reasons.
Its now January and to be as translucent as I can be, I sat on different issues with shipping and internal business specifics that shouldnt have been sat on for the length they did. Everything has now been finalized, invoices and extras have been paid for and GMK should be getting me Color samples in 2-3 weeks.
Assuming all goes well in these Samples and the congestion GMK is surly dealing with right now isnt too grand, I cant give a Shipping date of any sooner than roughly May 2020.
Ive learned an incredible amount from this buy and I dont know if Ill be doing more Keyset Gbs in the future but if I do I know that I will be infinitely more comfortable and confident in the process.
Almost all of the delays have been directly in-fault due to something on my end specifically. All external proxy vendors have been equally incredibly patient and tolerant of my ignorance during this GB and no frustration should be put on them in any way.
Anyone wishing to receive a refund from Kingly-Keys please contact me directly on Discord: @The_Royal/#3000 and we can deal with that immediately.
Thank you to everyone for there patience and I hope to keep bringing you guys continually better and better products, experiences, and support within our community and our exciting hobby.
