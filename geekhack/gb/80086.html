We(Me and Bocahgundul) have spoken to Life Zone and Leeku and figured out a way to offer an alps solution for all Alu LZ TKL keyboards(with and exception of LZ-R).
We will offer a plate + PCB(a kit) you will be able to add in RGB leds and a acrylic diffuser.
For those who are not familiar with the Life Zones work, you can check out his many keyboards at :
http://lzlife.tistory.com/
We need to achieve a MOQ of 18 pcbs+plates to get this to happen, so shout out to all your friends which has a LZ TKL board! We will not sell any diffusers or leds unless we hit MOQ of the plates and pcbs.
Order placed, 6 weeks approximately until the order fullfilment(Mid june)
Payment status 12/12
Offerings:

PCB + Plate
The PCB is made by Leeku and is called L3 ALPhaS.
The pcb is programmed with JigOn firmware, can be found here: http://kbdlab.co.kr/index.php?mid=board_sw&document_srl=2702787
here are some shots from kbdlab with the pcb sitting in a orion v2:
http://kbdlab.co.kr/index.php?document_srl=2381083&mid=board_Lsno50
The plate is made by lifezone, you will be able to choose one between 3 different layouts:

  • AEK II. Someone has to correct us if we are wrong but aek houses four 1.5, two 1,25 mods and a 6.5u spacebar. Only compatible with a LZ keyboards without winkey blockers
  • Dell at101 and SGI type plate, Standard WKL(ansi 84 key) layout originating from dell at101 and SGI keyboards.This is compatible with all LZ keyboards.
  • Dell at101W, this is a standard ANSI 87 key layout we all know from a standard tkl(filco, cmstorm quickfire, ducky etc) Only compatible with a LZ keyboards without winkey blockers.

    80$ (43+37) Right now we only offer a pcb with a plate. So no plate or pcb only orders.

We are glad to introduce PCB only orders at 43$
the pcbs are of the same dimension as a a87, and should fit in most korean custom cases.

RGB Leds
Enough leds to fit a pcb 6$

Acryllic Diffuser (3mm thick) 7$ LZ-FE ONLY
You will also be able to use this if you have a MX pcb with some kind of side leds, like a Nerd or tx87 etc. Again ths only fits LZ-FE.
This diffuser has been prototyped by yuktsi. And reworked by LZ
