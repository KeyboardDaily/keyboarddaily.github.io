SA GEOMA | UPDATE ON GB EXTENSION + FULFILLED MOQ
Firstly, thanks for your support with SA Geoma. We’ve been able to meet MOQ with 7/8 kits (🪦 rip Light Mods) with our vendor’s assistance.
We’ll be able to create all the molds we were originally planning to, with the exception of the custom alpha keys.
What does this mean?

  • SA Geoma’s first GB phase will NOT be cancelled.
  • Light Mods WILL be cancelled. If you purchased this kit, you will receieve communication from your vendor on your options for refunds or order changes.
  • SA Geoma will NOT have custom alphas like originally planned for price reduction. Renders are being re-done with the SA font. (the biggest differences are on “Q” and “M”)
    We understand many of your were expecting cancellation or GB extension, so we’ll be re-opening the GB period from 13th Dec 2021 to 20th Dec 2021 so that you may put in your order!
    Once again, thank you so much to those of you supporting Geoma, my wonderfully patient and flexible vendors, the assistance of all my friends to make this happen!
