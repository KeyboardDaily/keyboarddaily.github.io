Viktus Sinne
by
Viktus Design

Specifications:
 O-Ring/Burger Top Mount (3 mounts top, 3 mounts bottom)
 HHKB, WKL or WK Layout
 Polycarbonate case
 7 degree board angle
 Type-C PCB support only (more info below)
 ~1.7 Kg w/brass plate
 Designed in collaboration with OneCreativeMind.
Where to buy:
 The GB will go live on Viktus.Design
 I am accepting US and international orders.
Dates:
 November 1st, 2021 @ Noon CDT to November 30th, 2021 @ Midnight CDT
 Q2/Q3 2022 delivery (depending on order volume this might change)
Mounting:

 Above is showing the O-rings on top and bottom of the plate as designed but you can build the board with multiple different mounting methods if you wish.
 If you want a squishier mount and are willing to redrill the plate mount holes to ~4mm, you can use drone controller soft mounts (silicone) and M3 set screws for alignment. (note this will drop the plate down a bit lower than normal)
Viktus Sinne Info:
 Color options:
 Clear PC with sandblasted brass plate, weight, logo
 Smoked PC with sandblasted brass plate, weight, logo
 Atomic Purple PC with sandblasted brass plate, weight, logo
 Teal PC with sandblasted brass plate, weight, logo (not prototyped yet, reference image on the product page)
 Layout options:
 HHKB
 WKL
 WK, 7U bottom row only (with included/sold plates)
 Pricing:
 Board:

  • Dyed: $365
  • Clear: $360
     PCB not included.
     Extra Tops: $110 each (all layouts and colors. Colors may vary from your full board due to batch variance and the dye process)
     Plates:
     Brass: HHKB, WKL, WK, 10U WKL
     Smoked PC: HHKB, HHKB half, WKL, WKL half, WK, WK half
     Forged carbon fiber: HHKB, HHKB half, WKL, WKL half, WK, WK half
     Quantity:
     MOQ: 80
     MAX: Human limit, depends on how interest goes but probably somewhere around 200ish.
     Name:
     The name Sinne means “mind” in swedish because of OneCreativeMind

FAQ:
 Is a PCB included? I will not be including a PCB with the board. It has been confirmed to work with the AN-C (v1 and v2), Plain60, h60, DZ60, and WT60-D. Basically if it has a type-c connector it should work but I can’t test every single option.
 Can you release the files so I can 3D print it? No.
 Will there be an aluminum version? At the moment there isn’t plans for it but maybe later down the road.
