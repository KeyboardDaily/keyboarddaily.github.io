    Some design improvements were done after acquiring the prototype. Below is a summary of these changes:

Prototype photos:
The quality of the prototype is satisfactory. This is my first PC board so I do not have a point of comparison. In terms of machining, the prototype was made according to the design files that I sent to the factory. All pieces fit and there are no tolerance issues.
However, there is slight bowing on the bottom cover due to the absence of center point screws ( https://imgur.com/VpeYkjS). The bottom cover is attached to the top piece only on the four corners. I didn’t put screws in the middle to achieve uniformity on the plate. The bowing is only .2-.3mm and does not affect the typing experience at all. Adding the height of the bump-ons, the bottom will not touch the surface of your table.
To mitigate the issue, I added an extrusion to the lower part of the bottom cover ( https://keyboarddaily.oss-cn-shanghai.aliyuncs.com/geekhack/107476/4ACq7i1.jpg?x-oss-process=style/keyboarddaily). (More design changes can be found at the bottom of this post)
