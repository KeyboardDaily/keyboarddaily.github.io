2x3x4 LED and SIP socket Group Buy
Round 1: Cyan, Pink, Sky Blue
LEDs are coming from this aliexpress store. Later rounds will feature more colors assuming that this goes well.

Group Buy Terms
This group buy will follow the Bill and Ted principle for human interaction: Be excellent to each other. If you conduct yourself poorly toward to the members of the group buy or myself, I’m not obligated to take your order or your money. Unsolicited payments or orders that I am forced to cancel due to poor conduct will be refunded minus the cost of fees.
This group buy is being run for the GeekHack community and not for profit. I’m going to be building test packages for common order sizes using LEDs and SIP sockets already available. I’ll use these to make shipping estimates along with the Shipping guidelines. Any surplus of funds will either be used to provide everyone with more LEDs or donated to GeekHack.
While this is my first group buy as an organizer it’s far from my first as a participant. I’ll perform to my utmost to deliver a sweet experience for all involved. If you have feedback for me, I welcome it here in this thread or via PM.
