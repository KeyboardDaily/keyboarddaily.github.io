M6-A

Rama Works is proud to celebrate the new M6 Limited Collection. The companion sized keyboard fits perfectly between the M4-A and M10-A previously launched by Rama Works. This keyboard represents a long awaited return for Rama Works in the keyboard community. The M6 Limited Collection will only be available during the short pre-order period in November and will never be available again. To reiterate, this is the only chance to own this landmark entry in the Rama Works catalogue.
The board offers up the best of signature Rama Works aesthetic. The entire board is CNC-Machined Brass, using similar coating methods, tools and techniques used in the production of high-end watches. The M6 sports a hand-polished mirror finish across the entire board (Collectors Edition only), which offers that elevated level of personal detailing. In an effort to foster relationships with beginners and aficionados alike, the M6 includes switches that require no soldering thanks to the new Kailh sockets. Although they can be hot-swapped without soldering, they are still plate-mounted for an extra level of stability.

Hotswappable & Solderless Assembly
