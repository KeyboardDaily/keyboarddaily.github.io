Welcome !
I had this project to make a keyboard out of marble for a few years and after finally making my own, I also want to leave this opportunity to others.
Marble is not a common material in the keyboard community, while it definitively has its place on a desk, as a fancy and luxurious material.
For that purpose, as MarbleK, we worked hard to popularize marble wrist rests and we released multiple series of marble wrist rests of different colors: White/Black/Coral Pink/Green/Grey and more will come.
We are here today, to announce the GB for a limited 20 piecesof the FirstSlab60.


Image Gallery : https://imgur.com/a/6BuuhNR
Specs of the FirstSlab60:

  • 60% tray mounted case, supporting the widely available DZ60 (from kdbfans and other local resellers)

  • Removable PCB mounts with rubber damper, to give some flex to this tray mount.

  • This case is made of one block of the well-known Italian Carrara marble. This mesmerizing marble is white with some grey veins.

  • Each piece will look unique thanks to randomness of this natural material.

  • This case has a typing angle of 5 degrees, with its silicon feet.

  • A heavy weight of 2.8kg (6.2 lbs.) for the case alone. Definitively, it will not move on your desk.

  • Price:*

  • FirstSlab60 Keyboard Case: 280USD

  • (optional) Carrara Marble Wrist Rest to fit perfectly the design and size of the keyboard case: 65USD (I also need to add +10USD for shipping because it is almost 1.5kg)
