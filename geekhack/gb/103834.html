An Ortholinear Battlestation

Link to Original IC Thread
The time has finally come people! Its The Ave. Group Buy!! The ultimate, sexy, cute, and powerful, ortholinear-battlestation!
So things have changed a lot in the past month or so. Here are some main points of note…
1st:
E-Yellow has been tossed out for options just due to its complicated nature and that I know sometimes it can come out looking more Orange than Yellow. All of us are pretty tough and picky when it comes to colors and without a full and final prototype finished in E-Yellow, I just dont feel comfortable with going through with having it be availible and then people getting upset when it didn’t turn out like they expected. No one likes that.
In its place, however, I have spoken with the factory and Sandblasted Polycarbonate will be subsituting the E-Yellow!
Which I know a lot of people wanted and Im excited to be able to offer it for the first big Ave GB!
2nd:
the second notable change is that now, every single base kit configuration will come with BOTH a full brass Plate set and a full FR4 (PCB Material) Plate Set! Options are nice, but having them distributed all over the place isnt lol. SO NOW YOU GET BOTH SON.
