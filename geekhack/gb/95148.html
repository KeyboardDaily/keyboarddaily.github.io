Everyone has that moment when they need the right key to perfectly show how they feel about various things, from politics to how your teammates are doing in games, and here at Donut Cables, we think that “Heck” is just the word for those situations. Enter Heck-keys.

Designed with love, care, and a few carefully placed expletives by none other than @avmock of Discord, the heckster himself. Crafted with GMK’s high quality ABS and doubleshotting equipment, the legends on these caps will outlast your ability to “give a heck”, and come in four classic color combinations to either match your keyboard’s colorscheme or stand out from it.
This GB is using my pre-order system, meaning you can commit to buy now and pay once the GB is over, so you don’t have to have your money tied up for the entire GB period. This also means that price drops will automatically apply, so you only pay the lowest price at the end of the GB period.
Heck-keys are available in both single and quad packs. Single packs will be the white on red/WS1 on RO2 key only. The quad pack will be all four keys.
Price drops will occur at 250 units for both the single and quad pack. Further price drops are as of yet tbd.
