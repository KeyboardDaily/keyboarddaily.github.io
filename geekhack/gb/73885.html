Hello Geekhack,
Long time since we last spoke, I hope you’re doing fine.
I’m running a Group Buy featuring a new project and I’m making this post to let you know that the whole thing is live on Massdrop, right now. The idea is to release new color schemes periodically, to match Counter-Strike: Global Offensive in game weapon skins. We are starting with the Sci-Fi guns - Asiimov and Vulcan. Redline, Hyper-Beast and many others will come in the near future.
The project consists in a DSA keyset meant for regular users around the world, DSA fans and of course, Counter-Strike: Global Offensive players. I included custom novelties to match the key-bindings featured in the game and because the chosen profile is DSA, all of the keycaps are interchangeable. Therefore, every key-binding pattern that could possibly exist in the game is covered. Primary, secondary, knife, nades, movement arrows - we have it all.
As I mentioned, the keyset can also be used by a regular user, since it features regular text modifiers with more than 10 international compatibility kits - including Cyrilic. Wanna take it to work? Fine, you can do that. Wanna play at work? That’s okay too but don’t forget to bring your headset and microphone so you can rage at the russians, for an ultimate matchmaking experience. Your boss will be more than happy to have such a dedicated employee.
We also cover the Planck layout.

Below you can find some mock-ups, that I did featuring some of the possible combinations that you can achieve by mixing all of the child deals I created.

  • 40/60% ~ http://imgur.com/a/oqlO2
  • TKL ~ http://imgur.com/a/5DQop
  • 100% ~ http://imgur.com/a/9CbHw

I’m currently talking with Massdrop about the following additions to the current drop:

  • Text legended arrow keys to the Planck kit (8 keycaps, 4 on blue and 4 on orange) - ADDED!
  • Legended Ergodox kit (full kit on blue, full kit on orange) - ADDED!
  • Non-standard bottom row for Logitech G710/Corsair Kx0 users - ADDED!
    These are things requested by the community and I’m trying my best to deliver what people requested. If I fail to add these things, don’t worry because multiple drops are guaranteed.

For now, color tones are:

White - WW (pure white)
Blue - BFN (sky blue)
Orange - ON (vivid orange)
Black - NN (pure black)
Materials and Specs:

  • ABS 2shot semi-matte top and sizes for everything but Cyrilic (which is dsub)
    You’re more then welcome to post here with questions & feedback and I apreciate your attention.
