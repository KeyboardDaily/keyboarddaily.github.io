TREASURE TYPE-9 ( SERIES II )
Hello to all,
I am Eric from Treasure, Industrial Designer from France ( https://treasuretypes.com/ ).
This new project is the follow up of my first macropad keyboard of 2018 ( Macropad Type-9 : https://geekhack.org/index.php?topic=91252.0), in a new and improved version.
Interest check was here : https://geekhack.org/index.php?topic=100925.0
Group Buy Here : https://treasuretypes.com
TREASURE® Type-9 is a mechanical keyboard macropad in a small form factor
Inspired by Scandinavian and Japanese aesthetics, the design is bold and minimalist. timeless design.
The blend between rounded lines and sharp edges makes it stands out on your desk.
Machined from metal ( Brass and aluminum ),
On the technical side, the Type-9 is an easy to program and enterly solderless, USB C
The kit will be sold with 9 DSA keycaps, USB Type-C to A cable; so you just have to bring your key switches.
Part Number: MPD002
WHAT IS INCLUDED
1 x TREASURE® Type-9 (Series II) case in Brass or Aluminum
1 x Metal Plate
1 x Type-9 PCB ( USB-C )
2 x Custom Moulded Foot (Silicone) in the color of your choice
1 x USB-C to USB-A cable
9 x Blank DSA Keycaps
1 x Cleaning Cloth ( Brass versions only )

Screws and allen key
