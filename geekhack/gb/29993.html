The first 25 members to send payment are pretty much guaranteed a palm rest. The next 5 after that (so we’re ordering up to 30) will be on a wait list depending on the stock when we place our order. Orders will close as soon as we hit 30 or in about a week, whichever comes first.

TKL - $51.50
Full size (or TKL with original box) – $56.50
Canada -$56.50 (fixed $15 first class mail

Combined shipping is possible, up to 4 wrist rests can fit into one box!
