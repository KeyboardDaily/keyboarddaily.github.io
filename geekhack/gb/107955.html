General Info
Welcome and please have your boarding passes ready for flight SA Skyriter!
SA Skyriter is a keycap set based on the early 50s Smith-Corona Skyriter typewriter. SA Skyriter brings the deep rich forest, seafoam, and olive green colours from the original typewriter and applies them to keycaps fit for our modern MX style switches.

The group buy for SA Skyriter will run August 7 to 28, 2020 and will be available for unlimited pre-order world-wide at Daily Clack, DeskHero, Mykeyboard.eu, NovelKeys, and zFrontier. Please see the vendors section for pricing and regions. Expected shipping time is end of March 2021.
Specifications

  • Designed by Christopher Janzen (cijanzen)

  • Manufactured by Signature Plastics in USA

  • SA Profile (112343)

  • Doubleshot ABS Keycaps

  • Compatible with Cherry MX switches and clones
    Colours
    SA Skyriter will match Pantone colours specially selected to reference the original Smith-Corona Skyriter typewriter colours for the alphas (627 C) and legends (2260 C) along with original typewriter keycap samples for the modifiers (Custom).

    Kits
    Alphas
    The Alphas kit includes all your standard alpha keys + 6.25u and 7u Spacebars. Unless you plan on using another kit with a different SA set, youll need this kit.

    TKL/Specialty
    The TKL/Specialty kit covers most keyboards from 60%, 65%, 75%, up to tenkeyless keyboards. HHKB/Tsangan, and even non-standard keyboards like Minila (when purchased with Spacekeys). 66-key boards like Volcano, and even GK64 are also supported with this kit due to their inclusion of 2x 2u and 2.25u shift keys.

    Numpad/1800/96-key Add-on
    The Numpad kit offered here, when combined with the TKL kit, allows you to fill out full-sized keyboards along with additional keys for 1800 layout and 96-key layout boards.

    Ortho/40s Add-on
    The Ortho/40s kit provides full coverage for many ortho boards like Planck, Preonic, and Iris (please keep in mind, youll need the spacekeys kit as well). This kit also acts as an add-on when purchased with the TKL/Specialty kit for other 40-50% users who need additional uniquely sized modifier keys.

    Spacekeys
    Find all of your space key needs in this kit which provides necessary coverage for ortho, 40-50%, and split-space keyboards. The spacekeys kit includes an additional B alpha key for Alice and Alice clones.

    Novelties
    The novelties kit is full of 50s mid-century references designed to cover key modifiers. This kit offers full 65% right column and macro-column support so you can deck out your keyboard with classic breeze block designs. ISO users, youre not forgotten either, with a unique return key design.

    ISO
    The ISO kit, as you guessed it, adds ISO layout support. Those of you who need it know who you are!

    NorDe
    The NorDe kit adds additional European/Scandinavian language support. Dont forget to buy the ISO kit if you need it.

    Breeze Block Deskmat
    A deskmat will be available during the group buy period to accompany SA Skyriter. The breeze block wall concept is fully realized here in a seamless geometric pattern.
