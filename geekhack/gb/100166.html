—————————————THINK6.5°——————————————-

Hey my keeb friends, after receiving great feedback and testing the prototypes, it’s time THINK6.5° is ready for the Group Buy. Think6.5° is a keyboard I co-designed with Geekhack user Oldcat. Oldcat and I will be working together during the GB process. I will be primarily be the ground man responsible for manufacturing, fulfillment, and partial invoicing. And Oldcat will be coordinating with me for manufacturing/fulfillment, communicating with GB participants, and possibly doing partial invoicing.

Brandon Taylor’s Review of Think6.5 V1
The name Think6.5° comes from René Descartes’ philosophy that “I think, therefore I am.” Combining the fact that the board has a 6.5° degree angle, we name it Think6.5°.[/size]





Key Features

  1. Premium-quality and affordable 65% keyboard in different materials and colors
  2. 6.5 degree angle
  3. Default option includes PVD Brass Badge and PVD Brass Weight which will be premium upgrades in many other group buys
  4. 4.5mm, integrated plate, with switch slot and corner cuts to optimize switch tolerance.
  5. Four versions: ANSI with badge (compact 660), ANSI without badge, Compatible-layout with badge, and compatible-layout without badge
  6. PCB: fully programmable, QMK, type-C (center position connector), underglow, ESC 4XRGB, 3 hotswap LED slots above badge, Kaihua hotswap sockets for ANSI layouts, non-hotswap for compatible layouts. Compatible with PCB screw-in stabilizers.
  7. Limited Edition full brass versions
  8. Keyboard Design: oldcat and airpotter
  9. PCB Design: DEMO Studio
  10. Manufacturer: gray studio and factory
