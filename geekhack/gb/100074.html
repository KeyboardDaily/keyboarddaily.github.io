  • Render disclaimer:*
    Just to avoid any potential confusion, even though the set uses stock GMK colors. Please, if you are not familiar with N9 and U9, do look up existing sets with these colors for your reference (some named just below in the text).
    The renders are very neutral, and for example N9 can look more dark gray in different lighting from the renders. Here are some words from Oblotzky on his renders, copy pasted from the GMK Calm Depths thread:
  • “I do all my renders in a fully desaturated lighting environment, so whatever pantone/ral/hex/rgb colors I am using (https://deskthority.net/wiki/Signature_Plastics_ABS_colours in this case) should be unaffected. The only change would be brightness depending on how much light I use in the scene, and bounce light from neighboring keys (e.g. a red accent key will bounce off some light onto surrounding keys, giving them a small area of red highlight). However the overall tone should be rather close to what you would see IRL in a neutral lighting environment.”*
  • Numbers:*
