Big Cases for your Big Switch!
At long last, pre-orders are now open for the full lineup of Big Series cases that were announced a few weeks ago along with a special surprise.

That’s right, you can get a solid brass case for your Big Switch, polished to a mirror shine.
Also available is an aluminum case with the same design, with a large selection of anodized colors including rose gold (What I was told was rose gold was in fact not, I am working with the anodizers to see about adding it though). The aluminum case weighs about 1 pound, and the brass is over 3 lbs. Both include crystal clear acrylic by stratakb.com for maximum RGB!

As previously announced, there are options for 2-key, 3-key, and 4-key wood cases. The 1-key cases are still being produced and sold in rolling batches as well.es as well. All feature the same crystal clear acrylic and PCBs with programmable RGB LEDs.

About the design
More

All cases are comprised of 3 parts: a top section made of wood or metal, a crystal clear acrylic mid section, and and bottom. The tops and bottoms are CNC milled, the mid layer is laser cut.
The wood cases are press fit with hardwood dowels, the metal cases use screws and binding posts. These pass completely through the case, and will be visible on the top and bottom.
What you get
More

  • Top and Bottom in material of choice

  • Mid layer in crystal clear acrylic

  • Screws or dowels (depending on case type, see above)

  • PCB with RGB LEDs (optional)

  • Cork pads or rubber bumpons (depending on case type)
