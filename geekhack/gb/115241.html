Originally, we planned to learn the popular large flex cut plate and PCB, after proofing, we found that such a long cut PCB would lead to the consistency of typing is poor, the middle will be very soft, and the harder it will be towards both sides. The advantage of this is that when the switch is pressed, the switch will put pressure on the pe pad in the first time. The reason why we adopted the hardness of 40 is that the hardness of 40 is relatively neutral, which can be of great help in the consistency of resilience.

we will use two kinds of PCB (soldered and hot swap) (there will also be two kinds of plates).
