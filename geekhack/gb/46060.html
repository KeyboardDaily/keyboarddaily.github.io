Group buy items

  • Duckmini V2 Basic set : $245(without taxes and delivery fee)
    1 Aluminum hosuing.
    1 Aseembled PCB without switches.
    1 Frame for cherry switch.
    1 Frame for Alps switch.
  • Duck Raiser(Option) : $62(without taxes and delivery fee)
    1 Duck Raiser for adjusting Duck Mini2 housing angle from 7 degree to 11 degree.

Specs.

  • Aluminum Housing
    Made of Aluminum 6061-T6
    You can choose silver, red or black color.
    You can adjust housing angle from 7 degree to 11 degree with Duck Dummy(optional)
    You cannot use your Pocker PCB because the dip switch conflicts housing.
    Now, you may use Poker 1 PCB, and if the shape of Poker 2 PCB is the same as Poker 1, you may use Poker 2 PCB too. However, since the dip switch hall is not through out the case, you need to detach PCB from the case to adjust dip switch mode.
  • PCB
    All parts are installed by SMT machine except switches.
    You can install switches with/without frame.
    You can install Cherry or Alps switches.
    Support keymapping.(The keymap program is based on text and command,so you need a little bit knowledge of XML and command)
    Support full led.
    Support Macro.(TBD)
  • Cherry frame
    Frame for Cherry switches.

1.5T SUS

  • Alps Frame
    Frame for Alps switches.

1.2T SUS

  • Duck Raiser
    Made of Aluminum 6061-T6
    You can choose silver, red or black color.
