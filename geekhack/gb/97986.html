Update with answered questions:
As of 21st November there are 2 PC units and 3 Aluminium units left available.
Custom QMK PCB (click to see the KLE)
Split backspace is supported on both the PCB and the ANSI plate.
PCB is a mini-USB connector
ANSI plate is being made in aluminium, polycarbonate and brass. ISO only in brass due to the lower order qty.
I’ve decided to limit the time period left so that the process can be kicked off well in advance of Christmas shutdowns. That being said I’m imposing a time limit of two weeks from today for orders to complete. There’s a mere 5 units left so don’t miss out if you’re interested. The buy will be going ahead regardless at this point and I’m looking forward to the final results.
At the moment there is a trial running with the factory to ensure the standards are excellent. I will be working closely with them to make sure the product is the best it can be!
