About the 8L:
This keyboard is not only for left-handed people. Why?
The left numpad lets you use the numpad and a right-sided mouse at the same time. It also frees up more space for your mouse.
The first idea, 8L is a keyboard for work, especially for those who need to input a lot of numbers on daily work, the left numpad will maximize your workflow as well as efficiency.
Our original cause is to make tools not only for you to create other greater things but also an inspiration every time you sit at your desk.
You can find more detailed information at https://viendi.co/8l/ or our Discord server for more instant interactions.

8. Question:
/* Why was the price increased from 200  300 usd in the original IC?
More

Yes, we are able to make the board at this price range. But that is compromising to superficial and low quality product, also turns this into a negative profit project, which may kill our motivation to do next cool things.
We do not want to use cheap manufacturing for this beautiful design. We have to increase the price range to 300-400 usd because:
 To ensure the design has the finest possible manufacturing.
 To offer you the highest possible quality products, to worth your paying, expectation and long awaiting.
/* Why was the price increased from 388 usd as announced?

  • to be clear, Viendi has always been announced that the price $388 is excluded tax and shipping.
  • we don’t lie, you can buy at retail price $388 in local Viet Nam, without any overhead.
  • what more you pay for is the tax of your country and international shipping, not for Viendi nor vendors.
  • different countries have different amounts of tax > retail price differs depending on country.
  • We wish we can sell you at retail price $388 without any overhead, but we must comply with the law of your countries.
    For example: Apple announces the latest iPad price starting from $329 on their website, but in VN, I have to buy it at retail price $438 - retail price raises $109 in this case.
    The aluminum material price is almost doubled since we have started the project. Yes almost double, check this link:
    https://asia.nikkei.com/Business/Markets/Commodities/Aluminum-prices-hit-13-year-high-amid-power-shortage-in-China
    but Viendi did not raise the base price from what we announced 6 months ago, which is our big risk and a big cut-off to our profit. But it is ok.

**/* The Plate:*it is not easy to achieve the plate mirror finish.
You can take a closer look, you can see the outer edge of the plate has 1mm milled radius fillet, which has never been done on other boards before. Combining with the mirror finish makes the plate price double.
