I’m extremely happy to say that GMK Electric is finally in group buy! The IC for this set was posted in July of 2017, meaning there were years where I thought this set would never see the light of day. Thank you to everybody in the community who encouraged me to finish what I started. Since I founded my own vendor, Vala Supply, I did more than I ever thought I could for Electric! A direct fulfillled resin artisan, matching coiled cable, and the first ever custom Vala switch. Can’t wait to see Electric on everybody’s boards
