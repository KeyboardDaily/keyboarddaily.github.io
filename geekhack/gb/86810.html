Update 2/3/2017

The countdown has begun. Less than 2 days left to jump in on GMK Terminal. Thanks to everyone who has participated in the buy so far - with so many great keysets out there and many upcoming, we’re grateful you included >Terminal_ in your arsenal.
As Terminal’s GB phase winds down, we want to begin by apologizing one more time to those who were stoked about the International Essentials Kit. Refunds that have been requested have been processed, and we thank you again for the support!
On to the good stuff. Today we are excited to release the first images of Binge’s >Terminal_ artisan giveaways! To remind everyone, every 17th order (and the inaugural order!) of the first 200 base sets will find an amazing surprise artisan from Binge in their shipment! Besides crossing your fingers, you don’t need to do anything from here on out if you’ve already purchased a base kit. Big ups to Binge for all the hard work, his reputation can’t be denied - this dude is a badass.
Because of the aforementioned badassery, we thought you’d like to know - we ended up with some extra Bingecaps!
From now until the end of the GB (11:59pm PST 02/07/17), we will be picking 4 random /new/ Base kit purchasers and 4 previous already-bought-Base kit peoples (for a total of 8!) to win a limited-edition Bingecap. Whoa.
We will be adding a GB Status page to TKC next week, so everyone stays in the loop. As always, if you need us: reply to this post, email us at orders@thekey.company.

UPDATE 1/29/2017
Three weeks in, and we again want to start by thanking everyone for supporting **TKC**and **Terminal**. What started internally as a Wouldnt this be cool? idea is now reality. Oh yeah, baby.
If you remember in our last update, the **N9 Ergodox Kit** and **Terminal International Essentials Kit** were in dire need of a jump start. Out of the gate and halfway through, neither kit looked like it would tip MOQ.
This is one of those good news, bad news situations.
Ergodox peepsdrum rollyou did it! It was a nail-biter for sure, but everyone pulled through magnificently. The N9 Ergodox Kit has tipped. Ergonomic high fives to everyone! We cannot wait to see Doxs decked out in Terminal, Hydro, Sky Dolch, and whatever other cool combos you fellas come up with.
Ugh, and now the hard part. It truly pains us to announce that the International Essentials Kit is highly unlikely to happen. We will be completely transparent - MOQ is 150, and we are sitting at **22** ordered. We intended to stock the kit for a few future purchases, but we dont feel confident that the demand is there, now or in the future of this round of Terminal. There is great potential for an affordable barebones GMK international kit to tip, such as Wodans layout that we based Terminals on. We tried our best but for now, we want to apologize for letting those who wanted the kit down. If youre one of the 22 who consider this a dealbreaker, please email us if youd like to cancel your entire order. Well still love you, and we commend you for a valiant effort! For those who do not cancel, we will begin processing refunds for the International Essentials Kit upon the close of the GB 02/07/17.
Phew. Tough parts over. >Terminal_ is happening, and youre all awesome. Thanks to everyone for the kind words, criticisms, opinions, nudes, advice, orders, and for being part of this great community.
