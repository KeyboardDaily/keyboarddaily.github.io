MKC brought something new this time beside those zinc keycaps.
It’s Transparent Acrylic Frame. Designed for some of the Tenkeyless keyboards with the usb cable setting in the middle. Such as Filco TKL and Plu 87.
For now, the only thing I can be sure is that this acrylic frame would fit Filco 87 and Plu 87. Not sure if it fits on Leopold or the other TKL keyboards, I’ll try to make him go buy a Keycool 87 (which is kinda a pattern of Leopold 87) to see if it works.
The shell contains 2 parts as the cover and the base. You don’t need to use a tool to put them together because they are combined by the magnet. (As you can see from the pics, those little silver circles around the edge are the magnet that I’m talking about.) The magnets are pretty strong and the 2 parts of the frame combined tightly. When you wanna take them apart, you need to pull hard.

Now I provide two options for the acrylic frame: Total Clear Version and Frost Finished Version (covering the controller).
The pictures underneath show the frost finished version, you can hardly see the controller under the frame. And the total clear version will not has that frost finishing, which means you will be able to directly see the controller.
