Ever since I was young, exploring abandoned buildings and structures has been of great interest to me.
Trying to answer questions like: What was this built for? Why was it abandoned? How long has it been since it was last inhabited?
I wanted to use this to bring a unique set to the community. Welcome to KAT Overgrown.
Base Kits
This may seem unusual for KAT profile, but I want to provide a base kit option with 2 of my favorite color combos.
This kit will cover the most common layouts out there. It should also cut down on the price for those who just
want the keycaps they need to cover these layouts.
