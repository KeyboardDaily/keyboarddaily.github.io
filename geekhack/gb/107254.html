It’s about time.
This keyset is based on the original SA Nuclear Data group buy, ran in R3 SA way back in the day. I have attained permission from the original SA GB organizer, samwisekoi to organize this. The original inspiration of the SA set comes from the ND6600 computer.
The green and orange accents are matched to the SP SA colors via RAL, the legends are GMK CP. We brought some of the original ND6600 legend symbols to GMK style, as well as some new original ideas. Biip did a killer job with these.
Interest check link: https://geekhack.org/index.php?topic=106801
