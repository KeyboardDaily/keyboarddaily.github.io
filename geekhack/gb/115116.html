KAM Soaring Skies

Samples
Disclaimer: This was shot in neutral lighting with plenty of sunlight. The arrow keys are because the 1, Q, and A keys came with faulty stems, which should not be an issue in the final version.

Overall, light blue samples need adjustment. Need to get dark caps as well as a red sun novelty for more color matching.
