What is the Monarch?

  • Alps Compatible

  • Uses Apple Extended Keyboard II keycaps (dye sub PBT) and stabilizers or Alpine Winter keycaps with custom bent stabilizer wire

  • 0.090 alumium for the top and bottom plates on the case (switch plate is recessed to allow the switches and stabilizer inserts to properly snap into place)

  • 0.375 CNC milled Lexan for the center spacer

  • 60%+left-hand numberpad layout to allow for close mouse placement while still providing the convenience of the numberpad

  • Prototype Pictures:*

  • Differences Between the Production Version and the Prototype Pictured above:*

  • Cerakoted finish on the aluminum plates.

  • Plates will be laser cut and then the recesses with be milled to allow for completely square corners on all switch and stabilizer holes (the prototype was cut completely by CNC milling)

  • PCB will be provided as part of the kit, so no hand wiring necessary! Designed my skullydazed. Will be running TMK, documentation can be found here: https://github.com/skullydazed/tmk_keyboard/tree/master/keyboard/monarch/doc

  • Caps Lock and Number Lock LEDs

  • Case design slightly modified to accommodate the PCB and the indicator LEDs.

  • Additional mounting holes in the bottom plate compatible with GON’s Aluminum Feet.

  • Black and silver machine screws will be available.

  • Available Colors:*
    The aluminum plates used for the top and bottom of the case will be finished using Cerakote which is a very tough and durable ceramic coating often used on firearms.
    84 different colors will be available for you to choose from including an option for just bare aluminum in case you want to finish the plates your own special way! I highly recommend looking up the color(s) you are interested in to see what the actually look like when applied, as the difference between the swatch and the actual applied look can be quite different!
