DSA Berry Yogurt is a keyset inspired by the soft colors you see in store-brand yogurts.
The set is part of a project to create lower-case alpha molds for Signature Plastics ABS DSA keycaps.
GB runs from 7/5 to 8/6. Expected to ship in Q4 2021.
All orders will come with a special card to commemorate the DSA Berry Yogurt GB.
