**Layout and Color Choices:**
There will be a hard limit of 35 units available in this raffle. You will be able to choose from **Red**, **Dark Gray**, **Black**, or **Silver** anodizing colors. You will also be required to choose the plate that is included with the kit (full aluminum in silver/red OR a half-plate in frosted polycarbonate). You will be able to purchase a second plate (choosing from the same options) as an extra. There is only one top piece available -- there is no WKL or HHKB available in this group buy.
**Entry Process:**
Everyone will be permitted to enter in the raffle once -- please choose one entry window. Due to the potential for multiple entries, I will be going through them with a focus on weeding them out. Discovery will result in disqualification from this and future zekk.works group buys.
Your desired configuration (color, plate choice, etc.) will be determined from the choices you make in the entry form, so please make your choices carefully.
During entry, please use the email you use most for communications.
**Raffle Results:**
The 35 raffle winners will be chosen on the morning (Eastern US time) of July 5, 2020 and winners will be notified shortly after. Notification of winning the raffle will come in the form of an email from support@zekk.works as well as an invoice from my Shopify storefront. Notification of not securing a spot in this GB will be sent from support@zekk.works.
Completion of the raffle will also be noted in the Geekhack group buy thread.
**Order Changes and Payment:**
You will have 48 hours from the time of invoice receipt to make appropriate changes and pay for your slot. If, after 48 hours, an invoice remains unpaid, the slot will be re-raffled and allocated to a new winner/*. This new winner will have the full 48 hours to make any changes and pay for their slot. Should there be a second instance of non-payment, the slot will not be re-raffled but held by me to sell after GB units have been fulfilled.
As a very serious reminder, **PayPal will not be accepted as a valid form of payment.**
All order changes or inquiries must be sent to support@zekk.works, no order changes or support questions will be answered via Discord or Geekhack PM.
