We are pleased to present you the MKD-Custom keyboard by Teleport - made in Germany.
The idea and specs for this board were developed in cooperation with the MKD discord channel. It is the most biggest custom keyboard community in Germany.
After a poll, many fruitful discussions and a runoff, the choice has fallen on a 60% gasket mount. We are proud of the board - especially in terms of sound and feel - and hope you like it as much as we do. The flex can be varied by the different plate materials and the adjustable gasket mount with screws - there should be something for everyone. In addition to the standard additional weights in the back of the board back, we have further (small) brass additional weights integrated as carriers of the Gasket-Mount. Enjoy the folowing pics, no render!

The following specs are available:

  • 60 % Board-DIY set in seamless design with visible additional weights (without switches and keycaps)
  • 6 % Typing Angle - Aluminum milled case with option for WKL and HHKB
  • Front height: 17 mm
  • Additional weights integrated into the case in brass or steel (polished)
  • Various colors: white, red, black, gray (anodized or ceracoated)
  • Option for a steel or brass base plate (polished)
  • Gasket mount with brass weights as support (s. exploded view)
  • Mounting plate in aluminum, polycarbonate or brass
  • Tailored to the chosen keyboard/PCB layout
  • Solderable Teleport PCB (black/gold) with Ansi, Iso, Tsangan, Arrows (Ansi and Iso) and HHKB support (QMK and VIA supported, Stepped Capslock, Split Backspace and SplitShift (right) also supported)
  • USB-C connector with Daughterboard for a horizontal cable output
  • Optional custom engraving

The standard DIY set contains at the price of 335,99  (payment possible with Paypal etc.) the following components:

  • Aluminum case (6° typing angle) and base plate anodized in black, gray or red
  • Case in standard-layout (WK)
  • Additional weights in brass or steel (polished)
  • Gasket mount with brass brackets
  • Possible keyboard-layouts with Teleport PCB (solderable): Ansi and Iso layouts for Standard, Tsangan, Arrows, HHKB, WKL (Stepped Caps, Split-Shift right and Split-Backspace, see pics below)
  • Mounting plate in aluminum

This is a GROUP BUY!
The board will be produced in an edition of max. 50 pcs. In order for the GroupBuy to start, we need at least 25 orders (MoQ).
