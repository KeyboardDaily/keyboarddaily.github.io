Concept
The Synchronize deskmat is inspired by the concept of inner perfection. It symbolizes the balance between and the synchronization of perfection and imperfection within oneselfs mind. Although human minds are full of flaws and distorted thoughts, we believe that perfection in feeling is possible, and inner perfection only comes when we leave room for imperfection. The figure is generated using binary numbers, zeros and ones, symbolizing the potential and the creative spirit of Aiglatson Studio.
