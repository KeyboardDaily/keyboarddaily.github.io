FAQ

  • Q : What is Stripe? Do I need an account to use it? Which countries can I use it from?
    A: Stripe is a payment handling service for credit & debit cards similar to Paypal. It means that no card information is stored on my site, and allows everyone to buy with confidence. You don’t need an account (just a precision-buys.com account) and you can use it from ANY country so long as you have a supported card!
  • Q : Can I pay through Paypal?
    A : Not currently. There may be a chance for people to pay via Paypal at a later date if there are many people who cannot pay through Stripe. I encourage everyone to pay through Stripe unless they are quite literally unable to as I believe it to be a much better payment method, especially for group buys. If you absolutely cannot use Stripe (to clarify : “cannot”, not “would prefer not to”) please send me a PM here or an email through the site with the title “Cannot use Stripe” with no text body. This will help me gauge the number of people who cannot use Stripe.
  • Q : Is your website secure?
    A: Our site is perfectly secure. Checkout is done via SSL and we are PCI compliant, however none of your payment information is stored on our server. All payments go through Stripe, which is absolutely secure. Just as secure as Paypal or any other popular payment method.
  • Q : Why are the sets for EU orders more expensive?
    A : The NON-EU orders will be shipped by HoffmanMyster in the USA. Those sets will be shipped directly to him from GMK, and he will pay little to no import duties on them. The EU orders will be shipped by me in the UK. I will have to pay 19% VAT on these sets, hence the higher price for EU orders. However, since EU orders will be shipped from the UK no import VAT/duties will be paid on them.In the end it works out best for everyone, regardless of location. EU and NON-EU orders all end up cheapest this way.
  • Q : If I pay right away what happens if we don’t reach MOQ?
    A: You will be refunded completely. Neither party loses a cent with Stripe refunds.
