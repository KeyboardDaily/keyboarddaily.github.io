Runs from July 24th - August 24th.
Original IC link.

Just Another Gray Set featuring two cloudy grays, a teal accent, and WS2 legends.
Pricing:

Kits:
Base:
This kit provides compatibility for 40%, 60%, 65%, 75%, TKL, 1800, and full-size boards.

Hangul:
An alternate alpha kit with UV printed (pad printed) sublegends. This kit in combination with the base kit gives you the potential to cover two boards.

Spacebars:
Includes accented bars and Alice bars, along with the extra ‘B’ used in the Alice layout.

Extension:
This kit includes keys for XT columns, windowed keys, HHKB supers, and a few other extras.
Legends on windowed keys are UV printed.

R5:
The R5 kit includes compatibility for 7u bottom rows.

Collaborations:

Both caps are anodized aluminum with enamel infill.
