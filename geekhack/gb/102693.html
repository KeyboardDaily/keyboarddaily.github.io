Post-GB update:
Official GMK renders approved for production, added a green ISO return and a green tilde key to the base kit.




Dear Geekhackers,
It is so far, the GB of GMK Camping r2 is up!
This last round of GMK Camping contents a Japan themed base and novelty kit.
The base includes hiragana mono legend alphas while additional Latin alphas are available as child kit.
With base and Latin alphas, you will be able to fit a TKL/full size board with a 60% board.
The base kit supports full size/TKL/1800/HHKB/60%/65% and various layouts.
Additionally, a Rama cap is available with this GB.
Please see below for available kits.
Vendors:
US: https://novelkeys.xyz/collections/frontpage/products/gmk-camping-gb-r2?variant=30307643261021
EU: https://mykeyboard.eu/catalogue/category/group-buys/gmk-camping_195/
CN: https://www.zfrontier.com/m/5610
Asia: https://en.zfrontier.com/products/gmk-camping-r2
Oceania: https://dailyclack.com/collections/keycaps/products/gmk-camping-r2
GB window:
October 1st - October 25th
Shipping:
Shipping is 18-20 weeks after the GB has concluded. Shipping will be directly from GMK to vendors and distributed to GB participants.
Original IC thread:
https://geekhack.org/index.php?topic=101859.0
Colour:
We will be using the same custom colour as GMK Camping r1 did.

Collaboration - Golden Star Keycaps (GSK) Artisans
Three exclusive Camping-theme artisan keycaps will be offered by Golden Star Keycaps in a raffle buy. Anyone that has order at least a base kit of GMK Camping R2 will be eligible to enter the raffle by filling a form. There will only be a limited number of these caps available, and the invoicing and fulfilment will be separately handled by Golden Star Keycaps.
IMPORTANT INFORMATION
● Raffle form https://forms.gle/8zdEWHs2JVvHowv79
● Raffle form will be open until 2 days after the GB finished. You will need to fill in your GMK Camping order /# (with at least 1 base kit purchased) for cross-referencing. Duplicated order /# will be deleted.
● You will NOT be entered into the artisan raffle automatically. ONLY by filling in the raffle form will your entries be acknowledged.
● Artisan caps will start to ship 1 month after the GB concludes.
