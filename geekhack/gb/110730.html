[GB] Round One, Go-popper, A 2-in-1 Aluminum Switch Opener
Thanks to all of you who provided feedback and encouragement throughout the Interest Check! The final decision on the color for the 1st round Group Buy on Go-popper would be BLACK, E-WHITE, SPACE GREY, and NAVY BLUE, which occupied the top 4 preferences of colors.
Round One Group Buy on Go-popper
For those who have not notified the IC page for Go-popper, it is a compact, portable, handy, and durable switch opener that helps mechanical keyboard lovers to open the switch FAST & SAFELY and with 200% convenience. This idea was heavily driven by our personally experienced difficulties or unsatisfied experiences with opening switches with tools based on demands of lubing/replacing switches and replacing keycaps. It was the point that we realized we could not find a truly beneficial and useful tool for opening switches.
Secured by non-slip o-ring rubbers, Go-popper has two sides: one for Cherry MX switches, the other for Kailh BOX switches.
Features
-Both MX and Kailh box switch compatible
-Made of Aluminum, CNC processed and anodized
-Texture: Matte
-Packaging: Paper box with environmentally-friendly recycle paper & custom EVA foam
