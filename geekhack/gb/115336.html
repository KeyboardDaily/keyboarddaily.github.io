Hello! I’m Plop, CEO and founder of Plop Laboratories.
Here at Plop Labs, we strive to bring beautiful keycap sets straight to your Dorito powder-covered fingertips. We recently teamed up with a talented crew of kindergarten students to design the most blindingly colorful set yet. Introducing, ePBT Ping.

The development of ePBT ping was a long and painful journey. What initially started as a wingding typeface-inspired set, took more twists and turns than a daytime soap opera. After months of redesigning, color picking/matching, existential dreading, followed by two hours of sobbing, and consuming an unhealthy amount of caffeine, Ping was born. Epbt Ping was meticulously crafted with the utmost attention to detail. From its fully custom Latin alphas to its unnecessary hotkey sub legends and vibrant colors. It’s designed to help compensate for your lack of any cherishable personality.
