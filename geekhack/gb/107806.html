Colours approved

First I am a terrible photographer, this is captured in my office with warm light but does a great job of showing the gradient.(GMK caps in pic are from GMK Voyage) The rust colour in these pics is a bit off, it looks amazing, is such a great colour, just tricky to photograph (for me) I think anyone using the Rust will be super happy After Round 1 I had approved 4 colours, and after Round 2 I am approving the remaining 4. The only major change from the reference RALs that I made was to lighten the 2nd darkest colour, as in renders it was showing up different enough, but in real life it was nearly impossible to tell. Keyreative did an amazing job lightening it just enough so that it works perfectly. We dont have any finalized dates but I am told I should know be able to share more details in April. Sorry for the delay, thanks for your patience and I will keep everyone posted on the progress
