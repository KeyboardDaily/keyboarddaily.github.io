Introduction:
Got hook into this hobby from the sight of DanGWanG’s beautiful KMAC. Back then, KMAC came in like a wrecking ball, wrecking many, many wallets, and that kick start the Korean custom keyboard hype from that day onwards. Having tasted the many Korean custom boards, the ideas start pouring in, and in that mix, the best quality ingredients (or features, in this case) are picked up and thrown into the design. With the help of a group of enthusiasts with similar interest, the design is polished from time to time, prototypes after prototypes, until the satisfactory result is achieved. The TGR is ready.
Everything should be made as simple as possible, but no simpler.  Albert Einstein.
This is what TGR Jane is all about, a quality, clean, solid, elegant custom keyboard, no fancy pancy, only classy. The beauty of it is astounding despite its plain Jane design. But lo and behold, the value of it lies within, as all of the built focus is in making sure the keyboard delivers a solid feel to the one who wields it.
