Yuru Camp is an anime I thoroughly enjoyed, and knowing my previous history of making ‘weeb’ GMK sets (see: GMK Noel), I thought it was only obvious to make a Yuru Camp themed one given the popularity (season 2 soon ) and the vast oppurtunity for novelties. However, GMK Camping r2 (Camping in Japan) was already about to run at that time, and I didn’t want to overlap that set by creating something uncannily similar. That and most of the colours from the show didn’t appeal to me as being something that could be translated well into keycaps. That all changed when one day, when I was just at a uni event, one of my friends came in with a piece of merchandise: Shima Rin’s scarf from the show! I loved the colours of it, and knew I had to make a set based on it.
