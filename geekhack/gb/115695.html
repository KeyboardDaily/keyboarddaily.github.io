So why POM?

Max Burger (the designer of MBK keycaps): POM has a delightful feel. That is somewhere in between ABS and PBT. Intrinsic buttery feel together with the texture makes the typing experience more enjoyable.
/* POM is a great material for keycaps. The POM OG Cherry keycap is considered by some ( https://geekhack.org/index.php?topic=101249.msg2778351/#msg2778351) to be the best keycap ever
/* POM is very durable. Unlike ABS, POM is resistant to yellowing and developing shine over time
/* POM has a density of 1.41 g/cm3, which is much higher than ABS and PBT
/* POM keycaps naturally have a smooth buttery feel, which is complemented by the beautiful surface texture of the MCC-profile keycaps
/* POM is translucent, which makes it a perfect companion for RGB LEDs
