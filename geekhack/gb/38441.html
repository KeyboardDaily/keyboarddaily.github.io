First off, a thank you to jdcarpe to helping me getting this GB rolling, and, of course, to bpiphany for letting us use his Phantom design to run this round 2 GB. I think this is a really cool project for people to build their own custom boards at a reasonable price.
Intro
The Phantom PCB is a custom tenkeyless board designed by bpiphany (formally PrinsValium) to be used with the teensy 2 programmable usb controller and a mounting plate.
Main features:
 1.5x and 1.25x modifiers
 Center stemmed and off-center caps lock
 7x Cherry spacebars
 ISO and ANSI layouts
 5 extra keys above the arrow key cluster
 Costar and Cherry stabilizers
 6KRO over USB
 In-switch LED at caps/scroll lock and Filco style LEDs
 Switch top removal without desoldering
 LED brightness control
 Firmware source code can be used to remap keys
We will be keeping the PCB black with white LEDs like the original GB
What’s included
1  Phantom PCB
100  Diodes
2  LEDs
2  Resistors for LEDs
2- LED stand-offs for filco-style LEDs
What you’ll need
Cherry MX switches
A Teensy 2.0 controller with header pins—-Available through his GB for $18 per
Stabilizers (Costar or Cherry)
Mounting plate
—-Tools—-
Soldering iron
Solder
Wire cutter (or if youre cheap like me nail clippers)
Desoldering tool/braid (unless you never plan to desolder anything and wont make any mistakes)
A DMM(or an old school multimeter) is useful to test for continuity, even super cheap ones will work fine
Pricing
Everything in the Whats included section above will cost $35 per board.
Shipping
Ive calculated shipping based on estimated weights of various parts. Shipping boxes are 6 ounces, boards 5 ounces, diodes/leds/resistors are negligible, and 1 ounce extra for packing paper. Price below are calculated from costs from USPS (added $2 to each price to account for cost of packaging materials like boxes, paper, tape, etc).
