Hello, welcome to the groupbuy for a top-quality 96-key chinese custom keyboard!
This groupbuy is for a keyboard kit that include the following:

  • Full andonized aluminum case
  • Andonized aluminum plate
  • Fully programmable, pre-soldered PCB
  • acrylic diffuser insert
  • Aluminum Case*
    The aluminum case has a bottom and a top segment that are screwed together to form the full case. The case can be used either fully together, in which case the rgb diffuser acts as a strip of light between the two halves, or you can use only the top of the aluminum case, in which case the diffuser becomes an acrylic bottom, making the entire bottom of the keyboard glow with light.
    It weighs about 5kg, or 11 pounds. The dimensions are 373.9 x 125.7 x 43.4 in mm.

Aluminum plate
The aluminum plate comes with all the same color options as the case does (samples available above). You may choose a different color for the plate than you have for the case, simply note this in the “comments” section at checkout. If you do not make a note, it will be assumed that you want the same color plate as your case.

PCB
The PCB comes completely pre-soldered with diodes, resistors, and controllers. The programming software uses AMJ’s fork of TMK, available on their GitHub. The PCB includes compatibility for top-mounted LEDs, as well as 16 RGB LEDs on the underside. The PCB also supports all the same layouts as the plate (layout chart available above).

The PCB from AMJ includes a very interesting new feature: the ability to use either a mini usb or micro usb. The PCB includes both ports for easy switching, and the case includes holes for both. You may choose to detach one if you do not like having them both on.
