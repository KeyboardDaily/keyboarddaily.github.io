Hi GH, I’m making this post on behalf of Breadtamer (username on Discord), who does not have a GH account.
45-ARC is a collaboration between P3D Store, Breadtamer, and Checkerboards. P3D is handling the fulfillment, Breadtamer made the design, and Checkerboards made the PCBs.
What? Hotswap 45% acrylic keyboard (staggered or ortho) with a RGB light column and macro column
When? November 1st through December 1st, 2021 with fulfillment 3-4 weeks after GB end
Where? https://p3dstore.com/collections/group-buys/products/45-arc-acrylic-gasket-mount-keyboard-case-and-pcb-group-buy
How much? $115 for acrylic case and $50 for PCB (shipping is free for US orders)
Quote 45-ARC takes inspiration from 45-ATS featuring offset arrow keys and macro column. 45-ARC is a joint project from Nasp and Breadtamer. Nasp designed two hotswappable PCBs: staggered and ortho. Breadtamer designed an acrylic case in two styles: a unique vertical acrylic accent/insert and traditional three dot cutouts. The 45-ARC case is compatible with the original 45-ATS PCBs with a new plate. The 45-ARC PCBs are compatible with the original 45-ATS case with a new plate.
45-AXX PCBs by Checkerboards.xyz can fit either 45-ATS or 45-ARC cases depending on the plate you have. Please note that the plates for ATS and ARC are different and not interchangeable. P3D store will sell plates for both boards.
