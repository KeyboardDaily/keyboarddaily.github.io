Safa is intended to be a lightweight, simplistic take on the TKL form factor (i.e. it’s just a rectangle), with my primary focus being feel and sound.

Specs

  • 5 degree, 88 key (F13) TKL.

  • 1550g - 1700g weight depending on build

  • Winkey and WKL

  • Adjusted front height of 18.8mm (excluding case feet - 3M 8mm bumpons)

  • Included hard carry case

  • Primarily top mount, secondary gummy worm

  • Fixed ANSI, ISO plates. Variable JIS Plate available

  • Additional polypropylene (PP) plates will be available. Specifically chosen for its more dampening effect over PC

  • Aluminum half plates, ALPS and JIS Plates will be included. Note - ALPS requires hiney’s h88c

  • Internal brass weight only - not exposed externally. I live in sandland, its hot, humid, and I never look at the bottom of the board. If its on my desk how can I? I wanted this to change the sound of the board, no other reason.

  • Plates are also compatible with Jane v2, KFE and other similar mounted boards. You will be able to purchase all available plates, including ALPS Alu 1.2mm options, separate from the main GB*

  • Color options*

  • 432c Dark Grey

  • 533c Navy

  • 188c Dark Red

  • E-White (Dark Grey plate)

  • Silver (Red plate)
    Alu plates will be matched to case color by default - Grey alu plates are also an option.
    PP plates will be black. If you want colored PP plates, get some spray paint and mod it

A few QOL insights…
Brass weight coverage - made to cover as much of the alphas as possible. Being such a low profile board has its limitations, and this was as much coverage as I could reasonably achieve

Alignment pillars - a simple way to assemble the case and not worry about alignment. These cover both the X and Y axis’

USB tolerance - uh…yeah…kinda obvious. Also helpful for locating the port when not looking directly at it, as the entire ledge leads to this point.

Back and side profile - please ignore the ano color on this - it is not an option
