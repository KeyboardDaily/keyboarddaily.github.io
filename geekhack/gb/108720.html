Update post and photos here.
I got the deskmat samples. Other than me wanting to adjust the light blue color it looks good. The production version should have a darker blue stitching around the border. Keycap manu is on time and expected to ship to vendors early/mid April it seems. However, since I want to adjust the deskmat color it may or may not delay shipping a bit depending on how fast they can color match and if we get another sample or not.
