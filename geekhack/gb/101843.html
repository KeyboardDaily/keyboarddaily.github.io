About Artisan Case v2
For this revision, my goal is to provide an archival quality artisan storage experience.
After living with the Artisan Case v1 for over a year, an insight developed that home access, UV protection, and enjoyment were all important factors to account for.
This resulted in a cleaner aesthetic without the lid screws, and the addition of an acrylic stand to proudly display your resin collection.
You will find that this case is effortless to use, with precise internal measurements to ensure easy access and enough headspace to accommodate 99% of artisans on the market.
Included with each case is a museum grade UV filtering lid, travel foam, silicone locking band, timestamp ring, and display stand.
Specifications

  • 54 Slots (6x9)
  • Precision Machined CNC Acrylic Case
  • Glass Bead Blasted Finish
  • PlexiGlass Hard Coated Lid with UV Protection - more details on PlexiGlass UV Hard Coat
  • Acrylic Display Stand

  • Translucent Bump-on x 4
  • Timestamp Ring
