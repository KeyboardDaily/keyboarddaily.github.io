Hello,Geekhackers
It’s been too long since our first custom keyboard kit Zero.Now we bring you something from the Zero series. We call it Zero 0.9,in short term Zer0.9
New features of this kit:
1, Iron plate. We been trying to find out the best type feeling keyboard for a long time. Tried nicked iron plate when we did Zero,but it oxidated very quickly. And now we have been using electrophoresis. It gives a whole new type feeling. We feel like this is good choice for a tactile switch.
2, LDN series PCB,16.77M color RGB,fully programmable,with Type-C port.
3, High-light chamfering around the case.
4,Top mount,with O-ring under to reduce noise. And a silica gel plate between PCB and plate(it could reduce some of the noise)
5,Custom made Aluminium. After months of experiment, this aluminium can reduce the color difference between the top and bottom case.
