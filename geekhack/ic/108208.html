Ex Arkhes - TKL

After buying a few too many keyboards I wanted to design something myself. The name is in recognition of that; meaning literally “borne of the beginning”, and marks my first keyboard design.
Aesthetically I was inspired by the shape of the rowing shell and the katamaran, which manifests in curved profiles, bezels and a back/top piece reminiscent of a boats motion over water.
GB THREAD PENDING APPROVAL: https://geekhack.org/index.php?topic=113347.msg3058625
Specifications:
. WKL 7u only bottom row
. 4.2 degree typing angle
. O-ring gummy worm mount - ala Jane v2 CE, 356 Mini etc
. Curved seamless case profile
. H88 PCB by Hineybush - F13 TKL form factor
. 6063 Aluminium case and plate
