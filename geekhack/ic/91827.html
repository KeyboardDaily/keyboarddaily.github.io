Hi all,
Some photos:





I’m sure by now many of us have seen thesiscamper’s latest creation, the Kira60 case which looks bloody (pun intended) awesome.
I’ve had a chance to try it and I have to say, the case + those angles at the side might be the best 60% for me, for this year at least.
Anodizing is really smooth, colours look fantastic and the weight is a fresh design. The sides though are really a sight to behold.
That said, I’m partnering with thesiscamper to offer a very small run of these KIRA60 cases.
NO R2. Wrong info on my part thesiscamper has much more up his sleeves and is looking to have different designs if and when we get to another 60% again
Some info below:
What:
KIRA60 (Comes with case, alu plate and brass weight) The case and brass weight is about 1.75kg, so you can still bring it with you and yet have a solid board to work with.
PCBs that follow the Poker screw layout will work, so that includes bface, GH60 etc.
We will also have extra alu plates available, and these support ISO but it’s TBC. You might have to get your own ISO plates. Looking to get poly/brass ones as well.
Colours:
Black, grey, silver, blue (TBC, but probably these are the choices)
When:
We’re looking at doing this 2nd week of October, maybe earlier. Thread will be updated ahead of time with more info when we get there.
Where/Who:
I’ll handle the buy through monokei’s PP business account so that everyone gets the protection they need while there will not be any freezing of fees.
