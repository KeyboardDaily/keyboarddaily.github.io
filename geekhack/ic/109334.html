KBrenders Kit render: (might add to this or offer alternate kits depending on if people like the color variations proposed)

Hello everyone!
I’ve had this idea for a while, and I’m finally brave enough to put it out there! I’m really new to this and just want to see if people like the colors and general design before I go all out. This keycap set is the embodiment of many delightful things! One big inspiration was strawberries on mint chip ice cream. I really love mint chip ice cream and wanted to see it come to life in a keycap set! It was a treat at my house to add some strawberries on top. Pretty unorthodox I know, but very sweet! The base keys are a creamy tan with minty chocolate mods and and green/pink accents.
Artisans are in the works and will be posted as a secondary kit soon. (cute strawberry on a keycap?) (Or a cute little mint chip guy?)
PRICING AND KITTING ARE TBD
TKC has been chosen as the US Vendor and the set will be ran through Infinikey.
I want to at least provide support for 60%, 65%, 75%, TKL, and full-size boards but don’t want to overextend to too many kits. The current base kit offering is in the above render, let me know if anything should be added. There is potential to offer a pink mod kit like shown, and possibly other layout support.
Vendors have been selected for the US and in discussion. Pricing coming soon!
