I think it’s time the ortho crowd gets some attention. What say y’all to a gasket-mounted 40%?
Features
 6061-T6 aluminum case & plate; brass PVD or maybe sandblast weight & plate
 Planck-style 48-key layout (at 19.00mm spacing, as per OLKB specs), with support for 2u MIT spacebar
 Double gasket mount (dampening & isolation); 1/16” thick Sorbothane
 Flex-slotted PCB (MX only) with ESD, USB-C, and STM32 running QMK & VIA (or possibly a brand spanking new custom firmware)
 5.5° typing angle
IC Form: https://forms.gle/Bs3YXFJvg9d5Ykiw8
Cool Renders

So far, I’m thinking I’ll offer the case in Onyx Black, Space Gray, & Nightingale Blue. If you want to see any other colors, say so on the IC form!


As you can see, there’ll be either a PVD or sandblasted brass plate. It’s not final, thoughI’m thinking I’ll offer aluminum, as well.

The weight features a symbol reminiscent of a black hole.



It’s attached with two M2 screws.

The construction itself is a double-gasket mount using 1.5mm-thick Sorbothane (a shock-absorbent polymer) gaskets. You, the end-user, will be able to adjust the pressure applied to the gaskets at your leisure by adjusting the four assembly screws.
