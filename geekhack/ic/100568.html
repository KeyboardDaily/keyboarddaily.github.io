Hello all, this is DeanS, you may know me from the FC980C plate IC.
I’ve been working with kevinplus on custom Topre products, and we are happy to announce the launch our new store, Deskeys.io(coming soon). This store is mainly dedicated to efforts into Topre products and accessories. You may know kevinplus from the polaris 60%.
After trying different materials and factories, we are happy to present you our first product, DES rings. These silencer rings provide a quieter typing experience unrivalled by any other silencing ring on the market, and can compete head-to-head with genuine Topre silencing rings. They are soft with very good compression rate.

/#2, /#3, /#4, /#5, purple slider ring
Options
DES ring /#2 : 0.2mm thickness, ultra thin edition. Very minimal key travel reduction. Suits stock Topre stock domes.
DES ring /#3: 0.3mm thickness, thin edition. Better silencing performance than the /#2, while also keeping travel reduction minimal. Also suits stock Topre stock domes.
DES ring /#4: 0.4mm thickness, medium edition. Feels softer than the original rings from purple sliders. Suited for domes with harsher tactility (Sony BKE domes and BKE Redux).
DES ring /#5 0.5mm thickness, the Original. 99% identical to authentic Topre silencing rings. Suits those who wants the rings from purple sliders. Suits for domes with harsher tactility (Sony BKE domes and BKE Redux).

All of the rings will have a thin PET film coating for durability.
We have tested other silencing rings:
Kbdfans’ silence-x rings: Thin enough but doesn’t compress. Hard to install.
Hypersphere rings: Good compression performance but thicker than others at 0.6mm; the loss of key travel is noticeable.
