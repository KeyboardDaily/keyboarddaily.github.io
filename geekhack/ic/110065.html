I’m happy to announce a set I’ve been working on for a while!
I am interested in world history, and I am also interested in world mystery facts.
One of the biggest inspirations that I designed this set was Moai.
The moai stone statue was made of volcanic rock.  I think the color of volcanic rock is not completely black, but between black and brown.
Also, I think the inside of volcanic rock is somewhere between grey and dark gray.

For more information on moai, please see the link below. 
https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Moai
