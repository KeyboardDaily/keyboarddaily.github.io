【﻿Ｗｅｌｃｏｍｅ　ｔｏ　ｅＰＢＴ　Ａｅｓｔｈｅｔｉｃ】
Inspired by the vaporwave visual themes of Floral Shoppe and Arizona Green Tea.
ePBT Aesthetic seeks to bring out the beauty of these themes and colors.
Formerly introduced as GMK Cherry Blossom (and then KAT Aesthetic), I’ve sought to improve this set, and I’m very pleased with the result.
Whether you just enjoy the colors, or are a full weeb, there is a kit for you in ePBT Aesthetic.
GB thread is up!
【﻿Ｇｒｏｕｐ　Ｂｕｙ　Ｕｐｄａｔｅ　ａｎｄ　Ｆｅｅｄｂａｃｋ】
Unfortunately, I received notice last month from Keyreative that KAT Aesthetic would be indefinitely on hold while they improve their tooling.
In order to continue with the group buy in the best way possible, we have moved to ePBT. Group Buy will go from March 8th to April 8th!
I realize this feels like short notice and possibly like ‘too late for feedback’, but in the year and a half that this IC has existed there has been a ton of feedback taken and improvements made.
I’m confident we have a product that people will love here. That being said, I’ve created a form to take any last-minute issues with the set into account before Group Buy starts.
Please fill out the final IC Form if you feel so inclined! https://forms.gle/VRnwvY8DVn6SpAu1A
Join the discussion in the Aesthetic channel of my Discord server!
