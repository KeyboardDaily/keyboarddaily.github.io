Hello.
This is Kevin Lim from Lin Works.
First of all, thanks to your support, I was able to finish the Montage project.
Now, I drafted the next Geekhack IC for the Whale 75.
Its a 75% layout board combined with our previous board design, Whale
First when we planned this project, we only considered the classic 75% layout. But we got so many requests on putting a knob on the layout. And we considered market trends with different brands, which we found to be the same.
So, here is our answer for these requests, on behalf of Lin Works. We will give you the choice.
Hybrid
 PCB will be compatible for both: with and without knob.
You will have to make your choice on the top case layout. Either for a Knob or an F13 key.
The GB will be happen on our Discord and Instagram.
Lin Works Discord
https://discord.gg/ZPPRZ5CVkQ
Lin Works Instagram
https://www.instagram.com/linworks.333/
Heres the details of this keyboard.

  1. Name: Whale 75
  2. Angle: 9 degree
  3. Material: Alu
  4. Weight: 2.0 kg total as unbuilt kit
  5. Standard kit: Top and bottom case. PCB. Brass weight. (Brushed and clear-coated?)
  6. Mounting method: Sandwich
  7. Color Offerings  Silver / Black / Burgundy Red / Military Green / Lavender Purple
  8. GB date: Dec. 19th 00:00 ~ 23:59 (1 day)
  9. GB period: Will announce later after we get the final order qty.
  10. Quantity: We will accept all orders within 1 day.
    But if the order exceeds our capacity, there is a possibility that we will have to close the form sooner.
  11. Order limitation
     We will allow 1 of each layout.
  • This will be only for international buyers.
    (한국분들은 린웍스 블로그를 통해 별도 진행)
  1. Price: 550 USD
  2. Top Case Layout  2 layout (Knob type or F13 type)
  3. PCB  QMK and VIA compatible. Please check more detailed specifications below:

Collaboration
We are happy to announce that we are going to work with 3 artisan makers for this Whale 75 project.
Each artisan makers made the color way choice for whale 75. They will come up with the matching colorway of their works.

  • Hello caps : Burgundy Red Bongo
  • Alphakeys : Military Green Keypora
  • CYSM : Lavender Purple Keyby
  • Each order with the payment is completed, will get raffle entry as their choice.
  • All the entry will be randomly selected between 3 brands.
  • For those who buys each layout, will get 2 entry.
  • We will allocate the total number of the sculpt into 3 different groups.
