Introduce：
Sirius LinkPad, a pad with Bluetooth5.0, hotswaps , RGB LEDs. When used as an artisan keycaps storage box, it has a built-in lithium battery for LED power supply, so can display artisan keycaps that need backlighting.
Sirius LinkPad is cooperating with LNE Lab , as the first product of the new Bluetooth 5.0 solution.
The exterior design draws on the commonly used Casio calculator, uses the double-sided sliding cover design, and through the angled chute, realizes the switch between the use mode of inserting the square storage mode on the front side and the 6° inclination angle on the back side.
The Sirius LinkPad body is top mounted, made of anodized aluminum, and the transparent upper cover is made of plastic. The space between the body and the upper cover can accommodate up to the SA profile keycaps.
PCB and Plate：
has two layouts: all 1U and standard PAD.
The PCB has Kaihua hotswap, RGB Leds and built-in lithium battery.The plate is FR4 , built-in antenna to enhance the wireless signal strength.
There is a customized software to flash firmware and upgrade, support mac and windows.
Download software： https://github.com/ln-org/lne_link
