Introduction

Hey everyone! I am Slattt and I am proud to present you my new set, PBTfans Bank Account.
As you already know, PBTfans is a quite new manufacture made by KBDfans, but which managed to provide high quality sets in this short time. It is going to be a doubleshot set, which means the legends are going to be injected, so no more fading or stuff like that! There are a lot of advantages like short manufacturing/delivery time, which is a big thing comparing to other manufacturers.
