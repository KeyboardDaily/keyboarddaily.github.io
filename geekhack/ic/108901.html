Seamless / Screwless / Top Mount / 60% / WKL
Hello Geekhack, thanks for taking a look at my first IC. I’m Lucas, or ookey in most places, and I’m based in the UK.
It’s certainly nothing fancy, just a clean and minimal top mount 60%.
Renders





Here’s how the top piece and base will be screwed together once assembled, before putting on the keycaps.

Details

  • 7° Angle
  • Uniform Bezels
  • No Externally Visible Screws
  • Seamless
  • ~18mm Front Height
  • 1mm Neoprene Case Foam Included / Potentially 3.5mm Neoprene Plate Foam Included
  • 6061 6063 Aluminium Top and Base / Anodised Black or Potentially E-White
  • Top Mount 1.5mm Plate / Anodised Black Aluminium / Potentially Polycarbonate

PCB and Layout

  • Soldered-only
  • ESD Protection
  • QMK and VIA Compatibility
  • Centred USB-C Port
  • No In-switch LED Support

    Only ANSI plates will be available in the GB, with both ANSI and ISO plates files available for those wanting to get their own plates made. Keys in blue are supported by the available ANSI plate, while keys in red will only be supported by the PCB.

    For round one there will be 25 group buy slots open plus extras, with the price still being TBD. If the first run is successful and there is still demand, I’m happy to do a larger round two in the future.
    I’ve set up a Discord server if you’d like to join and keep up with any updates - https://discord.gg/7PrkJcZ
    Please reply with any feedback or questions, or leave them in the Discord server. Thanks for reading

Status

  • PCB prototypes arrived
  • Close to ordering case prototype
  • Neoprene case foam arriving soon
  • Waiting for silicone feet samples
