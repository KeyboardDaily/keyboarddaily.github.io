Alright guys I am stepping into the ring for keycap group buys. I will NOT be doing this through PMK. This will be personally handled by myself and the wife, Alexiss. I will be the point of contact for this group buy.
Profile and colors
This will be DSA double shot with RDA for the purple and looking at NN for the black.

Yes I realize that this is just a reverse of BL’s broset. I have spoken with him and I have his blessing with this. This image will be the base set as well.
More pics to follow most likely as I get stuff sorted out for layouts and kits to be offered.
Kits
I am planning on having 1.5 mods with 7x space bar included in the base set. The \ and ` keys will be offered in both colors as well as the F5 through F8 in both colors for the symmetrical and traditional look. I don’t what to have TONS of kits so ISO will probably be bundled with 1800 kit, JD40 might be bundled with planck, and I am planning on bundling Ergo Dox and GH36 kits.
Included in the base set will be extra homing keys for Dvorak and Colemak. This is the major reason for the DSA profile so I don’t have to offer essentially a full set to support those alternate layouts as I believe those are the most popular layouts outside of Qwerty and other language layouts.
I will leave discussion open for kit combinations, along with legends needed for GH36, Ergo Dox, JD40, other layouts that might be popular enough with few enough keys to bundle with other kits. This is also a reason for DSA to minimize keys needed due to profile differences between rows.
Pricing
Right now I have zero clue where the costs will be, as I have not spoken with SP yet. I wanted to get a feel for interest on kits and such before talking with them.
Payments will be via paypal.
Adding possibility of PBT dyesubed. Image attached here and later in the thread.
