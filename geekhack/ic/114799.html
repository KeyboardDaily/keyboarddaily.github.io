Hi everyone!
I wanted to make a simple set in a vintage sculpt celebrating legendary off road vehicles from the past like the Land Rover Defender, Ford Bronco, and my personal favorite, the Toyota FJ40.

Specifications
Manufacturer: Signature Plastics
Profile: DSS 1-1-2-3-4-3
Material: ABS
Legends: Doubleshot
Colors: 2 stock and 1 Pantone
IC Form & Social Media
Please do me a favor and fill out the IC form, as it really helps! If you’d like, you can also follow me and my other projects on Instagram.

Kits
Base
The goal here is to provide a relatively all-inclusive base kit that can cover most layouts from TKL down to 40s and ortho.

Alternate Modifiers

Numpad

Novelties
I’ve kept this kit fairly slim for now. Let me know if you guys would like to see some more. Lately, I’ve been interested in exploring some Shift key novelties, so perhaps those will make an appearance here.

Deskmat
The deskmat was inspired by some of Toyota’s vintage marketing material and features a subtle topographic pattern in the background.
