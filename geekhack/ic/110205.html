Hi everyone!
The Lancelot themed kits are designed by Hongoo, partner of Wuque studio. Hongoo has been working on the design for the past few months until we share the designs here. Besides the designs, we would also want to share with you how we come to this IC.
Some considerations:
/# We want a decent PBT base that works for as many layouts as possible.
/# We hope the price wont break the bank.
/# We dont want to die of waiting.
So, here are what we got
/# We upgraded the base from 117 keys to 135 keys. So it even works with Alice layout now.
/# Super big Dye-subbed PBT base at almost half price of normal kits
/# Delivery schedule of 4-5 months instead of 1 year+
What we have been considering really for a loooong time?
Its the manufacturer!
Weve been considering other familiar manufacturers for over months, but finally choose current one. They have been manufacturing keycaps for over ten years and we love their keycaps a lot. We think its time to show the community some good new choices, just like we always do with previous products we launched.
Million thanks to @SirTimmyTimbit, @Alex, @Fen, who help us to improve!
