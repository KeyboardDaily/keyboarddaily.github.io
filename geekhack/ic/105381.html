Hello Geekhack,
and welcome to my Interest Check for my first GMK Design - GMK Neon City
Intro:
I have had a Theme like this a long time in my mind: Something more colourful, but not too bright. Or in other words: a darker Pink + Purple.
As I am a huge fan of what I call them “Neon Cities”, I tried my best and making this into a Keycap Theme, and have therefore designed a colourway, I am very happy myself, so to say.
Just imagine, a great night, with a drink or 2 and just starring out to Mega buildings, Skyscrapers - and of course Neon Signs.
Technical Infos:
I have picked Pantone colours, why you ask?
Simple: I don’t own a RAL colour sheet and Every attempt I tried on finding a good matching RAL-colour, I ended up disliking it because it always didn’t have the “punch” in contrast I wanted it to have.

FAQ:
This set looks a lot similar to Laser!
Well, this set is not as blue-heavy as laser, but the theme is similar, that might be the reason why this has been thought.
The mod legends have a too low contrast!
I think, that in reality, the contrast will look alright, but I certainly think about brightening it up a bit.
