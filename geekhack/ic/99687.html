Info

  • Alu colors: E-white, Black, Gunmetal (Dark Grey), Navy (dark blue), Silver, Burgundy (dark red), Midnight Purple (Dark Purple)
  • Materials: Full alu keyboard available, Full Polycarbonate keyboard available, a mix between Alu and Polycarbonate available, a lot of options to mix and match
  • Founder edition available (Only 25 units), a full brass bottom
  • Typing Angle: 7 degrees
  • Mount: top mount
  • Plate: Alu, Brass, PC
  • PCB: Hotswap and standard solder, all VIA compatible. Also a daughterboard PCB for the USB port
  • Box: There will be a nice custom magnetic box
