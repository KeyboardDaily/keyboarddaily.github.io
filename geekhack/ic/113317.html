Geekark Deity Series - Egypt
Deity Series - Egypt is the first design from Kivision Studio and it is a collaboration of 3 designers: Southeart, Linnanxue and Gezijun. This keycap set will be manufactured by Geekark.

Inspiration and Design
This set is inspired by Ancient Egyptian elements. Horus is one of the most significant ancient Egyptian deities who served many functions, most notably god of kingship and the sky.

Abstracted element from Ancient Egyptian Constructions.


Colorways and Kits
This design has 3 colorways inspired by colors of Ancient Egypt. We have two heights from Geekark to choose from XDA ( learn more about XDA) and Cherry.
Moonlight
Blue represents the night sky with golden latin as stars. Horus most often was depicted as a lanner falcon or peregrine falcon or as a man with a falcon head. Red falcon symbolizes Horus.
