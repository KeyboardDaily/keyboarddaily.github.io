I’ve been working on the set a lot over the past few days and wanted to update you guys since I have made quite a bit of progress. After taking a lot of the feedback into consideration. I have updated alot of the proposed kits and added some.
Examples of some layouts: http://imgur.com/a/fj9HU
The kits! http://imgur.com/a/f4mws
The following changes have been made since my initial interest check.

  1. Added symmetry to the 60% Mod Kits.
  2. Fixed the font on the mods to be the same level.
  3. Added a TKL kit for MatchaxM
  4. Added CTRL to Tsangan Kit for TADA68 compatibility
  5. Complete Numpad kit by adding homing and extra keys
  6. Fixed some of the legends for Planck kit (still needs some tweaking) Don’t worry final product will be centered… I promise
  7. Added ISO, UK, and International Kits (will be split into 2-3 kits)
    Added 40% Kit (Alphas+60%mod+40%kit should be compatible with at least Minivan, Minorca, JD40, JD45 etc) Please let me know if there are issues
  8. Homing kit was added for Dvorak and Colemak Users
  9. Ergodox kit was added for… Ergodox users. Will fix the bracket sizing inconsistency
