GMK Tuzi Inspired Custom Keyboard Bag
Hello guys! I made the website https://fromscratchusa.com/pages/custom-bags! I make custom keyboard bags for many different keycap sets, for example, Milkshake:

Note: Waiting for the new tags to come in before stitching it for the client.
**We have 150 options for colors, 22 strap colors, and even choose the color of your thread, and clasps. I really try to keep prices reasonable because I make these bags with my friend out of love.
**
Design Considerations

  1. No zippers so you do not scratch your keyboard when pulling it out.
  2. Double clasp mechanism so it is extra secure and has a satisfying metallic click when closing.
  3. Most color and design options on the market - any set, any design. We can try to do it!
  4. Strap options, wear it the way you want, katana style, small handle, or none at all
  5. Foam padding option - keep your keyboards free of everyday dents and scratches
    Design:

    The bag would be designed with the following shape, which is my chess piece set.
    https://fromscratchusa.com/products/chess-piece-keyboard-bag

    The keyboard would be designed with the colors both “Plum” and “Lavender” on my website.

    Sadly Vinyl can not be exactly color matched but I think it is very close to the original set. The eyes would be Little Purple Rinestones which are rivets and would hold the metallic clasps together.
    Unfortunately there is no way for me to really render bags, like keyboards. However, the bag would be designed with industrial marine vinyl, which is resistant to fading and is very thick, its basically made for boat covers. The glue used to hold down different parts like the mouth is permanent, washable and made for vinyl and used in bag making. We double stitch our clasps and rings to make sure they don’t fall out.
    Customizability Options
  • Standard layouts, 60%, 65% or custom sizes for $5 more.
  • Optional Padding
  • Strap selection - wear the bag like a katana, on your back, wear it with a mini strap, or off shoulder like a bag.
  • Matching pouches! Yes, you can buy a pouch that straps onto your bag with a hook for your cables, money, etc. It WILL match your bags design.
    I have most of the materials required, however I am waiting on the “Plum” Vinyl to arrive so I can make a real prototype,
    but since I have SO many keycap sets to make bags for its hard to see what people really want. I am going to work in the future on GMK Fuji, GMK Dots, and many others.
