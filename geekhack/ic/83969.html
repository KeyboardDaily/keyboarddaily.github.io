Hey everyone. Today, I’ve got a fun set to share.
I call it, GMK Plum.



More can be found here http://imgur.com/a/GiXpQ (Some pictures are not colour accurate, they are labelled as such)
GROUP BUY IS HERE https://www.massdrop.com/buy/massdrop-x-jessica-gmk-plum-custom-keycap-set?mode=guest_open
=======
This is a very unique set (as far as I’m aware!) and one I’m very proud of. The colours are calm, cool and original, ones you’ve likely never seen on a set before.
It will be in standard Cherry/GMK profile (1-1-2-3-4-4), made with original Cherry legends and thickness. You know the drill by now, I’m sure.
This set will be produced to the best of my ability. I don’t have the ability to say “I’m going to make this happen, regardless” but I’m going to do my best to make it happen, because I really want it to come to life.
To address some common questions I’m sure to get:
Yes, the alphas and modifiers are custom colours. (The red keys are not custom, but I don’t think RO1 has been used on a set before.)
Yes, I have done the best I can do offer complete support to all common layouts.
As is, this keyset will support: Standard ANSI, ISO (sorry non-English speakers :/), 1800, WKL, HHKB, FC660M, standard 75%, includes both scoops and barred homing keys, as well as some other oddball layouts.
Yes, this idea is original. I did not draw inspiration from any other sets. I literally had a picture of a plum www.mvfruit.com/wp-content/uploads/2013/07/Angelino_plum_03.jpg for my inspiration.
No, I will not be handling the group buy myself. I simply don’t have the time or resources to handle this it myself.
Based on the results of the poll I had live for multiple weeks, I will be running GMK Plum on Massdrop. ~75% of people wanted Massdrop, while only 25% of people wanted Originative. That’s just an easy decision for me to make.
I will do my best to answer any question you have!
With warm regards,
Jessica
