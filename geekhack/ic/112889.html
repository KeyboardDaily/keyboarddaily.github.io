The Keeb Summon Deskmat
By KCK
**Hello! I want to share with you guys a simple deskmat that I designed earlier this month. The concept was heavily inspired by the anime, Naruto. The desk mat is suppose to resemble a scroll with a summoning symbol drawn on the middle. I’m sure if you have watched Naruto, you will be familiar with this design.
