Introduction
All the precious moments of the past stuck in various places around the world and in the luggage you haven’t touched in years. Destinations, relationships and family all preserved in souvenirs and smoke covered denim jeans. GMK Lancaster is a recollection of a time in history and in our own lives filled with adventure, boarding passes, heartstrings and cheap booze. The reckless abandon and anticipation to go somewhere or see someone. It’s these memories that find us at 4am when we can’t sleep or when we hear the roar of a train passing by.

Specifications and Description

Keycaps are made by GMK Electric Design in Germany.
Keycaps are made from high quality, 1.5mm, double-shot ABS plastic.
Keycaps use MX stems and are compatible with any keyboard that uses Cherry MX style switches.
Deskmats are 900mm X 400mm X 3mm with printed cloth top, rubberized bottom, and stitched edges.
Products shown will be sold via a Group Buy by your local vendors around the world. Please refer to vendor information pages regarding Group Buy details.
Product images shown are renders. Final product colors may vary, especially if you are viewing on an uncalibrated monitor or device. Gaming Monitors that use TN panels are especially inaccurate for viewing colors. Your smartphone is probably the most color accurate device unless you have a special monitor. Kingk22 and I have calibrated the renders based on this recommendation.
