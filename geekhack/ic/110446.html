Hello everybody, we are a arts and crafts factory from China, two years ago I was at a close friends began to contact under the misleading of customization, and open the floodgates, began to try to draw and processing aluminum case, is to play or just a few close friends to share, this time in GH to bring a RTX3090 industrial style of a keyboard, I hope you appreciate all efforts put in this project.

Suite name: Hunter 3090
Highlight the RGB fan at the bottom:
1 In the middle of the fan is a brushless motor,
2 The fan blades are made of 6063 aluminum and are anodized
3. The RGB placed above the fan moves as the fan rotates.
4 The bottom of the fan is a piece of tempered glass.

General specifications:
1 Typing Angle: 7°
2 Plate mounting style：Gasket
3 Material: 6063 aluminum, PC
4 weight: Brass (Transparent electrophoresis)
5 PCB: 1.6mm black gold FR4, support QMK&VIA
6. Installation mode: Welding & hot swaped
Interface: USB Type-C daughterboard
Layout: WKL/WK/ISO/ANSI







case Optional color:
1 anodized black
2 Electrophoresis blue
3 Electrophoresis warm white
4 spraying granite color (refer to the rendering, GB will give the color number)
5 Polycarbonate: white frosted
