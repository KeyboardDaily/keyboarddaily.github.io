GB July 24 - August 24
Hi everyone, welcome to the interest check for GMK JAGS.
Change log and thoughts on R5 are in first reply to this thread.
Interest check form: https://forms.gle/8Yya3ZLNDY9y1jPE8

This set uses WS2 for the primary legends. All other colors were selected from the RAL Classic and RAL Design catalogs.
Below are the current iterations of the kits I would like to run. Kits are subject to change based on feedback.
Base:
This kit provides compatibility for 40%, 60%, 65%, 75%, TKL, 1800, and full-size boards.

Previous base kit:
More


Hangul:
The sublegends are meant to be UV printed. I prefer the look of accented sublegends over doubleshot here, but may opt for doubleshot if quotes (assuming this set makes it to that stage) are too expensive.

Extension:
The legends on the windowed keys are UV printed.

Spacebars:
Includes accented bars and Alice bars. The extra B necessary for the Alice layout is also in the kit.

R5:
The R5 kit includes compatibility for 7u bottom rows. Slimmed the kit down by excluding mods for 6.25u bottom rows to keep the price more reasonable.

Collaborations:
RAMA WORKS x GMK JAGS
Both caps are anodized aluminum with enamel infill.

Deskmat:
