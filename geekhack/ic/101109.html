About
GMK Rudy is inspired by the classic American football film, Rudy. Based on a real life story, the movie captures Rudy Ruettigers journey to play football for the University of Notre Dame. To escape his impoverished life as a steel mill worker with poor grades, he works with a tutor to eventually gain admission to his dream school. Once there, he earns himself a spot on the universitys legendary team where he wears jersey /#45 (pictured below).
This movie means a lot to us as it is a story about overcoming hardship purely through hard work and perseverance. Warren is actually a college football player at a small Division III school in Southern California. It is the love for this movie and for the sport of American football that really gave us the inspiration to design this set. We hope you all enjoy it as much as we do. And we urge you to watch the movie if you havent!
One thing we want to guarantee is our hard work and open communication. We will look to routinely update and check in on all threads. The potential success of this set is determined by your interest and passion for it. We hope that our love for it shines through.

Design
The colors of this set are wholly inspired by the school colors of the University of Notre Dame Fighting Irish. The set uses two RAL colors and one stock GMK color, for the purpose of accurate GMK color matching.

Kits
Base Kit
We didn’t want to make any compromises so we’re offering numpad, accent arrow keys, and ISO compatibility in our base kit!

Dark Horse Kit (Navy Alphas)
This kit, inspired by Rudy’s drive, will create a uniform navy keyset. The kit is named the Dark Horse kit as Rudy was an underdog throughout his entire life and athletic career.

Spacebars
We kept the spacebar kit basic, included are 1.25u, 1.75u, 2u, 2.25u, 2.75u, 6u (centered), 6.25u and 7u bars.

Change Log
6.13.19 - Updated Kit Renders. We changed the mods to icon+text. We also got a new helmet design that is more representative of Rudy’s, changed the Enter novelty to prevent any infringement of intellectual property, and added “Sack!” R4 1u novelties.
6.14.19 - Updated Base Kit Render. We changed back to icon mods. We were trying to tailor the set to match the novelties, and think it should be the other way around. Walker and I agreed the set looks cleaner with just icon mods.
6.22.19 - Finalized all kits and uploaded updated kit renders and board renders. The helmet design has been given the green light for GMK. We have a few more board renders planned for the future so stay tuned.
