Hi fellow GHer,
This is an IC to see whether there are enough interest for me to start making braided USB cables and sell it here.
I have been making my own braided cables for quite awhile now, sometimes when I post a picture of my keyboard, someone will ask about the cable and some even requested me to make them one. After buying and testing lots of wires from many different sources and lots of trial and error, finally I managed to pick a few wires that make great cables.
If you’re still not so convinced, please take a look at the pictures

-Update 1 (02/10/15)
Hi, thanks for all the positive feedbacks
I will list out stuff that I can get my hands on right now and explain a little bit of what I’m offering now.
Currently I will offer 2 types of wires, one is the sky blue one, it’s about .6mm in thickness, so it will need more wires (braid into one) to make it feel more substantial instead of feeling like hair thin fragile. Another option is the ‘white’ and black wires which are 1mm thick in diameter, the ‘white’ one is a silver plated copper wire, definitely audio grade and most importantly made in USA (I actually made one audio cable with it for my SE535 iem). The black one is oxygen free copper wire, made in Japan and audio grade as well. There will be another option for a clear wrapping material, which shows the copper color.
Now comes to the color, the .6mm wire is sky blue color, the ‘white’ wire actually have a hint of silver-ish look to it irl. The black wire, same as the pic, it’s solid black. I see quite a few of you requesting for a grey cable and Hoff requested for a CMYK for quite awhile now. Sorry to say I don’t have them in hand now, but I will try to find them from different sources, and if they don’t pass, I won’t offer them. As usual, quality comes first, also some wires are really not suitable for braiding as they don’t hold their shape very well (I’ve dumped more than 60m of wires atm). I actually found a source that offers quite many colors, I will try to buy some and try it out and update here in near future. Currently I still haven’t see a very accurate magenta color wire, they have translucent red and a 456gt kind of pink.
As for braiding options, there are 3 options for 4-wire braid (round, flat/#1, flat/#2). For 8-wire braid (square, round, flat/#1, flat/#2). Usually for thinner wires, I won’t go for a 4-wire braid, it will look and feel pretty awful, not that it will break that easily, but it feels like it. For a slightly thicker wire, like the 1mm thick wire, we can do both and it will generally look more robust.
I will use the gold plated ‘naked’ connectors like shown in pic, but if you really want those with a plastic housing, I can source it for you. Currently only offers USB male to miniUSB male. I can’t seem to find fancy color heat shrinks, good quality ones generally comes in black, white, clear, red and blue.
Pricing. At this point, I can’t give you a solid answer, but I can set a rough base price of US$10 for 1ft of the sky blue cable (as shown in pics above), and everything else add onto that price. The USA and Japan made wires are a little more pricey, but they are better in terms of audio quality but totally overkill as a keyboard cable. /*that is estimated price, not final price
Shipping. I will use registered mail with tracking most probably and that will be about US$10 depends on where you live on earth.
Phew, that’s super long
Most probably I will start offering the cables in late November, my final exam is coming pretty soon so I have to prepare for that.
Keep in mind that cables are made to order, so you’ll definitely have to wait for it.
Those points that I stated above are not solid yet, so don’t quote me on that, I can’t promise you anything atm.
