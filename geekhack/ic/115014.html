Midcentury Modern
Inspired by the futuristic/retro furniture and consumer appliances of the Mid-Century Modern design movement.
The use of warm wood colors and playful accent colors makes for a contrast to the utilitarian designs that
came before


P.S I do have a GMK set also in IC. Was not my intention to run another IC in parallel but did not want to run this
IC after the Phase One GB had ended. Hope this is ok, if not let me know

Colors
Midcentury Modern Colors
Main colors are a retro beige alpha paired with a milk chocolate brown that represents the popular usage of wood
in furniture and consumer electronics during that period. Bright orange was selected as the base accent color as it best gives
off the retro vibe. Using all stock SA ABS colors so no color matching issues and can keep costs and time to a minimal


Comparisons Color Ring
I understand there are quite some similarities with SA Carbon with the main difference being the usage of brown
for the mods for the warm woodsy vibe instead of the industrial grey. Also the beige selected looks a bit less “yellowed” giving
the impression that it is a well preserved antique piece
