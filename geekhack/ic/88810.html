Inspired by the beauty of the night, I present you: GMK Night Sky!
Both colours and kit design are not final, every suggestion is very valuable!
Here is a Google form for input on the kit design and colours.
Current colour and kit design
The kit is designed to both work with popular layouts and still be relatively cost efficient. Some of the current supported layouts are:

  • 104 key

  • 96 key

  • 75%

  • HHKB Layouts

  • WKL Layouts

  • Clueboards/660 layouts
