Hey Friends! Welcome to the Interest Check for the Neuron v2.






Features:

  • Compatible layouts:

More

  • 4-point top-mount sandblasted polycarbonate case with anodized aluminum weight.
  • 2.5 degree typing angle.
  • QMK-compatible PCB with bluetooth module and a cutout in the weight for a LiPo battery providing 3 months of continuous use.
  • Stainless steel lasered Walletburner badge on the back of the case.
  • Choice of a sandblasted polycarbonate plate for flex or a sandblasted brass plate for a firm but forgiving typing experience.
  • Anodized aluminum weight available in: Mango, Robin’s Egg Blue, Drank (purple), and Jet Black.
  • Price: $299 + tax and shipping.
  • Black PCB with gold contacts and daughterboard to permit flex and isolation from USB-C port.
  • Launch date TBD!
