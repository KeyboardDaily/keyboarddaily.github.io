  7. Introduction
    Pomelo is designed to be The Universal Minivan Keyboard. Pomelo utilizes the open source plate design from the Coriander and Hull keyboards, designed by trashman, as well as some usb-c to jst trickery to enable compatibility with most existing minivan pcbs.
    Compatible Van PCBs:
    Low Rider, TKC Minivan HS, JetVan, Ketch, BigBoi, Airport Shuttle, GetawayVan, Lesovoz, Minibaen, Minisub, Model-V, Omnibus, Taco Truck, Wanten, and m3n3van
    Not Compatible Van PCBs:
    TheVan44, Sketch, and Ice Cream Truck
