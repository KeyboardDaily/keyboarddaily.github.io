We are proud to announce our first collab, FR87, with ELEMENTO, a Californian design studio of a group of talented enthusiasts.
zFrontier will be running the IC and the future GB for FR87 by Elementos request.

FR87 is Elementos debut in the custom mechanical keyboard community. FR87 will feature a half-plate and Bluetooth design.
Jan 23, 2018
Concept
Elegant and modern, FR87 is designed to be the centerpiece of your desk. Enabled by either USB-C or Bluetooth connection, FR87 is perfect for any typing scenarios. This beauty also utilizes a half-plate design which is only implemented in a few premium custom keyboards for a unique typing experience. Two RGB LED ambient light strips are placed on the sides of the keyboard which will not only highlight the cutting edges of the bottom case but also work as indication lights.

Design
FR87 features multiple cutting edges and beveled designs to provide a modern and sleek aesthetic.
This keyboard has a mild 6° incline.
The case will be made from CNC machined aluminum with an anodized finish.


Bluetooth
FR87 will support both USB-C and Bluetooth connection. Wireless mode will be powered by AA(AAA) batteries.
