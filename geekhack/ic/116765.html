Hi!
This is MechJunkies, just as the name, I am a keyboard addict myself too. We are new in this hobby and is located in Budapest, Hungary, an incredibly beautiful city in the heart of Europe.
As of those keyboard junkies in Europe like me, it is frustrating sometimes for us to lay hands on keyboard stuff or the fees just kills it for us. With that being said, I am thrilled to introduce you The Maze 64 and is available for EU countries!
The Maze 64 is an all aluminum compact 60% keyboard using a tray mount system. This board features a minimal design, VIA support, a custom weight, and a PCB with full RGB lighting. All of this comes at the affordable price of 115 (VAT Included).
Available in black and white.













For more information please visit: www.mechjunkies.com
Build & Sound test by: shirotosushi
Maze 64 Specifications:

  • Case: Aluminum

  • Plate: Aluminum

  • Mount Type: Tray Mount

  • Typing Angle: 6°

  • Weight: 0.83kg unbuilt

  • Front Height: ~20mm

  • Size: 29.6 x11.3 x 2.95cm

  • Additional Features: Removable bottom case standoffs

  • Kit Includes:*

  • Top Case (Aluminum)

  • Bottom Case (Aluminum)

  • Aluminum Plate

  • Wired Hotswap PCB

  • Custom Carrying Case

  • Screws and rubber feet
