**/12/9/2021 MICRO UPDATE : Timeline changed to Q1 2022 in light of recent delays./
**Hello everyone!
I’m very happy to come to you all with an update on the Kraken keycap set. A lot has changed since the last interest check
The manufacturer has switched from Geekark (reverse dye-sub) to Wuque Studio (double shot PBT). Wuque Studio has been working very hard the last few months getting their legends, moulds and manufacturing process perfect. We have spent countless hours together working on the kitting, with feedback from our discord members.
There will be a limited in-stock sale of Kraken base kits around August / September, followed by an unlimited group buy of all the kits a few weeks later. A lot of you will get to experience and judge the quality of the keycaps before the majority of you commits to a group buy.
Wuque Studio has given me a 3 - 5 month fulfillment time for the group buy. Both the in-stock and group buy keycaps will come in high quality polycarbonate trays.
There is a ton of new information below, including RAMA cap, artisan collabs, new renders and more! Looking forward to hearing all the feedback

Ikki68 Aurora x Kraken

I am very excited to announce the Ikki68 Aurora x WS Kraken interest check
Wuque Studio has let me know that now they have the option of going for an aluminum top instead of the standard PC one, this means the cost would have to increase but the material would feel more premium, the acoustics will change and the weight will increase. I do not have the prices yet but I will update once I have this information . More details will come if this version goes through the IC .
