Hey everyone!
Some of you might remember i made an IC for mouse pad and a mouse speicial editions which matches your keyset. Say mice and mouse pad which suit the style of the keyset - be it Nautilus, Hyperfuse or what ever the popular keyset is. Though the keysets were taken just as examples but i hope if this here succeeds to see mouse pads also for these awesome keysets. As the title says we are talking here about the Carbon version of the Artisan Hayate Large size (420 x 330 x 4 mm) mouse pad. Link: https://www.artisan-jp.com/item_hayate_mid_eng.html
Anyway we did some progress and we got in contact with Artisan. Someone might know him but most probably the majority does not know Artisan. Well Artisan is manufacturer of high quality Mouse pads and his mouse pads are regarded to be one of the best. Artisan mouse pads do cost quite high (around 45$ for large Hayate version) but they are worth every cent.
The MOQ for the mouse pads is 300 for one color. Thus i will represent you some versions and gather your feedback. In the end i will do a poll to choose the best suitable for the community version.
Just in case Artisan does prints only in the corners and full surface print is not possible but also full print does not make sense, since its wise to keep the surface clean and in one color to keep sensor work as intended.
