Hi players! Good summer, everyone~
This is HiFi Fox (designer of inky) who is indulging in Genshin Impact!
(the other two members of our studio had no choice but to let this develop, which led to jumping the queue of design themes .)

Inazuma, Japanese Kanji 稲妻, means Thunder.
In the famous game Genshin Impact, it is used as a region name of the seven regions in Teyvat.
Everyone who has played the Genshin Impact must have a deep memory of Inazuma’s thunder, cherry blossoms and that bad fox lady.
The stories range from locking the country and hunting Visions, to opening and prosperity of the country.
As travelers, we have experienced and witnessed them in person.
Inazuma people gathered their dreams to fight together against upper power, Raiden Shogun’s self denial and determination to wipe out old evils, and the final Devils Night of Yae Miko to bid farewell to their old friends
Stories of hope shining in the dark, made us moved by Inazuma, the country that borne too much pain in the past.
This time, please allow me to express my personal feelings in this way.
At present, we sincerely invite you to feel the magnificence of the lightning strike.
At this point, a new chapter of Genshin Impact theme officially starts! )
The keycap will feature in Cherry Profle and contain the doubleshot and tripleshot base kits.
After voting in KP’s disscord group chat, we have finally decided two colors for the novel sets, which are Raiden Shogun and Yae Miko, named after the charactors in GI.
