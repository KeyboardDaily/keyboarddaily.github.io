Milkyway Cultured

About
The colorway was mainly inspired by one of James Bond’s apparel in the latest movie.
Since it is a fashion inspired set, I have tried to incorporate fashion elements into the designs.
While working on the novelties, I have grown a liking of the mustache designs, hence you will see some of those in the novelties.
