Hi mechanical keyboard enthusiasts,
Welcome to the first Interest check for VIENDI 8L  a custom mechanical keyboard kit.
Firstly, please take a look at this video, remember to turn on audio and fullscreen ;-)


I am working in the architectural field, using numpad a lot, but can not use it and my mouse at the same time with my right hand. This is the reason for the left numpad.
I am obsessed with minimalism and elegant yet practical. That is the reason for the layout, as less as possible without compromising with practical usage.
This is my first keyboard project, so the goal is to fulfill my passion, gaining trust of the community, not profit at all. That is why I offer an affordable price, yet it has to have something nice to worth what you pay for. Nice things are the showing-off brass plate, rotary encoder and sleek design.
///* Every design lines put in has been carefully considered in terms of aesthetic, functions, comfortable usage.
⚫ MAIN SPECIFICATIONS:

  • Case size: W390mm x H116mm, front height: 20mm, made of CNC 6061 aluminium. 6/* tilt angle.
  • Sandwich mount.
  • Exposed brass plate with surface treated to prevent tarnishing and oxidizing.
  • PCB and Rotary Encoder can be fully programable by QMK via VIA Configurator.
  • USB Type C port.
  • 2 Colors: Dark gray (named Shadow), Silver (named Mist) and limited color options.
  • Kit includes: Case, plate, PCB, screws, silicon bumpons. Under considering: includes the rotary encoder (EC11) and sound dampener foam.
