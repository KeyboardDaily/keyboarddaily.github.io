ABOUT
GMK Finer Things is a cap set based on a signature colorway well known in the world of luxury and frivolous spending.
This set is meant to be simple and bold, a combination that is easy to achieve, hard to master.
ANNOUNCEMENTS
Colored Pantone has been adjusted and the renders as well to reflect that change.
40’s related spacebars have been moved to the spacebars kit
Deskmat has been added
Added RAMA artisan cap
Booked collaboration with CYSM for Boos and Keybys
Candy Keys will be offering custom cables for this buy available worldwide.
Group Buy Date has been set for February 1 with Cannon Keys, Candykeys, iLumKB, and Daily Clack.
Due to lower levels of interest in this set, kits have been consolidated to help everything get made. The base kit now includes basic 40s (besides spacebars), ISO, and Numpad compatibility.
