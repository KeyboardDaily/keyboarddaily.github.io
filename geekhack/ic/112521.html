Interest Check Form
**﻿﻿Inspiration**
This set was inspired by my two pet Axolotls. Axolotls are a kind of salamander native to Mexico that having been showing up a lot lately in pop culture so I wanted to share my love of axolotls with the keyboard community.
**﻿﻿Kits**
Base
All about that base, this kit will cover most tkl, 60/65%s, and features UK ISO, alice B and F13 [size]
Numbers


The numbers Mason, what do they mean?
Larva (40’s)
Pour one out for the homies who miss this set. This kit should cover most of the popular 40% keyboards out there.
I opted to use icon mods for enter, shift, tab, and backspace because I think those legends in text + mod form are ugly on smaller u keys.
PLEASE fill out the IC for if you dislike this decision or if you want the set but I don’t cover your board!
Tails (Space bars) and Mini Bars
The Tails kit features a space bar in each accent color in 7u and 6.25u as well as an alpha 6u. That makes 7 tails
The mini bars kit features alpha and accent bars in all your favorite miniature portions.
To be clear these are two different kits!
Morphs (Accents)
Different colored axolotls are called morphs. These accents will match the cute novelties you’re going to buy.
Novelties

Each novelty kit features a color based on some of my favorite axolotl morphs. Repetition of novs is by design. Different layouts will need the faces on different rows to accommodate all 4. E.G. a TKL can fit all 4 in an f cluster but a 65 could put them in a macro column. The leucistic kit also has Ñ.
NorDe
Axolotls are probably illegal to own where you live but this kit isn’t.
