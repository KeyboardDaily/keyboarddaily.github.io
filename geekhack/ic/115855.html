Moza
Hello Geekhack, I’d like to introduce my recent 65% keyboard Project.






Moza is a 65% Gummy O-Ring Mount and I’d like to bring some new typing experience with this Gummy O-Ring Mount with its Meme Cut Plate that comes together with 3.5mm Poron Rubber dampening foam between the plate and PCB. And also we are going to have 1.5mm Poron dampening foam underneath the PCB. Hopefully this scenario will bring different and better typing experience.
Prototype
We are planning to having a prototype in this Jan or next Feb after CNY
Specs

  • 6063 Monoblock Aluminium Case
  • 7 degree typing angle
  • 5mm Brass Weight Insert
  • FR4 Plate with Skeleton/Meme Cut
  • 3.5mm Poron Rubber Foam between the Plate and PCB
  • 1.5mm Poron Rubber Foam underneath the PCB
  • Hotswap or Solder PCB with Daughterboard USB
  • Comes with Carrying Bag
