【IC】TX87 - New Korean TKL Custom Keyboard

There’s a new custom keyboard being developed in Korea @ kbdlab (what new?)
It’s at the prototyping stage. I’ll try to post the actual photos as they become available.
I spoke with one of the developers and it sounds like they are willing to make it available to GHers as long as there’s enough interest
It will be affordable, relatively speaking
I don’t have the exact pricing but I was told to expect around 200USD including the PCB. But that’s before shipping and other fees etc.
Some bullet points….

  • KMAC2/Orion like structure. Top/Plate/Bottom, bottom is angled
  • Top : anodized aluminum with many color options available
  • Plate : stainless steel (heavier) or anodized aluminum
  • Bottom : CNC’d Polycarbonate. It’s designed to allow the LED light through
  • There’s a 2mm stainless steel plate mounted on the bottom to add weight
  • PCB : programmable of course. supports Winkey/Winkeyless/ISO/etc.
  • Supports in-switch LEDs & RGB LED underneath the PCB ( like this)
    So if you’re interested, show your interest in this thread
