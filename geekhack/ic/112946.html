Welcome to the interest check of GMK Iced Orange Soda!

A SET FOR REFRESHMENT, FOR YOUR AND YOUR KEYBOARD.
Lets ask ourselves, whats better than the sensation of gulping down an iced cold drink on a hot summer day? A glass of Iced orange soda.
Some might say its scientific, it might be. But is it refreshing enough that you want more? Yes, it sure is. Here I present - GMK Iced Orange Soda.
The feeling of that sensation lies in the color of this set, two shades of orange contrasting the baby blue, representing iced orange soda in a chilled glass,
a feeling you can now share on and off your keyboard. The light color contrast (mods) between the orange and the cream also mimics the reflections when
one looks through the glass before consumption.
