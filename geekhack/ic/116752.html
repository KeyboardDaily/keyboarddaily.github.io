Welcome to the Canvas Collection.

“This is only a piece of art if I don’t return the money.”
The concept of this collection is to inspire creativity and to challenge
the status quo as a love letter to the Keyboard community.
Inspiration
We aspired to perfectly encapsulate the thrill ride that is creating “art” and having something as your own.
As an individual who dabbles in the world of art, I most certainly enjoy the childish feeling of excitement
whenever I start a new project while thinking of all the different possibilities unfolding right before my eyes with
the flick of a wrist. But I must not forget the creeping feeling of doubt about the imminent failure of your project
as I go deeper and deeper in the process. If it wasn’t for the satisfaction of seeing the end-result of a laborious passion project,
not much would be made in our beautiful art-filled world.
TL;DR: The thought process behind it is that by having a blank canvas in front of you, it would inspire you to get up and create your own mark.
