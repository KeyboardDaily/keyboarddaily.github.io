KAT Mint Chip
by Jakk

M60-A by Rama Works
This is my set, KAT Mint Chip. This set is based on everyone’s favorite ice cream, Mint Chocolate Chip!
For the colors of the set, I color matched exactly to the real thing, to get that perfect, creamy, green/blue color everyone knows and loves.
I went for a nontraditional legend style. Lowercase. I feel that the lowercase better exemplifies the flavors and overall feeling of enjoying
some ice cream straight out of the freezer, and as much as you might want to,
please don’t eat the caps.
Colors

Disclaimer: Please note that these are digital representations in RGB color that approximate the original Pantones.
The colors are subject to change. I will do my best to make the colors as close to the pantones as possible.
Kits:
Notice: all kits follow the 4-4-3-2-1-1 pattern. This means the F-Row keys are the same sculpt as numrow.
