Once upon a time in a land far far away…
Meet ePBT Inconceivable! Inspired by the magic and nostalgic allure of fairy tales and adventure stories, Inconceivable! seeks to channel the whimsy, romance, and humor of those beloved stories into a classy, unique keyset. Featuring a serif font, the set’s sophisticated design pays homage to the charming printed works of some of our great storytellers. With two inverse colorways inspired by the Pirates and Princesses who lead so many fairy tales, ePBT Inconceivable! is sure to delight fairy tale lovers and mechanical keyboard enthusiasts alike.
Some current thoughts…
Greetings Geekhack! This is a set that I’ve been working on for some time now, and I am thrilled to be ready to bring it here to IC with all of you! I am certainly still seeking feedback and thank you in advance for your polite, constructive criticism. I will do my best to balance all of the feedback I get as I continue to refine the set!
I also want to note that I am new to rendering and have done my best to match the rendered colors to the Pantones, but of course they are still digital approximations. In fact, to my eye, both colors skew a bit warmer in real life than the official hex code equivalents. The pink is a light neutral to slightly warm pink, and the teal is a rich, saturated one that falls fairly evenly between green and blue.
