A fun, fruity, and most importantly chewy keycap set!
Please do not actually consume keycaps.

**Thoughts behind kitting
**

More
Because this set uses full normal dye sub, I was allowed to kit out a relatively expansive base kit, including alice bars, ISO support, F13, among others. Additionally, due to the inclusion of alternate Hiragana alphas, I was able to cater to the 40s community by giving support to a wide range of boards such as the Planck, Preonic, and other 40s/ortho boards, simply by purchasing the alternate alphas and the 40Â’s kit. When these two kits are paired with the spacebar kit, which includes 2x 3u bars as well as accented alice bars and a range of 6.25/7u bars, virtually any less-than-60 percent keyboard can be kitted out without purchasing the base. GB Date/Timeline
**Because ePBT is redoing their reverse dye sub molds and this set uses reverse dye sub in the accent kit, it would not be possible to run this set until the molds are completed. There is no specific timeline on when these molds will be done, it could be anywhere between a couple months. We do however know that they are on the last row, meaning it should be sooner rather than later. I hope to run the set soon after the molds are completed.
Accent kit has changed to full dye sub. This is for a number of reasons. First of all, it allows the set to run without waiting on molds. Second of all, I believe that the black legends make the accents easy to pair with other WOB and BOW sets. With this, we are aiming for a GB date of early September.
