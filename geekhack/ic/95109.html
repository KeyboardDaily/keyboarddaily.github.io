2018 is year of the yellow!
IC FORM https://goo.gl/forms/UVTRQxw2NllitlVq1
Yeah, uh huh, you know what it is

This keyset is heavily inspired by the colors of the watster keycap. Since many yellow keyset designs are popping out this 2018, why not make more?!

  • Colors used are: CV & CR from GMK

  • Rendered set is based from the base set of GMK Samurai
    Base Set

    Majority of the suggestions is to have an affordable set. I know GMK is not “cheap” but i’ll try to lower the cost as much as possible. I trimmed down the base kit with the goal to lessen the price. I basically removed the compatibility with ortho and 40%. The removed keys can be added with a 40s/ortho compatibility kit if people are interested
    Revised base set
    http://www.keyboard-layout-editor.com//#/gists/3884ac5f6fa47f9216478ad8e38c1d70
    Yellow add-on kit:
    http://www.keyboard-layout-editor.com//#/gists/fc1e9ba1ba752531706ecb9ef8d2136b
    To add: Novelties Kit, 40s/Ortho Kit
    I just rechecked the colors and here are the color samples from GMK including CR, GE1, CV, N6. Some suggested to use GE1 instead of CV but I think CV would be better. GE1 is too pale? It’s lighter but I think that makes it look dead and not really vibrant. So, final decision is that I will stick with CV



    Novelties

    R3 (for Capslock) From the song black and yellow. This can also fix the problem of adding multiple yellow accent keys. This can work as a universal key for FN, CTRL or Capslock since “You know what it is”
    R1 This key will come with the community icon kit. The text is in Baybayin, an ancient Philippine script and it says MKBWPH, our local mechanical keyboard community in the Philippines.
    R4 Bee and honeycomb novelties. Will come in 1u and 1.25u sizes.
    R4 Pixel art Thunderstone and pikachu novelties. Will also come in 1u and 1.25u sizes.
    Let me know what you think!
