GMK Juvenile Galant is a cherry profile keycap set inspired by the character XingQiu of MiHoYo’s Genshin Impact, which has been designed collaboratively between Buburoo & Nathan_.
The set takes inspiration from XingQius character appearance and attire. A jacket made up majorily of black, with a subtle blue-grey design and adorned with gold decals, highlighted by a white frilled undershirt.
