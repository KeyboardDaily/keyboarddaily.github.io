Hello friends of Geekhack!
I’m here to introduce you the Matrix-01 R2 keysetSHURIKEY Matrix-01!
The Matrix-01 R1 was launched by us 3 years ago, and the manufacturer was GMK. Most of them are sold in China, and only 250 sets were produced in the end. Over time, Matrix-01 has gradually been recognized and loved by Chinese players. Therefore we have decided to do another round for it with almost completely redesigned novelties and bring it to intl as well. After comprehensive consideration, we decided to cooperate with SHURIKEY this time.
btw, if you want to know more about R1, you can find it here: https://matrixzj.github.io/docs/gmk-keycaps/Matrix-01/
