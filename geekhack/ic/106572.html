Introductions:

Hello everyone, this is the first time I ever post on Geekhack, before getting to the keyboard, I would like to introduced myself a little:
I’m Zlane and I’ve been apart of the mechanical keyboard community since early 2019 when i bought my first mechanical keyboard, the gk61 and ever since then I’ve been really into custom mechanical keyboards, after sometime being in the hobby, I saw that there haven’t been alot of special or unique keyboard designs being put out that I like, most are just rectangle on a slope or a brick, I think theres nothing wrong in simplicity, I still love simple boards like the tofu, the60, rama, salamander,… but after a while I got bored of those repetitive designs, after being inspired by boards such as the Lynn dolphin, the cod67, matrix noah, I have decided to learn about designing keyboards, to bring something new to the table, to make a board that can differentiate itself from the other boards. After a few months of learning, I have finally made a keyboard that I really liked, so that brought us today to my new keyboard design, the MK-06





Inspirations:

This keyboard was inspired by Sci-fi spaceships and mecha designs in movies and anime and video games, with small details carved in to the weights to add depth to the design of the board
Keyboard Specs:

Mounting: Top mount
Angle: 8 degrees
One large weight with 3 accent weights on 2 side and on the back
Optional top corner blocker
Future plans:

Currently I have no plan of making a prototype or opening a gb of this keyboard anytime soon since I’m still in school and I will have to focus on my studies for the upcoming finals exams, if everything goes well, I might make a prototype of it and open a gb for this next year
