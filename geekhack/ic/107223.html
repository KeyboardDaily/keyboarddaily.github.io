Why it’s faster?
It has Unique co-processor with patented dynamic debounce technology plus 32bit ARM 70MHz main control chip to ensure every demand on every key can be handled in 1 ms, no double or missed key register. When traditional keyboards normally use 8bit ARM 16MHz alone, they will need 20 ms to handle debounce delay. Even worse, some keyboards just shorten the necessary 20 ms without debounce tech, when switches get ageing, double or missed click will happen frequently. You can see how fast the keys are scanned by the software and how quickly they react to software demand in the video too.
