Hey Geekhack!
Today I present to you GMK Deathly Queen! You may have previously seen an IC for this set on here, I took it down because I was not happy with the original design so I reworked the set! Changed the kitting, adjusted the sublegends and added a “Novelty” Kit! The set will feature 6 custom pantone colors and feature a Katakana sublegend base kit. My reasoning for the Katakana is because a lot of the names in Jojo are borrowed words, so Katakana was very fitting for the theme! The Novelty kit features a signature katakana character “Go” twice, when used in succession as an onomatopoeia it is associated as “Menacing” which has become popular in the Jojo series typically whenever a villain is around. I hope you enjoy this IC and have a good rest of your day!
