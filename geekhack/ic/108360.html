“I want you to make a contract with me and become a Magical Girl!”

SA Magica is a sculpted SA set based on the popular Magical Girl series, Madoka Magica. The colorway is inspired by the series protagonist, Akemi Homura, as well as Walpurgisnacht’s barrier.
GB OPEN NOW! https://geekhack.org/index.php?topic=111763.msg3025940
Join the MisonoWorks Discord for updates: http://discord.gg/Tk6Jw3Z
General Info:

  • To be manufactured by MAXKEY
  • 12343 sculpted profile
  • Doubleshot ABS
  • Purple on grey alphas, grey on purple mods, grey on white accents
    Update 2021/03/15
    The GB is tomorrow!
    The Akemi deskmat was axed due to copyright concerns, which is understandable. (this stuff is part of the reason why I am no longer going to run IP based sets in the future haaaah)
    We now have a cable collaboration, as well as a metal artisan with thok!
    HectoringMK, based in the UK, will be working on WEIPU connector cables for this set.

    As for THOK, we’re working things out… the artisan might come a bit after the GB date due to unforseen circumstances.
