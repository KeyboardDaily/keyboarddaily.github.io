XOX70
Introduction
Hello Geekhack friends, after a year in development please allow me to introduce XOX70 an FRL TKL or in my own words the better-looking 70%
XOX or Hugs and Kisses is a personal project I started designing back in May 2021, during the strictest lockdown in Melbourne Australia where I rarely get the chance to meet my friends and family and the easiest way to communicate is via typing. After using many keyboards and running a few proxy group buys Ive set out to design a board that meets my own expectations.

Spec Summary

  • 8-degree typing angle with 2 internal brass weights (E-Clear coat treated)

  • Screwless exterior design (adjusted for switch & plate clearance and ease of assembly)

  • Gasket Leaf Spring (full plate & half-plate, multiple materials)[//*]

  • Solder and Hot-Swap PCB with flex cut (VIA ready) 1.6mm thick

  • Carry case included

  • Pricing ~ 380 USD for a standard kit



    ﻿Standard Kit Planned Contents

  • Top & Bottom case with Badge, LED diffuser & weights pre-installed

  • Solder PCB + Daughter board + JST cable

  • Full Aluminium Plate

  • JST cable cover

  • Gasket poron foams (with extra)

  • Rubber Feet

  • Assembly Screws + Torx driver

  • Badge light-blocking foam

  • 3u stabilizer wire x 2

  • Anti Static sheet, Poron case & plate foam

  • Carry Case
    ﻿Extras

  • Full & Half plates in FR4, PC, Aluminium

  • Solder & Hot-swap PCB with Daughterboard and cable

  • 3u stabilizer wire pack (2 wires per pack)
