The GB thread is now live.
GB has been cancelled. I am determined to make this set a reality as I trully love it, and I will revisit it in Q2-Q3 2022.
I’ve always been fascinated by Peru, it’s landscape and its mysteries.
After taking you to the desert with Oasis and into space with my own spin of Hyperfuse, I am taking you with my fiancée and I to Peru.
Fasten your seatbelt, we are leaving soon! Welcome to the IC for


Let’s get right into it, shall we?

I went for pretty standard kitting for those but feel free to let me know if I missed anything







The deskmat is really simple and represents the Nazca lines visible in the country, some are also present in the novelty kit, others are just for the deskmat. One of those has been made especially for the set, as an homage to the original Nazca lines, as it actually does not exist.

I am happy to say I am doing a collab with Asero Foundry and we will offer both a return and an escape key in the same color as the modifiers:

These Asero Foundry keys will be available from all local vendors, except for KBDFans who will unfortunately not run them.


GB Date: November 17th!











For those that are interested, here are the colors I picked for the set. Samples have been asked and I’ll make sure to get the before the GB.
As the name indicates, the main inspiration for the set comes from the Maras salt ponds which I’ve always been fascinated by; the accent kits are inspired by the reflects in those salt ponds. I hope you’ll like them!
