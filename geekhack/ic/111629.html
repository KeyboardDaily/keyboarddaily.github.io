Hello Geekhack!

We’re KeebsForAll, a small new business aiming to bring you affordable keyboard parts to get started in the hobby, and we’re very happy to present to you this breakfast set dubbed the Sunny Side Up!
IC FORM LINK
We’ve teamed up again with Nhils, who designed the novelties on our previous PBT Doggie set and worked hand in hand to bring you this colorful kit.
Here’s what Nhils has to say about it :
I can’t start my day without a hearty breakfast, and I hope this set captures even a tiny bit of the joy I feel waking up fresh in the morning to some warm toast and a good cup of coffee (tea is also very good!). The colors were Pantone matched to actual egg yolks, avocado, and strawberry jam, for full authenticity, and the font is called MADE Waffle because I truly had to go all the way on this set!
We’ve tried to make this kit as beginner friendly & accessible as possible, without compromising on looks nor compatibility :]
Because we only carry 60% layouts in our shop so far, the kit was meant to cover that size or TKLs, as they are the more beginner friendly layouts. As we’re still just starting out, and mean for our items to stay in stock, we’re trying to keep the costs affordable to us, thus this kitting choice. We welcome your feedback in the IC form or on this thread as to how to best fit your board!
This set is mostly reverse dye sub, on a DSA profile ; and we’ve chosen to go full-on novelty mod legends because… why not? We remember where control alt and shift are, having them be a smiling banana is all the more fun, and having a uniform profile makes them adaptable.
This kit comes along with two deskmat designs and a cable collab! We’re very excited to have a matching set.
