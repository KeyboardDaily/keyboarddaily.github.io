1. INFORMATION

  • Top housing: Custom nylon blend BBN top housing

  • Spring: dual stage 63.6g (builtbynim recommended/Researched) or 68g gold plated spring

2. PROGRESS/ RESULTS
2019-2021
-Builtbynim linear switch was designed using client commission data and personal switch sound preferences to produce a unique sound profile and a snappy feel.
12/08/2021
-Initial prototyping done with JWK and TECSEE,
-TECSEE manufacturing was chosen for v2 prototypes.
01/09/2021- (testing phase)
-Dual stage springs tested for spring sound stock vs lubed (105g0)
disclaimer/* due to the lack of research and current developments in dual stage springs, there are some spring sound in stock form/*
Results: spring sound little to non-existent with 105g0 application in bag lube form.
-Dual stage spring characteristics tested on switch housing lubed with thick 205g0
Results: due to the snappiness and higher rebounding properties of the dual stage spring, switches retained their snappy and forceful upstroke even after a thick 205g0 application. 205g0 application increased the “thockky” low pitch sound of the switch while remaining snappy. (205g0 recommended for modification).
(1:Opblack, 2:Lavenders, 3:NK creams 4: builtbynim dual stage spring)

06/09/2021- (testing phase)
-BBN (builtbynim) switches v2 tested against popular switches
BBN vs lavenders, milky yellows, opblack, marshmallows, banana splits (all switches used were lubed by 205g0 on housing (top+bottom only), 105g0 on springs and tested on actylic switch tester)
Results:
smoothness/scratchiness: similar to lavenders and banana splits, smoother than milky yellows, marshmallows and opblacks
sound profile: Unique sound profile, deeper tone than lavenders, opblacks, milky yellows, marshmallows, and banana splits on acrylic tester
(1:Opblack, 2:Lavenders, 3:NK creams, 4:Builtbynim stem)
