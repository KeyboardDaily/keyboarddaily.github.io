Update /#1, and More Renders! (8/18/19):
GMK Delta will be running on CannonKeys, sometime in November. Proxies still in the works.
The set will be divided in 2 kits, Base, and Spacebars. (ISO is in the base kit!)
There will only be ONE novelty in two colors, which is the Esc/Triangle. in R1.
We will be going with the Turquoise/Teal called VBV, from Signature Plastics also known as /#5dceba. Instead of Pantone 570 C, as originally shown.
We will also be ditching the remaining chosen Pantones, and instead are going with RAL for the Black Modifiers, and GMK’s WS2 (Cool White) for the White Alphas.
