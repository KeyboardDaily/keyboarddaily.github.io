Blu, Is Beautiful [IC-/#1]

Hello there people!
The amount of beautiful, simple sets we have coming in from GMK and SP is stunning. One of my favorites being the themes around Space Cadet & Symbiosis and its simple All blue kits.
But what about my DSA People?! We, IMO, dont have enough high end beautiful double shot DSA sets out in the market and Im wanting to add at least one to the count.
This is aimed at the look of Blue Cadet but without so much accent. Though that can change with Interest.
Full DSA Space Cadet would be amazing IMO
