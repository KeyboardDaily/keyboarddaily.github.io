Venn
65% leaf-spring keyboard

This is a 65% keyboard that combines the aesthetic of my first design, the Chiwi60 with elements from the 7V. I normally prefer 75% over 65% but I’ve noticed that my fiance pushes her 7V forward a bit with her Macbook to make space when she wants to use the latter so I thought that a 65% would suit her better.

  • 1. DETAILS*

  • Case Angle: 7°

  • Materials: Anodised aluminium chassis with coated brass or stainless steel weight.

  • Plate: 1.5mm POM leaf-spring

  • PCB: WT-65G designed by Wilba.tech with USB-C daughterboard - configurable with QMK/VIA. You can purchase compatible PCBs through Salvun and Keycult.
