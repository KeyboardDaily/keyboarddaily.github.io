ELLYA - Linear/Tactile Keyboard

I would like to introduce you to Ellya, a premium keyboard based on the Alice layout.
Just like switches I would like to offer two different variants of Ellya, one Tactile and one Linear.
The Tactile Variant which will include the horizontal ridges across the board
And the Linear Variant which will have a smooth surface finish.
Ellya - Tactile Variant
(Note: This was a version 0.1 prototype of only the top plate.)


Ellya - Linear Variant
(Note: These are renders I do not have a prototype of this variant yet.)


First IC form: https://forms.gle/3pygktRM5vNCAkwKA
The Build:
Mounting: Gasket Mount
Colors: TBA
Plate Material: Waiting for feedback from IC Form
PCB: Not yet designed-
Waiting for feedback from IC Form but will include a USB C daughter board, ESD protection and will be QMK configurable
Rotary encoder/s: Waiting for feedback from IC Form(see below for more details)
Hot swap: Waiting for feedback from IC Form (Soldered version/hot swap version)
RGB: Waiting for feedback from IC Form (No RGB/ Per key)
Rotary Encoder/s:

In the design of the PCB I plan to allow for a switch or rotary encoder to be soldered into place - you can choose if you want to solder a rotary encoder in place or not
PCB Design Option 1: 1 Optional Rotary Encoder positioned at 1 indicated in the picture above.
PCB Design Option 2: 3 Optional Rotary Encoders positioned from 1 - 3 indicated in the picture above.
Extra Info :
Length: 180mm/7.087inches
Width: 400mm/15.748inches
Peak Height: 27mm/1.062inches
The markings on the Tactile Variant are not steps, they are ridges and are not sharp so they will not cut you.
Images of the Tactile Ellya Variant: https://imgur.com/a/nDDtGvP
Renders of the Linear Ellya Variant: https://imgur.com/a/j9VvASj
