Basic Info.

  • Layout. 75%
  • Color: Aluminum with anti-oxidation surface treatment
  • Material. Top and bottom case aluminum, logo is Brass
  • Degree: 7°.
  • Structure: no plate spring washers
  • PCB: Hot-swap and C-type PCB (including LED part)
  • Plates：1.5mm PC
  • Weight: Nickel-cobalt alloy
  • Logo：Brass
  • Spacer: 3mm poron
  • Will use Type-C
  • Supports Bluetooth
