Hello and welcome!
After searching through many different sets of keycaps, I was shocked to find we were severely lacking in green and brown keycap sets. So I felt like it was time to change that. And what better way than to honor the planet we all live on.
The Story
Quote It seems to me that the natural world is the greatest source of excitement; the greatest source of visual beauty; the greatest source of intellectual interest. It is the greatest source of so much in life that makes life worth living.
 David Attenborough
From a brand new creator and designer, Omnipotent/io2red brings us, Planet Earth! A keycap set that is inspired by the beautiful and amazing planet that gives us all life.
May I present to you…

FAQ
Q: Where will this be dropped?
A: I am not sure yet. But there is a good chance it will be on Massdrop or PMK, unless someone can help me organize it.
Q: Why doeshave this button? [pic]
A: I am a usually a Full keyboard user. All of these child kits have been developed with my own personal research and might not be perfect. Some could potentially be optimized better. If you have any suggestions I would love to hear from you!
Q: Is this set compatible with Logitech G710 or Corsair Strafe?
A: No, not entirely sorry. There is no 6.5u spacebar included in this kit. You will need to find or use your own.
Q: What are the color chips used in this set?
A: &
Q: How would I cover a…?
A: Each keyboard can vary greatly. But I have tried to accommodate as many different layouts as possible.
Full Size keyboard: Alphas + Modifiers + TKL Kit + pH Tester
ISO keyboards: Alphas + Modifiers + ISO / UK Kit (+ TKL Kit if TKL or larger & + pH Tester if you have a Numpad)
Standard TKL: Alphas + Modifiers + TKL Kit
Winkeyless keyboard: Base + Mods + TKL Kit + Tsangan + Spacebars
Infinity 60% Hacker/Standard: Base + Mods
Standard 60% (ANSI): Base + Mods
Standard 60% (ISO): Base + Mods (Depending on layout, ISO / UK Kit may also be necessary)
Whitefox, Neutrino, RedScarf68: Base + Mods + TKL Kit + Tsangan + Spacebars
Leopold FC660M, Clueboard: Base + Mods + TKL Kit + (Tsangan OR Novelties)
JD40: Alphas + 40’s Kit + Spacebars
Planck or Preonic: Planck Kit
ErgoDox: Alphas + ErgoDox Kit
HHKB-like keyboard (Duck Viper, Duck Eagle, some GH60 layouts, some KMAC Happy layouts): Base + Mods + Tsangan
