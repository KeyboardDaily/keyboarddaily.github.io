  • 1. Design Insight*

  • Dual Rotary Wheels*
    Dual, independent, programmable, and hand-sized rotary wheels that are user-replaceable with a multitude of materials and finishes. Independently reprogram them in the HYTE nexus software; control your window scroll, system volume, app volume, lighting, magnification, and much more with API support! Integrate them into adobe API or OBS API to control brush size or scene volume; use them as you would a mouse and have the freedom to reprogram them for any occasion.

  • Architectural Light Blades*
    Inspired by the NYC Oculus, keeb’s light blades absorb the light from 64 equally spaced LEDs and softly bend around the casing, doubling as a sound baffle. Sitting behind the frosted polycarbonate, the blades create a diffused light show that envelops the space around you with active ambient lighting. Every action you take on-screen will spill out into the physical world, fostering a more seamless conversation between man and machine.

  • Case Design Philosophy*
    We started with the idea that a keyboard is a conduit between the digital and emotional world, existing in the physical space. Its top half was designed to cater to physical touch. The dramatic angles fill the joints of your fingers and the soft-contoured surfaces hug your fingertips. The bottom half was designed to immerse the physical world with the digital visuals we experience on screen. Inspired by black holes, the hand-sized rotary wheels anchor the middle acting as an event horizon, where physical touch creates precise digital control.

  • 2. Specs, Parameters, Dimensions*

  • Typing Angle: L1 5.5°, L2 9.5° with stackable magnetic feet

  • Mounting: Suspended Gasket Mount

  • Case Material: Top - Aluminum l Bottom - Polycarbonate (frosted, bead blasted)

  • Plate Material: 1.5mm Aluminum

  • Switches (included choice of): Durock Linear Yellow Smokey (55g), Durock Linear Pink Smokey (65g), T1 Tactile Teal Smokey (67g)

  • Stabilizers: Durock V2 Smokey Gold-Plated Stabilizers (PCB Screw-In)

  • Rotary Wheel: Aluminum, independent dual-scroll, programmable, and user-replaceable

  • Case Dampener: Black silicone (Shore 00)

  • Weight: 1.24 kg (2.73 lbs)

  • Dimensions: L - 348.97mm l W - 144.94mm l H - 59.07mm

  • Front Height 17.78mm

  • Colors: Top Case: Silver PVD finish - (Black and Chrome finishes will be available if there is enough interest)

  • 2a. All-Inclusive and Hassle-Free*
    We partnered with Durock to provide you their best switches and stabilizers out of the box - guaranteeing a pleasant building experience. Beginners and enthusiasts alike will have all the tools and guidance necessary to create an amazing typing experience. By removing the uncertainty and wildcard aspect when purchasing parts you can warmly welcome your friends and family to the hobby with our all-inclusive keyboard.

  • CMF Changes*

  • Scrapped original glossy white color and replaced with bead blasted aluminum finish

  • Added top cover bead blasted black and chrome finishes

  • adjusted finish on bottom plastic to be more frosted for better light diffusion

  • Changed plate finish to bead blasted aluminum for better LED reflection

  • Changed gasket color to black to contrast plate

  • Mechanical Changes*

  • Reinforce structure to add rigidity and weight

  • Changed blocker removal mechanism to keycap puller instead

  • Reduced the types of screws used in assembly

  • Adjusted the light bridge for better light diffusion and less hotspots

  • Revised magnetic feet design

  • Electronic*

  • Add additional LEDs at keyboard corners for better coverage

  • Changed PCB wire routing and color to be less visible through the case.
