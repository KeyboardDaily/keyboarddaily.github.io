About
This is a gold on white set with versatility thats only limited by your imagination. White and gold invokes the image of divinity to some, a symbol of royalty to others, and for the rest another cute af color scheme. Whatever you imagine this keyset to be, make it your own.
GMK Honey is here to stay, with the support of a solid team. Scroll down to see the list of vendors and artisans.
Honey captures the versatility we were looking for; the versatility to treat wounds for the Egyptians, preserve seeds for the Greek, flavor tea for our mornings, and provide a theme for this unique keycap set.
This is a design that me and my roommate have been working on the past few months. We have a few color variations and novelties available to solidify the theme while offering more flexibility.
Is this your Israel? Your next piece to your end-game board? Your next cutesy keycap addition? Let us know if youre interested and what we can do to bring this one step closer to your dream keycap set.

Color Picking - COMPLETED
❁ Oct 30, 2019 - Additional pantone chips received; PANTONE COLORS UPDATED: 124 C > 123 C | 7571 C > 7570 C
We acknowledge the concerns of the colors being too bright with possible contrast issues. We thought so as well at first, but the colors come out much differently when they’re on an actual chip. After reviewing multiple shades similar to the originally decided pantone colors, we believe these colors will better reflect what people expect from the renders. No camera and monitor will perfectly reflect the colors, but we uploaded a picture on our Instagram if you’re interested in seeing the chips.
