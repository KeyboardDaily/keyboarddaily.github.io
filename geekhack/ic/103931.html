Void Radiant
Hello geekhack, this is smol from Void Case.
Void is a one man’s project out of Finland set to provide premium keyboards and SFF PC cases, with a focus on customer satisfaction.
What is Radiant?
Radiant is a tenkeyless keyboard design a long while in the making. Radiant started as a project to create the perfect TKL for myself, and later evolved into something I hope to offer to the public.
The weight for the keyboard is inspired by the Greek goddess of the dawn, Eos. She is represented by the sun in the bottom and the bright brass finish of the weight, spanning nearly the whole bottom of the case.
DETAILS

  • Case: Sandblasted and anodized aluminium 6061, WKL and WK options
  • Weight: Polished brass with a radiant logo
  • Plate Material: Aluminium stock, brass, PC half and full plate optional
  • Mounting style: Top mount
  • Typing angle: 7 Degrees
  • Color Options: Matte Black, Purple, Deep Blue, Bordeaux, White Gold
  • Logo optional
  • PCB: USB-C with QMK compatibility
    (compatible with H87A and Hiney FR4 plates)
  • Manufactured by Salvun
