About

Hi everyone, we are Milkyway Keys.
We are a relatively new PBT keycap manufacturer from China and we have ran several China exclusive GB since 2020. We have been continuously improving our product and now we are confident to present them to keyboard community around the world.
Currently we have very short lead time (1~2 months after GB) and we are open to designers to run their sets with us!
Today we present you our own set: PBT 02 & PBT 015. We designed these two sets due to popular demands from our fans from China. We are aware that is existing set on the same theme but this is our brand new take on the theme.
Please fill out IC Form if you are interested!
Please join Milkway’s Discord Server for more updates!
