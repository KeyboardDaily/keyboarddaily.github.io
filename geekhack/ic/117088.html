DEEP SEA KRAKEN DESKMAT
Hello guys, this is our very first post on GH since the first day we joined the hobby. Since we are all amazed by the beauty of deep sea creatures.
We come up with an idea, “Why don’t we create an art of a Kraken and spread it out to everyone?”.
It was an evening back in last August when I talked the idea to my friend - Kien.tap.ve/#1645 and then the whole thing started!
At first we wanted to run something like this

But then we decided to follow up the first sketch of the Kraken. And now - May 2022 Kraken deskmat IC is live!!!
When we finished the work, we are so happy with how it turns out to be I really hope you enjoy it as much as we do!!
Your comments and suggestions will be very much appreciated.
