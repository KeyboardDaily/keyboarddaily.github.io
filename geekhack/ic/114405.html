Introducing AlphaKeys latest development, KTT MALLO!
As of late KTT has made an astounding appearance with their high-quality switches paired with budget-friendly prices. KTT has made their presence known with their linear line of switches but lack a tactile line. We at AlphaKeys have been working very closely to bring you a never seen before KTT switch. The switches offer a premium light tactile feeling from which cherry mx brown users would love.
The KTT Mallo are based of off the loved (or hated) Peeps marshmallows!
Why did we make Peeps themed switches?
Well in the simplest explanation, my wonderful girlfriend wanted to impact the latest AlphaKeys project! While in development she was eating a Peep and decided she wanted these two pretty colors presented to you all!
Why are the switches named Mallo?
We were looking to avoid any troubles we could have potentially faced by using the name Peeps and being there is already a marshmallow switch on the market we decided Mallo would be the perfect fit!

Specifications:

Polycarbonate (PC) top housing
Nylon (PA66) bottom housing
55g & 68g (bottom out) spring
5-pin (PCB mount)
Factory unlubed
How much would it cost?
$0.30 USD per switch (Sold in packs of 10 for $3.00). During the Pre-order phase the switches will be $0.25 USD per switch.

There will only be a limited amount available for Pre-order to keep most for in-stock.
