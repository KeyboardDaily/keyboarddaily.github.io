    Kit Renders
    Please note that kits are NOT final, but these are pretty much 97% set.
    Base Kit

    OG Icon mods.
    Dank compatibility includes all of the rigid brain, 300IQ 60%, 65%, 75%, TKL, 96-key, 1800 and fullsize keyboards.
    There’s some ISO and HHKB love sprinkled in there as well.
    Little love, hugs, and kisses for every layout in the base kit, but not 100% coverage for the more…uh… under 300IQ layouts.
    Please note that there is an extra dank S T E P P E D C O N T R O L for the 400IQ brain users.
    Latin Alphas Kit

    Given that the keyset is inpsired from a Japanese medium, you know I gotta choose the improved Hiragana sublegends from GMK Striker.
    That being said, I do understand some peeps prefer the cleaner look of solo legends, so here you go champs, have at it.
    Novelties Kit – Updated! Added a second “Emilia” enter key!

    The caviar of the kits, the strawberry on top of the shortcake (RIP Mugi), the TRD trim of Toyotas, the Patagucci of hikergear, the Yeezy 720s of Addidas – the novelties.
    Created by the GOAT OneCreativeMind, the novelties includes the Great Spirit Puck (the cat), mana crystals (sorry Ishtar/Rin Toshaka), uwu’d snowflake, a cute flower, and Emilia’s eagle insignia. The 1 unit keys in R1 and the 1.25/1.50 unit keys are in R4.
    Can’t forget the “Emilia” Enter of course, just in case you forgot who’s best girl.
    Spacebar Kit

    Fairly comprehensive spacebar kit. Two colours with the following sizes: 1u(2x), 1.25u, 1.75u, 2.25u, 2.75u, 6u center-stem, and 6u off-center stem.
    Weeb “B” and non-weeb “B” are in the kit as well for the Alice layouts.
