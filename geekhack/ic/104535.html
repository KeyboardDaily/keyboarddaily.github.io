Hey everyone! I’ve been wanting to show off a blue set that I’ve had in the works for a little bit. The theme is Ice Dragon but I wanted to use the eastern dragon rather than the western dragon. The kit images are just there to demonstrate the keys in each kit. We will be using the default KAT fonts for both regular alphas and Cangjie Alphas. I’ll make sure to reach out to zfrontier about the novelty details to confirm if those are possible. I’ve reached out for some samples and hope to receive some in the next month or so. I don’t have a deskmat designed for it yet but if there’s enough interest, I’ll look to get one done. Without further ado, KAT Ice Dragon!
