We went to a coffee shop and asked for a matcha latte and since then we fell in love with the flavor.

Colors

Thats right only 2 colors in all the set, no accent color, we wanted to preserve the essence of the drink at its best.

We hope you like this set and we would be happy to read your opinion in the form below.
Interest Check 2.0

The Base Kit Will cover all your basic needs like the extra B for Alice and basic 40s key.
(In case you didn’t know, Matcha powder is used in the Japanese ceremony of tea influenced by zen Buddhism, that’s why the hiragana Sublegends)
Some of you suggest that I swap the colors, but my vision for this set was to do something different in the color combinations, 90% of the sets that go on sales in the alphas are light colors and the modifiers are dark, so I ventured to do the opposite and I think I came up with a combination that works.

Not a fan of hiragana sublegends? I have this kit for you.

The Matcha can be prepared in a lot of different varieties, so here you can have the basic props to make your own tea or latte.
