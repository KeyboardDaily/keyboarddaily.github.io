WinCable Studio - Colourful Balck-Gold Premium Cable
One of the most high-end cables you can get.
About Us:
Hi, we are Win Shop. As an enthusiast of customised keyboards, we strive to make the perfect cables to match the sets on your hands.

About this Cable:
After a few runs of cable group buys, we believe our products are mature enough to be introduced to the world.
Not like the normal premium cable that is delivered with a premium USB cable plus some CNC connectors, we are trying
to bring the premium pattern on the cable as well.
One highlight of the cable is the continuous golden line which we spent two months doing dozens of prototypes to finally make right.

Spec:
Length: 17cmCoil+100cmextendcable.
Ports: USB-A and C.
Cable Colour: Premium Black.
Detachables: LEMO aviator connector (FGG+PHG) 4/5pin.
Cable: 5pin fast charging USB cable(up to 22watt).
