GMK Dolch R5
GMK Dolch has always been one of my favorite keycap sets, a timeless classic. However, despite having owned 2 or 3 iterations of this set, the versions that have been sold in the past have not been fully tailored to my taste. This interest-check is for a revisited original-color Dolch keyset with classic Cherry icon-text modifiers and an R5 bottom row (also known as Row A) .
The set is also aptly named “Dolch R5”, with an obvious reference to the provided R5 bottom-row support, as well as for it being the fifth GB-like iteration of its coming (prior runs: (1) IvanIvanovich’s GMK DOLCH, (2) Originative Co preorder, (3) Dolch Reborn by kekkon, and (4) Originative Co 2020 GB). There have been other iterations such as zFrontier’s, a Korean vendor’s, among others, but these were restricted to regions.
Though I have given these kits a lot of consideration over the past few months, they may be revised over the course of the interest-check and feedback will be taken into account. A Forties kit had been strongly considered for this run, but I have decided against it unless there is overwhelming demand, due to MOQ-related concerns.
Features
As currently proposed, this set has:

  • Cherry profile, ABS plastic, thick doubleshot keycaps manufactured by GMK.
  • Alphas: CC light grey base, CP white legends.
  • Modifiers: N9 dark grey base, CP white legends.
  • Accents: P3 red base, CP white legends; custom color-matched Dolch turquoise base, CP white legends.
  • OG Cherry-style Icon-Text modifiers. (may be changed in case of overwhelming demand for Icon-only)
  • Complete R5 bottom row support!
  • Support for most common layouts, as well as ISO-UK.
  • Alt Gr (thanks LordK for the suggestion)
    Other sets that use the CP (white legends) color code, that you may use to mix-and-match: Olivia, Honeywell, Ursa, Barebones Stormtrooper, Bingsu.
    For N9 (dark grey modifier color): Skidata, Terminal, Wasabi, Nines, Q:01, etc…
