Hello all! Thank you for visiting! I am a small business owner in New York City who manufactures anything from custom exercise equipment to cabinetry! I stock, work with and cut anything from Solid Maple to Aluminum to Corian.
Recently I noticed excess Corian sheets lying around my shop, and was thinking about complimenting a few boards I own.
So without further ado, I present:
THE SIMPLE REST
From 40% to Full Size boards and everything in between!
COLORS:
Corian does blacks, greys, and whites well. Vivid colors not too much. I have physical rests on hand in multiple colors, but am not using these colors for the final pieces. I will be using Modern White, Gray Onyx, and Deep Black Quartz (pictured in hand, below).
Modern White goes incredibly well with E-White or other white ano/ plastic builds.
Gray Onyx has a slight opaqueness effect to it that matches polycarb builds.
Deep Black Quartz adds galaxy-like volume to any board, going especially well with sandblasted black or black ano builds.
SPECS:
Currently I am awaiting feedback (please fill out the IC form!) to see what form factor is most popular.
[IC] Google Form: https://forms.gle/cXpaDnEJXyGTfHnm9
Some things to note:
-40% boards can use a 60% rest (will be just a tad larger, about 1/4”).
-65% rests will work for most 75% cases.
-TKL (80%) rests will work for 96% compact layout cases.
-Full Size is huge, well done for having one
SPECS:
.5” height, 3.5” width. Length varies. Small rubber bump-ons will be added to one side of the rest to slightly increase angle, as well as provide a sturdier feel.
