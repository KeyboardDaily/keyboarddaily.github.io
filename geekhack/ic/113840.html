Hello Geekhack!
This is Freather from CMM.Studio
After the great success of our last project, Fuji65, we are proud to finally
annouce our international IC for Fuji75!
Our design team worked hard and is thrilled to bring you the next iteration of our Fuji line, keeping the popular design language as last time, but in an all new form factor
(We thank Mellperbia with the case design, Rifen with the Fuji logo, and Freather with the PCB)
Links:
IC: https://forms.gle/3XPxn3YEbybkvm688
CMM.Studio DC: https://discord.gg/cmmstudio
If you are a keyset designer, please join our discord and DM Freather for a role that allow you to access the render file
Below are some specs related to the board
Case Material: Aluminum / PC
Degree: 7.5
Mount: Poron Gaskets
Solder PCB: Multilayout flex cut PCB + daughterboard (Ai03’s Unified Daughterboard supported)
Hotswap PCB: Multilayout Hotswap PCB + dautherboard (Ai03’s Unified Daughterboard supported)
Note/: The hotswap PCB has some switches vertically mounted to compensate for ISO support.
Structure

Pieces: Top and Bottom Case, Plate, Moutain Weight, Back Weight, Badge Block
Plate Material: Aluminum, Carbon Fiber, PC
Foam: Plate Foam, Switch Foam, Case Foam, Stab Foam
(The sakura on the right coner is cnced, alternative color options may be available during the GB as an extra)
Versions: SE, FE, JTK Dreaming Girl Collab
Rough Pricing:
SE: unlimited, TBD
FE: Limited Raffle / FCFS, Pricing TBD
DG Collab: Limited Raffle/ FCFS, Pricing TBD
Extra Addons: Pink badge block (DG option only), insert pack (includes a set of plate, PCB, and a set of foams)
Rough GB Date: Early 2022, after all Fuji65 units and all China Fuji75 GB Units are delivered
