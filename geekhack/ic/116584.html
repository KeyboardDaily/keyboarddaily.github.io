specifications

  • 60% WKL XT layout
  • 6 degree angle
  • Seamless, “screwless” design
  • PCB bottom mount
  • Big through weight
  • SKUF feet
  • PCB with integrated USB-C, ESD, underglow RGB, all the bells and whistles
