Dear Geekhackers:
This is kingnestea from Percent studio, and Iâ€™m here to introduce our latest project Skog-reboot.
Some words
As the first keyboard designed by Percent studio, Skog is not that wide spread as the Canoe.
It was late 2019 when we had the idea to give this iconic board a refresh.



After more than 10 revisions, we have finally come to a design that meets our standard and obsession for details.
Long story short, letâ€™s get into the details.
Key features

  • Programmable rotary encoder above the arrow cluster
  • RGB led strip above the arrow cluster
  • Solderable PCB and Hot-swap PCB up to choice
  • QMK/VIA PCB
  • Bluetooth 4.2
  • Isolated gasket mount
  • Dampener between plate and PCB
  • Adjustable typing angle from 5.8Â° to 6.8Â°
  • 1.5mm aluminum/FR4 plate
  • The case*
    The case will be anodised/powder coating/cerakote/electrophoresis 6063 aluminum depending on colour.
    As a standard approach, we will provide colour comboâ€™s picked by us during the GB phase.
    Different from the previous Percent studio GBs, this time we will provide Pantone colour code or colour samples for each colour available.







    Available colour combo is tbd at the moment, we will probably put up a poll for this at a later stage.
  • Plate and PCB*
    At Percent studio, we agree that besides aesthetics, typing experience is not negligible.
    We have added gaskets to the Canoe gen 2 and feedback is very positive.
    This time, we are doing fully isolated gaskets to enhance this.
    The keyboard kit will also come with a silicon dampener between the plate and PCB and a foam dampener between the PCB and bottom case, this together with the 1.5mm plate to reduce the hollow sounding.
    FR4 will also be provided as material of the plate.
