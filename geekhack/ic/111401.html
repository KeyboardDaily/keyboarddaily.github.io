Squishee65
An o-ring isolation mount gasket 65% keyboard.
Join our discord server for faster updates: https://discord.gg/hXcpWvg5zB
The Squishy65 was a stacked acrylic group buy done locally in the Philippines that featured an isolation mount plate in a stacked acrylic case. The aim of the acrylic Squishy65 was to provide high end features at an affordable price point for both new-comers and enthusiasts in the hobby. The Squishee65 aims to offer the Squishy65 keyboard with a more premium feel, featuring a machined polycarbonate case, a beefy brass weight, and a centered USB slot.
Stacked acrylic Squishy65 Squishee65 3mm stacked acrylic layers High quality polycarbonate No brass weight Sandblasted brass weight 5 mounting points 6 mounting points Left side USB on main board Centered USB via daughterboard Bare aluminum plates CNC aluminum plates with clear anodization
The mounting style consists of six tabs on the plate where you insert the o-rings onto. The plate then gets squished between the top and bottom portions of the case which gives its gasket mounting. The squishing of the o-rings is where the name comes from!
So far Ive done three group buys locally: the Wete, Herringbone, and the Squishy65 which is currently under fulfillment. I also worked on the GHS.RAR and the Monarch PCB.
Two rounds of prototyping have already been done for the Squishee65, the first round being the frosted and black versions, and the second being the purple version. The second revision of the Squishee65 has the addition of the sixth mounting point below the spacebar which adds stability to the plate.




O-rings colored red for clairty. Clear/white o-rings will be provided.
