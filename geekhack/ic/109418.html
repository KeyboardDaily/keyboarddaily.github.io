Hello all, my name is Lysol and for the past 2 months, I’ve been designing my very own through-hole board. since there are currently no through-hole designs up for the 75% form factor(aside from one) I wanted to change that and create my own unique design.
Enter the LCK75
The design itself is inspired by the Sat75 from cannon keys, the Discipline/Mysterium from CFTKB, and Dissatisfaction 65 from Nicell from the beginning of the year.
The main layout features: “standard” ANSI layout with the option of a 7u bar with 2x 1.5u mods to the left

The target price for a normal kit: $100+shipping & a cap of 200 kits(100 black and 100 white).
The kit includes the mainboard, plate, and bottom platform in a choice of either a black or white theme. kits will also include a clear acrylic guard, board components, 1x EC11 rotary encoder, 1x 32x128mm white OLED, mounting hardware, and bumpon rubber feet.
The kits will come in an LCK themed box with the same topographic design elements as seen on the forehead of the PCB(design still a WIP).

I’ll be working with Stupidfish1 to have 6mm case foam for each kit(great guy)
I’ll be working with P3D(another great guy) and will have acrylic hipro cases that I designed up for sale on his storefront at the time of the GB.
The GB will be in about 1-2 weeks from this IC and will be a google form Raffle that will last 3 hours then close
i’ll announce when that will happen soontm
The estimated shipping time will be at most 2.5 months from GB end.
