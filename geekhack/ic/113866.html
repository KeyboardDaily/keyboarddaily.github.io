Welcome to the IC for EPBT Avilo!
Creme and I have been working on this set for months now. After being burnt out we got demotivated for a while, we finally pull through and have started working on this set again. We really hope you enjoy this set as much as we have enjoyed the struggle of making it!
GB Geekhack post


This set takes most of it’s inspiration from the Spanish city called “Ávila”, and the name for this set is taken from the Esperanto translation  Avilo.

We came upon this city while researching historical Spanish places and the town’s beauty really pulled our breath away with it’s beautiful buildings, but especially it’s walls.
We were enamored by the rustic Spanish town with it’s breathtaking buildings and captivating city walls. We instantly knew that we wanted to capture the beauty of this world’s treasure and channel it into this keycap set.


19TH NOVEMBER 2021 - 19TH DECEMBER 2021


When seeking feedback internally amongst friends, it was brought to our attention that our set may have been reminiscent of GMK Noel; although we believed our theme and vision were different, we preemptively reached out to (Cutéshy), and they responded cordially and acknowledged and approved our set. We are thankful for their helpful feedback.
