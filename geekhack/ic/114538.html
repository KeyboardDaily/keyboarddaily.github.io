DSA PETRIKEYS

GB Dates: April 05, 2022 to May 5, 2022
Hello! Welcome to the IC for DSA PetriKeys, a keycap set inspired by bioluminescent petri dishes.
The gradient colors were chosen based off of the greens and blues I found in bioluminescence.
The white balances everything out while alluding to the image of a clean laboratory.
All of the mod icons are custom, and are based off of the rounded shapes of the petri dishes.
This set will be doubleshot SP DSA.
Please fill out the IC Form if you’re interested/have feedback!
