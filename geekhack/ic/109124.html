Introduction
Hello Geekhack. Today I want to bring to you the Interest Check for the Freyr TKL.
Freyr is a classic Top mount TKL that utilizes the popular A87 form factor, a step back into the era of OTD, LZ, Kustom TKL.
It’s a keyboard we can offers at a reasonable price with very short lead time(if not out right in-stock) that can rival many much more expensive customs.
For this revision we update the board with some modern features such as USB-C, plate that support more layouts etc… But it’s a yesteryear board (at yesteryear price).
This is not a new design, you may have seen a numbers of Freyr TKL floating around under the tag “prototype” or “Founders Edition” with various degree of finishes, but it had never have a proper GB.
One owner of such prototype brought it up in our Discord (Thanks Eleven), a discussion ensued, people were expressing interests in the boards, a few calls later and long story short, here we are.

Specification:

  • 87 Keyboard TKL.
  • Top Mount.
  • CNC Anodized Aluminum (maybe brass plate and weight depend on level of interests)
  • 8 Degree Typing Angle (detailed dimensions in first comment).
  • Compatible with many A87 PCB such as the popular h87a.
  • Included PCB support MX Switch, QMK/VIA Compatible Atmega32u4-au controller.
  • Come in WKL and WK option.
  • ~1.7kg unbuilt (aluminum plate and weight, no PCB).
