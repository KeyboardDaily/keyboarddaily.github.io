【IC】THE TK | An In-Stock Numpad Featuring All the Keys Missing from a 60％

Ever used a 60% or smaller and wondered where all of your keys went? Or simply wanted a removeable numpad with everything, looks included?
Introducing The TK, a Model M inspired numpad with a hotswap, 46 key layout. Available in 3D printed cases in the next couple months.

The TK will be sold in stock at $110. The first batch will consist of 25 PCB’s - to make 25 units in stock. The cases will be 3D printed when an order comes in and shipped out as soon as possible. All of the components will be sold separately, allowing for tons of different options for colors!
Tentatively going to be offering Off White, Granite (White with Black Speckle), Black and a Light Teal as the Main Body colors. Those same colors PLUS Silver and Gold will be offered for the accents, which include the TK Badge, the flip out feet, and the daughterboard sled.
YES, you read that right! This board has Flip-out Feet, which can make the board go from a 6.5 degree angle to a 9.5 degree!

The Bottom Mount TK will be offered in Hotswap only right now, with an FR4 mounting plate.

TLDR Details of the TK:

  • 3D Printed Hotswap Numpad
  • In-Stock at $110, 25 units available at launch
  • Bottom Mount FR4 Plate
  • 6.5 degree angle, with additional 3 degree flip out feet
  • QMK / VIA Compatible PCB designed by Elliot at epkb.design
  • All Hardware Included, including cork dot feet and manufacturing sticker
