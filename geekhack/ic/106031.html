1.Story
More

First of all, let me introduce myself. I was the former designer of MAB.
During the quarantine times, I did thought a lot and felt so regret of everything that happened. However, I am very lucky to have some friends in community who are kind and supportive to me so that I would like to follow my passion once more time. When they knew I have had a desire to make a product on my own, they encouraged and made me feel so motivative to do what I could not done before.
In the last time, I was just a designer of the team and had no relevance to finance as well as permission to decide the production.
This time, I want to show you my own product. It is totally my own design and my own finance. Its name is ORI. What does ORI mean? It is a characters in a video games that has to fight for survival and go on an adventure finding shine. I was inspired by ORI, just like my story, I want to enjoy this hobby whenever I am possible. I dont fear lose, I fear regret.
This project maybe will not become GB because the production of it depends on the manufactorys quality where I put my faith on. If the protype is bad, I will cancel this project. I only come back until I find another better manufactory
Lastly, how my project go on is based on your supportiveness.
Best regards,
2.Details
Case Angle: 7.0°
Materials: Aluminium 6063 T6 & Brass ( Aluminium/brass as weight)
Colours: Anodised Black, Silver, Red
Mounting method: Gasket + topmount
Plate: 1.5mm aluminium
FR4 if MOQ > 10
PCB: PCB + daughter USB-C

More
PCB is programmable via QMK and VIA
 PCB thickness: 1.6mm and 1.2mm; USB TypeC port; MX Mount
 ESD protection
 1.0mm-pitch connector for daughterboards (pinout description on PCB’s silk screen)
 Underglow RGBs pre-soldered for both variants.
Weight: 2kg (Case + plate + weight)
