In the prototype set the navy/dark blue color was darker than intended. The final product will be 5% lighter to closer match the pantone chips, unfortunately the backlog for protos was too long to get a another set done.
After much discussion with friends and fellow designers, looking at poll numbers, and considering my original vision, there won’t be a latin kit option for this GB. For those of you curious I consistently got only around 30 folks confirming they want a Latin alpha kit across multiple polls that ranged between 85 to 155 responses. I understand this will disappoint folks and you those of you whose decision to get this set is based on the availability of a Latin alphas kit may no longer want to join. Nevertheless, thank you for your interest and support! Perhaps if this GB does well and the demand for a KAT version is there we can offer a Latin alpha kit next time!

  • Design & Theme*
    More

The theme of the set is the feeling of an lazing around in an oversized hoodie on a cold day. The colors of this set is inspired by the work of artist Daisuke Richard, specifically this illustration. I’ve always liked Daisuke Richard’s use of colors in his artwork. I’ve always thought of it as ‘soft/comfy colors’ even though he does use bold, bright colors in many of his illustrations. I’ve been wanting more subtle/muted/soft color keycaps sets so I’ve made the alphas the light pink while using the dark blue for mods and the pale teal color as an accent.The set has custom icon legends on some of the modifiers and I welcome any feedback you have on the set, including the name.I would like to touch on the discussion on adding white to the design. I understand and agree that having white on the keyset would be really great. The white modifier/accent legends look really good and fit Daisuke Richard’s design. in fact, I had even prepared an off white to use on the legends. However, what I found is that the more I worked on using the white legends the more I felt like it wasn’t as close as the comfy theme is. I understand that the pink legends on blue will disappoint some folks and perhaps make the set no longer interesting but as my first keyset design I’m trying very hard to stick to my original vision. I hope you understand and I think a cooler color palette with Daisuke Richard’s colors would look really cool and maybe that can be a future keycap set
