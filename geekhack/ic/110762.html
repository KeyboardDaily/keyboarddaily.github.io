Drive Us Crazy!
I would like to introduce everyone GMK Power Chord



About
This set was inspired by two of my interests, HKS and Raise A Suilen. HKS is a Japanese tuning company that focuses on producing aftermarket car parts that improve the performance of your car. Raise A Suilen is also from the series BanG Dream! If you know GMK Devoted, you already know. The color scheme is based on both HKS’ and RAS’ color schemes. The colors have been matched physically with a RAL book with official merch.
Please fill out this IC Form if you’re interested!
Inspirations


References and Colors Chosen

Colors: CR, RAL 180 60 50, RAL 300 50 40, RO2, MG1, WS1
The main colors are Green and Purple. The purple is lighter than GMK DY to improve readability.
The red is for the essential icons that show up on your car’s dashboard. MG1 is for the pink stripes found in all HKS livery.

Kits
The kits are designed to fit most 60, 65, 70, 75, TKL, 96, 1800, Full-size keyboards in ANSI, tsangan/WKL, ISO, HHKB, Alice and more.
Fill out the IC form for more layout support!
