About the Designers:
We’re a group of friends, and we decided that we wanted to attempt to design a keycap set; After about a year of learning and designing, we present to you ePBT Basin! We designed this set to reflect the dynamic blue-grays of the ocean. Amidst the vastness of an oceanic basin are a wide variety of colors, and we have chosen a sinking gradient to portray diving into the depths of the ocean.
