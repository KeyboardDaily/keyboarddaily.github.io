***Foreword

With colours inspired by a smith corona typewriter sitting in my cupboard. As I was putting together the initial KLE for it I noticed that the colours that I’d chosen also matched closely to a lot of red, black, and beige propaganda posters. Combining the two I decided that future dystopia was a great theme for the set. I will be running the set through massdrop and will have a poll up in a week or two.
More

Some have raised concerns over the similarity of this set to Wolf mark, I PMed the creator of the set and asked permission and he agreed, so away we go!

Profile
I decided on Signature Plastics’ SA to keep with the retro vibe of my typewriter. Sculpted SA initially seemed like the way to go, however due to compatibility and ergonomics, R3 uniform also seemed tempting. To compromise I decided that Filco Profile SA, that is 333322, with number and function rows being r2 and the rest r3.

Colours

The set will be ABS Double shot with colours RAS, WBO, and GB. Scans are from Flickr.
