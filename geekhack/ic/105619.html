ePBT Musician


Ladies and Gentlemen may I present to you ePBT Musician. This set is inspired by the magnificent musical instrument Piano. A hobby of mine which I practice 7 years. 1 Year ago I got into the keyboard community. Then my goal was to create a set by my favourite musical instrument piano. The sets aims to simplicity and minimalism using gmk colors. Yes I know it’s a bow and wob set but with a little change to it
