Were [INSERT] Studios, a two-man design studio determined to bring high end quality keyboards to the market. After being in the hobby for a while, we wanted to bring our own unique style, and push the envelope on what keyboards are.
Introduction
This is our very first design and we are excited to finally share with the community on what weve been working on for the past year. We realized there arent that many mounting methods and decided to create a new one thats just as unique and hopefully a great option in terms of acoustics and tactility. The boards design was inspired by many keyboards that we had a high appreciation for such as: Jaes Lodestone, jjws Cloudline, TGRs Jane, Geons Frog TKL, and goks beauty.
MagLev Mount

To create the mounting system, there are 24 magnets in total excluding the 4 magnets that hold onto the rear badge. There are 8 magnets adhered to each the bottom, top, and plate in an orientation where the poles are facing in the same polarity to introduce repulsion, ultimately levitation. There are small cutouts in the plate to seat the magnets into place and a pin alignment system to keep the plate aligned and for smooth, linear travel.

FAQ
Can we modify/customize the strength of magnets to change the typing feel?
No, we did thorough research when it came to sourcing the type/size of magnets. The arrangement of these specific magnets we are using allows more than enough force to repulse without the unwanted contacts of the board assembly. In addition, we think the magnets’ strength is suitable for the majority of typists.
What vendors will you be partnering with to sell this board?
We are still in the R/D phase of our keyboard and have yet to determine whether or not we would want to sell this board ourselves or through established vendors.
Layout Options?
Initially we only wanted to support one particular layout, tsangan, but if there is enough demand for additional layout expansion we will highly consider.
