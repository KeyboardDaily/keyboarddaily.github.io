Everyone is Bob, and this time I bring you a keyboard that allows you to “graduate” directly, an X-ray dual-mode (Bluetooth wireless, wired) 102-key full-size keyboard.


This time, bob studio brings you a product that is basically a blank in the market, because I am also a heavy user of the digital area in my daily life, so the common 80 arrangement or 60 arrangement is also a problem for my personal use. Many troubles, this time we bring you x-ray to meet the use of digital area, adding the function of wireless bluetooth. Moreover, the host computer can be used to edit the keys. I think these functions have been able to meet the requirements of most people.



Different from the production philosophy of many studios in the past, we will not blindly pile materials on the X-ray, making the keyboard very heavy. Our design philosophy is to be as light as possible. Make it not a burden on you.

For product positioning, we carefully analyzed the needs of everyone in the market. Some people are serious rgb lovers, some people just like a simple and elegant light, so we have made some small designs to meet the different needs of different groups of people.

GB’s products will come with two four-pointed star nameplates (aluminum alloy material is opaque, pc material is transparent). If you need a strong rgb effect, you can block the four-pointed star area with an opaque nameplate. Make the nameplate next to it more dazzling.



If you need something more elegant, you can design a solid nameplate, such as carbon fiber material, or aluminum alloy lettering, or a wooden nameplate. Let only the four-pointed star shine.

In terms of structure, we have redefined a new structure this time. The two structures give full play to their characteristics, allowing you to enjoy two different structural feel with one keyboard, and our pcb is also a two-stage design. 80% area gasket, digital area top. Let you have a new improvement in both the variety of gameplay and the feel of the hand. Get full satisfaction.


When it comes to Bluetooth, you may think of the problem of signal and power consumption. This time we use a dual battery interface design, and the battery will use a common mobile phone battery as a carrier. We also designed a battery compartment, which can make the battery more secure and neat at the bottom of the keyboard. If you feel that a 3200mAh battery is not enough , you can add one piece, so theoretically the battery capacity is doubled. Of course, if you use rgb for a long time, it will consume more power, but if you only use a single lamp, the overall Bluetooth will often exceed 2 months.

In terms of nameplates, we will still open the nameplate size file for everyone to design by themselves. We will also launch some official designs, and an expansion pack may be formed later for everyone to purchase. Below are some of our nameplate designs. The basic matching is some themed keycaps.
