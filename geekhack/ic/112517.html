Introducing the Scylla
A modern, compact ergonomic split keyboard based on the Dactyl Manuform.
If you are interested, please fill in this form:
https://docs.google.com/forms/d/e/1FAIpQLSf7fMUA1SxY5Kq29Nh8jqGQ9h0IxySnbKjqaCTc-QrbH_tffA/viewform?usp=sf_link. Thanks !!
I’ve been designing Dactyl Manuforms for two years, and made the open-source Skeletyl - https://github.com/Bastardkb/Skeletyl, and TBK Mini - https://github.com/Bastardkb/TBK-Mini
Now with all that CAD experience, I set out to design a brand new 6x4+5 dacman, made for no compromises. Low profile, sharp and compact design, high-quality print and components.
With 1 year of building Dactyl Manuforms, I did all I could to ensure a great product and build experience.
I’m posting here to get a general idea of the interest behind this keyboard, and gather some feedback before launch.
General information

  • split ergonomic keyboard
  • switch well and column-staggered
  • same layout as a dactyl manuform Mini
  • per-key RGB (optional)
  • low profile
  • built and shipped from the Netherlands, worldwide
  • comes with QMK and online configurator
  • tenting base, 30 degrees (optional)

Technical details

  • flexible PCBs
  • ribbon cables
  • USB-C on both sides (using elite-C and open-sourced Shield PCB)
  • audio jack
  • 3d printed case, .15mm layer height prusament galaxy black printed on prusa mk3s
  • M4 torx screws

Options:

  • per-key RGB
  • DIY Kit (with all electronics)
  • Prebuilt
  • tenting cases (30deg) (if there is interest)

Pricing and lead times
Lead time: I always keep <2 weeks lead time for DIY kits, <5 weeks for prebuilts. If it gets too busy, I will close orders temporarily.

Kit and prebuilt contents
DIY Kit content:

  • 2 cases and plates (prepared, supports removed)
  • flexible PCBs
  • 2 elite-Cs
  • screws and screw inserts
  • keycaps, switches and cables not included

Prebuilt:

  • you choose your switches
  • included in the price (except Zealios, have to pay a premium)
  • I order them, build the keyboard, ship it to you
  • keycaps and cables not included
