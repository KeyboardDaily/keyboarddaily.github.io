Design Concept
The story is about passing the fire. Making self as firewood burnt is to last the brightness of the world.
When burning self, peoples body keeps in good condition, however, the soul is fading gradually. Since that, those who burn themselves turns to be a walking dead.
Though there are many people burning themselves generations by generations, the world still goes to the Dark. Would you like to burn yourself like those pinoeers?
Will you retain a trace of warm fire in a desperate cold world?
The whole story is full of desperation. But there is still a weak but warm flame in such dark world.
Therefore, the Link Fire theme features dark blue as its key tone.
Also, adding retro grey white colour stands for the ash and bones in burning.
The warm orange colour means the little flame in the dark world.

The little flame lights up the dark world, generating the bright blue KIT.
