Hello Geekhack!
I would like to introduce everyone to BNR1
BNR1 is an CNC machined rotary encoder knob from a solid rod of aluminium.
It can be configured via QMK flashable Atmega32U4 to do just about anything you want it for. After almost 6 months of development and prototyping the BNR1 has arrived. It uses EC11 encoder with no detent that means it is smooth when you rotate the knob. It takes both rotary and click input.
