Hello all, my name is Lysol and this is the interest check for round 2 of the LCK75.
There will be slight changes compared to round one with the main one being possible ISO support.




Sound test of the board w/ JWICK Yellows and KAT Alpha
Board details and GB info
The board is open-sourced as of now on my Github. so if youd like to do runs with your friends you can.
I will not be held liable for that though but if you have any questions on the matter,
feel free to reach out to me at Lysol/#5640 on discord. BOM list is also available on my discord as well
Like last time, the design itself is an fr4 sandwich mount type board
akin to other THT kits and Is inspired by the Sat75 from CannonKeys,
the Discipline/Mysterium from CFTKB, and Dissatisfaction 65 from Nicell from last year.
The main layout features a standard ANSI layout with the option of a
7u bar with 2x 1.5u mods to the left with possible ISO being added from feedback.
The target price for each kit:
$105USD +shipping for black and white PCB kits
$130USD +shipping for purple PCB kits
Shipping:
$14USD for CONUS
$35USD for International
There will be a cap of 300 kits with it coming down to 100 black, 100 white & 100 purple for now but could change.
The kit includes the mainboard, plate, and bottom plate in a choice of either a black, white, or purple theme.
Kits will also include a clear acrylic guard, board components, 1x EC11 rotary encoder, alu 19mm knob, 1x 32x128mm white OLED,
mounting hardware, and bumpon rubber feet with two big ones for angling the board.
The kits will come in an LCK themed box similar to round 1 with the same topographic
design elements as seen on the forehead of the PCB.(box design might change from r1)

I’ll be working with Stupidfish1 again to have 6mm case foam for each kit(great guy),
and for the acrylic guards, P3D will be helping with that.
P3D will have gasket acrylic cases as well as the original acrylic case designed by me available as an extra for those that want it.
***My designed case
P3D’s Gasket case

The GB will be FCFS and will be open to all and will take place on my site LCKeyboards.com
The estimated shipping time will be around 4 months from the GB end time/date. And will take place in 1-2 weeks
There will be a limit to 1 kit per person.
Lastly my LCK Discord.
I strongly urge anyone interested to join as I update there more frequently compared to any other platform. [/font]
Many thanks to my friends and family that have helped me bring this project to its current point.
