About
The inspiration for this set was from the “Yami Kawaii” aesthetic in Japan.
Yami kawaii means “dark/sick cute” in English and is based around pastel, cute designs with a subtle and dark twist.
My idea for this set was to make the main focus the pastel and cute colors, with dark and edgy accents,
to create an interesting and somewhat jarring contrast.
