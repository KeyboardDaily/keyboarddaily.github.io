TREASURE TYPE-9 ( SERIES II )
Hello to all,
I am is Eric from Treasure, Industrial Designer from France ( https://www.instagram.com/treasuretypes/ ).
This new project is the follow up of my first macropad keyboard of last year ( Macropad Type-9 : https://geekhack.org/index.php?topic=91252.0 & macropad.co), in a new and improved version.
Treasure Type-9 is stylish metal macropad keyboard with a lot of keys in the smallest form factor.
On the technical side, the Treasure Type-9 ( Series II ) is now enterly solderless, as it have Kailh hot swap stockets and smd leds already on the PCB.
The kit will be sold with 9 DSA keycaps, a travel case and an USB Type-C to A cable; so you just have to bring your keyswitches !
The aim of this interest check is to find how many people are interested, and discuss about color / finish options.
GROUP BUY IS NOW LIVE : https://treasuretypes.com


Specifications :

  • No soldering at all

  • USB Type-C Connection

  • Kailh hot swap sockets

  • PCB leds

  • Plate for easy mount, for all kind of keyswitches

  • Low Profile custom silicone pads

  • Pre-Assembled PCB with Microcontroller

  • Powder coated case / mirror finish

  • In the kit, you will find :*

  • 1 Unibody CNC aluminum or brass case

  • 1 Pre-Assembled PCB with Kailh hot swap sockets + SMD leds

  • 1 custom metal plate

  • 1 USB Type-C cable

  • 9 Custom DSA Keycaps

  • 9 Keyswitches (Optional)

  • 1 Travel Case (Optional)

  • Color options - Renders ( Subject to change )* :

Back of the Treasure Type-9 with the silicone feets
