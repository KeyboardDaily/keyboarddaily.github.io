PBTfans Purpolch
In collaboration with KBDfans
This set was inspired by the classic Dolch colorway, but with a splash of purple. I recently posted this concept on reddit, and to my surprise garnered a huge amount of interest, so we had to make this a reality.
Since then I have further developed this set with KBDfans, and decided to make it a PBTfans set: a faster, cheaper, and high quality keycap manufacturer. With that being said, we proudly present to you the official PBTfans Purpolch interest check.
