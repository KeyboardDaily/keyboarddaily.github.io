Sun68 is a compact gasket mount keyboard kit designed with inspiration of current kits. We also modified internal structure Deatails to improve typing feels and sound, we use a full set of foams, including foams between switches and PCB, around screws,gaskets between plate sides and cases, etc. We hope it can stand out among current nice 68% keyboards with more details improved, and design renewed.
Please note that they only have standard hot-swap PCB, current plate (not updated with the one compatible with split spacebars and step capslock), brass weight.
