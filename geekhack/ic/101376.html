Hi, long time no see!
On behalf of DT.Heart, I am introducing you Morgan 40.
Morgan 40 is DT.Heart’s experiment on a truly wireless keyboard with better connection and portability.
Traditional wireless keyboard uses bluetooth connection and bt usually will have a shorter battery life and take longer to setup and connect. Morgan 40 will be using logitech’s unifying components to provide a 2.4ghz wireless connection with a longer battery life, faster wake up and easy pairing: plug-to-use.
Prototypes are finished and please check the photos!



Join DT.Heart’s discord here: https://discord.gg/RteVhRY

QA
Why not cable + BT for wired and wireless connection?
We think that most custom cables are too large when paired with a 40% board and if we are going to make a small keyboard, we want it to be truly PORTABLE. Therefore, something truly WIRELESS.
As I mentioned in the initial paragraphs, BT lacks when it comes to stability, battery consumption, and set up time. Although Unifying will occupy 1 USB slot, we still think that having a more stable and faster responding wireless device with longer battery life is more important.
