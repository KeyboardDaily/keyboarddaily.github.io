IV KANKETSU
Kanketsu is a Japanese design philosophy that is rooted in simplicity. The word itself can translate to completion, simplicity”, or brevity.
The philosophy teaches that true simplicity can often be a complex path requiring careful choices and intentional decisions.
We are very pleased to be working on this project with IV Works, a long time friend of the Studio and collaborator on other adventures!
IC FORM
KITTING
Our aim is to release one single kit with a wide range of compatibility for the most common layouts.
The set will be Cherry profile dye sublimated 1.55mm PBT.
Novelties and accents are included within a single kit, with a target price of $100.
The manufacturer of the set is the same as IV Ashes. As per IV Works usual procedure, we will have physical samples in hand to share before the buy.
