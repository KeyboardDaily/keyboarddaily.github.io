Synth Labs Solo
A IIDX-inspired Macropad

Interest Form:
Please fill out this form if you are interested in this macropad - doing so will help inform purchasing logistics.
https://forms.gle/7LzZujByGWDW1kgy5
Inspiration:
The Synth Labs Solo is a 7 key macropad with a rotary encoder. It is inspired Beatmania IIDX cabinets, and is intended to be usable as a mini-controller for those games on PC simulators. The design also draws inspiration from turntables, such as those from Pro-Ject audio.

Details:
The Solo is a sandwich-mount macropad. It is designed so that it can be assembled with the keys either on the left or right side of the knob.
Switches will be hotswappable. Rotary encoder will also be hotswappable with any Bourns EC11 encoder.
All 7 keys and the rotary encoder will be programmable in QMK / VIA. Rotary encoder will show up in VIA as 3 mappable keys (representing CW turn, CCW turn, and click-in).
What’s Included
Currently, I’m intending on selling this as an entire pre-assembled built-to-order macropad, including:

  • 1x Aluminum Base Chassis
  • 1x Aluminum Bottom Chassis
  • 1x Brass or Aluminum Knob
  • 1x Brass Plate
  • 1x Aluminum or Polycarbonate Sidewall
  • 7x SLK 1.5u blank keycaps
  • 7x Switches (options will depend on what I can get my hands on)
  • 1x Bourns EC11 rotary encoder (detent w/ button, detentless w/ button, detent w/o button, or detentless w/o button)
  • 1x Custom-fit carry case
  • 1x Braided USB-C to USB-A cable
    A cheaper, unassembled kit, which will not include switches, keycaps, carry case, or the cable, may be available depending on demand.

Color Options

  • The Base chassis will be available in E-White, Dark Gray Ano, or Silver
  • The Bottom chassis will be available in E-White or Dark Gray Ano
  • The Sidewall will be available in E-White or Dark Gray Anodized Aluminum, or Frosted Clear Polycarbonate
  • The Plate will be available in PVD Silver or PVD Rose Gold
  • The Knob will be available in PVD Silver (brass), PVD Rose Gold (brass), E-White (aluminum), or Dark Gray Ano (aluminum)
  • Keycaps will be available in White or Dark Gray
