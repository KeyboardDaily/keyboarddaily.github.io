Hello Everybody, Some of the member may recognize us from cable making scene but we would like to expand further into mechanical keyboard since i (Alfred) am an enthusiast myself and i do enjoy all the time i had with my keyboards.
This project started as an idea of designing a decent gasket mounted keyboard design while offering a considerable price.

Information about the kit
Price: Starting at $235-265
Kit Comes with Case + Weight, Alu Plate, PCB, Carrying Case (Picture soon),
Extra M2.5 SS screws, Poron Gasket Strips (tabbed gasket with adhesive on one side) and rubber feet
Group Buy Date: 9th of January 2021 - 15th of Feb.

Specification
Mounting: Isolated Gasket mount (PORON single side adhesive tabs)
Typing Angle: 6 Degree
Adjusted Front Height 18.5mm
Mass: Total 1.5kg Unbuilt with Weight
Plates: Aluminum, Brass, Polycarbonate, More to come after IC
PCB: Solderable PCB, Hotswap PCB (ESD Protected) with QMK/Via Supported
Material Used: Aluminum 6063 (Sandblasted Anodize or E-Coated), Brass Weight (Sandblasted + Clear Coated)
Layout: ANSI, Tsangan, ISO for solder, Tsangan for Hotswap
