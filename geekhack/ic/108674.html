Vita Juice Deskmats
Hi Everyone
I’m back with more mats!
The Design
I’d like to present to you all the Vita Juice Deskmats, based off of juice boxes that were a big part in many of our childhoods. To celebrate these little cartons I’ve designed 5 mats based off of 5 of the boxes. Chrysanthemum Tea, Lemon Tea, Sugarcane Juice, Lychee Juice and Soy milk.
INFO
-900x400x4 mm
-Stitched Edges
-Rubber non-slip bottom
