PBTFans Frontier
I keep hitting the spacebar.. But somehow I’m still on Earth.
(Inspired by the beauty of space)
If you are interested in this set, please fill out the IC here!
Kitting

The Base kit supports a variety of boards, from 60 to TKL.

The Numpad kit supports full-size, 1800 boards, and separated numpads.

The 40s kit supports many Ortho and 40% boards.

The International kit covers most European layouts.

The Spacebar kit includes many spacebar options.

The Icon kit allows you to change up your modifiers’ look.

The Novelty kit (art designed by Stellaidoscope) will bring your board beyond color and into space.
