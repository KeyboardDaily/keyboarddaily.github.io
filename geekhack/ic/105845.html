GROUP BUY THREAD IS LIVE, CLICK ON ME

Rendered on Keycult No2 rev1 by Janglad
Group Buy Date: 4/20
(that’s April 20th, 2021, of course)
Backstory
Hits vape. I was sat at home one night like normal and looking at my keyboards n that. All the beautiful keyboards right. And I have this board with GMK Muted on and I was all like oh man I love GMK Muted right, like everybody does, and I was messing around with it with my imagination and I like tinted it green a bit and I was like yo thats pretty nice yo its muted and green, green like weed man ha ha and I was like yo its like muted but ZOOTED ha ha man thats pretty funny and I hit that vape again to celebrate. Then I was like oh man wouldnt it be great to have a nice weed themed set cos like, I dont really care about anime girls but I still like beautiful German keycaps, and weed, and like its pretty unlikely Im gonna get any 420 Clacks so yeah why not mock that //// up for real yo. Then I was like but wait, some people dont even like weed, and thats cool man, ydy, but they might still like the colour green. So I figured hey you know what lets keep the base kit straight, and put the weed refs in the novelties kit extras and collabs so if you dont like it you dont have to buy it. Pretty cool.
Based Kit

This is some pretty comprehensive //// my dude. Theres a numpad, and ISO support, basic 40s support, all the usual not too weird layouts. Yep, chances are this will cover your board. If it doesn’t, you probably know it doesn’t, and you’re probably not surprised.
Spliffs

The kit is called Spliffs but it contains spacebars. Two of them are purple! Two of them are 3u! Crazy right? Im not doing mod coloured spacebars, they always look wack af.
