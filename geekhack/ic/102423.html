Hey guys!
I’m happy to bring you my new keyset KAT “Atlantis” Or if you will “ KATlantis”
The inspiration for this set comes from the lost city of Atlantis and all the mysteries of the deep ocean. It also just comes from my insane love of all things water-related
The set is featuring new custom icons we have made to follow the theme of Atlantis text/runes.
I am considering custom icons for all keys if these are received well and requested!
Let me know if you would like to see something added and well consider it!
