SERIES TY TYPE-00
CNC machined. Anodized aluminum. Tray style container.

Hello everyone!

Were back to show everyone what weve been working on since Series 00, our first product.
This interest check is for Series TY, a CNC machined aluminum tray that sits on your desk. Its weight park itself and is not easily moved by accidental bumps with a soft silicone bottom. Wide opening for easily viewing your contents, sorting, and pouring objects in and out of the tray.
Today, were finally ready to show you Series TY Type-00.
We hope you like what you see, and as always, feel free to leave any feedback.

2. DESIGN VARIATIONS
Our plan is to only produce one of the two design variations, and we would like to get your feedback.

Design A is simpler and thinner. Design B is taller with the added functionality of holding pens in various diameters.


3. DESIGN DETAILS
Silicone feet to protect bottom case, grips to the table.

Silicone feet also protects itself from scratch during stacking with enough clearance for the stem.

The stem design allows it to hold a variety of pen in different diameters - from thin to thick.
