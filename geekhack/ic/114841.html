The Kamo60 is a top mount 60% keyboard with burger-mount compatibility.
Being a car enthusiast I wanted to incorporate some design traits from one of my favorite european sedans, the 1995 BMW M5 Touring.
With that being said I present to you…
The Kamo60








As mentioned, the design is inspired by the 1995 BMW M5 Touring. With its sharp angles and sleek wagon shape, it is truly a beautiful car.
I tried to incorporate the sloped rear windscreen and the aggressive stance of the car into the bottom rear design of the Kamo60.
Specs:
Plate material: Aluminium, Brass, Polycarbonate
Colors: Blue, Pink, Grayish, Black
Mounting style: Top-mount with burger-mount compatibility
Layout: ANSI, ISO, split backspace, split right shift, stepped CAPS
Bottom row: 6.25U and 7U
Typing angle: 7 degrees
PCB: TBD, but currently compatible with WT-60D and WT-60C from wilba.tech
