Collabs
The collab with Artkey has already happened and you can go here and here if you want to see the keys that were offered. I have a couple more things on the way, like a collab with Alphakeycaps and GSK! Stay tuned!
Rama will also be making a key for Raspberry - I previously reached out to them and they were going to produce 2 keys (Esc and Enter), but since they do not offer KAT Enter key, I’ll work we’ll only go for 1 (Esc) - let me know by filling the form if there is interest for a raspberry in accented colors as well

Vendors, GB dates and Pricing
More details soon - previous vendors might or might not be joining again after this switch to KAT, this will be updated once I get closer to the GB date. Speaking of which, there is not set GB date right now - I understand the frustration for those that have been following the project since I announced it, trust me, but I want it to be done well, so I’ll wait and announce the GB date when everything is set and locked in
As for pricing, it will be announced once kits are finalized - as per usual, I will try and make them as low as possible
