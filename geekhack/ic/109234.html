Forged Carbon Fiber Plates for KBD67mkII
Project Timeline:

Not The Carbon Fiber You Already Know
Forged Carbon Fiber is made from small chopped carbon fibers, this creates a unique and individual pattern for each plate.
No two plates are identical.

Lightweight and Strong
Each plate weighs roughly 41g, compared to brass weighing in at ~190g.

Inspired by Lamborghini Forged Composites
In 2008, Dr Paolo Feraboli created what would become Forged Composites for Lamborghini.
Taking this concept and applying it as a plate material creates unique patterns on each individual piece.

Difference between Forged Carbon and Carbon Fiber
The main difference is aesthetic. Forged Carbon has a marbled appearance compared to the typically Carbon Fiber woven look.
Forged also typically has a higher thread count, making the plate stronger.
In real world applications the feel and sound is very similar to traditional woven carbon fiber.

Additional Information
Both hotswap and solder versions will be available.
Standoff holes are on the board for those that wish to use them, however they are not required and I personally do not use them.
Plates will be shipped from Ireland, and can be shipped to both EU and US.
Plates are between 1.5 and 1.6mm thick.
