In collaboration with Project Keyboard, I welcome you to the interest check for GMK Gold Rush. GMK Gold Rush focuses on humanitys desire for gold and is inspired by dusty mine shafts, gravel filled carts, and the glimmer of gold as it starts to peek through the rubble.
Please show your support and interest by filling out this form
https://forms.gle/f9zhuzFaZaAXKb6L8
Colors Used
GMK Gold Rush uses custom colors
Alphas: RAL 1013
Mods: RAL 8019
Legends: RAL 1023(mods), RAL 8019(alphas)
Similar Sets:
While no one technically can own a color, I find that it is important to acknowledge sets that have been run, currently running, or will be run. These are the ones I am aware of and what the similarities are.
GMK Cafe: Uses the same mod color as GMK Cafe - RAL 8019.
GMK Yellowjacket: Similar yellow on dark color(black/brown) mods with lighter colored alphas color scheme.
MG Ember: Similar yellow on dark color(black/brown) mods with lighter colored alphas color scheme.
JTK Yolch: Similar yellow on dark colored(black/brown) mods color scheme.
