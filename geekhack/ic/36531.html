We had a lot of information in the last RGBY interest check. I’m going to try and consolidate everything from memory that we mentioned and see if we can get everything that we want. Since the other interest check was up for a decent amount of time I’m hoping to keep this one to maybe a week or less.
Here are the bullet points that I think we wanted:

  • Double shots from SP
  • Colors - Red, Green, Blue, Yellow, and Orange (Two 1.25x red, blue, and yellow / one 1.25x orange, one green left shift and another green right shift)
  • Black and White legends (Cherry)
  • Iso keys as a two piece set (smallfry can elaborate)
  • 1.75x shift for Pure, and 1x green key to go next to the shift
  • Selling the keys as individuals so that people that think they may wear out left shifts gaming can order extras
  • Maybe slightly different shades of RGBYO than have been done previously. Open to suggestions
  • Maybe 1x size yellow keys to go with 1.5x modifier set
  • Maybe purple or pink alternate colors for orange since we may build the sets a la cart
  • Escape key?
