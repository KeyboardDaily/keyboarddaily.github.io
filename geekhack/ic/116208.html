Introduction(skip if you don’t care)
Lysol here, been working on my ideal 65xt board for the last 9+ months, I made a couple of prototypes with another one
on the way of this version, I wanted to see if anyone would be interested in a run of this design I made.
I like xt layout 65%s but wanted said layout with the 0.75u symmetrical blockers as first seen on the Bauer by Omnitype. I felt like there
was a lack of 65s that had this option and wanted to make/design one myself. which ultimately leaded to the making of… LeFishe
IC FORM
Board Specs

● Layout:WKL 65xt with 0.75u symmetrical blockers (a non WKL top option will be offered if there’s interest.)
● Case Materials: 6063 Aluminum with Brass through weight
● Typing Angle: 7 degrees
● Mount Style: Dual Mount - Top mount with option of o’ring gummy worm gasket mounting
● Plate Materials: Anodized 5052 Aluminum, PC(undecided if frosted or clear) or FR4
● PCB: QMK & VIA/VIAL compatible PCB
● Board Weight: 2.2kg built/1.9kg unbuilt
● O-Rings Included: 60a & 30a 230mmOD CS3.5mm o-rings
● Price Target: $399USD @ 150 moq
● Kit Contents: 1x Aluminum Top and Bottom Case, 1x anodized plate 5052 alu Plate, 3M bumpons, 2x daughterboards & 2x cables(unsleeved),
1x MX+Alps solderable PCB(alps plate files will be supplied), 1x 30a & 60a o-rings, silver m3(m2.5 for the plate) stainless hardware and Custom carrying case with foam inserts
● Effective Keyboard Height(not including bumpons) - 24.52mm

Prototype Pics
this is a previous version of the board, there were some issues I found and iterated on for the final version this IC is for.
some changes include refining the o-ring mount for better o-ring friction on the sides of the inside of the board, changing interior weight
fillets & weight position, and moving weight screws over, and reducing the amount of space beneath the PCB, blemishes on the brass
are from me and ano banding on the right side of the board is a one-off issue just to this prototype.
