So what exactly is the Golbat?
The Golbat is a 40% keyboard that has an adjustable bottom row. This means that you can desolder and resolder in the way that you like. Here are some examples of how Greatfirewall, Ryu have setup their own Golbat:



The case is 4 layers. Top and bottom layers are aluminum and the middle layers are acrylic.
The colors available are the following:
Aluminum：sliver, black, pink, blue, green,purple http://imgsrc.baidu.com/forum/pic/item/9ec0381f95cad1c8298f95cd7a3e6709c83d51b6.jpg
Acrylic：you can choose the colors in this link： http://item.taobao.com/item.htm?spm=a1z10.1-c.w137644-66242819.2.ZNnbyp&id=19615052779
So this gives a really wide availability of colors for your color matching OCD.
The PCB comes with the diodes and resistors soldered on so the only thing left for you to buy is switches, LEDs, stabilizers.



One part of the acrylic goes above the PCB and the other below the PCB.
If you have any other questions regarding the keyboard itself, let me know and I’ll either answer them or forward them to GFW.
Of course I haven’t programmed my PCB yet since it’s not built but I’ll have it built before I start the GB. This is purely the IC.
The aim for the group buy is 20 units with each unit being about 1KG for shipping.
The units will be produced by the friend of GFW in China and shipped to me.
The standard Golbat 40% set will cost $80USD + fees and shipping.
For an extra $15 the HHKB plate will be included (so you’ll get both the standard plate and the HHKB style plate). This is $95USD + fees and shipping.
Shipping it being done from Quebec, Canada and should be $12-17USD anywhere in Canada with insurance for $200 CDN.
To ship to the USA the price is $15-20USD including insurance for $100CDN. (It’s a big jump for the extra insurance).
The range is because Canada Post doesn’t have flat rate boxes and is all dependent on the distance. British Columbia is more expensive than Ontario to ship to. Some ZIP codes in the USA are more expensive but in general it’s $15.
Anodized bolts: Before people ask, unfortunately GFW can’t source them for the GB so they’ll be plain grey.
If there’s something else to clarify, let me know!
