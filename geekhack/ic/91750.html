Greetings!
I have been working on the idea of doing a run of sculpted caps through Maxkey stylized similar to Danger Zone. I will say first I have already contacted Data and I have permission for the usage of the colorway, but the name won’t be used, so the name of this set is WIP. Currently I am working on what keys should be in the set and just seeing if there’s interest out there to hit a 200MOQ. My goal is for the entire base kit to be compatible with a large amount of boards and be 100USD flat. I want an addon pack with yellow, so working on designing that too. I have not contacted Maxkey yet, but I have purchased a few kits from different GBs so I know what pricing to expect. Time frame wise, I heard they’re getting a little backed up, but I would still expect a sub 3 month time frame from GB completion to delivery.
Here’s a render of the current idea I’ve put together. The R2 for Eject should read R1, it’s a Backspace replacement.

I appreciate any ideas you have for addon kit items and such. the little I have is purely based off of Danger Zone and what I enjoyed from Danger Zone. Let me know what you think!
