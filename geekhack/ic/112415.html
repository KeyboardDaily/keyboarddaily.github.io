【IC】Aria F13 TKL - Prototype is here

Hello. This is an IC for the Aria, a F13 TKL.
My goals with this board was to create a TKL with modern features, as well as incorporating some of my favorite designs.
Discord: https://discord.gg/EaDGGqvW3D
Join for more frequent updates, and exclusive renders!
IC Form: https://forms.gle/BF6JTe6uDoU9PpPM6
Features:

  • Centered USB C port with daughterboard

  • Seamless design

  • Dual weight design - Brass and Stainless steel

  • Low profile Silicone Strip Feet

  • Weight centered around alphas for acoustics
    Specs:

  • Top mount or O-Ring mount (Planned orings are 55A silicone and 70A nitrile)

  • F13 WKL Layout

  • 6.9 degree angle

  • 2.6kg or 5lb 11oz without plate or PCB

  • Front Height of 16mm factoring in lip or 20mm without

  • Custom PCB designed by RMI.
    GB info:

  • Price target is $525-550

  • 2 colors, black and silver, fixed WKL.

  • 40-60 units, subject to change. (Will also have some extras/QC backups.)

  • No fixed GB date, but late June at the earliest.

  • Extra PCBs and daughterboards will be available. ($35/$6)
    Accessories (Subject to change):

  • Board, PCB, Plate, assembly hardware

  • 55 and 70A O-rings

  • Microfiber cloth, custom box

  • stickers, card
    Other misc info:

  • A prototype has been ordered, as well as all planned accessories.

  • Engraving on top says “Aria” in katakana

  • Compat with H88, Mechlovin, and other 88 key pcbs, however the JST is in a different position, so YMMV.

  • Here are plate files (non-commercial ONLY): Half_Plate_v3_1635397316.dxf (89.46 kB - downloaded 63 times.) Full_Plate_v2_v1_1639424884.dxf (127.86 kB - downloaded 28 times.) Fixed layout, only adjustability is stepped caps. (No split backspace, no iso, no split right shift.)

  • Plates should have cross compatibility with some other F13 boards, but no guarantees.
