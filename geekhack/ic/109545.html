DSA Lantern is based off of Lisa Minci from Genshin Impact. This set is aimed at Lisa’s overall design and the purple motif that most electro users all seem to share in the game.
Lisa is the librarian of the Knights of Favonius, and is a talented user of Electro magic; she is said to have been the most talented sorceress to study at the Sumeru Arcademia in the last two centuries.
