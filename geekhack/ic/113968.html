Kitting
Here’s a brief explanation for my kitting; the kits are split out this way because of how the MOQ system works for signature plastics.
Transparent keycaps work well with all the other cap sets out there so having them split out this way means more people can buy replacements for keys/bits they might not have otherwise picked up with some other sets.
R2/R4 SA are the same sculpt on rotation, so please take this into account.
I have most of the more common 40s boards supported within Alphas/40s kits, unfortunately due to little to no overlap with other boards and the increase in price for those extra keys I wasn’t able to support the Prime-E or TMOV2 with just the Alphas/40s.
There’s QAZ, Minivan, NeuronV2 and Whimsy support just to name a few! KLE if it’s easier to work with for you.

Q/A
With lead times being so long what’s the lead time on this set?
I’ve been talking with Signature Plastics for a while they’ve allowed me to say that from the point of being invoiced the lead time for this set will be 3months.
There’s 2u,2.25u and 2.75u Convex bars in the Alphas are they actually convex?
Yes, I confirmed with SP that they have those moulds available.
How long is the IC going to be?
Roughly 1 month if all things go well, most of the groundwork has been done. I just need to gauge interest and get feedback on any changes needed.
Are these caps made of polycarbonate?
Yes.
I’m concerned about getting scratches
Polycarbonate is a harder material than ABS and is equivalent to PBT, under normal use you should not be scratching your keycaps.
