About Artisan Case v2
For this revision, my goal is to increase the quality and experience of storing and displaying artisans. These cases are stackable, with a museum quality UV filtering heavy-duty lid to ensure protection from yellowing for your resin collection.
These will come equipped with a foam insert and translucent silicon band to safely secure your caps during transport.
6x9 (54 Slots) - Archival Quality Artisan Storage Case

  • Precision Machined CNC Acrylic
  • Glass Bead Blasted Finish
  • PlexiGlass Hard Coated Lid with UV Protection - more details on PlexiGlass HC
  • Acrylic Display Stand
  • Charcoal Travel Foam
  • Translucent Silicon Travel Band
  • Translucent Bump-on x 4
  • Timestamp Ring
    Cell Dimensions (LxWxH): 0.83” x .0.83” x 0.81”
    Cell Wall Height: 0.34”


  • Acrylic display stand included
  • Soft travel foam and silicon travel band to keep your caps safe during travel

*/note that production silicon band will be translucent

  • Stackable with thick 3/8” bump-ons

Lead Times, Quantity, and Price
