Midway60 | Bolsa Supply


The Midway60 is the first board designed in house by Bolsa Supply in collaboration with fellow community board designer and good friend N.Rdy. The goal Midway60 aims to achieve is extremely simple: to look, sound, and feel good. Midway60 is essentially a combination of features and aspects that we as keyboard enthusiasts like in keyboards. 60% layout, flex, and o-ring sandwich gasket because of the sound signature that it provides. N.Rdy took these features and effectively brought those ideas to reality with the Midway60.
The name is not anything special or related to keyboards, but holds meaning to us and Bolsa as a brand. Where Bolsa Supply is originated from, there is a city named Midway City in which Bolsa Avenue (where the brand name comes from) is the main street. Midway city; Midway60

—–
Design Highlights

  • Exaggerated seam
  • Front/rear slant on the top and bottom side of the case
  • Enlarged o-ring channels on the interior to accommodate the usage of screw-in stabilizers
  • Low front height due to slant at 13.75mm from tip of angle (17.75mm from highest point of slant)

—–
Board Details

  • 60% Layout, available in both WK, WKL, and HHKB.
  • 7 degree typing angle
  • Materials: Anodized Aluminum (6063) chassis, brass weight
  • Color options: Black, Gray, and Navy
  • Plate Options: Aluminum default, Polycarbonate and Carbon Fiber as addons
  • PCB: 60% Solder PCB using an Ai03 styled Daughterboard (center USB). Designed by FJLabs.
  • Mounting Method: O-Ring Sandwich Mount utilizing a AS568-262 O-Ring.
  • Roughly 1.5kg fully built

Vendors, Pricing, and Sales Format

  • This board will be sold FCFS via raffle
  • Standard edition will be priced at $370, LE will be priced at $425.
  • This will be a limited group buy, limited to 125 spots. We are keeping it limited not for the sake of exclusivity, but because this is our first board group buy. As with any first time project we want to make sure we are not biting more than we can chew and that this group buy goes as smoothly as it possibly can for a 2 man team.
  • Group buy date is still TBD, but we are aiming to have the sale happen closer to the end of Q2.
  • Group buy turn around time will be estimated at 4-6 months after the sale has ended. As with all group buys please take this timeline with a grain of salt as projects like these can be subjected to delays.

5/10/2021

  • We have found that there is a slight tolerance issue with the plate sandwiching in between the top and bottom piece which causes the assembly to have some movement even when the case is fully put together. To alleviate this issue, all kits will receive thin poron strips that can be applied to the top case piece. These strips will help keep the assembly in place and will eliminate any movement while keeping typing sound and feel the same. Alternatively, you can also use electrical tape on the plate tabs if you do not wish to use the poron strips, or you can just leave the plate as is (the board is 100% usable without any of these modifications).
  • We are on track for a end of June release! The sale is now being switched to a raffle style sale which we think will be a bit more fair. Additionally, this will allow users to select whichever plate they want versus having the board come standard with an aluminum plate. Users can also add in any extra’s (PCB’s and plates) they would like to include. Pricing is solidified at $370 before shipping.
