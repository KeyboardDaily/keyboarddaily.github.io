    The REDACTIONS will be pad printed over the double shot legends. We have gotten samples from GMK and they are really cool.

More
They sent us a version with one layer of pad print and one with double pad print.
The single pad print has nearly no difference in texture from the regular cap, yet you can slightly see the legend showing through.
The double pad printed is lifted a tiny bit where you can feel the edge of the print if you rub your finger back and forth on it.
But the legend beneath is completely covered.
I have spent a few weeks with both of these options on the E and R keys of my board and to be honest, I really cant tell a difference
in them when I am just in the rhythm of typing. My wife has also typed on them for a while and I asked her if she could tell the difference,
and she couldn’t either. The redactions are not placed on the main keys most people rest on so I believe the double would be okay.
The double will succeed in the theme of this set the best.
