【IC】Prime_M - Prototype delivered!! (updated 2|7|2018)

September 30, 2017 IC, 其他 本文总阅读量次

作者: holtenc
发布时间: 2017-09-30 11:56:21

原链接: https://geekhack.org/index.php?topic=91821.0

Features:

  • CNC aluminum case with integrated thick switch plate

  • Brass bottom cover for the extra weight

  • A full kit that includes fully assembled PCB - ATMEGA32u2

  • High profile sides to cover gap between plate and bottom of caps

  • Support for aluminum feet

  • In-switch back lighting

  • Flexible layout on bottom row and right-most column

  • Standard backlight support

  • Weighs 1.25lbs fully assembled
    Kit will include the following:

  • Aluminum Case & Brass Bottom

  • PCB

  • 3x Cherry PCB mount Stabilizers

  • 10x Stainless steel scews

  • 6x Rubber bump-on pads

  • Updated 2/7/2018*
    Just a quick update. Unfortunately in order to keep overall costs/kit down to a reasonable level I’ve decided to limit the color choices to black, silver and blue. The particular anodizing provider only offers those 3 colors as “standard”. Any other colors comes with a substantial price increase. All that being said, I don’t think there is much of a reason to do a group buy / pre-order sort of thing.
    For anyone that is interested in black, silver or blue, you will be able to purchase those kits from my site in around a 1-2 months. I haven’t yet ordered the PCBs so I can’t give an exact price. But I can guarantee that it will be below $150/kit. Hopefully around $130.
    Sorry if this is disappointing to anyone that was looking forward to other colors. I’m pretty disappointed myself. Any future projects I do (prime_O will be next) will be done differently to provide more color choices.
