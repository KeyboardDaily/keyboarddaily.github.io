Have you ever procrastinated so hard that you made an entirely new keyboard, while trying to finish a keyboard?
Well I’m only half joking, here is my second keyboard design I would like to share with you all:
Aqua F-rowless TKL



More renders of different colors and angles can be found here: https://ellipse-antelope-7c7s.squarespace.com/keyboardcatalog/aqua-f-rowless-tkl
Password:keyboards
https://imgur.com/a/nJ25vvd
Some of the renders might be a bit outdated, the design now uses an unified daughterboard on the right side.
Inspiration and Design:
I wanted to make a flowing water-themed keyboard, so I spent a long time trying to experiment with different corner designs and symmetries trying to make metal resemble water flowing over the edge. After a while, I finally arrived at this design. The bottom carries over the flowing corners from above but I tried to do something different with the feet by having it resemble a canyon with a river cutting through the middle.
So yeah, I don’t want to make this too long, but I have one really important note:I WOULD LIKE TO COLLABORATE WITH SOMEONE THAT CAN DESIGN AND PROTOTYPE A PCB FOR THIS BOARD.I don’t know enough about pcb manufacturing and prototyping to feel comfortable trying to do this alone. If you are interested, please reach out to me, we can work out something together. Once a pcb prototype is ready I will make at least one prototype, and if there is enough interest I will start a small private run where people can contact me if they want to join. I don’t want to take on too much at once and not be able to deliver properly. If you are a vendor, please leave a note if you would be ok with taking orders for this board too, I want to be as transparent and honest as possible.
Please take this strawpoll:
https://www.strawpoll.me/45589374/
Specs:
5 degree typing angle
frowless TKL
gasket mount
CNC aluminum with either aluminum or brass weight
Notes:
Are the feet uneven?: No, every part of the keyboard that contacts the desk is flat at 5 degrees.
Will the keyboard be unstable?: No, the feet run along the entire length of the top and bottom.
Will X layout be supported?: I’m not sure, maybe during an actual run, but right now I’m just trying to make it as simple as possible to prevent mistakes along the way.
Estimated price?: I know the prototype pricing for 1 is around 1k, but I would need to know how many I’m making for a better estimate.
Does it use unified daughterboard?: yes
Questions:
What colors would you guys be interested in?
Would you guys also like a brass weight option?
I’m thinking of making something like brass bottom for a single run only:

but it would probably be super expensive. Would that be something you would be interested in?
