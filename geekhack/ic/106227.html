//About: //
Hello everyone, I hope all of you are doing well during this unstable time. My name is Mellperbia.
Today I am brining you the latest products by CMM.Studio (藏猫猫, Hide and Seek).
Here it is, a reborn 75% kit —- Returned Wing
If you are familiar with the design, you might see some shadows from Yellow Chickens half-feather design a while ago ( https://geekhack.org/index.php?topic=101196.0). Sadly, half-feather did not come to reality. Fortunately, we were able to pick it up from where it was left off and giving the half-feather a new life. The brand-new half-feather is named as //Returned Wing //. The first concept is a design for Yellow Chicken himself to hit his endgame and enjoy his life. Secondly, it also means the reborn of half-feather project. We polished and perfected the half-feather and we are very proud to bring this wing to you. Let the wing guide you the path and fly among the sky!
While keeping all the original half-feather layout, front face logo weight, in order to improve the touch and feel, we almost redone the structure and make it more precise, more logical. At the same time, we also did some changes to the appearance. We bestow the wing clearer lines with back side plate.
Of course, with the new look of Returned Wing, we believe as a keyboard, it must perfect its basic task, typing. We are tremendously proud to announce our PCB partnership with Obins.lab, which designed the visual interface for Anne Pro 2. Our firmware will be supported by Obins.lab and we are thrilled to say perhaps this will be one of the most advance and mature PCB. We customized special driver, optimized features and visual interface. Undoubtedly, this PCB will meet all your expectation for a DIY mech keyboard.

//Details and Pricing: //

  • Price: From $360 USD (Full aluminum)
  • Case color: Light Blue, Pink, Dark Green, Midnight Blue, Red, Lilac Purple, Black, Silver, Dust Blue and Space Grey
  • Housing material: 6061 anodized aluminum, 180 mesh emery
  • Color of switch plate: For aluminum plates, the same as the case. Optional, PVD/sandblasting brass/copper
  • Weight material: standard aluminum, same as case. Optional CNC polished stainless steel and CNC polished brass
  • Material of inscription bar: Standard aluminum, same shell, optional: same as weight
  • Nameplate Material: Standard double nameplate with logo and no logo; polycarbonate primary color and optional aluminum color same as the case+Copper/brass primary color(rose gold or gold)
  • Structure: Top mount, there are rubber o rings at both sides of the switch plate to absorb shock.
  • Layout: non-classic 75% with all 1.25u bottom line
  • Slope: 7.5 degrees
    The GB will start around June to mid-July (until the prototypes are satisfied)

    //Compatibility: //
  • 5 pins PCB mount: Gateron standard (compatible with all switches)
  • Soldering / hot swapping optional
  • Type C interfacing
  • Full key customization
  • All keys has no conflict when pressing them together
  • 2mm thickness
  • Cross Platforms Support (macOS, iOS, Windows, Linux)
  • Online firmware update
  • Multi-languages support
    //RGB lighting: //
  • Full-key RGB, closed by default
  • Shortcut keys control and driver control. Multi presets can be chosen.
  • Independent nameplate lighting module can be linked with keys RGB lighting, switched on by default and support two lighting effects: breathing and constant light
