The teaser



//////BUILD STREAM LIVE ON TopClack today, 11/17 @ 12pm PST//////
https://www.twitch.tv/topclack
The StoryI really like 65% keyboards. Something about dedicated arrow keys. But I also really like split boards. I think there was a time in the 90’s when I would read 2600 or PC Mag and I would dream of owning a super cool ergo board. The fancy split microsoft boards. But I never owned one. But I have always had that ergo life in me. I enjoyed the em7, but it was big. I really enjoy the Alice, but its like a super model and what I need is a bottom. So thats what this is designed to be: semi ergo workhorse that has all the features I like and allows for my wrists to go home happy at the end of the day. Ive been through almost 40 “high end” keyboards this year, and though this wont be my “end game” board, my goal is for it to be my daily board that I use for everything. And instead of having 10+ boards I can have 3-5. Hopefully some of you are looking for something similar.
The Name: Doppelgänger. Needs no explanation.
The Details

  • Split 65%++ With Macro Pad

  • Top Mount

  • 6 degree typing angle

  • Split or Unsplit

  • Bottom Row: 1.25 1 1.25 2.25 1 2.75 1.25 1 1.25

  • QMK firmware

  • Left: single plate, full left shift or split

  • Right: iso plate, ansi plate + split bs option

  • Same pcb on both sides

  • USB-C for all connections

  • There are 4 USB-C connectors. 2 on each half. the insides will connect the boards together. The outsides give you the option of connecting to the PC. So it can come form the left, or from the right.

  • The case angles are 90 degrees from the plane on which it sits. This is a small detail that would normally be lost. Most keyboads have the same angle as their typing angle, but I really wanted a 90 degree. It just gives it a good stature on the desk.

  • Dimensions:
    Left half: 189.449 mm L /* 117.351 mm W /* 32.358 mm D
    right half: 194.305 mm D /* 117.351 mm W /* 32.358 mm D

  • *The Proto/Design**This started its life back in June. I worked with Westfoxtrot to get the drawings where I wanted them. Yiancar designed a PCB to work. Then it started getting built in August. It has a high failure rate on the tops because the design had a top that increased 1.8 degrees more than the plate, so that the top row would be 2.5mm sucken into the top of the board.) So that will change. But now I have a real proto, and it will be built by Jae of Top Clack on 11/17.

  • *Revisions**After the build stream, the board is going to hop in a box and then get shipped to me. Once on hand, Ill do a through inspection and qc, and make some changes. There are already a few things that will change. One is the top angle. The next are the blocker vents will be increased in size very slightly. Right now they are the 1u size, but instead I will match them to the case and not the switch size. There will be a logo/print engraved on the weights. There may be some other small revisions.

  • *Interest Check**Ill get a form going on 11/18 for feedback and wishes. Most things are set but if there is an overwhelming request for something or some new epiphany, I will make the revisions. This will take us into early December.

  • *Communication**I will be running this through GH. I will not have a discord server or discord updates. Everthing will be communicated through here, including additional pictures/videos. I am available here or Discord, so if you want to chat and just talk about anything, the stars alignment or how lemon water is delicious, dm me or im noahf/#2971 on discord.

  • Group Buy*
    Im hoping this can be ran early December. If everything is good and I can get all the revisions done by end of month, I dont see why that cant happen early December. If not, then it may get moved Jan. The goal is 40 units, fcfs. I dont see this being super hard to get. Price is expected to be 375 shipped to CONUS, with a little more (real shipping costs) for international shipping.
    GB Options: Most important: Colors will be black, silver, and then one or two colors. Maybe purple, red, or navy. I will need ano chips before moving forward on color. Plate options: Left plate is the same for all, right plate will be either ISO or ANSI. Plate materials: plan to offer brass, alu, pc, pom, cf. Brass will likely be default because why not.

  • Two Fancy Additional Goals*
    To provide some type of tenting/palm rest option. Also to get a cable maker to do a fancy interconnect cable that will make your reddit posts really pop.

  • Failed experimentation*

    I tried to do a top angle that was different from the plate, so that gmk style keycaps would “sink” a little towards the top, allowing for the top blockers to really “pop” and not get lost. Sadly, this had a really high failure rate. a 2-3 week proto took 2-3 months. So the top will be boring.
