Hello everyone. After thinking about it for a little bit I have decided to launch an IC for my Ferrari F1 themed keycap set.
Even if you are not a fan of Ferrari, it is a household name in motorsports and their legacy is undeniable. This is a pretty early stage IC and a lot of work would need to be done if this ever moved forward
The general idea is to match the SF90, the 2019 Ferrari F1 Car.

It currently features one GMK custom color, Ferrari Red, /#FF2800. The yellow in the set matches the yellow of the Ferrari logo, not the shell logo on the car.
I am currently using GMK stock color CV and while it is not an exact match, it is very close and I am considering custom matching if there is interest.
I will not be using any Ferrari or Formula 1 IP for novelties or naming.
I am currently toying with the two following color schemes.
White on red alphas, red on black mods.

Black on red alphas, white on black mods.

If you are at all interested in this set, please take a few moments to fill out this form. Thanks!
