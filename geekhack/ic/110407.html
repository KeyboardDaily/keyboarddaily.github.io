Hi everyone, I would like to present a new tripleshot hiragana sub-legend keycap set called “JTK Tanjun”.
Tanjun (単純) is the Japanese word for simplicity. This set focuses on simple, subtle, and unobtrusive beauty.
The japanese word for this aesthetic is “shibui (渋い)”.
This set will be produced by JTK using tripleshot technology, made with ABS in their CA (cherry) profile.
