Many people have contacted me that “Missing Keys” was too big of a set and they only needed the short right shift which is so difficult to come by. Others would have liked to see 2 short right shift keys instead of one in the kit of “Missing Keys”.
So without further ado, I hereby present to you the GB for just that short right shift (and possibly a complementary fn key).

In case you are wondering what this short shift is good for?
It is an extremely cheap way to make good use of ABS doubleshot keycaps from an OG keyboard on a 60% or HHKB Layout (using the uparrow as Fn key). With the additional Fn key, it would even allow to build 65% and 75% (because the uparrow is used otherwise and you need an additional R4 key to the right of it).
Just to be clear. This IC is for ONE (1) key or possibly TWO (2) keys.
You must have this to benefit from it.
