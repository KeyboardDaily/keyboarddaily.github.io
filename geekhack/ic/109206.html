GB is live at https://shop.wuquestudio.com/products/wuque-bear-switch-opener
Some people like to use a switch opener on a desk, some like to use one by holding it in their hands. So weve designed a switch opener that both groups can enjoy
Meet our Wuque Bear Switch Opener! Traditionally the switch openers are 2-in-1, but the Wuque Bear is a 4-in one switch opener. Each half has an MX style and a Kailh style switch opener.
The bottom half has a flat, sturdy base that can be rested on a desk and used. The bigger than usual size and weight should make it harder to move around as youre using it.
The top half has a rounded base thats better to grim with your hands. Magnets hold the two halves together when youre not using it.
Comes in a few different anodized and electrophoresis finish. Please keep in mind that over time the ano/coating will come off from the parts that come in contact with the switch due to friction.
