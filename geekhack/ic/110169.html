GMK Seafarer
“Master of the seven seas”
Inspiration
The original colorway was inspired by the uniforms of our Navy sailors on the high seas, and with novelties inspired by sailing and navigation.
Why?
I love minimal sets, LOVE blue, and have always been fascinated with

everything about sailing and the sea but could not really find a nice set
that would fit my requirements, so here we are.
