【IC】Promise87 - The Next Level of Customization in TKL

Hi Everyone,
Customization was one of the focal points from the very beginning of this design. We’ve been thinking about ways to offer the next level of customization, not just customization through changing just the plate, switches, and keycaps. Our keyboards have always been highly customizable, and we hope we’ve pushed the options a little further with this new addition - The Promise87
Along with lots of other customizable options, we are giving you the option to engrave your own text/graphic to the bottom of the Promise87!
Promise87 Prototype Photos: https://imgur.com/a/XfqOQYQ




Ultimate Customization Options
Promise87 - Standard Units
A Promise87 bespoke unit can have 8 different customizable components. We will pick some of the most popular combos and offer them as standard units, without any custom engraving on the back weights.
Read on below to see more details on the customizable components.

    Improved Gasket Material & Design
    Open-source Gasket Slip Design with New Material

  • Gasket slip design, easier to install

  • Strong stability, fireproof, its very durable and keeps original flex after long-time usage

  • Density between PORON and silicone. There will be 2-3 different stiffness in different colors for you to choose the feeling you like

  • Open source
    Wuque studio will keep stocking the gasket slips and also welcome other board designers to use our slips, we can provide specs you need and make sure that everyone will be able to replace different slips all the time.
    New Plate & Switch Foam Design
    We created a mold using our new foaming material to make an 2 in 1 dampener kit, it will make the overall dampening system more stable and helps to make typing sound better. We will release typing sound test in the next update.
    Pricing

  • Standard Edition: ~$500 USD (PVD editions may be more)

  • Bespoke Edition: ~$800 USD

  • Bespoke Edition (with Moissanite): ~$900 USD
