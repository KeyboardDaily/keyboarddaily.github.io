Hi everyone!
My name’s HOBI3CAT, and you may know me as the one-man-show behind Fraktal Kaps ( fraktalkaps.com).
However, after seeing some other acrylic artisan cases out there, and thinking “I can do it better”,
I’ve decided to put my Mechanical Engineering degree towards something other than artisan keycaps.
Without further ado, I present The Fraktal Chest!

First prototype in clear
SPECS
 Semi-modular 6x8 grid, holds 48 1u caps
 0.80” x 0.80” x 0.24” boxes will fit any standard-sized keycaps
 Can fit caps up to ~0.85” tall (may change slightly with new acrylic supplier)
 Constructed of stacked and interlocking acrylic layers
 Magnetic clear lid
 Thumb cutouts for easy lid removal
 Competitive price point (more info below)
 Optional 30° stand
 Will include foam to keep artisans in place during travel
The Fraktal Chest is a 6x8 artisan storage box with semi-modular dividers that can be removed to suit your artisan sizing needs.
The case itself is an acrylic sandwich design, but each layer is split into two “L” shapes, allowing MUCH less material to be wasted in the
cutting process, and significantly decreasing overall material cost (a savings which we pass on to you!). Like most sandwich-style case
designs, it uses button head bolts and threaded standoffs to hold it together, but this case also uses press-fit magnets used to hold the lid
on. After some initial feedback and testing, I deemed that magnets were a much cleaner and user-friendly option over thumb screws.
The lid is secure, but with some thumb cutouts and the magnets, it is very easy to remove and replace (for the overly-active trader ).
The standout feature of this case, however, is that the dividers that make up the “grid” are removable, which will let you take some out
to fit artisans that are sized larger than 1u!
