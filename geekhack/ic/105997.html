Introduction
안녕하세요!

I’ve been working on this set for a while, and I’m super excited to introduce PBT Modern Hangul
Here is the original KLE. I will be using proper OG icon mods, so please ignore the text mods! (More on sublegends below)

Inspiration/Naming
With a name like Modern Hangul, it only makes sense that the set is clean and minimal, with a splash of colour. I started by wanting a monochrome kit made in PBT. Red and blue are deeply personal colours and add a lot of personality to a rather minimal set. Since these two colours have followed me throughout the years, I wanted to bring them to my keycaps as well. I’ve really come to enjoy these two colours, and I hope that you will too! All colours are either Pantone or RAL.
I noticed that we have a distinct lack of Hangul kits, so I’ve decided to make those the default legends in this set. However, if there is enough interest in the IC phase, I will strongly consider adding in regular alphas as well! I’m trying to have this made in PBT, and I am currently waiting to hear back from HammerWorks CRP as well as ePBT to decide which PBT manufacturer I will go with. The black mods have been purposely chosen to be lighter than previous attempts at reverse dye-subbed black keycaps, so they should be a fair bit easier to achieve.
Sublegends
As shown in the KLE, I’d love to aim for grey sublegends to stay true to the minimal design and to have the main legends be the primary focus. They’re sublegends after all! Initial renders don’t show this, so I will work on that ASAP. Additionally, I’d like to ask for your feedback on the inclusion of sublegends on the mods. Is that something you would want?
Kits
Considering the large number of kits that most keycap sets have, I wanted to keep it nice and simple, while including the maximum amount of compatibility in the main sets. I’m open to feedback for the kits, so if you have any suggestions, please remember to fill out the form at the bottom of this post!
Base

Base kit supports pretty much every standard layout from 60% and up, minus full-size and Alice.
Numpad/ISO

Numpad and ISO kit together for my European and full-size friends
Spacebars

Spacebars kit adds a bit more colour, as well as support for Alice-style boards.
