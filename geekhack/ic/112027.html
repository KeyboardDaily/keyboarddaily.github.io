Same set, different manu

[IMPORTANT] NEWS AND UPDATES
Why leave GMK?
My plan for this set was always to run through GMK. Why? Because it was ironic. But recently GMK has been very unappealing as a manufacturer.
We originally requested quotes in September of 2021 and even until now, they have not given any sort of update or response other than the typical ‘we’re working on it’ (just paraphrasing).
I finally decided that enough was enough, and that if I couldn’t trust them with something as simple as communicating about quotes, it was time to take matters into my own hands and give the people what they want.
Who is KBM?
KeyBee Manufacturing is a new manufacturer based in the US. They’ve already taken on sets such as Love Detective, Purseidon, and Pink Poodle just to name a few.
