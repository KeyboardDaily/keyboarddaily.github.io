Okey v1 Keyboard
Hello Keeb community!
I am newish to the community and newer to keyboard design. But am having allot of fun dipping my hand into something different and challenging. I have been working on what i call the Okey v1. A 65% board with a small Macro on the left. Addtionally i wanted to be more customizable with the layout so I added removable and replaceable blockers.
IC Forum: https://docs.google.com/forms/d/1fN4iG_H_JOQmOwb26AIL9l9OGj1sFXSGeGN0tevfax8/edit
Here are the features I have added so far:
1.**Mounting -**Top mount design (Simple and Easy)
2.**Blockers -**There is a top right 1u blocker and a middle right 2u. Both can be replaced by “Blank” blockers that allow for the full row
3.**Case Material -**Aluminum
4.Plate Material - Plate is planned to be aluminum, but looking into brass as well
5.Plate Design - Plate will have options for stepped caps, split left shift, split space, ISO enter and split backspace

6.**PCB -**Will be black with no under-glow(No case options to see it anyway)
7.**Colors -**Black, white and Silver are planned. As IC continues it can change
8.**Price -**TBD
9.GB - This i will need some assistance with. I am not an experienced vendor (and will not pretend to be). So if anyone knows a guy or would like to help im am open to it!
10.Timeframe - This is still early, working with cnc machinest to find what processes are possible and what are not.
Updates:

  1. Navy and Green! From the IC so far Navy is a popular color as well as a forest-ish green (Dark Green). I will work on making them available as well.
