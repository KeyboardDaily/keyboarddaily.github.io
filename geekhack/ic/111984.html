Hey guys, huge thanks for your patience on this set, we very much appreciate your continued support. We have quite a considerable amount of things to share with you all. Firstly, we are going to reveal prices for the kitting as well as finally set a group buy date for the set in the very near future. There is also a change to vendors adding an SEA and Mexico vendor to the list.
Secondly, If you have not heard, we are collaborating with CMM to bring a special Dreaming Girl themed version of the Fuji 75 keyboard. We also have partnered with Gateron to make our own unique switch that will go along with the set, otherwise known as the Usagi switch. The top housing will be a clear PC with a KS-8 bottom housing and a UHMWPE stem. The price for the switches is also included on here.
We have also decided that what better to provide our own colored stabilizers (white POM stem with blue POM bottom) along with our switches.
We additionally would like to announce that we will be making our own chibi bunny girl trading cards. One will follow the Dreaming Girl keycap set and the other card will go along with the purchase of the limited Dreaming Girl Fuji 75.
Finally, we have also been noticing concerns about sublegends for the UK ISO keys, and we have tried to work with JTK to put the sublegends on the keys, however, it unfortunately will not happen as it requires completely new molds.
Please join the JTK and Otakeebs discord for more frequent updates!
