Introduction
Hello keyboard enthusiasts, we are excited to introduce the Serratus by Keynergy. Serratus is the first project by Keynergy started back in 2019, we’ve made several prototypes and ran a local group buy in Korea last year. (These units are being done and started shipping) Serratus features the 2mm plate exposed between the top and bottom housings, PVD brass weight bar on the bottom and beautifully CNC machined sides.

Sale Info
This would be more of pre order than group buy as we already have cases CNCed and plates/ weights are produced. We have 3 options for the case finish including anodization, e-coating and chrome plating. Each base kit will include 1 PCB, 1 Aluminum plate or PVD Brass plate($20 additional), 1 Polycarbonate plate, PVD Brass Weight. Total units for this pre order will be 60 pieces and 5 of them will be the limited Chrome edition. We expect 4 months before the shipping starts.
