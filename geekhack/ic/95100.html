Been working on this since I saw Isle of Dogs last month. I absolutely loved the typography and wanted to use the typeface and colors in a set.
The type uses Lineto’s Brown in Bold Italic just like the film and alphas also include hiragana characters. Many SP produced keycaps use their very large text but I’ve always been a fan of smaller, more minimal type. It’s my first time designing a keycap set so feedback and advice is appreciated! Would be so cool to make this real.
Also noting I have the font licensed from Lineto, and my design doesn’t infringe on any copyrights (no images or assets from the film, colors are not exact).
