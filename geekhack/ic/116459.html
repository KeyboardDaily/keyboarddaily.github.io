DESIGN
The design and direction I’m going for this time still revolves around the ramen cart with keyboard switch elements but the biggest difference would be the theme of the era which is sci-fi.
I chose sci-fi to portray the face pace nature of technology and time and the ever need to keep moving forward with the change of times.
It also serves as a reflection that ramen carts or food carts are fast disappearing from our food culture and identity being one day replaced by androids to serve.
It is still a WIP so any feedback would be appreciated
