Hello everyone.
Welcome to the Adélie [Ah-day-lee].
Pronunciation may vary by your region, this is how the English pronounce it an how I will be saying it.

IC Form: https://forms.gle/NyBcXXk8jVNVA975A
Inspiration
As more people should, I take this hobby pretty lightly. The Adélie is named after a type of penguin. I like penguins.
The board layout inspiration comes from a combination of the CA66, my previous board, the 45-ATS, and the TMO50 v1.
The board shape inspiration comes from a combination of the CA66, Elephant S7, and the side “weights” are something that I pulled from the GMK Q100.
There was a lot of interest in the 45-ATS but some people were wishing for the full punctuation. I hear your call - here’s my answer.
Board Details
Gasket mount.
6 Degree typing angle.
Side weight accents.
WS28 Indicator LED’s underneath the caps like newer Minivan.
Daughter board, centered USB.
[Bottom/Internal] Brass Weight.
Approx. 1.94 kg [4.285 lbs] unbuilt assuming brass plate.
Color Options
Black - Brass, PVD Chrome Weights
Dark Navy - Brass, PVD Chrome Weights
Rose Red - Brass, PVD Gold Weights
E-White - Brass, PVD Gold Weights
Color options are only speculated; materials and finishes are not final.
Anticipating offering other plate options.
