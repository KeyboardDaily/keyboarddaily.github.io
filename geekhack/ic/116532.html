Key Specs

  • Slider on the right side of the board where the Ins-PgDn keys are on a 75%

  • Top mount

  • 5° typing angle

  • CNC aluminum case (6061, 6063, or 7075)

  • ~Half a kilo brass weight

  • Total case and plate weight 1830 g (4 freedom pounds)

  • Specs In detail*
    The FjordBoard is a top-mounted 75% keyboard that removed the PrtSc, Pause, Ins, End, PgUp, and PgDn keys. In the place of the Ins-PgDn keys are replaced with a slider that has knob-like functionality without the overused knob. In my opinion, knobs are cool but are being overused so instead of just removing these keys because I don’t like them I added a slider. It should be programmable with QMK. The case will be CNC aluminum (6061,6063, or 7075) anodized for all colors except white (white will be E-coated). The weight will be brass should weigh just under half a kilo. I plan to ship with an FR4 plate but provide plate files so you can cut your own other materials. 5° typing angle. Hot-swap USB c PCB.

  • Expected Pricing*
    The target price is 350usd. E-white (if used) will cost more. This may change please don’t take this ad anywhere close to the final. Yes, it’s up sorry about that. Not much I can do; my original was unrealistically low. I am going to try it minimize costs as much as I can.
