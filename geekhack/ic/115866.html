DesignerStudio

Greetings! We are DesignerStudio. We are currently dedicated to designing unique and excellent feeling switches. Since we were formerly working in the antique industry, blending the traditional and elegant Chinese culture and elements into our design is our goal. And we hope to bring more interesting and insightful custom mechanical keyboard products in the near future.
White Jade Switches
White Jade Switch is our first switch product. After its release, users have positive comments on this product, describing it as strong tactile feels and quick feedback. And many have expected us to restock the product. So it comes back again!
Specifications
Tactile
PC top, nylon bottom cases
Pom long pole stem
Spring: 67g gold plated spring
Pin Mounting: 5-Pin
Standard Factory lube
Manufacturer: JWK
Force Curve

Graphite Gold Switches
*Graphite Gold Switches is our second switch product and first linear switch product. On this product, we applied our exclusive G1® material composed of several different materials like Pom, UPE, paraffin, etc (Ratio ≈ 1:1:1). The coefficient of friction is //0.05//(based on /JIS K 7125/ testing method). And the great self-lubing ability helps to reduce the noise after a short time of use. The overall typing experience will be smooth and delightful! exclusive*
Specifications
Linear
Full Nylon housing(gray and black)
G1 long pole stem (mix of pom, upe, paraffin, etc.)
Spring: 63.5g 18mm gold plated spring
Pin Mounting: 5-Pin
Standard Factory lube
Manufacturer: JWK
Force Curve
