STAY CREATIVE DESKMATS
Designed by stellaidoscope.
VENDORS UPDATED, FEBRUARY 2022. GB FEBRUARY 28th, 2022.
IC FORM
Quote Ideas swim around in the pond of the internet, and sometimes we get swept by the wave of burnouts as we fish for inspiration.
We’re all creative, one way or another. Everything is an art form– visuals, code, even keyboards. This design came forth from me wanting to remind myself to keep going, when I feel like there’s nothing more to create.
What’s decided:
900mm x 400mm x 5mm.
Stitched edges, colourmatched to Pantone TCX.
Maximum of 6 colourways.
“Not another deskmat IC!”
This IC aims to collect data mainly for the following:
Deskmat thiccness of choice: 4mm or 5mm. 5mm it is.
Regions of interest. - IC responses so far have been fruitful, but will still wait for more responses until the week leading up to GB. Vendor list changed!
Other colourways. Sixth and final colourway added! Black and White / Monochrome was a top choice from IC responses.
Changes to be made:
None.
