Taoyao (Peach Bloom) Switches

*Hello again, this is DesignerStudio. We are proud to announce our third linear product Taoyao (Peach Bloom) switches. The switches use the same mold as graphite gold switches, but we aimed to modify our G1® Stem to address some assembly problems. The key inspiration for the switch was taken from the Chinese classic literature work Classic of Poetry(诗经). The name Taoyao 桃夭 means the peach blossoms are blooming at their best age. We want to share this beautiful message with everyone as spring is coming!*
Specifications
Linear
Full Nylon housing(pink and light red)
G1 long pole stem (mix of pom, upe, paraffin, etc.)
Spring: 63.5g 18mm gold plated spring
Actuation Force: 48g
Bottom-out Force: 63.5g
Pin Mounting: 5-Pin
Travel Distance: 3.8mm
Standard Factory lube or No lube
Manufacturer: JWK
