Hey Guys! This is an IC for my first pass at a custom set, meant to evoke the feeling of diving in some tropical paradise, DSA Aquanaut!
I’ve designed a full set of custom legends for this, including both text and icon modifiers and a few novelties.
DSA profile was chosen because I love the versatility of non-sculpted profiles, but I’d definitely be open to considering something else. (Maybe all SA Row 3?)
Keyreative is going to be be producing the set, so it will be KAM profile!
I’ve included some faux-renders (and some actual renders!) that I put together, as well as the different kits I’m proposing.
Thanks for taking a look, and I’d love to hear your feedback!
Google Poll: https://goo.gl/forms/jO3HpMYs5Rpe2nG93
Updates
02/19/19 - Slight tweaks to legends (M, Y, 3, Comma, Semicolon)
02/27/19 - This past week I’ve been trying to line up some partners to help get this made. I think I should have a bigger update on that soon. Added Polarnaut test.
03/15/19 - Tweaked colors after getting physical pantone chips, and added a few renders. zFrontier and Keyreative will be helping to get this made.
