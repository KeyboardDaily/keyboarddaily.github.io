Welcome to GMK EDC!
Hi everyone! GMK EDC is originally inspired by my old everyday carry backpack. Unfortunately, I recently had to retire it (rip), but I love the colorway and thought it would fit well on a keyboard. This is my first design so any type of feedback would be amazing.
