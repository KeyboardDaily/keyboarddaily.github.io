Design
I love avocados. I mean, who doesn’t love avocados? Okay maybe… not everyone… but you have to admit, not only they are healthy, but the colours they have also look very pleasing. Nowadays, we have avocado-coloured cars, avocado-coloured clothes and even fridges, why not bring them on our keyboard?
The colours of this keyset are taken straight from an avocado. Iconic avocado green legends over greenish-blond and dark reddish-brown coloured alpha base and modifier base. With the keyset on, your keyboard just looks like a fresh cut avocado!
No avocados were harmed during the design process.
Updates:
1. New kits
Since some people are asking for a green color themed alphas kit and also the first version of the kits have some problems( ie. some unneeded keys could have been removed), I then decided to re-think about what to put in each kits. Me personally is not a big fan of having too much kits(which could be really frustrating when deciding which kits to buy).
So in order to simplify the kits, JTK Avocado will only be having 4 kits: Green Alphas kit, Yellow Alphas kit, Modifiers kit and Spacebars kit. Novelties will be included in the alphas kit and modifiers kits.
2. Icon kits removed.
I have decided to remove the icon kit which I have designed before. Since new icons will needs new moulds and will lead to a rise in price, JTK Avocado will be using the icons that JTK already have(Same to JTK Arctic’s Icons). I will try my best to lower the price.
3. About the yellow colour that I have chosen.
Many of my friends keeps worry about that the yellow color that I have chosen because it is similar to GMK Corsa‘s color. And GMK Corsa is a keyset that wasn’t selling very well since the color looked so much different in real life comparing to the renders. To prevent this (Actual product colour different from renders) from happening, after the group buy is closed, JTK will send me color samples that they have ordered from the factory which makes raw ABS plastics. If the samples aren’t accurate enough, I will let JTK to ask them to re-adjust the colours until it meets my standards. Also, all my renders are done by myself and the colours are chosen from Pantone colours
4. Added another deskmat design.
