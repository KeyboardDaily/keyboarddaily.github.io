About Me
I’m a UK based Swedish media producer who has been quietly lurking geekhack / reddit / discord for the past 5 years. Like many of you, I fell for the Discipline and Mysterium. They embody a few of the things that I love about this hobby - the sense of accomplishment of a DIY project, the showcasing of raw components that you have lovingly soldered yourself. They gave me the impetus to leave my cave and finally participate in the community with you lovely & amazing people. Thus these cases were born. This is my first IC & potential group buy, and am working hard to be as prepared and take as many considerations as I can. As a buyer I’ve participated in many group buys.. I’ve seen smooth ones, bumpy ones, heartbreaking ones. I will do my utmost to deliver in the first category.
Thanks so much for reading, and I look forward to your feedback.[/list]
