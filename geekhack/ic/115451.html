Everyone loves the outer space, always full of awe and fascination for the universe, so our products are all related to astrology.

==============================================================================
About ORION-87
As a designer, I am more than love 87 series, so our first customized work is also 87.
Not only is it elegantly proportioned, its also block wise.
In the past November, orion87 has been shipped in the Chinese market.
We have received various feedback. Players have a good evaluation of its hand feel and sound performance.
Of course, the workmanship of the product has also been highly praised.
We first used TTC hotswap connection socket, and its performance has surpassed all the switches we have contacted before.
Therefore, we will continue to take the lead in using it in this version。
On the Bluetooth link
we use our own technology to realize Bluetooth low delay switching and switching between 6 Bluetooth devices.
For the dual-mode version, we will also present the 2.4G receiver we developed.
You can try to use it and turn on the performance mode when the signal is good.
The delay we actually tested can be stable at about 3ms.

Based on the idea of interspersing and encircling, the beauty is reflected as much as possible.

Just because of the love and yearn for the space, we designed a sculpt like radio telescope.
