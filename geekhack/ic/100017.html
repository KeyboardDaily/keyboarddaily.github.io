【IC】EPTKL - TKL w| Slider, Rotary encoder and more.

UPDATE:1/06/2019 This project is currently on hold until I am happy with the quality and software implementations I can achieve for the design

Hi All, Im Elliot. Over the past 5 months I have been working on this project alongside Rud who helped with the case design.Like my other projects, the EPTKL started as a passion project for myself, and over time it gained interest from others in the community leading me to write this Interest Check.


Link to IC Form: https://forms.gle/vyvmsXAArY4xEJVV8
So straight up youve probably noticed two big features on this board so I will elaborate on those first.

  • Slider - This feature inspired by the SlideBar and keebwerks nano bento. It acts as a midi device meaning it can control anything from volume to modulation to photoshop.

  • Rotary Encoder - This rotates and does what you want, Options include: Controlling Volume, Controlling Brightness, Scrolling and much more. It also acts a key when pressed down this can be programmed like any other key in QMK
    .
    [/list]
    Other Features:

  • PerKeyRGB - This features self explanatory, each key has a pre installed RGB LED that you can control and set to whatever colour you want.

  • USB-C - Its 2019 who doesnt like not worrying about which way you have to put your cable in

  • ARM Processor

  • HotSwap PCB - It allows you to easily change out what switches you are using without going through having to desolder and resolder switches. - There may also be an option for a non HotSwap version as an optional extra for those who prefer to solder switches.

  • Integrated Plate - This goes hand in hand with HotSwap, It helps to keep spacing between the switches and pcb to spec.
    There are 2 Layouts Being offered:

  • ISO

  • ANSI
    Due to the features these two fixed layouts are set and there is no plan to change these.
    Case Material:

  • Aluminium 6061

  • There is also a possibility of a Polycarbonate version
    Other Case Features:

  • 7 degrees angle

  • 4.5mm Integrated Plate

  • 2 piece
    Pricing:

  • For the aluminium Case version the price will be around 360$-380$ but I am currently working to bring this down.

  • For the PC version I am still waiting for quotes from my manufacturer.
