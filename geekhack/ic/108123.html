Greetings everyone! My name is PRJKT_ and Im here to present you Infinikey Marshmallow!
I got the idea months ago during my sisters graduation party when the kids were in the backyard making s mores! Now, it wont be an
s mores theme but more focused on the cute marshmallow novelty that I have drawn! As for the colors, I got the combination from trial and error but primarily I got the idea from the make up eye shadow palette I bought for my sister as a gift.
