hello everyone
this is an artisan keycaps box in the wrist rest. Designed by Xun Xun. Qingxi production.photo by s1t9e9v4e
The idea for this wrist rest comes from Xunxun’s finishing keycaps. He found that the height of the keycap was lower than the height of the hand rest, so he thought about whether the keycap could fit into the hand rest. This gave birth to this idea.
The following is the size of the wrist rest: the height is 2cm, not including the 1mm footpad. Can fit most keyboards. The width is around 9cm. The length can be selected as required. Other requirements can also provide customization.
The main material is wood. The material and pattern of the wood can be chosen by yourself. The surface supports lettering. You can even customize the silver painting, which will be introduced in another post. The top layer is an acrylic plate.
Can accommodate keycaps of 14mm and below. Or you can put anything you want to put, such as a pen or USB disk.
If you are interested, you can use qq group: 721984701, or leave a message below the post. You can also send an e-mail for information. Email is 1658592461@qq.com. More HD pictures and designs can be found at ins: s1t9e9v4e https://www.instagram.com/p/CCgdnSanhow/?igshid=7o7130agzb9q
