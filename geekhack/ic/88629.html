【IC】SA Swiss Cheese

Today I would like to share my latest project with you all, the SA Swiss Cheese Keycap set!
The Set
Designing a colorway after food, such as fruits and beverages, has been very popular lately. So on a beautiful sunny morning while eating my breakfast, I had an epiphany to design a set after biting into a slice of bread with delicious Swiss cheese. I jumped on my PC immediately and started drawing up this beautiful set. I hope you like it as much as I do!
The Kit
I intend to have just one base kit, like the SA Camping set has, to make things a bit more easier and more likely to hit production. I’m sorry if you have an Ergodox keyboard you’d want to fill with this design, maybe in the future!
Additionally to the base color, I have added a bunch of Mold Accent keys to make your keyboard more delicious than it would already be with this set.
I invite all of you to take a close look at the set and let me know if any keys are missing that would render you unable to equip your keyboard with it!
When/Where?
I am not quite sure yet if I want to have this produced by Signature Plastics or Maxkeys, the latter would be a very good option considering the SA backlog Signature Plastics is experiencing.
Regarding where it will be sold, I am currently open to any GB runners to run this set for me, PM me and we can talk things out!
Much love,
Oblotzky
