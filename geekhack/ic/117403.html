After 1.5 years of development, we are proud to present our new project: Ziggurat (XT 65% keyboard)

Ziggurat 65XT is influenced by ancient temples in Mesopotamia. Nowadays, Ziggurats can be seen as ancient temples, modern architectural works, and have even inspired futuristic designs, such as Blade Runner. The Ziggurat symbolizes opulence, power, and shared common vision to unite us all.
The idea was to create a keyboard that prioritizes a bouncy typing experience, clean acoustics, and a design language that would look great, even back in 3000 BC.

SPECIFICATIONS

Keyboard Dimensions: 363x125x31
Anodized or coated aluminum top case
Clear coated brass bottom case
Mounting style: Plateless, PCB-mounted, leaf-spring gasket
Typing angle: 6.5 degrees
Front height: 18 mm
Weight: 3 kg built
Colors include anodized Black, Silver, and Red
M3x10 front screws, M3x20 back screws
Standard, easily replaceable 5/16’’ rubber bump-ons
PCB designed by drewskeys, to be in-stock at ELOQUENT CLICKS
1.2 or 1.6 PCBs will be offered on the base kit, with their respective gasket thicknesses included with each PCB. As well as daughterboard and JST cable. Each PCB thickness has a different gasket to make sure compression is maintained.
DESIGN
“To protrude, To build High”

Ziggurats often apply simple bases and symmetrical lines that mirror mathematics. We aimed to take inspiration in this design language, while also adding a few things of our own.
The stepped pattern on the bottom piece represents the iconic “stairway to heaven” that connects earthlings to the Gods, and the Ziggurat, its medium.

WHAT’S INCLUDED IN THE BOX?
Each kit will come with the following components:
1 x Anodized or coated Aluminum top case (Ziggurat v3, which can be seen on leftmost render in design progression)
1 x Clear-coated brass bottom case
1 x PCB, daughterboard, and JST (1.2mm or 1.6mm )
5x M3x10 screws
5x M3x20 screws
4 rubber bumpons
required gaskets + 2 spares
1 x Carrying case
1 x Microfiber cloth
1 x Ziggurat sticker
/*Anodized color options for the top piece will be: Black, Red, and Silver. Extra tops will be available for purchase
A Premium, powder-coated option, “Limestones of Ur” coated by ELOQUENT COATS. Due to logistic challenges, this color will only be offered at ELOQUENT CLICKS
To ensure proper clearance and compression, two different gasket thicknesses are needed depending on the PCB thickness. Top case gaskets are always the same while bottom gaskets will depend on what PCB you build with. Each PCB will come with appropriately sized gaskets. Top gaskets will go on the case, bottom gaskets will go on the PCB.
