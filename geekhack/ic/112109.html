【22/4/2021 : Update on Renders and Kits】
Hi, We have received your ideas and opinions and we have make some changes on the kits and remake the renders. Also, We have a new IC Form, please kindly fill it out again.
Major Kit Changes:

  1. Introduced Hiragana sublegends kit as another kit.
  2. Removed some keys on the novelties kit.
  3. Fillets added on novelties designs
  4. Made another set of Numpad with different colors, and thinking about combining one of them into the Base kit, please tell us which do you like in the IC Form.
    Again, this is our first time participating in a keycap project, we appreciate every response. Those feedbacks have let us improve.

【4/4/2021 : Small Update】
Hello, we had received a lot useful and helpful suggestions just within the first day. Therefore we had the following updates.

  1. Novelties : We are definitely going to review the design (adding fillets so it contains no sharp corners etc.), it will be made to fit GMK’s custom keycap set guide. There will be minimal aesthetic impacts.
  2. Renders : We will remake all the Rendering. We will make the update later on, as some design details still need to be confirmed and the rendering rework will take some time.
  3. Numpad set : We received some comments on the Numpad colour scheme, if you have any other suggestion, feel free to use the IC form to tell us.
  4. Japanese Alphas : Japanese Alphas is an option under our consideration, also, feel free to use the IC form to tell us what you think.
    It is our first time making keycap related works, any suggestions and comments are welcomed. Thank you for your advises and patience to let our work become better.
