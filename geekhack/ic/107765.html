Hey everyone!

I’m happy to present you GMK Hanami Dango. Hanami Dango is a japanese sweet very popular in spring during the cherry blossom viewing (Hanami 花見, “flower viewing”). It’s also known as Sanshoku Dango (three coloured Dango) because of the iconic pink white and green! It’s made out of sweet mochi rice dumplings on a skewer. Pink is often flavored with sakura, green with matcha and white is just sweet rice. The set is based on the flower viewing tradition of eating hanami dango and dreaking matcha tea.
