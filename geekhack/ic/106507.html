The Prominence 68

This is a redesign of my previous project - The OIL-68/Infinity-68. While it borrows a lot from that design, I decided to change the name yet again to Prominence 68 due to the separation of the key groups and how the escape key sits above the rest of the board. What I present here is largely the finished design.

    5/23 Edit: First off, I’d like to thank everyone who took the time to respond to the Interest Check form. While I’ve been silent, I’ve not been absent from reading everyone’s comments and I found a common theme…There was an overwhelming number of people requesting hotswap. So Gondolindrim and I talked about adding it to the board. Ultimately, he agreed to design a new hotswap PCB that we will offer during the group buy to be purchased as an extra while the default board will be shipped with a solderable PCB.. The decision to leave hotswap out of the plans for the board was the result of a few thoughts.
    1: My initial concern was that the removal of all of the switches would cause the pcb to fall down to the bottom of the case, rendering the “hotswap” function useless. While this is true, I was overlooking the fact that a significant reason hotswap is popular is that a majority of people don’t have a soldering station or experience soldering.
    2: Gondolindrim and I both felt there would be a large amount of support for the variable switch plate, which makes implementing the hotswap sockets more difficult to accomplish. We had expected some requests for hotswap, but not nearly as many as we received.
    8/20 Edit: Name Change to Prominence 68. Colors/quantities have been set at 150 total boards (50 in black, 50 in white, 50 in navy blue). Prototype is en route. I will provide notes and pictures once I get a chance to assemble it.
