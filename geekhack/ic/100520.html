Hi everyone! Today, I’m glad to present you mu first keyset - GMK Osaka, inspired by Osaka’s Kanjozoku racers. First, we want to decide the lettering on caps. We went with English, English with Hiragana sublegends, Hiragana, Katakana. So here you can vote for the one you actually liked.
