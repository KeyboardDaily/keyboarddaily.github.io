zekk.works R.B.B. (Really Bad Boy)

IC FORM

Prototype



Renders







All renders done by me, and I’m improving with each new render. I’ll do my best to update this thread with new and improved renders as much as I can until completion of the IC period.
Keyboard Details

  • Top mount
  • “Seamless”
  • Internal fastening
  • 6.9deg typing angle
  • Front face height: 17.8mm
  • Effective keyboard height (EKH): 22.1mm
  • Stainless steel (sandblasted) internal weight
  • Semi-custom H88 + daughterboard from Hineybush Keyboards
  • Unbuilt/Built Weight: TBA
  • Potential Colors*
    More




All colors are rendered to be as close to reality as possible. However, it is not 100% possible to replicate real world environments so please do take these colors with a grain of salt.
GB Details

  • Sale format: 75 units (50 public raffle/25 FnF)
  • Start Time: ~July 2021
  • Fixed kit: Keyboard, 2 plates (1x 2mm PC/1x 1.5mm Aluminum), 1-2 PCBs (TBD in IC)
  • Price: TBD after receipt of prototype
  • Words about the Keyboard*
    I’ve designed a few different keyboards named after the same K-pop idol, each time utilizing the knowledge I’ve accumulated while designing keyboards. I intend for this to be the final iteration of this idea, utilizing everything I’ve learned thus far. At this time the design is mostly final, with a prototype on the way to make sure everything fits together as it should. In the event it does not, I’ll go through another round of prototyping.
  • The main purpose of this IC will be to review the prototype and to decide on what colors will be available to participants. Additionally I’m unsure if the kit should include 2 PCBs to match the 2 plates, so I’ll ask for your help on making that decision as well.*
    A sincere thank you to everyone who has shown me support during my time here in this community.
    IC FORM

Milestones

  • Order prototype - complete March 1 2021
  • Start interest check - complete March 21 2021
  • Determine color choices
  • Determine /# of PCBS to include w/ kit
  • Close interest check
  • Q&A*
  • Q: What is a fixed kit?*
  • A: A fixed kit is a way for me to reduce complexity for myself downstream while optimizing the cool things that come with the keyboard. The only choice you will have in this GB is the color of the keyboard.*
  • Q: What is the significance of the bear?*
  • A: It is on theme for the idol this is named after; the bear is a part of her signature.*
  • Q: Ogre R2?*
  • A: No Ogre…only RBB.*
  • More Q&A will be added as questions are answered throughout the thread.*
