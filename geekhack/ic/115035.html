Group Buy Update
KBDfans and ePBT have decided to hold all sets without a set GB date until they can begin to fulfill the backlog of ePBT Sets in production. As a result of this, I will not be able to run ePBT Real in late February as I had planned. Instead GB date will be moved to Q3 2022, but hopefully with much shorter lead times! Thank you all for your support. I will continue to make updates on this set so I can improve it more and more leading up to Group Buy. Thank you all so much!
As someone that has struggled with my own mental health, especially in the last few years being apart, I know that music can play a big part in helping you find the light at the end of the tunnel. One artist that I was able to connect to was NF. He has inspired me and made me a better person, the inspiration for this set. So here is ePBT Real, enjoy!
