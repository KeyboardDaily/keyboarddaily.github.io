PINNACLE 巅峰 65
Korea design in 65% Body


Hello World!
We are Flash.Studios! A new group of keyboard enthusiast who would like to debut our newest keyboard design.

After looking extensive market research and our internal design prototyping, we are please to debut our board that incorporates all the major design element in the current keyboard market.
We discover a gap in the market despite many 65% clone around. All of them have very elusive blocker, but no one of them have blocker on all 4 side for optimal typing experience.
Our novel Pinnacle65 is inspire by many of the popular 65% in the market, and it is sad people have to buy many 65% in order to collect all the badge. With the Pinnacle65, we now offer swappable badge for all popular 65% blocker inserts!
Board will also be come gasket “burger” mount for maximum isolation and being able to tune your different feelings in keyboard.
USB type to be confirm but we want to try to offer USBC or lightning port for best speed and connectivity but are working on the bus design.
