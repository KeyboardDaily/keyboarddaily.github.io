*If there is one thing I enjoy about TV and movies, its the notion that for an hour or more I can live in a different time and place. And my favorite shows have characters I can relate to because they remind me of myself: charismatic, witty, audacious, often brush up against the law, know how to sweet-talk their way out of a conflict, and they are usually too arrogant for their own good. Alright, perhaps I only represent 31 of those qualities. Anywaythis set is inspired by those heroes who in particular lead a team that is fighting for their survival. But even though their life is full of risk, they often have a good time anyway. After all, its hard to take things too seriously when the leaders ego is as large as a freighter. Good thing the crew knows how to keep him from ending up alone in the desert by himself and naked, wellexcept for that one time but they dont talk about that since the smuggling mission was largely successful.
*

Note about Renders: Renders shown are an approximation and are not meant to represent a 100% accurate depiction of the final product. Colors and legends will slightly vary. That being said, we will strive to make sure they are as accurate as possible.
Also, please note that SP’s stock colors vary slightly batch to batch. For example, SP TBN (mod and alpha legend color) is the same stock color used in SA Chocolatier; however, due to SP variance in stock colors between batches, SA Outlander’s TBN color may not match perfectly up SA Chocolatier’s TBN color. The same can be said for SP TM color, which is used in SA Jukebox alpha base color.
