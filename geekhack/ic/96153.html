JER-A06 Custom Keyboard


This a custom keyboard of PCB mounted. Cherry Original Keyboard is famous for soft and comfortable type-feel when typing to bottom of PCB-mounted keyboard. Inspired by the unique type-feel, the OTD custom keyboard emerged. Subsequently, many custom keyboard emerged with more functions, more creativeness, more improvement. More and more enthusiasts come into for custom keyboards. Due to design for top housing and bottom housing, CNC has to make compromise between configuration and type-feel.
By designer
Round 1 of 80% has been launched last year. This round designer changes the name from J80 to JER-A06.
I want to design a metal custom keyboard with a type-feel just like OG cherry keyboard. A custom keyboard is 80% PCB-mounted but with pretty typing experience.
Below are photos from R1


Specifications:
1, PCB-amounted, for original and comfortable type-feel without compromise
2,PCB is fixed to inside of top housing, suspended in middle to avoid contacting with metal bottom housing, which offer more resilience when typing.
Configuration:
1, Wide frame, round corner , slope angle ,all the details of OG cherry keyboards are all in JER-A06.
2, Layout: WKL and WK in 80%
3, Colors:
Top: Black, Gray, White(microarc oxidation).
Bottom: Black, Gray, White(microarc oxidation)
4, Led sticker: two ways for assembling
5, CNC and anodizing will be done by EVE, who have done CNC for Moon, MIRA, zero, Atom, Meteor, Kira and so on.
6, Materials:6063 Aluminum
7, USB interface and integrated to PCB
Estimate GB time: In a week or so.
