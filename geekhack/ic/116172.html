Say Aloha (Hello in Hawaiian) to MechWilds next board, the Mokulua, named after the Mokulua islands, a pair of islands off the coast of Oahu.

This is our first split board and we went with a 65% Alice-style layout. Both halves use Pro Micros and the halves connect using USB-C ports. This kit will be another FR4 sandwich kit but with a twist! The bottom plate will be laser cut acrylic this time.
Group Buy Details:

  • GB Dates: February 18th - March 14th

  • Fulfillment: Late May/early June

  • Target Price: 60 USD

  • Product Page: https://mechwild.com/product/mokulua/


    Features:

  • 65% Alice-style layout

  • SMD RGB LEDs

  • Both halves can be used standalone

  • OLED and optional knob on both halves

  • FR4 switch plate, acrylic bottom plate

  • QMK and VIA support

  • Supports all Pro Micro compatible dev boards
    Addons:

  • Upgrade to USB-C Pro Micro

  • USB port soldering

  • C to C cable
    These are just the most notable addons well be offering for the GB. There will be our usual selection of addons as well on the product page.
    Layouts

    Currently, the prototype uses a reversible PCB so the right half stagger is a bit different from traditional boards. We are working on a right half PCB with a more typical layout. Other changes we are making are changing the screw placement and OLED cutout. We are deciding on what final appearance the acrylic bottom plates should have. We are also considering some acrylic embellishments to go on top of the board.
    Things we still need to do:

  • Finalize bottom acrylic designs and options

  • Topside acrylic pieces

  • Modify OLED cutout

  • Right half PCB with traditional stagger

  • Adjust screw placement
    IC Form again.
    Mahalo for your time and feedback on the Mokulua split keyboard. Aloha, a hui hou! (Aloha also means goodbye in Hawaiian)
