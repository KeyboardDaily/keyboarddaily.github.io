Hello World,
I am Chanmin Kang, a keyboard designer and manufacturer based in Korea. As a keeb enthusiast (just like all of you guys!), I have dedicated a lot of time and effort into coming up with some creative keyboard designs with funky appearance and deep sounds, and have run quite a few domestic GBs under “4K” or “KKKK” branding so far. Now I plan to launch overseas sales for those who may be interested, and am pleased to present to you ㄲㅣㅇㄲㅗㅇ DGL TKL


Just like any other GBs around here, the process will kick off with an interest check (IC) to gauge your interest in the board. Happy to hear your thoughts and feedback on the design, but just a declaimer that below design is pretty much finalized and won’t be changed unless absolutely necessary. But please don’t be shy to show your reaction if you like the board - assume y’all are all stuck at home and have nothing else to do anyways!
※The design below has been finalized and will not be changed.
[Board Specs]

  • Sandwich-mount
  • Indicator LEDs
  • Centered USB Type-C Port
  • Pointed plate
  • RGB strip at the bottom
  • 7 degree angle
  • 1.5cm (~0.6”) height from the front
  • 4T plate (THICK) for quiet and uniform typing feels
  • PCB also supports QMK and Type-C and includes 17 RGB LEDs
  • Supports both ANSI and ISO layouts

※ Typing Video ※


We paid a lot of attention to the design and the quiet typing.
We used a 4T thick plate for quietness and uniformity.




The PCB also supports QMK and Type-C and includes 17 RGB LEDs.
It also supports ANSI and ISO layouts.
We offer several customization options.
You can choose between anodizing and a CERAKOTE coating.
The following colors are available for selection:
Top and Bottom : Silver, Black, Gray, Red, Orange gold, Metal Green, White/, Beige/
Plate : Silver, Black, Gray, Red, Orange gold, Metal Green
Point Plate(Located at the bottom of the keyboard.) : Silver, Black, Gray, Red, Orange gold, Metal Green, White/, Beige/
/*: Cerakote Coating, There is an additional charge for this.
Base set with case, plate, PCB, front acrylic diffuser : $450
If you purchase the product, it will be sent via EMS.
We are closely monitoring the evolving situation with the COVID-19 Virus spreading around the world.
Currently, there is no problem with our shipping process and shipment.
However, some countries are currently blocking shipments from Korea.
If you do not receive the products shipped from Korea from your area of residence, we can ship them after COVID-19 is resolved and the restriction is lifted.
