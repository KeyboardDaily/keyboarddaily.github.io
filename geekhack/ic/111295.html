[/b] Hello,
We are Monstargear in Korea. We’ve launched few keyboards and about to GB a new 60% keyboard.
It’s our first 60% alu housing and multi layout boards, so want to check the demands of layout before making it.
“Sangeo” means shark in Korea. We named it after cute shark weight.
Sangeo weight will be made with mat finish PVD coated brass.
(Maybe gloss finish depends on the IC)
First sample picture

During test, we got some scratches, that won’t happen for actual product.
But there might be a round marks from machining like in the picture.
And the weight will not bulged, we changed 0.5mm lower the bottom housing.
