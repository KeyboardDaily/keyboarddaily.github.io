11/17/20:

  • New kit and board renders (by me)

  • Supports British, Icelandic, Norwegian, Swedish, Finnish, Faroese, Danish, Deutsch, and Portuguese layouts. I also included an Ñ key for Spanish layouts

  • Alt Gr and ISO enter keys are now a part of the ISO kit
