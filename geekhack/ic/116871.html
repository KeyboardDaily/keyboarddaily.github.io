Han60 - 2022 Update


Join the Discord for the most up to date information
Final IC Form
Hey everyone! Farhandsome (han) here. I’m happy to announce that after tons of designing, prototyping, and gathering feedback that the Han60 is finally going into production! This project has been really fun and challenging to work on and I’m glad to be a part of a community that really supports small designers such as myself. Several iterations of design and prototyping have gone into this KB and I’m finally excited to bring this board to life.
Sale Info
Timeline / Sale

  • Production begins mid-March

  • Production time estimated to be completed in April

  • Sale time will be May/June timeframe

  • This will be an in-stock sale, with units shipping within days of purchase

  • Extras such as extra tops, PCBs and plates are currently being finalized
    Pricing / Quantity

  • Full kit (PC or FR4 plate): $170

  • Full kit (Alu plate): $180

  • There will fixed kits with an estimated MOQ of 50-60

  • Details*
    Specifications

  • Aluminum Case

  • Gasket Strip Mount

  • Custom Han60 PCB (QMK / VIA compatible)

  • Simplified USB-C Daughterboard (ai03 / elmo)

  • Option of Plate (PC, FR4, Alu)
    Contents

  • Top Frame

  • Bottom Frame

  • Plate: Polycarbonate OR FR4 OR Aluminum

  • USB-C Daughterboard & Sleeved JST Cable

  • Case Foam and Plate Foam
