Hello everyone, this is my first ever keycap set that I have designed and have been working on for the past few months.
This was inspired by the classic rubber squawking chicken that are in toy shops everywhere and hella annoying. I got them long long time ago and suddenly got a crazy obsession with chickens leading me to not eat them for many years and I am still continuing the streak.
I decided to combine my obsession of the cute cuddly farm bird with my new found interest in keyboards to create GMK Cluck, it started out normal, but under pressure from peers, I broke and added a face kit slightly based on the minecraft chicken (an entity that I have been breeding for many years with seeds and eggs) and tada! GMK Cluck!
