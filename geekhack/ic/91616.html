This is my 2nd creation following on from X60. Everything is different if I compare the two boards directly, but the philosophy is still the same: 8 degrees angle, top mount, and a simple silhouette. I started with two main goals of decreasing the bezel size and making the plate lower to the desk. I believe I’ve gone to the maximum that’s possible with the material, and not much could be improved without sacrificing structural integrity. Hopefully it’s worth the hundreds of hours that’s gone into this project.
The buy will begin shortly after I receive the sample (about 3 weeks away).
Case Information:

  • 6061 Aluminum

  • 8 Degrees Angle

  • Brass Weight with Engravings

  • Thinnest Top Mount Bezels (4.6/3mm)

  • Lowest Plate to Desk (7.7mm)

  • Optional .5u Blocker

  • Supported Layouts:*

  • 68 key may change. To put it simply, it will be the layout I want to use.*

  • Plates:*

  • Top Mounted Plate with Switch Opening

  • Universal or 68 Key Plate

  • Clear Polycarbonate, Anodized Aluminum, or Polished Brass

  • Partial Polycarbonate 68 Key Available

  • PCB:*

  • Custom QMK PCB by Rozakiin

  • Presoldered Controller, Diodes & Resistors

  • Mini USB

  • Interchangable For winkeyless.kr 22mini

  • 268 AG:*

  • Limited to 22 Units

  • Anodizing or Cerakoting

  • Brass Through Weight with Individual Numbering

  • Polycarbonate, Brass, or Aluminium Plate

  • Cerakoting is likely to add $50 or so to the case price.*

  • 268:*

  • 20 Units

  • Dark Gray or Silver Case

  • Brass Through Weight

  • Polycarbonate, Brass, or Red Aluminium Plate

  • Price (excluding fees + shipping):*

  • Case (Top/Weight/Bottom) - $320

  • Plate - $30

  • PCB - $30

  • Shipping:*
    I’ll be shipping with DHL Express again, with most packages taking 1-3 days for a good price. I’ll be shipping from the United Kingdom; local taxes and fees apply. Estimates for shipping are as follows: United States - $40; United Kingdom - $20; Europe - $30-35; Asia - $50-60.

  • Deadline & Lead Times:*
    I’ll allow 1 week after starting for payment. After this, the order can be submitted to the factory and will take about 6-8 weeks to arrive.
