Eau

A Gasket Mount 75% Keyboard with decoration F Row Module

Hi Everyone,
We are the new customization team “CHAOSERA”. In 2021, 75% keyboard becomes more and more popular. Since this is the first keyboard from our team, and we want to make something different.

Gallery


Highlight

  • Clean Case Design
    We aimed to finish the keyboard free of any visible screws on both sides, both top, and bottom.
  • Magnet F-row decoration block
    We use a magnet base to make the F-row decoration block hot-swappable. It is easy for users to change the decoration block to keys without and screws. There also will be more kinds of decoration blocks in the future. Users can even create their own decoration block.
  • All Included
    Plate poron foam, bottom poron foam, aluminum plate, PC plate, F-row decoration block, and stabilizers are all included. Users just need to buy switches and good to go.
  • Easy to assemble
    We rethought and redesigned the assembly method of the inner part of the gasket structure. Users do not need to stick the poron foam to the case anymore.

Features
-Typing angle: 7 degrees

  • Layout: 75%/65%
  • Material: 6063 Aluminum
  • Mounting Method: Gasket Mount

Kit Contents

  • Aluminum Top Case
  • Aluminum Bottom Case
  • Magnet F-row decoration block protection film
  • Magnet F-row decoration block
  • PC Plate
  • Aluminum Plate
  • RGB Hotswap PCB
  • Everglide V2 Screw-in stabilizer set
  • PORON gaskets
  • Silicone gaskets
  • PORON dampener between plate and PCB
  • PORON foam between PCB and bottom case
  • Anti-cutting silicone strip between PCB and bottom case
  • Individual PORON foam between PCB and switches (100)
  • Screws and silicone feet
