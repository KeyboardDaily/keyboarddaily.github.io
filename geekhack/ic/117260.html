Hello and welcome to the EARLY IC for DSA POKI!
As this is my first set I thought it might be good to give a quick lil introduction. You can call me berryn, Ive been in this hobby a little over 4 years now and only recently built my first keeb (lol).
This set is inspired by my character, Poki. Poki enjoys ear rubs, strawberries, and milk (she can and WILL steal yours!). When I look at Poki, I feel happy. Ive tried to reflect that dreamy feeling in this set and I hope this color palette will be able to bring you the same warmth!

POKI WRIST REST DESIGN + INFO

Keep all 16 inches of Poki with you and complete the set. Poki will be made of soft, mochi like plush fabric with 4 way stretch to allow her to contour to your wrist placement. She is filled with a mix of plush beads (near the bottom) to provide stability as well as loose plush filling so you can squish her all you want.

  • Professionally embroidered details
  • Premium, soft 4-way plush fabric
  • Printed fur pattern
  • 16 inches long, perfect for most 60-75% layouts
  • Stuffed loosely with plush beads to help keep her grounded
