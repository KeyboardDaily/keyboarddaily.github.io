Sorry for lack of updates on this project, the PCB took so many time to finish but here we are
The Chinese GB has been fulfilled, and we should expect an International GB very soon.
Hi, ScrambledEggz here.
On behalf of zFrontier and EVE, I present you METEOR.
METEOR is the latest design by EVE, the team that brought you MIRA SE and the MOON.
This GB has been run in China via zFrontier and 100 sets were sold within a very short period of time. And we are planning to bring this beauty to you.
Design:
Meteor is a board with a 60% + F row design. The case is a 3-pcs design with a top case, middle frame and a bottom case. This board will also be using a new pcb by EVE with driver support.
Prototypes (New samples INCOMING! will update soon):


Parts:
Top case ( 60% + F row or HHKB + F row)
Middle frame
Aluminum plate (multiple layout support)
Bottom case
Weight (Aluminum, could upgrade to BRASS)
PCB
Custom keyboard bag
Accessories (M3 304 stainless steel screws, 3M plastic feet)
PCB Features:
EVE & EQUALZ Driver support
Left sided Mini USB
Multiple backlighting effects
Multiple layout support (arrow cluster layouts supported!)

Color Options:
