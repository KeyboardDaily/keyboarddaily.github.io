Yep, I am at it again. I am trying to come up with more keycap designs that everyone can enjoy. Is this a good idea or bad idea. Dr. No or Red Grant vs. Bond and Q, anyone?

Here are some mock-ups (these have not been verified to work as double shots yet, but will give a general idea):

A1 - From Russia with Love
A2 - Original SPECTRE ring logo from the first shots of Ernst Blofeld’s ring in From Russia with Love
A3 - Oddjob’s hat from Goldfinger
A4 - Baron Samedi from Live and Let Die
A5 - Janus Group symbol from the Goldeneye Wii video game
The rest are pretty self explanatory. (Disclaimer: These keycap idea mockups have not all passed the SP approval process; and all colors subject to change.)
I am seriously considering running all group buys from now on in series pairs. So, please take a moment to look at the mock ups and vote for which pair of Villain/Film and 007 keycap you would like to see. For example, if you would like to see Baron Samedi and the Goldeneye reply with A4/B5. The two diamond icons are only place setters right now, as I plan to create a couple more villain/henchmen keycap ideas.

And here is the final pair for 007 Series I Modifiers?:

Red Polycarbonate with White ABS Legends

Black ABS with Silver Legends
