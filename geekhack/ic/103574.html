Welcome to my debut board, Infinitum.

The group buy for this board will occur onSaturday, May 16th, 2020 at 9AM EST through my store, https://eniigmakeyboards.com!
I’ve been working on this board for a while now, and I’m happy to finally unveil it.


Here are some more details about the board itself:
Top mount
Silver aluminum plate (polycarbonate plate will be available as an add-on)
Mirror-finished stainless steel weight
60 FCFS units for the public
1 color - Muted Blue
Will be run through my website, eniigmakeyboards.com
Pricing: $635
PCB: Custom PCB with Unified Daughterboard
90-120 day lead time.
