Info

Hi! As the designer of this set I’d like to get a few things out of the way.
What is the planned price? At this point I cannot give a definitive answer, but the entire set is completely reverse dyesub and will require far more quality assurance than regular dyesub.
How will the quality be? Simply put, I am a perfectionist. Slanted legends, inconsistent legends, warped caps, poor colormatching, will not be tolerated. I expect prototyping to be strenuous and I will be documenting the progress of how it goes here.
What would the turnaround time be? You can expect to get the set 5 months after the group buy ends.
What’s up with that novelty? It’s Seoul’s flag, which plays into the Splash deskmat as the circle resembles the sun in both.
Does Milkyway have anything to show yet? They do! I should add that the legends will not be too similar to the Lilith keyset and what is shown in the renders here will be what they look like in reality.
