Price
Although I can not say for sure yet, according to ALF’s factory, the price of the prototype is $ 200 ~ 230.
When actually doing GB, it can be lower than the price of the prototype depending on the number of products manufactured.
(The PC version is expected to be 1.2 to 1.3 times higher than aluminum.)
Schedule
Ordering prototypes this week.
It will be manufactured in around 20 days and will be transported internationally.
It has not been decided until the scheduled GB date, but after the GB of “Uncertainty60” is finished.
Q&A from Google form, Reddit etc…
Q: Proxy?
A: I’m planning to set up a proxy, but please wait as I haven’t talked yet.
Q: HHKB Top too please?
A: Sorry, This project is only WKL.
Q: Do you offer a PC model? (Another similar question.)
A: Yes, You can get PC model.
Q: Leave out a PCB to keep costs down.
A: Yes, I have no plans to provide PCBs for this project. Because it is compatible with many 60% PCBs.
Q: I’d like to see some renders with keycaps!
A: Wait, wait….
Q: I want to put a boardwalk PCB inside. The PCB is GH60-compatible & supports WKL cases. I’ll just laser cut my own plates & adapts to this case. A DXF file for cutting plates would be useful.
A: When GB is over, publish using Github etc. (The corner rounding is set to 0.5 mm)
Q: Please list estimated price and date of GB!
A: Wait, wait….
Q: Would it be a possibility for a version num/macropad edit
A: There is no design plan for the moment.
If there are many such requests, I consider.
Q: How big is the clearance between the case and the switch hole? Because it looks really tight.
A: The distance from the bottom of the plate to the bottom of the case is approximately 8.8 mm. I think there is probably no interference.
