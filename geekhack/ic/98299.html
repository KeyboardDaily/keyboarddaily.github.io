// Intro //
Revamping this IC. I’ve taken a short hiatus from the community and got IRL busy, but I’m still passionate about this set. I’ve made a couple adjustments, mostly nailing down the grays used in this set. I’ve looked through RAL codes and decided to color match to actual plastic, chosen from PMK’s ABS color selection. I’ve decided to go this route to get the closest color matching possible to actual plastic- not print. Currently looking through BOTH options to match the grays I’m looking for
PROPOSED color chip codes areGSZ, GTA, and GTL.
These are subject to change.

This set is inspired by, well, boringness. I really believe people should have neutral, yet attractive, keysets in their repertoire. The “boring” nature of the set is its greatest asset, versatility. I also aim to break the mold and push for a reverse color scheme, dark gray alphas, gray mods, and light accents. I’m also pushing for all text mods, and maybe will be looking for all text novelties as well, those are always fun.
What’s happening right now?
06/18/19: Currently looking through more options for color (RAL and Signature Plastics chips) to match the ‘right’ grays.
