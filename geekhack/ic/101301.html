The Details of SOAP:

Key Points & Features:

  • Designed with SOAP in mind
  • Simple Top-Mount
  • Smooth, Rounded edges and inner chamfers (Inspired by nature)
  • Subtle and Yet, Bold Color Options
  • Milled from A solid block of Aluminum. (For Heft and Quality Meme Value)
  • Beautiful Enamel Filled Inlay of “SOAP” lettering (Similar to Enamel Pins)

PCB Features:

  • USB Type-C
  • x3 RGB LED Indicators (Controllable)
  • x1 EC11 “Short-Stem” Rotary Encoder
  • Powered by QMK

The Following are the Color Options/Materials that will be Available -

  • All SOAP Macro Pad Cases to be Cerakoted/Powder Coated In the Following Colors
  • Bazooka Pink- Cerakote
  • Robin’s Egg Blue - Cerakote
  • Winter White - Cerakote (No Enamel Inlay)
  • Soot Grey - Cerakote (Maybe Ano.)

Plate Options:
ProtoType Plates Arrived!

  • 1.5mm Polished Brass
  • 1.5mm Stainless Steel

CNC Milled Rotary Encoder Options:

  • Electro-Polished Brass
  • Polished Stainless Steel
  • “Winter White” Cerakote Finished Aluminum
