Hello and welcome to the Interest Check form for Acrylic Keyboard Stands byDigmo.
This is for collecting ideas, as well as an approx count of how many will be sold, and how many I will have to produce.
The Prototypes were made in Switzerland by me. If there is enough interest in the Stands they are going to be produce by a Manufacturer here in Switzerland.
Group Buy now Live. If you have any questions or comments or want to keep up with the progress, you can follow my socials Discord or on my socials! Thanks
Specifications:

  • 5 mm Acrylic
  • Aluminum Hardware
  • Rubber Feet
  • Rubber Protectors
  • MOQ 30 Pieces
    Price: $25 for the Solo and $35 for the Duo
    Update 1

17.09.2020

More
Rubber Feet have been designed and are sent off for prototype production.
Stacked version of the stand is going to be redesigned to allow for 75% and maybe even TKL.
Colors that came out on top of the interest check so far: Clear, Black and White
If the manufacturer can get their hands on iridescent acrylic, it will be an option as well.
Update 2
03.10.2020
More

Small update on the KeebStand:
I’m making progress on the Metal hardware as well as on the Acryl.
The metal rods will be made out of Aluminum and there will be two option: Silver and Black.
Now waiting on the offer from the factory.
Also, I’m waiting on the Acrylic Prototype from the manufacturer, should be here next week.
The designs have been altered to accommodate better for TKL and heavier boards.
Acrylic colors that will be available are going to be: Translucent, Frosted and Smokey Black.
Rubber feet have arrived. There also will be two options: semi translucent and Black.
Price
$25 for the Single Stand and $35 for the Double Stand.
Shipping
I’m working with FedEx to provide you with the best price and fast shipping times during the Pandemic. The shipping with FedEx will be more expensive compared with shipping with the Swiss Post but there is the added benefit of tracking and guaranteed in time delivery even during the pandemic.
Misc
As you may have notice, my branding changed! From now on everything I design and produce will be made under the name keebit.studio! Website is still in the works and will be up for the upcoming group buy of the KeebStand and the still secret project.
I’m working on another project as well. If all goes to plan it may be ready for interest check in the following weeks and group buy soon after.
