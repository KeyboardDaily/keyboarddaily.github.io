Mimicking the symbiosis of flesh and bone, the base set is sure to give you shivers.
We set out a goal to manufacture each keycap with a unique gradient accomplished via thermal sublimation of PBT plastic.
Inspired by various aesthetics, the set was designed to be something that hasn’t been done before.
The release features two sets: A base set with ANSI, ISO, numpad, extra spacebars, and a corresponding aluminum novelty keycap
with a special electroplated gradient hue, which will also be available separately. We decided to go with the SA profile, as it’s a smooth,
robust, rounded profile, perfect for the vision of meshing gradients we set out for this release.

About us:

We are a small company composed of keyboard enthusiasts and folks who work in industrial design.
Our goal is to experiment with various manufacturing techniques on different types of Aluminium, PBT/ABS plastic sublimation and dyeing procedures in order to bring unique,
one-of-a-kind keycap ideas to life. The brand aesthetic we are looking to build is of functionality, minimalism and designs narrated by a strong foundation of thought and story.
