Hello,This is Moyu.studio
In 2021, we designed one of the trilogy of Greek mythology-Poseidon switch
Now, we have brought the Hades switch!

Specifications:

  • Linear
  • 63.5g/42g (bottom out)three-section gold-plated spring
  • P3 Lengthen stem
  • Nylon top housing
  • Nylon bottom housing
  • No Factory lubrication
  • 5 pin
  • Hard pin
  • Made by JWK

    Hades switch uses a slightly longer stem instead of a super longer stem like Poseidon switch, which means that it can be accepted and used by more people.
    We have also optimized the original three-stage gold-plated spring so that it will not produce the sound of spring friction while maintaining a wonderful hand feel.
    In terms of materials, we chose the stem of P3 mixed material to make it smoother, and made the shell of transparent nylon material,It has a beautiful appearance while maintaining a dull sound.
    Hades switch（63.5g）Black stem

    Hades switch（42g）Dark green stem

    Hades deskmat

    Size thickness: 900mm x 400mm x 4mm
    High quality printing
    Bottom natural rubber
