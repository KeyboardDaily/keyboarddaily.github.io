MA Element Suite is inspired by the periodic table of chemical elements. The names and numbers of some elements are written on the corresponding letters, and the corresponding letters that do not exist will be 0. The upper left corner of the keycap is detailed with triangles, circles and squares.And the triangle, circle and square are the most basic graphics, which can be combined and collided to form a variety of graphics. The structure of the elements is also the same. The combination and superposition of various elements can collide with different chemical changes. The same is true for the MA keycap. The structure of the upper circle below also reflects the changing process of the connection between the square and the circular structure,For this color.we are reference this Carbon set
