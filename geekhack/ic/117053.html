DesignerStudio X Zero-G Midnight Switches

Hi, this is DesignerStudio. We are here to announce our first collaboration product with Zero-G Studio  Midnight switches. To match the theme of their Domikey Midnight Keycaps, we deliberately choose the colorways of these switches. As requested, we re-adapt our Graphite Gold/ Taoyao(Peach Bloom) Switches’ bottom housing to this product with some modifications. We hope these configurations will bring out the thocky-est and cleanest sound profile. And the regular travel distance must meet some users’ needs who are sort of tired of the popular long pole stems. Overall, we want to present the most pleasant experience to the users!
Specifications
Linear
Composite Nylon housing(white and dark gray)
LY stem (mainly compose of UPE)
Spring: 63.5g 18mm gold plated spring
contacts: High-quality gold alloy
Actuation Force: 48g
Bottom-out Force: 63.5g
Pin Mounting: 5-Pin
Travel Distance: 4mm
Standard Factory lube or No lube
Manufacturer: JWK
Force Curve
