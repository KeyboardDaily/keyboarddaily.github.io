Introduction
Our goal with the Novus is to develop an addictingly pure typing experience. The Novus utilizes a traditional top mounted plate to
ensure that each keystroke will feel raw and satisfying. The case design is simple but aesthetic, and it is sure to match any setup.
Oh, and it comes in a neato plum color in case you want it to catch the eye.

Specifications
Disclaimer: We will be ordering our first prototype soon
Keyboard

  • 60% keyboard

  • Top mount

  • 5° typing angle

  • 6061 aluminum

  • WK only

  • Plum, Jet Black, and Dark Gray colorways

  • PCB*

  • High-quality solderable ANSI PCB

  • Support for split backspace, stepped caps lock, and 7u and 6.25u spacebars

  • Center-placed USB-C, no RGB lighting

  • QMK and VIA support

  • Designed by Cutie Club

  • Plate*

  • Support for split backspace, stepped caps lock, and 7u and 6.25u spacebars

  • Available in polypropylene, aluminum, and sandblasted brass
