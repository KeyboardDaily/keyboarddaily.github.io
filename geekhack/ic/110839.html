Quick March update: I’m working on some new kitting and novelty designs. IC should be fully up and running sometime in april or may.
GMK Jay
A set inspired by jays, my favorite birds
Inspiration
The colorway inspired by the blue feathers commonly seen on jays and muted and toned down to fit my own personal taste. With novelties inspired by the birds themselves and the trees they nest in, conifers, pine bearing trees.

Kits/Renders
