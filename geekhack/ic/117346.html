Hello,
I was asked to run Fuyu again, and well.. here it is - I’m very excited about it
For anyone confused, blossoms do appear in winter time in Japan and Fuyu translate to both Persimmons and Winter.
If you haven’t caught on yet but I have a tendency to do winter themes.
There are two base kits and several child kits to supplement them.
One will be Katakana based and the other will be Latin based since a lot of people really wanted the set to be ran in Latin instead of Hiragana subbed.
The novelties are loosely based on new year traditions in Japan or home comforts.
I know you like your inspirations and I’m here to tell you it’s just a pastel set but I do like the cold weather and it’s nearly reaching 38°C / 100°F here where I live.[/color][/size]
The white used is an in between WS1 and WS2 - A specific color unique to MW.
Blue and Pink are obtained through RAL
I’ll obtain a sample kit sometime in July and we are hoping to start the GB in August hopefully as I have been working with MW for quite a bit.
Sit back and scroll through the “weeb”
Renders and kits were provided by ElvisXSS / Jr.Mars the runner and designer of MW-STONE Age and ePBT-Winter Breath
