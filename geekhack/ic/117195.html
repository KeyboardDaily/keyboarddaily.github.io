Introduction

Design inspiration
In the 1970s, the American sociologist Ray Oldenburg proposed the concept of “third space” from the perspective of urban and social research. He calls the place of residence the “first space”, the place where he spends a lot of time working is called the “second space”, and the “third space” is the informal public gathering place outside the place of residence and work, and the concept of the “third space” emphasizes the social role of the place, such as cafes, tea houses, bars, community centers, and so on.
My third space was playing games in my carefully furnished study. I upgrade my computer room every year to make it the way I like it, filled with decorations from my collection. And I hope that the third space is like the movie “Ready Player One”, you can swim freely in the virtual world, you can do whatever you want! Let go of the fatigue of life and work, relax and enjoy!
So today we bring you this set of blue and white color theme design “Third Space”.
Blue makes people calm, it brings people a sense of technology, futuristic, we also designed a very exquisite NOVEL, I hope you like.
Do you have your own third space? If so, please share it like I did!
