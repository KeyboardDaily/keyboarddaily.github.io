Hello everybody,
today I’m more than happy to present you my keycap set!
It is inspired by Kirito - character from the 2D series SAO.
In a second I will take you through my thought process of choosing colors for this set.
Colors
For the main color I have chosen black, just black. Nothing special, move along.
Specifically speaking - RAL 8022

For the accents I wanted to go with gray, similar to the one used for Kirito’s robe.
robe.png (91.7 kB - downloaded 92 times.)
I have modified it a bit to match the set better and finally went with RAL 7037.

I was also thinking about RAL 7022, but it turned out to be too dark, in my opinion.

For the final color - the one for the legends I needed some contrast and that is when I remembered one of his swords - Dark Repulser.
Dark Repulser.png (67.66 kB - downloaded 87 times.)
Same as for the gray color, I modified it a bit and finally came up with - RAL 6027.

Concept


Plans
As far as I feel like there are enough people intrested in GMK Dual Wield, I will make some novelty keycaps and upload more renders.
When it comes to the novelty keycaps I’m planing to make “Link, start!” enter with some fancy font
For the shift keys I was thinking about adding 2 different arts of Kirito’s swords.
To make it a kit worth buying, I will need to come up with a few more novelties. That is why I’m open for any ideas from you guys!
All of the above wrap-up to 3 kits:

  1. Base - possibly with Latin legends.
  2. Hiragana sublegends kit
  3. Last but not least, possible novelty keycaps.
