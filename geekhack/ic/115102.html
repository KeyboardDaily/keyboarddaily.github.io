Hi Everyone!
It’s been a while since we’ve launched the first Ikki68 Aurora group buy. Most people have gotten their units by now. We are taking in feedback from users to make sure the R2 is even better. We are introducing new colors, aluminum top cases, new default badges and more!
We realize different people have different preference when it comes to type feel and sound, so we are adding new plates, PCBs, dampening material to our existing library of a multitude of different options in each category. We hope there will be something in it for everyone!
This is just the first part to a series of updates to come. We will include more information in future updates.
We’re still adjusting and taking in feedback, so if you have a suggestion please fill out our IC form at the bottom of the page.

Optional aluminum top cases compatible with R1 & R2
An add-on introduced by popular demand. A lot of people have asked for aluminum top cases for a more premium feel. We’re offering E-white, Anodized Black, Pink, Navy and Red top cases that can be purchased separately this round. These top cases are compatible with all Auroras released to date or in the near future.

New PCBs
We have two new PCBs this round, joining the other four from R1:
05 - Bodo
It’s the default PCB of R2. It’s a thinner, flexier version of the default PCB of R1- Fairbanks. The 1.2mm thickness paired with flex cuts allows for a great amount of flex while still being stable.
06 - Iceland
A bluetooth version of Bodo that supports VIA. Supports all the splits except for split spacebars.
We have an early prototype video here
Please refer to this tablefor a quick glance on what sets the PCBs apart.
New plates with more flex
We are adding more flex to our PC and FR4 plates. The others will stay the same.

Dampening pads
Softer default silicone dampening kit
The silicone dampening pad in R2 will be 30% softer than R1.
New optional PORON dampening kit
We’re offering a PORON sheet and PORON plate foam as add-ons in R2. The thin PORON sheet goes between switches and PCB similar to a PE sheet, but it’s much more durable in our experience.
New default badge
Our community member @VAMP has designed this beautiful badge for R2. The default badge will be PVD coated copper, which has a shinier, sleeker look than the R1 finish.
Compatibility
All of the Aurora R2 new parts discussed here, including the new plate, PCB, foam etc. are fully compatible with all the Auroras announced and sold to date.

WS Stabs - Aurora Series
For the past several months, we have been hard at work designing and prototyping these stabilizers specifically for the Aurora. We think you will be very pleased with the upgrades
Features
Designed specifically for 1.2mm PCBs, such as the new Aurora PCBs, for the perfect fit. No need for washers or shims, which means they’re not compatible with the traditional 1.6mm PCBs
The housing design makes it impossible for wires to pop out since they’re installed from the bottom.
There are ribs inside the housing to minimize lube from sticking to the stem, causing them to feel sluggish.
Comes with PCB mounting pads and Teflon adhesive strips already cut to size to make holee modding easier. You can have a tight fit to minimize rattle without spending a lot of time on it.
Comes in Aurora Fog, Aurora Clear, Aurora Pink and OA Blue. Just like R1, they’re purchased separately for stock colorways but come included in the kit for collaboration colorways.
