Thank you for even clicking on this thread for the first keyboard kit from saevus. This is a passion project I have been working on for a while. Please find the information below.
Sui Generis, meaning unique in Latin. Shortened to Sui, which means self in Latin, was inspired by the fluidity of self identity. Although ever changing, having an identity is an ever important goal and quest simultaneously. Flowing side profile, highly productive layout, interesting color options and unique weights (big boi and top left/right accent weights) add to this theme. This layout was designed with productivity, streamlined aesthetics, and ease of outfitting in mind.
