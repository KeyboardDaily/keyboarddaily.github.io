ABOUT

The BEAK 6.5 is designed to look elegant, yet aggressive.
Featuring a big weight doubling as part of the feet to strike a balance between looks, and practicality.
The large rotary encoder makes it easy to use, as well as precise.
SPECIFICATIONS
Case weight: ~2kg (4.4 lbs)
Typing angle: 6.5deg
Mounting style: Top Mount
Front height: 19.4 mm
Plate materials: Brass, Aluminium, Polycarbonate (Possibly more depending on feedback)
Case material: 6061 Aluminium
Weight material: Brass (Possibly more)
PCB: Designed by Trip-trap with the Unified C3 Daughterboard - configurable with QMK and VIA.

UPDATE MAY 14TH 2021
THE PROTOTYPE

After feedback from the IC, we made a few design changes for the next prototype (Which has been ordered!)
Multiple small details were changed in the second version, the biggest of which being the removal of the feet.
Some of you might be worried that the keyboard will tilt/wobble when typing.
This is NOT the case - for the keyboard to wobble you would have to press way harder than you ever would in a real-world scenario.
Other changes include multiple small changes - primarily larger cutouts to make the tolerances more forgiving.
