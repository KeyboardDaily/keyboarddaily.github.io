Hey All,
I love the look of Ubuntu’s purple and orange, and I wanted to share a set I had stuck in my head for a few days that is based off of the looks of a fresh Ubuntu install.





This set would be in the standard Cherry profile, and would require custom colors across the board. I did a few mock-ups like the above (just with fewer keys) on a different thread that include colors from the GMK Swatch on Keyboard Layout Editor, but it brightened up the colors far too much. I really enjoy the contrast between the relaxing dark purple and vibrant orange. This could be a crazy, “Ty, that looks awful.” set, but I genuinely enjoy the look of this, and I hope some other folks do as well.
Edit:
Added updated images with a few more caps included.
Included a few renders from Blender. I used a plugin developed by the Reddit user kdem007 from the following link: https://www.reddit.com/r/MechanicalKeyboards/comments/4tm7b3/i_made_a_plugin_to_easily_import_and_render/. Kdem007 if you ever see this, thank you very much for making such a useful tool!
