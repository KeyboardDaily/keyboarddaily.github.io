BBA-60
Stacked accrylic and brass | Tray-mount 60% | WK - WKL - HHKB

What is this ?
Its a tray mount 60% keyboard made out of stacked accrylic and it also include brass layers as weight and inclinaison feet (5 degrees angle).
I originally made this keyboard at my local maker space and since I got good feedback I decided to make a “production” prototype with more features than the first one.
It turned out very good and all the pictures in this thread are real picture of this second prototype.
I’m very happy with the current design but external feedback is always good so here it is. you can find the IC form below to answer some basic questions and maybe share your tough about this keyboard
IC form
IC Form Link

Specifications :
Case : Stacked accrylic (7 layers), Brass weight (3 layers, sand blasted and clear coated to prevent aging)
Mounting system : tray mount with case foam
Inclinaison angle : 5°
Color : matte black
Case mass : about 1250g unbuilt (case + weight + plate/pcb)
Plate : FR4 1.6mm plate (color tbd)
**PCB :**Plain60 Flex edition, universal pcb compatible with most 60%
Layout : all layout are available using the same plate and PCB

Units : 100 total
Price : 175~200$ (+ shipping)
Vendor : TBD
What will be included in the kit :

  • Accrylic case
  • Brass weight
  • 1x FR4 plate
  • 1x Plain60 Flex edition
  • 1x Case foam
  • Screws and standoff for assembly
  • Rubber feet
  • TX-like case for storage or transport

FAQ (Frequently Asked Questions)
What is the project timeline after this IC ?
The current design is fully ready for productions (I have quotes) so I plan to start the GB around late January unless there are some major changes to the design after the IC results.
Once the GB period will be complete I plan on delivering after 3 months.
Will there be delay due to the current PCB components shortage ?
No, I already have all the PCB in my possession so this will not cause any delay
What colours are available ?
One single colour available for now (matte black) but feel free to suggest alternatives colours in the IC Form and I’ll consider the options if the demand is high enough.
Same goes for the weight material, brass is not the only option possible.
How many units are you planning to make ?
As stated above I will make no more than 100 units rather than doing an unlimited GB for human capabilities reasons. I don’t have access to a warehouse so I don’t think I can handle very large productions.
You mentioned WK WKL and HHKB support, how many units of each are available ?
The current prototype is WK but I will include 2 extra layers for HHKB and WKL layout in the base kit, so you will be able to choose or switch between the options at any time.
What will be the GB format ?
The GB will be FCFS (First Come First Served). I don’t have proxies for now but if most of the potential buyers lives far from me I can contact them to work something out (I’m living in EU).
What about a Founder Edition ?
I can make a full brass version of this board (that would weigh a whopping 3kg unbuilt) if the demand is high enough but it would be a very limited run.
