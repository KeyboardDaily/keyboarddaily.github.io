01 May 2019
Hello guys, I am sorry that it took so long but here we have some updates!
UD_70’s sample production was a bit delayed than originally planned, but the drawings have now been submitted to the factory and the sample production is currently in progress. We expect the sample production to be completed within May, and if nothing is wrong with the prototype and everything goes smooth we could put the GB timeline at about June. I will come back with an update when the prototype is out. Thank you.

I’m sorry I have not had any updates in the meantime.

Hi, I am goldworms, who recently started an IC thread for 40% keyboard UD_40.
UD_40 is in the IC state in geekhack, but GB has already begun in Korea.
Thus I would like to introduce you to my next project.
It is a 75% board, and the name is UD_70.
UD_70 supports two layouts, Winkeyless and Winkey.
Minimal winkeyless or easy-to-use winkey, the choice is yours.
Sample is in production at the moment and scheduled to finish by the end of March.
For now it’s just the render images but I should be able to provide you with the real pictures by April.
Hope it becomes April soon so that I can show you guys how it looks like irl.
You can check out more details on my discord channel and feel free to ask questions, I’ll get back to you asap.
Thank you.
