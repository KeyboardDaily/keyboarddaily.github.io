Specifications

  • New JWK Linear that IS a modified version of their linear switch with a custom extra-long stem so call it a recolor if you like it’s technically not depending on what you call a jwk recolor
  • 5-Pin Housing
  • POM Stem, PC Top and Polyamide mix bottom (Polyamide mix is NOT the same as regular nylon, same polyamide mix as H1)
  • Extra long stem for deeper sound
  • 3.8mm travel and 2.0mm actuation
  • 67g spring, and maybe 62g variant
  • No factory lube
    XM1ST are by no means a JWK Recolor. These switches use the same housing molds as H1’s and a custom mold for the extra long stem. These also use a different spring then
    the H1. These switches have a goal to be “thocky” but at the same time being one of the smoothest switches on the market.
    Color options
    The two color options I’m deciding between is pastel lilac housing and black stem, OR Black housing and pastel lilac stem
