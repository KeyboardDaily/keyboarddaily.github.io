【IC】GMK Forge

April 20, 2017 IC, 键帽, GMK 本文总阅读量次

类型: IC
作者: RyuDragoon
发布时间: 2017-04-20 14:34:34
更新时间: 2018-01-31 15:20:03
原链接: https://geekhack.org/index.php?topic=89025.0

I’ve gone through a couple Red on Black sets and I have gotten fedup with the quality of the sets. So I took it upon myself to design something myself. Lo and behold! GMK Forge has been born.
Here is my sweet KLE that took forever to figure out how to make:

Here are some of the renders I have had made up (Shoutout to ThesisCamper!):




I know that the set is rather minimal and plain. But I don’t enjoy these fancy colorful sets and not am not very fond of white/grey when it comes to keycap sets either. I believe that this set should cover almost every single set that is most common. Ideally I want to have this happen so I can put it on my all black RAMA M65-A. If anyone has some constructive criticisms or solid ideas for different novelties please let me know. If this gains popularity I will be working with a certain Artisan to collaborate on getting some limited edition artisans to be given away in the group buy. I have a contact inside Massdrop to make this happen if I can gain some traction/popularity if this helps distill hope for everyone interested.
Edit 4/23/17: I have sent an email to my contact at MassDrop. I have started revisions to the KLE to include some slight modifications requested by the community.
Edit 5/2/17: I have sent the vectors of the novelties to GMK and am having the novelties redone on my renders as they were not licensed images available for selling and reproduction. Remember to follow the proper channels everyone! Thanks to /u/hineybush for doing the original novelty designs.
Edit 5/3/17: Novelties have been updated with the proper licensed images. Still waiting to hear back from GMK and Massdrop.
Edit 5/10/17: I have received my first draft from GMK. I think that it turned out very nice! I am requesting to get one with the Icon Modifiers as many have requested. My current concern is that it will be more orange then it will red and will turn into a Skidata clone instead of the set I want. Here’s the draft sent to me by GMK. I just noticed it doesn’t have the R3 1.25u “Tab” key. I will request that to be added in the next mock up.

Edit 5/15/17: New second draft from GMK. P3 Looks amazing! This is definitely the color I was going for. I am still inquiring about the R3 1.25u Tab key, potential kits for the inverse colorway for modifiers and other keys, and pricing in general. Still waiting to hear back from MassDrop about our potential workings. I just recently had an idea to maybe have a “gamer” kit that supports the infamous K70 bottom row and the like. Here’s the new and improved render from GMK:

Edit 5/18/17: Good news everyone! I have received pricing from GMK(@loud_asian)! Based on the keys from the render above and the 1.25u Tab key [Shoutout to @AlexAtPanc (153 keys in total)] I have been quoted MOQ 150 sets, 104.40/set (net, EXW, individual sorting and packing in trays is included) and MOQ 250 sets, 94.22/set (net, EXW, individual sorting and packing in trays is included). Shipping and banking fees are not included in the price of the item. This pricing doesn’t include shipping or import fees (will have to look into this since I am US based, I don’t know the fees with importing). GMK’s current lead time is around 14 weeks after receipt of the purchase order.
Bad news, Massdrop is literally booked solid for the next 6 months and they can only run a couple sets a month. I will be doing my best to get my set ran with them as I think it will provide the best coverage and help push the pricing down significantly. I think this is a good and bad thing. There are a lot of awesome sets being run currently and I think giving people’s wallets a break would be a good thing. I just hate having to wait such a long time to even get this thing going.
Edit 5/22/17: New Render completed to show off the accent caps a little bit.

Edit 7/22/17: Some more renders here for you guys! Special thanks to /u/janglad over on Reddit! If you have heard through the grapevine, I am working with KeyClack right now to get this set pushed to GB phase! We are trying to get it out ASAP for you guys so we have it by the end of the year. I am working with GMK now to minmax the pricing and caps included and it seems we are pushing towards the same kits as GMK Terminal.


I also had some custom box art mocked up as well. Still waiting to get official resolution from GMK but this is what I have worked up so far.

Edit 1/30/18:
This is it. The home stretch. I got the final renders setup from GMK and am only waiting for pricing. I have made some small modifications, VIM keys have been corrected and novelties have been moved to 1u size per request.
GMK doesn’t support a single spacebar that works as off-center and centered 6u so I will either have to choose one or go with both.
