Specification

  • 86 keys tkl - WKL only

  • Material: 6063 Aluminium and Brass weight and SS Weight

  • Color: TBD

  • Plate: Sandblasted Polycarbonate / 5052 Aluminium - plates files will be made public later

  • Mounting: Gasket mount with 4mm NBR cords

  • PCB: Solder only - Designed by Sleepdealer (H88c NORTH variant compatible)

  • Typing angle: 6°

  • Front Height: 19mm without bumpons

  • Weight: ~2.3 kg built

Availibility & Vendors

  • Issues noticed:*

  • Sparkles are there because I did not specify the grit blast with the manu. I talked to the manu and gave them specific grits for the finishing for the GB units.

  • Slightly tighter tolerance than expected - solved in Fusion.

  • Keys on r4 row are /*/*ed up (see X and Y) - this was due to half-plate tolerance - now solved in Fusion.

  • The USB cutout of the case is slightly offset - this made the whole plate/PCB lean left. This is also now solved in Fusion.
