4/23/17 -
Whats going on guys, so I think I have mostly finalized the kits, and have gotten them down to a price that I would consider reasonable and have now opened a MassDrop poll to gain interest. I have changed up the Non-Standard Modifier kit a bit, to hopefully lower the price a bit, while trying to not lose too many keys. I have added two kits to try and accommodate more layouts (Blanks and Spacebar), so check those out and let me know what you guys think.
MASSDROP POLL
https://www.massdrop.com/vote/DSA-Freshly-Minted
4/6/17 -
So I come to you guys with some good news. Thank all of you guys for taking my survey, I took a lot of feedback from it. I added more keys to the non standard modifier pack (HHKB, MiniVan, FCC660C) but also had to make some compromises.
Summary of changes to kit

  • 50% majority wanted to go with DSA, so I am planning on running the set in that profile.
  • The purchasing place will likely be Massdrop or PMK Set of the Month, as those were the most common responses.
  • There was only 6 potential Ergodox buyers (I know that is partially my fault because I only had blanks) so that kit is going to be scraped.
  • Also a resounding 80% of you guys were down for the Minty Accent kit, so I decided to include that in the TKL mods kit, and replace it with a new Fruity Accent Kit.
    More

  • The ISO kit might need some help as only 15/180 were down to purchase one, but I think it should still be run, as I will purchase a few kits if it needs help reaching MOQ.
  • A lot of people were asking for a Mint leaf novelty and I would love to include this, however I would like to keep this set as cheap as possible (In order for it to reach MOQ), however if enough of you want it, with a bit of an extra price increase, then I will gladly invoice for that. Thank you for the vault door suggestion, I definitely like that.
  • The name “Freshly Minted” seems to be a hit, so I think I will be going with that, but thanks for the other suggestions.
  • Pricing*
    I have contacted Melissa from Signature Plastic for a quote and have gotten a response and the pricing is looking pretty solid so far. The only thing that might worry me is the non-standard kit seems a bit high, so that kit might have to be re-done.

Hi all, here is an interest check for a Midnight & Mint type keyset (dark grey/blue & mint).
I was looking at my phone one day (Nextbit Robin) and really liked the design and colors of it. That color combo has been one of my favorites, and was wondering if there were others out there too.
This is just an interest check to see if enough people like the colors, and also if people are okay with the colors, ex. it doesn’t look too much like HyperFuse or LightCycle.
I personally think the mods definitely look a bit like HyperFuse but I am not a big fan of the purple for that set, but I’d love to get a discussion going on this.
