    Hello everybody
    This is the Interest Check for my second board, the J-02. A fully featured 60% with Brass internal weighting, removable pen rail, full isolation mount design, carbon fibre plate and with a USB-C daughterboard.
    After the success in proving the execution of my first board (the J-01; more info and pics/videos HEREand a build stream HERE), I wanted to do something a little less extreme from a layout perspective and that was more accessible both in terms of layout options and availability.

Design & Inspiration
The original idea for this board actually precedes the J-01; but at the time I wanted to prove that the design, aesthetics and mechanisms worked. This design is centred around a top and bottom piece anodised in the same colour, with a thick brass insert through the middle; and extruding through the top to form part of a pen rail.
This was influenced by the old school vintage boards which used to have pen rails incorporated in the design for office workers; and I wanted to do a modern take on that. Its currently magnet mounted; although this may change in the final design to something more robust.

The dark colours were inspired by the current black and brass movement that is seen in lots of household design and features, but I would like to offer dark grey and perhaps a white if there is interest.
In terms of the shape, again I wanted to do a take on vintage boards, offering a slab of a keyboard, nestled neatly on a wedge. This makes the board easy to pick up, reduces the final weight of a full sided and back design, and gives it a timeless floating look in line with the J-01.


J-01 side profile by Kwer
The board is constructed from multiple pieces, and is fairly involved. The mounting system is full isolation mount using Nitrile rubber, as seen on the J-01 and the Kepler. Below are some exploded views of the board showing construction; although Im looking at opportunities to enable the gaskets to come pre-assembled as one unit that stretches around the plate post soldering.
The idea behind this design is to remove all contact of the plate with the case - removing any inherent ping or resonance transference by contact. This works exceptionally well as seen on the J-01 and Kepler. The idea behind the CF plate is to retain a robust typing experience with a softness added to the bottom out from the gaskets incorporated into the design. As a material CF feels very much like brass; however as its not a metal it doesn’t have any issues with ping, and presents a strong note with a pleasant timbre and acoustics. The plates will also have acoustic assisting cuts made in strategic places for both ISO and ANSI variants.



Features

  • Full Isolation Gasket Mount
  • USB-C via a daughterboard
  • Custom 60% PCB with JST breakout and ISO/ANSI support
  • Fixed Carbon Fibre Plates  offering ISO and ANSI variants (plate files avail once GB starts)
  • Unique Pen Rail  Currently Magnet Mounted ; may change before GB
  • Thick brass middle and Pen rail for weight and aesthetics
  • Top and bottom are anodised aluminium  colours TBC
  • Tops will be available in WKL, full 60 and HHKB most likely if interest is there for all three
  • 7 degree typing angle
  • Layouts*
    Here is a simple KLE showing the layouts that will be available on the PCB. If there is enough feedback to add other things I’m open to that - please let me know in the comments below. Plates will be fixed as ANSI or ISO with both caps options and split right shift. The ANSI File will also support split backspace for HHKB. Plates files will be available if anyone wants to get one made to something more specific based on the PCB support that gets added/changed.
