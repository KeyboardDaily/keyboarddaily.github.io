Garrett from Dixie Mech here!
Most of us cherish our keyboards like they are our children and love to take them places to show off / hang out with others. However, most people do not have an efficient/safe way to carry their most prized keyboards outside of just having them in a sleeve. A couple years ago I bought a Pelican Air Case to use to fly with my boards to meetups, and it has been incredible. I decided to bring this to rest of you! And of course it gives you the perfect place to show off all those keyboard stickers you have collected.
These are the best cases on the market for protecting your keyboards.
Features:

  • Crushproof
  • Dustproof
  • Weather resistant.
  • Include Foam
  • Vault Cases have layered foam
  • All others have pick and pluck foam so you can customize the storage layout you need
    I am going to attempt to have prices lower than the prices you will see from other places. The prices listed below are still up in the air. The final price will hopefully be lower than the price listed below. It all depends how many are purchased. However there is no MOQ.
    Shipping is not included in these prices.
    After the first round of this groupbuy, I will have a better sense of what cases are popular and will begin to stock them for quicker shipping. However, even the Groupbuy should only take two weeks to be delivered to me.
