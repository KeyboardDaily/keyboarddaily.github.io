    Hello, friends of geekhack.
    Please let me introduce our first switch product: Wild Switch.
    Wild switches are inspired by the GMK Wild, a keyset designed by one of our team members, TD Lemon 1900.
    Wild switches shares the same color with GMK Wild, and will provide two color options:

  • 42g with creamy top and green stem ;

  • 55g with green top and creamy stem;
    Both are linear switches.
    The Wild switch is manufactured by TTC, and will use their latest switch structure, same as the TTC gold pink switch. The gold pink switch has won a very good reputation in China. They are smooth and stable. TTC OEM switches for many large companies for several years. We are confident in their timeliness and quality.
    To get better typing feelings, we gonna use a spring structure that has recently become popular in the community. A golden spring with a closed coil in the middle. It will make the actuation weight of the switch become light and soft, but the rebound force is still very strong. We were fascinated by the feeling after we tried it.

  • Detail:*

  • TTC gold spring with a close ring in the middle

  • 5 pin bottom housing

  • Material: Stem-POM, Top housing-PC, Bottom housing - PA66(Nylon)
