Hello Geekhack.
Earlier this year I ran a successful group buy for the Lumberjack PCB kit, we had a huge response and shipped over 150 kits to people around the World. Thanks to everyone who took part. It’s been 9 months, so it’s perhaps time to start thinking about a round 2.

The plan is to pretty much do the same thing as last time but with some optimisations and tweaks here and there, but if there are any suggestions for additional features or quality of life improvements, let me know in the comments below.
I need to do one more round of prototypes, so hope to be ready for GB beginning of next year so the PCB order can be run straight after Chinese New Year.
Price will be 22 + postage (7-11) send through the mail in a padded envelope, just like last time.
