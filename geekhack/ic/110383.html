Ladies and gentlemen, this is Patrick. Im here to present you the second project of NEO Keys, Palette Carbon G67. Join discord ( https://discord.gg/2MCKpQQ) for more updates and details.
The idea of building a full carbon fiber keyboard started from some random discussions in my discord server. Somebody suggested that I should build a carbon board just for fun, I did and it turned out to be pretty awesome.
Design is based off the last NEO Keys board Palette G67(IC: https://geekhack.org/index.php?topic=108630.msg2958210/#msg2958210; GB: https://geekhack.org/index.php?topic=109107.msg2968989/#msg2968989), an acrylic sandwich gasket mount keyboard.
Palette Carbon and Palette acrylic share the same:

  1. Switch plate
  2. PCB
  3. General dimension and typing angle
    So if you already ordered a Palette, you can switch the plate/pcb between these two cases.
    Different
  4. Much less color options, only 3k twill weave carbon fiber available.
  5. No top color display tab design, just a simple, clean and slim bezel frame.
  6. Chamfered corners and angled bottom.
  7. Gasket material, authentic Rogers Poron in stead of silicone + EVA
    Palette Carbon G67 kit will include:
  8. Full carbon fiber case (4 layers of case body, 2 layers of feet), 1 extra acrylic bottom layer in the color of your choice.
  9. 1.5mm carbon fiber or 1.5mm brass plate
  10. Hotswap or soldered PCB
  11. Poron Gasket strips
  12. Plate/PCB foam and PCB/case foam
  13. Silicone Feet
  14. Mounting hard wares and tools
  15. USB-C Cable
  16. Microfabric cloth
    Hotswap PCB features:
    ANSI only layout
    per key RGB light
    Underglow RGB lights
    Center mounted USB port
    North facing switches

    Soldered PCB features:
    ANSI/ISO compatible layout, split backspace and 7u spacebar supported
    Ultra bright underglow RGB lights, 18xLEDs!
    Center mounted USB port
    South facing switches

    Quantity: MOQ = 50, no maximum order limit.
