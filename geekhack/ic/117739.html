    Prologue
    Hi there, welcome back to yet another ClickClack post! Working with a new designer, BA54, we’re excited to show you
    this cool mecha-inspired keycap set: The Warrior Mk II keycaps! Over a year of toil, effort and research has gone into
    this, so we hope you enjoy this set as much as we do. As you will see, the set is themed around mechs, with a clear
    industrial theme. The black, white, grey along with the rusty reds, give a strong sense of steel, and the text mods
    have been designed to simulate warning signs as one might find in science fiction, hopefully to your tastes.
