Welcome to ePBT Night Market!
Lets cut to the chase and show some renders:
Renders by Manzel


Full render album here.
Inspiration
ePBT Night Market was loosely inspired by the cool dark & meaty colours of Thai Night Markets and the night in general.
Kitting
Stand
The base kit. Alice & Split support in base (because im a split user myself ). Another weird thing - has media keys, in case youre someone who likes those. Includes an extra tab, so if you get Sign, you can fit 2 boards.
Sign
Thai Alphas. Replaces the alphas of Base and Roof. Disclaimer: These legends will be tweaked for the final set to be more accurate, but this render will let you have a good idea of what it’ll look like.
Poles
Spacebars. Includes dual 2.25 & 2.75 for boards such as the BMEK and SP-111
Roof
40s + Ortho kit.
Goods
Novelty kit based on various foods that can be found in the market. Includes iconography of common foods found at night markets.
Cooker
UKISO + Vim. Also includes F14 and F15 for some weird layouts.
Deskmats
These will have black borders and stitching, and will be available in 900mmx400mm.

Colours
Colours were chosen to be not too far apart from each other, yet still be different, to properly separate the alphas and the mods. Here you can see the difference between ePBT NM and similar colourways:

Colour matching is in progress, but currently I am not in possession of a Pantone Colour Bridge book. Find the album here.

FAQ
“ePBT doesnt do reverse dyesub”
They do, but at the moment it isnt stable enough for a full set. The GB will be after they confirm with me that its stable.
“Base kit is too big. it’ll make it more expensive.”
Since it’s ePBT, it’ll much cheaper than if a kit this size was run in a manufacturer such as GMK. As it stands, I’m aiming for a price around 110 USD.
