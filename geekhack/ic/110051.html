Hi everyone, I have decided to indefinitely delay GMK Vélo. The reason for this is that this set is not really something I want to pursue at this time, and just does not fall in line with what I want do to currently. I have some other projects I am way more excited about than this, and will be sharing those very soon! I might come back to this set on a later date, so for everyone who was genuinely interested in this set, please don’t lose hope! I appreciate every single one who has filled out the IC forms, left a positive comment and overall just enjoyed the set, I really do!
