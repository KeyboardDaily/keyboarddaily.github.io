‘’LEAFPAD’’’
hi everyone, welcome to the interest check of my first project, ‘’ LEAFPAD ‘’.
I am an Italian guy passionate about technology for as long as I can remember.
I finally managed to combine 2 of my greatest passions, namely 3d printing and mechanical keyboards, to create a project that could satisfy the other members of the community

LEAFPAD its an affordable trough hole kit with a pla 3d printed case.
the name and the project is inspired by nature, to which I am very attached,i have loved nature since I was a child, the outings in the countryside with my parents, the meetings and friendships at the playground, the time spent helping my grandmother in the garden, the noises, the smells of spring, are sensations and moments that I bring in the heart and that made me the person I am now
the design also incorporates the somewhat clunky and rounded shape of the space cadet, which I personally love.

IT FEATURES:
-diy trough hole kit (to keep the cost down)
-rotary Encoder
-bottom mount case
-fr4 plate
-3d printed pla case with either a flat or 8 degree typing angle,possible thanks to a magnetic riser
The kit will contain all the pieces necessary for the realization.
If this project were to like and go well, I would do a limited group buy to better manage everything
The price isn’t final , but it will be around (40 euros)/ (48 dollars) .
Also , this is my first interest check so be kind with me
