The Liminal Keyboard came about when I saw the new style of a few sub-60’s that focused there boards on being easier to outfit with the ABUNDANCE of gmk sets we have on the market today. A lot of big sets run with 40’s specific kits but that is almost always an additional cost. Us 40’s folk are die-hard makers and enjoy the hobby not just for its $8000 Keycult TKL’s or $400 Avenues… we like to BUILD!. Thats half the fun of the hobby!
The Equinox, TMOv2, and others were amazing boards that sold well and really opened people up to the ideas of sub-60s without the “need” for specialized 40’s kits.
BUT SATISFIED I WAS NOT I SAY!
I wanted MORE.
After speaking with Perrine of P3D, he expressed interest in designing and running a case of his design and running GB for the Liminal. I am very excited to be able to work with such a fantastic maker and well established vendor within the community.
The Liminal is a fun, new, 50% keyboard kit that’s a great way for newcomers to dip your toes in the small form factor, or for seasoned 40% veterans looking for a fresh layout.
Liminal PCB
Main Features:

  • Concept and PCB Design by Me The_Royal
  • Spectacular Keycap Compatibility (can be outfitted with most GMK and ePBT base kits)
  • Full ISO and ANSI Configurations (Split Left-Shift, ISO Enter, as well as 1.25u “Enter”)
  • Split and Full-Size Bottom Row Options
  • USB Type-C Connection
  • RGB LED Indicator below “CAPSLOCK” Key
  • Breakout Connection Pins for RGB Underglow Strips
  • Aesthetically pleasing PCB (for the classy folk who care).
  • Sub 11mm Height (Basic FR4 Kit) Height
  • High Quality 3D Printed PLA Case (optional upgrade) for a discounted price only for this GB
  • Fully Supported and Powered by QMK (I will be trying to Get VIA setup to be an option as well
