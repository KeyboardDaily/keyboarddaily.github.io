Hi! We are a new team from China called MOYUKEY, this is our first project.
We are glad to introduce this DMK Keycaps based on a unique nebula, a crab nebula M01.

3 months ago, we watched James Webb Space Telescope’s launch.We were all impressed by it.
After the launch, we googled more details about JWST telescope, fortunately founded many beautiful pictures taken by other telescopes.
Among them, one photo came to the eyes which is made by ESA, Nebula M01, a crab type nebula.

https://www.esa.int/ESA_Multimedia/Images/2013/12/Crab_Nebula
Bingo! We love this color. We decided to make a Keycap based on purple,blue and space grey.
Consider long time user experience, we didn’t want the color too shiny.
