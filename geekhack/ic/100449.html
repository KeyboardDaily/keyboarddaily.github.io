Hello, my friends on Geekhack,
Last year, we created a very lovely switch opener tool and we called it “Nutcracker”
It has really really aroused intense interest among enthusiasts from the community. After we finished production and shipped the product,
we also try to do many surveys to get feedback from buyers/users, we want:

  1. To hear real feedback from the user AFTER they used this tool, about the user experience, about how they feel when they use it and etc.
  2. Personal discussion about the Pros and Cons.
    The final goal is to improve our product in the future.
    And today is the date!
    We are proud to announce that Nutcracker V2 here, made by TianYue Lab.
    V2 has been designed after getting user feedbacks and has been given careful consideration to it.
    Every aspect including ergonomics of the tool, not just the cosmetic or looks since it is a tool that has to be held in the user’s hand for long periods of time during the usage.
    (“TianYue” in Chinese means add happiness, that is our initial expectations.)
