I’m happy to finally present the Dalco 959 Mini:
A High-end Porsche 959 inspired 60
We’ve worked to capture the brilliant industrial aesthetic of the Porche 959, while staying true to the iconic simplicity of keyboard designers before us.
The side profile is inspired by the elegant curves of the 959, bringing elements of this classic style into a modern keyboard.
The weight and rear accent are exaggerated but tasteful, scratching that itch for more brass and steel most of us have. We hope you enjoy!

Brand: Dalco
Design: Hand Engineering x Dalesnail
Series: 959
Model: Mini

 2 build options: O-ring gasket or top mount
 Colors: Silver w/ Red plate.
 Materials: Anodized aluminum body, Large internal brass weight, Polished Stainless steel accent weight
 Typing Angle: 7deg
 Front Height: 17.6mm
 Layout: Tsangan or WKL (w/ only split backspace and split right shift options), Semi-Fixed ISO option
 1950g weight unbuilt
 Custom PCB and Daughterboard by Hiney

Price: $650


Typing Video by Koenromers
Plate: Aluminium
Sound dampening: 1mm neoprene foam
Switches: H1 (TX films, SPRiT 60g Slow springs)
Lube: Krytox 205g0 (springs with Krytox 106)
Stabilisers: OG Cherry clip-in with Everglide wires (lubed with Krytox 205g0)
Keycaps: CRP Desko R2
Mount: Gasket
