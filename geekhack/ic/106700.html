Specs:

  • Mounting Type: Top Mount
  • Typing Angle: 6 degree
  • Material: 6061 aluminium bead-blasted/anodized finish
  • Colours: Black/Grey/Silver/Dark Navy/Aquamarine/Blue Grey (Aquamarine = 5463 C / Blue Grey = 433C)
  • Number of Units: 150 units
  • Layout: Supports both ANSI & ISO Layout (Supports split backspace & stepped caps lock)
  • Plate Material Options: Aluminium/Brass/Carbon Fibre
  • PCB: Custom KY-01 USB Type-C PCB (Supports QMK, has ESD-Protection)
  • Screws: 7x M2.5 Phillips pan-head screws / 8x M3 Torx Screw
  • Switch Support: MX style switches only
  • [Estimated Price]: $275 for alu plate kit / $285 for brass plate kit / $290 for carbon fibre plate kit*
  • (FCFS)*
  • [Shipping Prices]: UK ($20) / Europe ($35) / USA & Canada ($35) / Rest of the world ($60)*
  • [Estimated shipping date]: Late September 2020 - Early November 2020*
  • (Parts)*
  • Top case
  • Bottom case
  • 1.5mm plate
  • USB type-C PCB
  • 7x M2.5 Phillips pan-head screws / 8x M3 Torx screw
  • 2x Silicone Adhesive Strips + 2x replacement strips
