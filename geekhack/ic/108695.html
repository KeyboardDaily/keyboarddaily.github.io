Welcome to Meso.







Introduction
Meso is a 60% sandwich mount keyboard, designed using similar concepts as my previous keyboards (Cajal, Campsite). The goal of this board is to return to sandwich mount in an age of mediocre gasketed designs. Meso features HHKB, WKL, and Standard layout top pieces, with a plate choices encompassing two layouts (Universal or 7u Fixed Layout) and two material options (Brass or PC Half-plate). I would love to hear your feedback to make this keyboard the best it possibly can be. Thanks for reading!
Details:

  • 4.2lb aluminum case with a 4 degree typing angle and sandblasted brass weight.
  • Choice of Drank (purple), E-White, Space Grey, Bushido Red or Blue Steel anodized finishes.
  • QMK-compatible black PCB with copper traces.
  • Choice of HHKB, WKL or Standard layout top pieces to suit your preferences. (WKL and HHKB only compatible with 7u Fixed plate.)
  • Plate layouts available in Universal or 7u Fixed Layout.
