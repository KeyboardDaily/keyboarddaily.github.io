Hello everyone! I would like to introduce my keyset, Asphalt.
For this design I wanted to capture the lower contrast and minimalism of asphalt, while mimicking the colors and adding the vibrant yellow of street markings. I posted a basic feedback thread on /r/mk for fun and it got some really good feedback so I thought I would continue this project.
Some info about the set:

  • Signature Plastics ABS double shot
  • SA R3 Profile
    …more info to come.
    I am working on some better mockups and needing to get a few renders done. Below are screenshots of the current state of the set design. Feedback on the child sets would be very helpful, I was having difficulty deciding how to split them and what should be in each.
    Yellow legend child sets were in consideration but removed to keep it simple and costs low. The same decision was made for icon modifiers and RGB child sets, feedback welcome. There are also a few novelty caps that are still being developed.
    Thank you in advance for all the feedback and tips!
