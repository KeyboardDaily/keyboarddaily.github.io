Welcome
Hello! My name is Koobaczech and I love designing cool, fun and unique bespoke boards! Some of you may know me from Pearl 40%, but I’ve made other boards as well such as Espectro, a 65% for my friend and the first Pandora years ago. Heres a link to the Pearl groupbuy I ran a while back! Pearl
Pandora and its Features
Pandora is a super premium 60% that is packed with all the Koob tech you would expect. I worked very hard on this project some years ago, but never ran it. I have resurrected and refined it for todays market with the best features I could add. Everything was designed in house and with patience and love. Before going further, I want to extend a kind note to any newcomers that this is not a beginner friendly board. It requires patience, knowledge, and proper handling to ensure its proper function. I say this in regard to things such as aligning plates with gasket systems, not over-tightening polycarb parts, understanding which plates are best for you, and properly using a hotswap pcb. With that being said, lets start listing some things that I put into these bad boys. One last note, whats listed here is currently being exercised in my two Prototypes!

  • MAIN Features*

  • ANSI, ISO, TSANGAN layouts

  • Split lshift, rshift, backspace and stepped caps

  • 10u spacebar support

  • 9.5 degree typing angle

  • Big weight

  • Hotswap availability

  • Many colors, materials, finishes

  • USB C Daughterboard

  • Gasket mounted

  • HHKB, ANSI, ISO, WKL

  • Rotary Encoder Knob button in the back. Push/rotate for things like volume, mute, page scroll etc

  • USB 2.0 HUB

  • Indicator led and under RGB

  • Case*

  • “Full Isolation” Poron Gasket mounting system w/ 12 gaskets total

  • Alu 6063 anodized or ecoated

  • Polished and PVD Coated Stainless Steel or Brass

  • Dyed Polycarbonate

  • Laser engraved accent pieces

  • 2mm Frosted Polycarbonate mid diffuser piece

  • Switchplates*

  • Alu, Brass, Stainless Steel, Pom, Polycarbonate, Fr4, Acrylic

  • Ansi or Iso support via different plates

  • Halfplate or Fullplate

  • Relief “meme” cuts on all halfplates

  • PCB*

  • USB C Daughterboard C3 Variant (latest daughterboard tech)

  • ESD, Fuse, etc protection

  • Main PCB and USB Hub designed in house by me

  • For Hotswap pcb, it has a fixed tsangan layout

  • Via support already working

  • Flex cuts across pcb center

  • RGB Underglow but no in switch leds

  • Caps corner indicator led on case or caps key indicator under key

  • Top facing Reset switch accessible via plate relief cut

  • Center mounted USB-C port

  • No shorting on flex

  • EVA Foam dampening available for case and pcb/plate

  • Gold plated mounting hardware
