KITTING
AWAKENING BASE KIT

Compatibility for full size, 1800, TKL, 75%, 65%, and 60%, an extra “B” for Alice layouts, and F13 support.
The top-left aligned icon caps-control was added for a cleaner more consistent look with the icon-only modifiers on HHKB’s.
Now with UK-ISO and 40% support!
FULL MOON ALT ALPHAS KIT

Inverted accent/alphas for a full blood moon look that is unapologetically red.
Now with an extra tab key for double the keyboard coverage.
ELDRITCH KNOWLEDGE NORDE KIT

Classic NorDe kit for all your language needs.
TENDRILS SPACEBARS KIT

Support for Alice and split spacebar in both alpha and accent colors.
HORRORS NOVELTIES KIT

Support for 2 macro columns with unique designs for each key and additional R4’s to replace win/super keys.
