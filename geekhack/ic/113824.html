SA Squeeze ABS | Retail’s Bloomberg Terminal | Phase 1.0 (7/15/21 update)

About: Deep in the red, you look at your losses and turn to your wife. She has not had a real date in months and she knows the look in your eyes. As the intro to her favorite show plays, she wonders, “What is it this time,” shrinking into the couch. Is it an AMC or GME dip? A once in a lifetime chance to buy another poocoin? She’s unsure what a mechanical keyboard is, but one thing she knows, it’s ramen and netflix tonight.
Concept: Late while sitting on the toilet browsing r/wallstreetbets and r/superstonk, a vision strikes of a keycap set with all red surrounding the enter key. You don’t care about the price, there is only one option. The diamond blue and dollar green reminds you of why you buy, a red sell button reminds you of the fated paperhands. Kits selected to simplify choosing your preferred color scheme. This is a unique mix and match selection with an uncommon colorway.
Reality: Design started June ‘21 with a gradient of red on right of board and designed ‘Ape’ font used for legends. It was a mess, pre-interest provided feedback and a redesign cleaned up the look. This is a first design and group buy, so kitting was a learning experience, to say the least. Kits may be rearranged pending feedback here, thank you for reading and please help out if you enjoy the work!
