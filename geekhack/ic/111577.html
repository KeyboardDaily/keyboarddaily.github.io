Introducing: GMK Orochi, a keycap set based on the Japanese legend of an eight-headed, eight-tailed serpent dragon.****

A woodblock print by 月岡 芳年 was used as inspiration. It depicts Yamata-no-Orochi, the dragon, being slain by the banished god Susano-o.
