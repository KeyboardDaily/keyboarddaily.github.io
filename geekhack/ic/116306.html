Background Info:
The OGR was originally designed as an entry for the OG Summer competition on r/customkeyboards in 2019, which it won. It was originally inspired by various vintage keyboards, but chiefly the Cherry/NCR G81-3077 for the iconic Cherry nose, the pronounced seam and the large forehead. It also drew inspiration from the Cherry G80-0832 for the accented piece around the keycaps to give it a truly unique look. The name is a portmanteau of OG Summer and NCR, whose branding on the aforementioned G81-3077 was also the inspiration for the retro-styled AKB logo. While the PCB is USB-C compatible, the port is enclosed deep inside the case and the case parts gently clamp the cable down to give the aesthetic of a hard-wired cable.
Also, for those who are interested, there will be a numpad (OGR-N) available either as an optional extra or separately.
Specs/features:

  • Inspired by Cherry G81-3077/G80-0832
  • 10° typing angle
  • Full 6063 aluminium construction
  • Bead-blasted and anodised finish
  • Top mounted plate
  • 21.5mm effective front height (with bumpons)
  • QMK and VIA/VIAL-compatible USB-C PCBs
  • LED indicators on caps lock, numlock on numpad
  • Integrated cable aesthetic
  • Bold, pronounced seam
  • Period-authentic product stickers
  • Plate materials: 5052 Aluminium, Polycarbonate, Carbon Fibre (may change)
  • Is an absolute unit
  • 2.9kg (6.5lbs) / Numpad 0.9kg (2lbs) [unbuilt]

  • Layout Support:*
    Two tops will be available - one for 7u bottom row with dual 0.75u blockers, and one with a single small blocker for 6.25u support.

    The OGR-N PCB will have support for regular and southpaw configurations, as well as the option to have a full 1u setup. The plate available will support regular and southpaw layouts, due to its reversible design.
