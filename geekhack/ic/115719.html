Greetings,
Our first switch, the Red Jacket Linear, is themed after the popular Squid Games TV show!
It is so hard to be original nowadays when creating your own switch. Its either riding a trendy colorway, paying a lot for a new mold, or being the first to play around with new materials that manufacturers release. My goal when creating a switch was to provide a first of its kind without having to pay for the new mold. I feel as if Ive done that by going with a new manufacturer. Well Us at KNC Keys LLC arent the first to use Aflion, a new switch manufacturer, but we can at the very least say, we are the first to use them with the switch material and spring combination.
The first to use Aflion, to our knowledge, is Invokeys with their Matcha Latte Linear switch. They had a very successful release, and thats because the switch is PHENOMENAL stock. No need to crack them open and modify them!
What makes Aflion special IMO is the factory lubing technique. If you havent tried the Matchas from Invokeys, I highly suggest you do! They are amazing stock switches. Just watch Andy Nguyens video on them. /I am not an affiliate of Invokeys, I am just a really big fan/
The material combination is solely based on my preference for a linear switch. From the full nylon housing, double-stage spring, weight, and longer pole stem.
We have already purchased the proofing, branding, and sample fee. This means we have initiated production and have the samples on hand!
Our plan is to have this switch be IN-STOCK. If we see plenty of interest, we would love to release a tactile version (Green Jacket).
