Hello fellow keyboard enthusiasts, we are Key Code.
Today I want to present you KWA.6-60% gasket mounted keyboard.
The keyboard supports WK WKL and HHKB layouts.
When we designed this keyboard in 2020 we wanted to pursue an old-school or vintage designed might you say.
We wanted to keep the overall design nice and clean, with a bit of extra weights on top and the bottom.
