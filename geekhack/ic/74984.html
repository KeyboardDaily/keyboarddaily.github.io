【IC】Nantucket Selectric - Modern Selectric goes sailing (SA 1-1-2-3-4-4)

With Modern Selectric scheduled to ship this week I’ve been thinking a lot about what my next set will be. Once again looking to my Selectric for inspiration, I focused on the keys themselves. Mine is a Selectric I with a beige case and dark navy blue keys. The combination is very pleasing, but doesn’t quite pop the way I wanted it to. I spent some time thinking about this, and looking at design blogs for inspiration. After a while, this question arose:
What would a Modern Selectric typewriter look like in the timeless Navy/Cream/Yellow palette?


(Direct link: https://keyboarddaily.oss-cn-shanghai.aliyuncs.com/geekhack/74984/ggKvwq5.jpg?x-oss-process=style/keyboarddaily)
This set mimics a Selectric typewriter that never was, but could have been. One that would look right at home in your tiny coastal town, sitting on a big oak desk. The navy blue legends sit upon slightly creamy keys, providing a bright contrast that seems to defy the natural darkness of Navy Blue. The golden yellow of YCF on the mods brings out the handsomeness of BBI in a way that no other shade could.
Examples of placement and various child deals can be found in the full imgur album starting here.
