As for some relatively suggested ideas from a Vietnamese group concerned about the aesthetic of our product. We now have released the new and completely different keyboard even though it is still called “ZODIAC” but not limited in “PISCES constellation” anymore. Instead, it will be a full optional collection for a total of 12 ZODIAC SIGNS. The bottom part is more solid, and the mounting style now will be “SANDWICH” with a better typing feeling than top mount (in my opinion).
—-Some basic specs:
1 - Mount style: Sandwich with 1.5mm thich plate
2- Angle: 6.5 degree
3- Weight thickness: 2mm with alu as standard, could be upgrade to brass
4- Estimated weight: 2kg excluding keycap and switches
5- PCB: 2 options for solderable and hot-swap with $10 different, type C, fullly compatible QMK
6- I will let the first 15 people who purchase this keyboard have a chance to design their own symbols to be engraved into that weight (if they need) with a small extra cost.
7- Prototype expected: at the final week of Feb, 2020
8- GB expected: a month after prototype revealed
