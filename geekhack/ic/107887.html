Hello everyone!
Thank you all so much for the support throughout this project. I wanted to update you all on the current status of the case as well as what to look for in the coming weeks. This will also become my first post on Geekhack, where I hope to find as warm of a welcome as over on Reddit.
First, as you can see from the Imgur album above, I have the final samples and they look amazing! The case looks and feels great and I cant wait for you guys to get your hands on some of your own.
Also, I wanted to announce the dates the group buy will be open as well as the tentative date for when the case will be shipped/be officially available on my website. I will list those below:
Group buy open: 8/10/20 10am cst
Group buy close: 8/24/20 11:59pm cst
Tentative shipping/release date: Mid-September (I will have a solidified date for shipping on the group buy open post)
For anyone who is new here, Ill give some details on the case here:

  • 6063 aluminum case (electrophoresis treatment on both the white and black)
  • Brushed brass weight and front insert
  • ~7° typing angle
  • 3.1 lbs/1.4 kg total weight (before PCB, switches and keys)
  • Compatible with GH60 PCB and other comparable PCBs
    Of course, if you guys have any further questions, feel free to leave them below and Ill get back to you at my earliest convenience.
    If you or someone you know is looking to build a new 60% keyboard soon, make sure to fill out the IC form above. I will send out an email to all those who have filled out the form regarding the group buy and release of the Stripe 60. Make sure youre signed up so you dont miss out!
