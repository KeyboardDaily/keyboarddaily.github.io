Good morning fellow Geekhacker
As our PBT lord close to shipping right now, we would like to announce our 2nd project with Milkyway.
Perhaps many have heard that our first run of Geekark BOW in China was a success, but due to some insider thing, we decided to switch manu to Milkyway to provide a higher quality BOW keycaps.
The set will be normal BOW (black, ws1) with accent kits available to purchase. The accent kits will include all 6 classic colors along with 3 brand new colors.
Projected GB is early Oct, and the expected shipping time is 2-3 months after GB closing.
Since this is our first run of the set with milkyway, we will have limited vendor. For future run, we will be asking vendors around the globe to provide more options.
