HI WOULD LIKE TO ANNOUNCE THAT GB IS LIVE!!!
Hello Everyone!
Thank you for taking the time to check out my IC.
I’ve decided to start a new thread since some time has passed from the previous update. I initially planned the GB for the deskmats to be around November 2021, work seems to have caught up hence the delay. I apologise for those that have been eagerly waiting for the GB announcement.
I also decided to remove one of the design - “Tranquil Bleak” because of some manufacturing limitation. I do not wish to have a design produced that will eventually not look as good as I want it to be.
More

These deskmats design are my take on tranquillity.
Mountains & Lakes have always brought me calmness despite life’s chaos, thus I thought that since work had always been a stress factor of mine (& others), these illustrations would make my workstation less heavy.
I had taken the time to take care of the tiniest details, hope I didn’t dissapoint!
