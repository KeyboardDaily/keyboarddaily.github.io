Design Concept
February 25, we have a few friends set up the CAMP team, the design team of friends and started to discuss the design of our own “toy”.Then, more and more friends to join our team. They believe in our ability and we designed this keyboard together first.We use “CAMP C225” to commemorate the name of a group of friends have their own customized camp on February 25.During the design, we repeatedly modify the design and some experienced friend gave us good advice. We hope that more friends to join our team and give us a new perspective.
The Technogical Characters and Paramenters

  1. Imports of aluminum (6063 T)
  2. CNC integration process
  3. Industrial anodizing
  4. Keyboard slope: 9 °
