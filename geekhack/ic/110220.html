We made a one-off Matrix-Cyberspace-Blade-Runner-Hangul-Rain 90s themed Hacker aesthetic stacked acrylic keyboard (say it 10 times fast?) for Mysterium PCBs, based on a concept by ULLIAM and designed by our team at Protozoa Studio. We got a lot of interest in making a production version.
The matrix hangul text is a translation of Roy Battys monologue in Blade Runner (tears in the rain), which ULLIAM used on his desk mats for KAT Cyberspace. The acid green edge glow acrylic matches the well known cyberspace aesthetic.
A one-off GLITCH was built by MaxF: https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=MEEEKezeniI. The design has been refined and changed (see renders and description). Production units of the GLITCH will have the etched design but no GID infill (this can be DIYed at your own risk).
There are 2 versions, (1) P.00 GLITCH (matrix rain style), (2) ‘P.00 FROST (regular style).
Both are compatible with Mysterium PCBs from CFTKB.com or P.01 PCBs from protozoa.studio.
Looking forward to your questions and feedback.
