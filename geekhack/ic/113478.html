Specifications and Materials
Completely designed and manufactured by us
All components are produced with CNC equipment
Weight: 385g (13,60 oz)
Height: 9cm (3.54 in)
The stand consists of 4 parts:
Stand base
Laser cut
Processed on a milling machine
Bent on a bending machine and sandblasted
Painted or anodized (optional)
Bottom holder
Fully milled
Sandblasted
Painted or anodized
Upper support plate
CNC machined
Lightly hand polished to maintain texture after machining
Covered with varnish
Logo
Fully milled bronze or copper
Lightly hand polished to maintain texture after machining
Varnished
Painting options

  1. Anodizing to black
  2. Powder painting
