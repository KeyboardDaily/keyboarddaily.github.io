Introducing new custom keyboard - the Moon. Astonishing curve is what the Moon brings you. The moon is designed and will be manufactured by EVE - the same manufacturer of Tank and Mira. These curves requires advanced machinery and rich experiences to complete. Although what you see here is a un-finalized render, only small details will be modified.
Feel free to leave a comment or suggestion. Let us know what you like or don’t like about the Moon. Also let us know what colors would you like to see. Can’t promise to satisfy everyone but we will d o our best to accommodate

Najs Prototype Album: http://imgur.com/a/d9dTS
Please keep in mind that this album is just to give you folks an idea what the final product will look like or close to. More importantly to illustrate the manufacturer’s capability. I know the design is not perfectly ideal, and we are still working on it.

-Different color options will be proposed for top case, bot case and plate. There will be four different colors for every part mentioned. I’ve asked for a list of color demo, will update once I got that
-USB hole design has been finalized, located on right side of the bot case. (CLOSELY) See link: http://imgur.com/flXYMaq
-You probably noticed that logo has also been modified from the link above, that is final
-Bottom weight will not be changed so it will remain as it was: http://imgur.com/W5SZEtn
