Hey everyone, thanks for taking some time out of your day to look at my keyset; GMK Kouhai is a keycap set formerly known as mashu.
Color Palette:

Kits
Base kit
The base kit provides compatibility for 60%, 65%, 75%, TKL, 1800, HHKB, and full-size boards, with support for Terminal ISO. Accent keys are included for those who want to bring a bit of flare to their board.

Katakana Kit
For those who prefer an alternative look for the alphas.

Spaces Kit
A spacebars kit for that sweet sweet Alice-layout compatibility.

Extensions Kit
Macros. Windowed. Sugoi.

Novelties
Regarding the recent concerns of IP from GMK, it would be the best move to cut the novelties out and play it safe. (Senpai novelty has been moved to the base kit).
Deskmat - Collaboration with Kuno.
Regarding the deskmat samples, the photos were taken when it was eyeball blasting sun+snow lighting, the colors are more saturated than depicted in the photos.
