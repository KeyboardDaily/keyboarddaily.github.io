Hey keyboard enthusiasts,
We are Phase Studio. Two designers in Toronto, looking to run a keyboard group buy. We’ve gotten lots of responses from r/MechanicalKeyboards and r/Mechmarket and by popular request, we are now posting here.
To avoid breaking TOS, not all renders and links will be found in this thread. If I’m breaking TOS at any point please let me know and I will correct it. I’m trying to follow as best as I can.
Updates

  • May 26 - Vendors getting in contact with manufacturers this week for everything except PCBs. Held up by COVID for PCBs.

  • May 11 - Some other prototypes ordered to check one last thing. All units will be ordered after that.

  • May 4 - Preparing to order boards from the manufacturer. Ironing out some details.

  • April 8 - Releasing plate files by month-end. Will have multiple variants.

  • April 2nd - MOQ reached for cases!

  • April 1st - Group buys are going live around noon EST. Sorry for the delays.

  • Mar 25 - The colours were murdered. Making a GB thread though!

  • Mar 11 - Groupbuy happening April 1st.

  • Mar 2 - Alexotos stream on Saturday, March 6th 1PM EST!.

  • Feb 10 - PCBs arrived and working! We will be scheduling streams soon.

  • Jan 14 - Slowly getting things together now. PCBs still on their way. Solderable plate tested and works great!

  • Jan 3 - Happy new year! New yellow colour sample is in and we’re pretty happy with it, PCBs coming soon!

  • Dec 6 - New Prototypes being sent out soon, slow progress but still progress. PCB components have been sourced.

  • Nov 9 - PCBs have been ordered, final prototypes ordered. Hopefully, build streams by the end of the month!

  • Oct 24 - We are continuing development of the hot-swap PCB and also offering Wilbas soldered PCB

  • Oct 13 - Looking into Wilbas new Graphite PCBs instead, new IC posted.

  • Sept 22 - Photos from manufacturer https://imgur.com/gallery/ub6jLiZ

  • Sept 16 - JST Connector Pinout is wrong, jumping connections for prototype, will have to reroute

  • Sept 3 - Case being manufactured, looking into new PCB manufacturers

  • Aug 30 - White, Black, Blue, Purple, Yellow confirmed as colours

  • Aug 25 - New prototypes received, waiting on PCBs still

  • Aug 12 - PCB Prototypes Ordered, Case Prototype Shipping Tomorrow
