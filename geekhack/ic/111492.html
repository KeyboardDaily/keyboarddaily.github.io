Hi, everyone! I’m Hammer
I want to welcome you to the introduction of DSS Hand Work Redux.
DSS is a unique profile, similar to that of DSA but more sculpted
My goal with using Signature Plastics’ DSS profile is to not only make the set as affordable and accessible as possible, but also get it into the hands of fan much sooner than GMK queue times. The color pallet is very reminiscent of 90s children’s toys with an wacky twist!
Signature Plastics queue time is about 10-12 weeks.
What you may notice from the very start is the interesting kitting!
The main idea for this set is to offer a wide range of compatibility encompassing everything from ortho and 40s all the way up to 1800.
As an avid user of all things 40% I decided to make my main focus to support those awesome boards, however, I did not want to leave anyone out!
As for right now, the set is scheduled to release early April
I have a bunch of surprises in store and I hope everyone enjoys the set.
