Cinnn recently just finished making those iron man metal keycaps. And now we’re working on something different with high quality and unique materials and designs. From now on, our products will be presented under the name of Monster. We have two product lines right now including regular metal keycaps (aluminum/zinc) and luxury metal keycap (silver and gold).
The keycaps will be made of pure silver.
Each of these keycaps will be hand made. Since it’s silver, we will use coating to protect the surface. The coating will last for at least a year.
We will have 11 designs available as follows:


  11. The price range will depend on the number of keycaps we ordered. The price is for one single keycap. The price will decrese if only we reach the next MOQ of one certain design. Each design has its own price range and they don’t share a discount on MOQ.
    10-30: $82-$90
    30-50: $74-$82
    50-100: $66-$74
    We will also provide an additional service (about $10 for one keycap) for the engravings on the four sides around the keycap. We have many different engravings and we haven’t decided yet which ones we’re going to use. Persoanlly I so much want the flaming engraving.

Please post your words here if you’re interested in these pure silver keycaps.
One last thing, anyone interested in pure GOLD keycaps? It would cost about 10 times of the silver ones. If a silver one is $80, then a gold one will be $800.
Yes we might be crazy, but we’re not kidding.
If you consider youself as crazy as we are (even crazier), you might want to think about this.
The 11 designs above can be used for your custom orders of pure gold keycaps as well.
Thanks!
