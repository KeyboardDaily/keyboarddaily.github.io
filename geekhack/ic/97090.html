Introducing the TKC CandyBar. This Click-O-Stick is brought to you by TKC to satisfy the need for a compact board with full size features and an incredibly attractive price-point!
Honch0 here (Jason). Ill level with you. I like my arrow keys. I also like my numpad. This has kept me away from using my compact boards. I still collect them, but they are show-pieces more than battle station wares. I recently sold my Minivan to a really nice guy in Australia. I thought to myself, “why cant there be a 40 with a numpad? And arrows..” I drove straight home and drew up the keymap. I got u/blindassassin111 on the horn and said “LETS DO THIS!”. 3 weeks later, we have prototypes in hand and were testing. u/terrymathews is working on the firmware and we will have some solid numbers in the coming weeks. We plan to have a group buy going in October.
So heres the details. There are 2 versions and 2 kits. You can get either PCB with in either kit.
The PCBs

  • Kaihl hot swap sockets

  • LEFT AND RIGHT HAND VERSIONS AVAILABLE
    [/b]

  • Options for spacebar, 6.25u or split

  • MX compatible

  • /Both PCBs use QMK firmware, Mini-USB and will have all SMD components factory installed.

  • THE KITS:*

  • CANDYBAR STANDARD*

  • Switchplate, PCB, and bottom plate all made of PCB material

  • Includes switches, stabilizers, bolts, and standoffs

  • Price $85 depending on options


  • CANDYBAR PREMIUM*

  • CNC machined anodized 6063 aluminum in a variety of colors

  • Tray mount

  • Includes PCB of choice, plate, case, bolts, switches and stabs.

  • Price TBD

  • Renders by Oblotzky*

  • DSA KITS*

  • DSA PBT Dye Sub

  • Font will appear as in the renders from oblotzky, not the KLE mockups

  • Fits most 40% boards on the market

  • Available in 2 colorways

  • Price TBD

  • FAQ:*

  • Will I be able to buy just a PCB?*

  • Yes*

  • Can I get a kit with no switches?*

  • Yes*

  • Is there backlighting?*

  • We are considering a backlit strip under the PCB. There will be no in switch lighting on this PCB. Perhaps a future board will offer in-switch RGB but there are no official plans as of yet.*

  • Can you make one with the numpad on the left side?*

  • Were working on it but because the PCB is so tight, we have to start from scratch. Stay tuned.*

  • Can you guys stop decimating my bank account?*

  • Were trying. This one is going to be nice and affordable, so your wallet shouldn’t turn into meme fodder.*
