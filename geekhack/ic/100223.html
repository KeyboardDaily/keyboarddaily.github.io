Overview
Recently saw someone with a F62-PE IRL, CNC’d by ALF, and very bumped out that I didn’t get into this hobby sooner. Well, I got in touch with the guy behind ALF (Xuxian), and he told me he could make a batch of F62-PE, with an MOQ of 20, which would cost $200 just for the case EDIT: the sale price is completely the same as before, price and everything. Communication issue.
Details
Features are listed in original link above (minus details about PCB), and here I would add a few things that was told to me by Xuxian:

  • There were only three rounds of these keyboards, less than 50 of them in total
  • Top and bottom of case is equal in height
  • The case itself weight a freaking 4KG
  • The keyboard can be shipped in 60 days
  • Case has standard screw stems as other 60% and is a tray mount kb
  • If anyone is interested, I can work with a local Cerakoat vendor to get more colours than the grayscale. Won’t be cheap though.
    To share your interest for this GB, please comment below or fill out this form https://forms.gle/kusXH8SEunu8fFEb8
  • Who’s running this*
    I am a programmer, with two kids, situated in Hong Kong, and started using mechs for about 3 years now. Started to get serious this year and now settled on a 30g capacitative keyboard…for now. I realised that the coolest keyboards are all GBs and limited in quantity. So, I got in touch with some manufacturers and see if they can re-run some stuff. Anyways, if this run is successful I can probably do this part time and make pretty keyboards a bit more accessible. Cheers.
