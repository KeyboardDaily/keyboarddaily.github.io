Design inspiration
个性设计均来自于中国古典园林中的各种元素：
The design of Novelty kit comes from various elements in Chinese classical gardens
1.瓦当 Eaves tile
瓦当是中国古代建筑中屋檐最前端的一片圆形瓦片，带有各式花纹。
Eaves tile is a round tile with various patterns at the front end of the eaves in ancient Chinese architecture.
2.脊兽 Spinal Animal
脊兽是中国古代建筑屋脊上的装饰，题材主要是带有神话色彩的动物。
Spinal Animal is the decoration on the ridge of ancient Chinese architecture. The theme is mainly about animals with mythological color
3.花窗 Rose window
花窗是中国古代建筑对于窗户的装饰，兼具美感与实用性。
Rose window is the beautiful and practical decoration of windows in ancient Chinese architecture.
4.石拱桥 Stone arch bridge
石拱桥是中国传统桥梁四大基本形式之一。
Stone arch bridge is one of the four basic forms of Chinese traditional bridges
5.祥云纹 Auspicious cloud pattern
祥云纹象征祥瑞的云气
The auspicious cloud pattern expressed good luck, happiness and wishes for a better life.
6.狻猊
狻猊是中国古代神话中的神兽，是龙的九个儿子之一
The Suanni, one of the nine sons of the dragon, is a mythical beast in Chinese mythology.
7.亭 Pavilion
用于点缀园林景观的实用性小型建筑
Pavilion is a practical small building used to decorate garden landscape.
8.天坛 Temple of Heaven /祈年殿Hall of Prayer for Good Harvests
天坛是中国古代帝王祭祀祈祷的场所，祈年殿是天坛的主要建筑之一。
The Temple of Heaven is a place for ancient Chinese emperors to worship and pray. The Hall of Prayer for Good Harvests is one of the main buildings of the Temple of Heaven .
9.马头墙 Ma Tau Wall
马头墙是中国传统赣派建筑和徽派建筑的重要元素
The Ma Tau Wall is an important element of Chinese traditional Gan style architecture and Hui style architecture.
