Hi Everyone,
After IKKI68 OG is delivered in 2020, both our team and our users want to have a 75% board. So we started to design in late 2020. There are so many people who want to make 75% smaller, but we feel like our community deserves something new and special. We finally come to an idea for a heavy, sturdy, and elegant one - Mammoth 75. With this idea in mind, we discuss a lot with our focus team for every designs. From the top case to bottom, from the corners shape to the material we want, from the size of the knob to how much mirror PVD treatment we can do. And today, after almost 1 years effort, we can finally show you how it is now!

Mammoth 75 info
-Typing angle: 6.5 degrees.
-Front Height: ~20mm
-Weight (unbuilt): 2.5 kg
-Case material: Aluminum
-Mounting Method: Gasket Mount
PCB Specifications:
-Hot-swap PCB: 1.2mm thick, flex cuts.

  • Multi layout: supports split space bars, Left/Right shift split,backspace split, step caps lock
  • QMK / VIA support: Yes.
  • In-switch LEDs: No
  • Underglow: No
  • ESD Protection: Yes.
    Mammoth 75 Highlights

  • Full Mirror Bottom Case

  • Sandblasted Diamond Cut Mammoth Weight

  • Special Gasket Structure with Daughter board
    We make the plate poron foam the same size as the plate, and it will touch the entire bottom case to achieve a stable typing feel. We also use adhesive poron under PCB for a pleasant deeper sound.

  • Two Magnet Knobs
    We use a magnet base to make knob even more friendly hotswap. The kit comes with 2 knobs for your choice: One Aluminium knurled knob, one brass CD pattern knob.

  • The Case Design
    Thicker front bezel for a larger footprint, the gentle sloped case are reminiscent of a mammoth. But we further simplify its sides and back from IKKI68 OG (check how we change the prototypes here) and give it a silver decoration to make it look vigorous.
