About DSS Profile
Click MORE for more information
More

DSS was created by Signature Plastics in the 80s to meet the requirements of the (then) new DIN standard for keyboards. It has a spherical top and is lower profile than their SA profile. The top surface resembles DSA.
DSS Lightcycle uses a 1-1-2-3-4-3 sculpt.
To compare DSS to keycap profiles you know, check out Keycaps.info
Further Reading:
SPs DSS Spec Sheet
SPs History of Keycap Families
Note: As SP does not offer spacebars smaller than 6u in the DSS profile, DSA blanks are offered as an alternative in the base, 40s, and spacebar kits.
