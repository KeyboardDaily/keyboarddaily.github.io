My Story
About a year and a half into the COVID-19 restrictions and lockdown, I was weary of what had become my routine: watching Netflix every night after working  from home  and then going to bed. I yearned to be more passionate and creative. I wanted to make life during this sad and anxious time a little more beautiful.
People around the globe have been pursuing a favorite hobby or starting a new one, as for me, I recently picked up a knack for mechanical keyboards. Having something to put energy into besides scrolling sad content on my social feed, paved a way for me to express myself and release stress. It turned out that my project is a healthy diversion from a constant barrage of bad news.
Its been really fun building my own unique keyboard and experimenting on different mods. Wanting to create something that would represent my newly found hobby, I designed Keeb Anatomy deskmat to show appreciation to an underrated craft and how its helped enthusiasts and beginners alike to balance the stress brought by the pandemic.
