Hi guys,
It’s Enjoy here again bringing you guys some exciting news coming up! As the drop date for GMK Wavez is getting closer than ever, we are proud to announce some of the collaborations behind the scene with some other members of our community that Ive been working on.

This is an Interest Check for our new Sonic switches lineupto pair with your GMK Wavez keyset! The color of the stems and housings will be customized to fit with the main theme of Wavez. We are planning on doing only ONE round of these awesome Sonic switches, so get yours while you still can. We are considering two variants of Sonic switches offered in this buy  either tactile (KBDs T1 switches) or linear (gateron yellows). Depending on the outcome of the voting polls, we will decide whether we would like to move forward with either one, or both.
Disclaimer: I will not be making any profit on these switches. I decided to do this as a token of goodwill due to the overwhelmed amount of support the community has been giving me throughout the Interest Check phase of GMK Wavez. Thank you everyone! I am very grateful for everything. Please help me filling out the poll if you are interested. It really helps a ton!
