Hey there!
As this is my first set, I should introduce myself. I’m eskimojo, and have been in the community for a year or so.
This set is inspired by the character Wiz from Konosuba. In the anime, she is a well-meaning shopkeeper who happens to be undead.

Profile
I have chosen to design this set for KAT, as its silky smooth PBT surface combined with a comfortable and subtle sculpt makes it my favourite profile by far.
Additionally, they have the capability to do reverse dye sub, as seen in samples for KAT Eternal and KAT Oasis.
The MOQ system for KAT is also unique, and is done in such a way that allows for child kits that may not usually get enough support to be made. I’ve taken advantage of this by offering multiple international kits and colevrak support.
Colours
This set uses four Pantones as these are preferred for dye sublimation. However, I have backup RALs if those are required.
I have samples of these pantones in-hand, and am happy with them.

Google Form to register interest
Currently I have been updating my progress in Emir’s Discord server, as he kindly let me have a channel there. However, I may create other methods of communication (and this thread will be kept updated, of course).
