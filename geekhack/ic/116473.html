About cupping

More

Coffee cupping as known as cup tasting is how coffee is tasted by producers and buyers around the world to check the quality of a batch of coffee. Baristas usually use the same brewing method and cups for different coffees in cupping. Coffees are scored for aspects such as cleanness, sweetness, acidity, mouthfeel, and aftertaste.
