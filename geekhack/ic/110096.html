The Mercutio is a 40% through hole kit with a rotary encoder and OLED display. This will be another FR4 sandwich board similar to the Big Dill Extended. If youre not familiar, a sandwich board is composed of a top plate, the pcb, and bottom plate. The top and bottom plates are spaced apart using standoffs. The top plate functions as a typical keyboard plate and the switches hold up the pcb. This kit will be sold as a full through hole kit. We previously considered doing a Kailh hotswap version but we have decided against it for this round at least.
Sandwich case:

This our second board and we learned a lot of lessons from the BDE. One of the common points of feedback about the BDE was it didnt have enough keys and were hoping this new, larger 40% board will be more usable. We are also looking at offering more colors. Theyre the standard PCB colors (black, white, red, green, yellow, blue) but we would love to hear your preferences. We cant promise all the colors for the GB sale, but well provide as many as were capable of. Maybe well even let you guys mix and match fun color combos.
We chose a 40% because its a keyboard size that doesnt have a whole lot of options out there and what is out there isnt as readily available as wed like. Anyone can use a 60% or larger, but once you get down to the 40% boards, it takes a little more effort to get accustomed to the layout. Our main goal is to provide affordable options for less common sizes and layouts of boards so you dont have to drop mad stacks and wait forever just to try something out.
KLE:

Now, we could have kept it as a simple through hole kit but we wanted to add a little something extra for additional utility. The top right of the board will house an OLED and rotary encoder. Instead of having an acrylic guard going across the entire forehead of the board, the Mercutios acrylic guard will meet the plate in a smooth curve right before the OLED.
