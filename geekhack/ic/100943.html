Follow up IC:
Hello fellow mechanical keyboard enthusiasts,
This is a follow up to the original IC for cypher with a few more refined details as well as another form to help us gauge a few things (like colours and plate materials). This time I am also collecting emails to send a notification for the start of the group buy.
The price will be $335(295) ex vat + shipping (VAT charged by default for EU buyers, ROW will be your local taxes) (shipping inside Europe should be 10-20 and worldwide will be 30-50)
Key Specifications:

  • Sub 17mm height without bumpons (16.25mm). Bumpons add 1.3mm height still keeping the whole board under 18mm :-)

  • Top Mount (a few balanced points to create a nice even typing feel)6° typing angle

  • Supports ANSI/ISO, a few bottom rows for keycap compatibility and split or 2u backspace KLE of layouts

  • Poly-carbonate or aluminium plate (potential to change based on poll)

  • 3 Colours: to be chosen from a poll in the IC form

  • Type C
    Something requested a lot in the first IC was split space support and there will be a separate plate option to support this available.
    Plates will also be available for ANSI/ISO layouts that support all bottom rows and stepped/unstepped caps. It will also support all 1u numpad or 2u plus and enter. The ANSI plate will also support split backspace.
    The other changes requested included re-adding the logo from the original run to the base so this has returned.
    Group buy is planned to launch on Monday 8th July and will run for 4 weeks or until we hit the upper limit of 100 boards.
    For the second IC form please see here: Link to form
    We will be available to answer questions in the mykeyboard.eu discord serverand will reply to anything posted here or in the reddit thread
    Max (& MyKeyboard.eu)
