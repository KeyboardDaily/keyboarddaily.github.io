You are sitting at a picnic bench waiting for your deli sandwich to be delivered. It is a hot July day and youre enjoying a chilled bottle of pop. Then, suddenly you see your order come around. A plate with a wax-wrapped sandwich cut in half, glistening in the sun as its brought to you. Its placed on the table. A deli sandwich, a plate of fries, and a pickle spear right between. The dill and vinegar smell takes over, wafts of the deli sandwich with its fresh bread and accompanied fries join in. Its a cacophony of tingling delights. You grab the pickle spear, take a bite…. crunch……. Perfection. Now you can evoke this feeling every day at your desk without worrying about crumbs in your
board.
