Hi all!
After running a couple private group buys on Discord, I’m looking to do my first Interest Check on Geekhack! So hopefully this turns out okay
This IC is for the WRISTBREAKER TKL board that I have designed and protoyped.
The WRISTBREAKER is a high angle board which takes inspiration from several other kustoms such as the OTD 456GT, TX1800v2, Duck boards, TGR Jane, etc. This was at first just a fun little meme project that I didn’t think would go anywhere, but once I got the prototype I was really happy with how it turned out. Although not the heaviest board around, this thing is one hefty beast.
Pictures:







///More photos*: **https://imgur.com/a/4JqEs7t///*
Specs

  • 14° angle

  • 1.5mm brass plate (maybe stainless steel?)

  • 2x 8mm brass weights (maybe copper?)

  • 6063 aluminum - Black anodized

  • RGB underglow, two frosted acrylic diffusers on the sides

  • USB-C (with daughterboard) compatible with Hineybush h87a PCBs

  • USB port parallel to desk

  • Fixed ANSI WKL layout (will provide plate file)

  • Compatible with TGR Jane V2 Plates

  • 8.5lbs/3.85kg built
    Other technical info:

  • 18.8mm front height

  • 7.8mm bezel height

  • .5mm switch cutout fillets

  • 15mm top bezel, 10mm bottom bezel, 6mm side bezels

  • 19.05mm switch spacing

  • 9mm from top of plate to bottom case

  • 8x M3 10mm (case)

  • 7x M2.5 6mm (plate)

  • 4x M8 16mm countersink (weights)

  • 4x M2 4mm countersink (diffusers)
