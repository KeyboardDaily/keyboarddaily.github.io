IC FORM
Hi Everyone,
This is an interest check for a new deskmat called Fever Dreams. The deskmat depicts a vivid series of events in a comic like pattern. Each panel is a separate piece of art and can be viewed independently and in any order. Each of these designs has been drawn by the same artist and pieced together perfectly to create a deskmat we are extremely proud of.
The artist is a wonderful friend of ours who has a very unique art style. His instagram is : HERE.
SPECS

  • 900mm x 400mm x 4mm
    Currently the plan is to make two versions of deskmats. One in full colour and another in monochrome. These will both be made with polyester.
    There is also the potential that the mats will be made with Jacquard material, which is commonly used for “rough” deskmats and is a different fabric on top. The MOQ on this is a lot higher, but if there is sufficient interest - they may also be made.

VENDOR
This will be run as an in-stock deskmat by several vendors across the world.
US: Mechs & Co
CA: Ashkeebs
EU: Keygem
OCE: Mountain Keyboards
UK: Prototypist
Mexico: Rheset
TO DO

  • Confirm manufacturer - samples have been ordered from 2 other manufacturers - one uses recycled car tyre rubber which I am excited to use if it fits the part.
  • One sample of the deskmat made from the Jacquard material has also been ordered - pictures will be updated shortly
  • Adjust design based on interest discussion
  • Order production run
