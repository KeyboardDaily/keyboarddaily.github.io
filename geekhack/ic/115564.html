K8 Pro
by Keychron
Keychron
Discord

  • TKL layout

  • Wireless and Wired useable

  • Connects up to 3 devices via Bluetooth

  • Out of the box QMK & VIA support

  • 5Pin ANSI/ISO hotswap PCB [southfacing]

  • Added sound absorbing foam and silicone bottom pad

  • South facing LEDs

  • Physical switch to switch between layers **/Explanation down belown*

  • Two-level adjustable feet

    The Keychron K8 Pro is our newest addition that may revolutionize the way you use a mechanical keyboard. For the first time ever, you can enjoy both wireless connectivity and QMK/VIA customization capabilities on a Keychron keyboard right out of the box. The K8 Pro is designed to take productivity to the next level.
