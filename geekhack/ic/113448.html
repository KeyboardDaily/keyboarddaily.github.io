FLAME 60% IC From CLAP Studio
Good day to my fellow geekhackers, today I present you the Flame. This is a gasket mount designed keyboard supporting WK and WKL layouts.



Keyboard specs:
6063 aluminum
6.5° degree typing angle
PCB: QMK/VIA Compatible, Daughter Board Type C design
Gasket mount (poron gaskets)
3.5MM dampener between plate and PCB
1.5-2MM bottom case dampener
Keyboard layout


Keyboard parts:
Top, bottom case (aluminum anodized+e finishes)
Middle frame Brass+sand blasted finish
Bottom plate (brass+sand blast)
Plates (Aluminum, Fr4 supported full and half)
Badge (aluminum anodized) E-white or yellow ish-white is yet to decide due to the machining complications.
PCB Solder NO RGB
Weight and dimensions:
Width: 297.75MM or (11.7 inch)
Depth: 108.25MM or (4.2 inch)
Front height: 20MM or (0.7 inch)
Weight (without any kind of plate): 1.6KG or (3.5LBS)
Case finishes:
Anodized: Black, blue, wine red
E-white
E-yellow white ish or aka soya
Maybe more colors later depending on the prototypes and anodization.
Render colors might slightly differ from the final anodization.
