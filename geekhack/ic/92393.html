Although we have already ran an IC on reddit, we need to also run one on GH in order to make a GB post. Below is the cost and the schedule, although it might be pushed back a little to accommodate for the delay in getting up out GH GB page.
We will be offering the lubricant in quantities of 1.8ml units and 5ml quanities.
If you are unfamiliar with RO-59, it is a rare lubricant that is often hailed as one of the best possible lubricants for keyboards. While popular in Korea, it is rarely seen in the Western world. We spoke to the manufacturers to obtain the best lube specifically for keyboards.
