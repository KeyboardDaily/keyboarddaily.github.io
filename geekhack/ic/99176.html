It is that time to begin looking at getting Round 3 going. I am starting to work on forumating the new kits right now, essentially adding the keys to cover the new layouts that have gotten more popular since the last run. I will be probably be including all novelties from round 2, and certainly look into adding some new ones as well.
I’ve also been working on adding a few new colors and brand new kits to the mix, I hope to have those ready and rendered sooner than later to show and see what everyone thinks about them. I’d like this to be the biggest and most unique run thus far!
I would like to get feedback specifically on these points:

  • Ergo Kit - For ErgoDox/Atreus - Legends or no legends?

  • Ortho Kit - Legends or no legends?
