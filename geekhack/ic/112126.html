Inspiration

Hello everyone, I’m happy I can finally share with all of you JTK Monke
Inspired by everyone’s favorite little banana-lovers, this set not only captures the fundamental essence of Monke’s physical appearance, but also the spiritual yearning of Mankind to break free from the chains of modernity and adopt a more simplified state of being
Although the set has an overall light hearted design direction, I wanted to steer clear of some community gripes for sets of this nature (like face kits), and believe that my decision to run with JTK strikes a balance between having fun with the design and pragmatism
