I am very happy to work with PBTfans to make this set of keycaps. The keycaps are made of PBT material, Cherry profile, using doubleshot/tripleshot craftsmanship, and the characters are designed by the well-known designer Biip. I believe that the quality of the keycaps will not disappoint everyone.
Inspiration
The keycap is inspired by the character Rim (リム) in “迷宮ブラックカンパニー”, which is a very cute character. The color matching refers to the color of Rim’s hair and horns, and a color similar to ruby is chosen as an embellishment.
