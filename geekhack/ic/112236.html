-S161-

-Intro-
I am so happy to be apart of the mechanical keyboard community, so I wanted to give
to everyone by making my own case. I went with a 60% because that is the layout that I was dying to get back when I used to use a full-sized keyboard and my favorite between 60% and a 65%. I do plan for the case to be made in the USA so pricing won’t be pretty to you guys, but with an IC I’ll have data to bring down the price as much as possible. This is my first project after observing a few projects so reflection along the way through discord is appreciated.

ESD Protection, not HS
7 degree typing angle

Aluminum Plate
