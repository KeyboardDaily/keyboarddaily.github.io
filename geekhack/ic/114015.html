    About Angle - “Play the angles, Know the angles.”

    Good design is open to interpretation and perspective is everything. Applying this philosophy, Vertex wanted to design a keyboard that is full of intentions yet subtle and inclusive. Angle aims to capture the very essence of a typing machine with precision and simplicity.





    Form Factor
    As Vertexs founding project, TKL was chosen instead of other form factors. The meaning behind TKL/80% outweighs the potential structural design challenges.


    Angle supports both ANSI and ISO Layout. Both layouts have options for Tsangan/WKL and stepped. To minimize the impact on typing sound and potential loosen stabilizers/switch installation introduced by bigger openings on Left Shift and Enter keys, Angle will have ANSI and ISO as different options.
    Case Design

    Vertex believes in simplistic design and values ease of use above everything else.

    Right above the arrow keys, a moonstone is embedded. Moonstone is a sodium potassium aluminium silicate of the feldspar group that displays a pearly and opalescent schiller.

    The visual effect of a moonstone is very subtle and will only reveal from a particular angle. It is a symbol of Angle design. Because Vertex believes your typing experience should not be disturbed.

    Weight was arranged to be close to the lower edge in order to balance the angled bottom case. Angled bottom case made the lower edge thinner, which introduced challenges to add weight. This was overcomed by adding visual compensation on the bottom of weight with Vertex Logo on the lower right of weight.


    Design is in the details. Vertex was founded on 03/05/2020, 庚子惊蛰 in Chinese Solar Calendar, which is engraved on the inner weight.

    Side view is progressive and dynamic.

    Dynamic sculpture with harmonious proportions.

8 degrees, based on experiments and research, is the most ergonomic angle for typing on average.
Mounting Structure


Clicky yet comfortable, firm but gentle, is what you would feel when typing with Angles Leaf Spring Structure.

Special Foams are added to absorb the vibrations from the Leaf Spring and improve the stability of the keyboard while typing fast.


Silicon Pad is added to make PCB better fit in the space and more tightly aligned with surrounding parts. Silicon Pad also helps balance out the structural differences between central area and corners, so the typing experience is more consistent overall.

In order to provide uniform softness, Flex Cuts are introduced throughout the inner parts from Plates, Silicon Pad to PCB.

Custom designed PET-Coated Poron Foams are added to prevent damages from leaf spring overload.

More vertical space is reserved for leaf spring.
Overall, with all the trials and errors, Vertex and we are confident to give keyboard lovers a brand-new typing experience.
We will also showcase how Vertex Studio designed Angles Structure in a follow up post. Stay tuned!

    FAQ
  6. Is RGB supported?
    No. You can only add Leds on Caps Lock and Scroll Lock. Vertex believes that RGB lighting is not a good companion for Angle.
  7. Will there be choices of material for Plates?
    During the prototyping phase, Vertex had tested plates Aluminum, Brass, PC, POM, FR4 and Carbon Fiber. Only Aluminum and Brass met expectations.
  8. Hot-swap support?
    No. Because Angle is using Leaf Spring Structure. Hot-swap will not be able to provide enough structural support for holding PCB.
  9. How long will Angle production take?
    Depends on the amount of Group Buy orders we receive. We will provide weekly production updates after the Group Buy conclusion.
  10. Will there be more choices of colorway?
    Vertex may consider more colorways if manufacturing technologies for new colors meet quality and design expectations.
  11. Pricing?
    We haven’t set the international pricing yet. FYI, CN GB run’s pricing is 2850 CNY (441.04 USD) for Anodizing version (other colorways) and 2950 CNY (456.52 USD) for Electrophoresis version (Bianco). It might be slightly more expensive than CN GB, because of the price hike of raw material and yield rate of CN GB.
