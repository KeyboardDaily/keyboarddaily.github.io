Would like to see if anyone else is interested in a group buy for an Autism Awareness PBT dyesub cap. My 4 year old son is Autistic and I would like to spread awareness, and what better way to do it than through my new hobby?
Meiosis had an idea to open this up to other disorders / disabilities, and I think that’s a great idea. If you have other initiatives you would like to see caps for to raise awareness this will be somewhat of a charity benefit.
With Meiosis’ support we have met the MOQ for 50 caps. Let’s keep it going and everyone submit their ideas so we can make this a great charity related Group Buy!!
