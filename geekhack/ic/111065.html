Hello everyone, welcome to the IC for GMK Fuji.
GB March 15th - April 15th

Inspired by the colours and landscape surrounding the legendary mountain, this set brings a stylish look to your keyboard. The set features dark blue Latin and Katakana legends on white alphas, with lighter blue-grey mods. The pink of the accents resemble the iconic sakura. All colours come from the RAL design catalogue.
Special thanks to BK over the past months for helping me develop this idea.
Without further ado, here are the kits - all renders done by the talented pikku-allu.
Katakana Base:
Pretty straightforward. Provides the compatibility you need for most layouts, while keeping it as trimmed down as possible. Katakana sublegends are there as an homage to the inspiration of the set.

Latin Base:
Pretty straightforward. Provides the compatibility you need for most layouts, while keeping it as trimmed down as possible. For those that prefer a cleaner, sublegendless look.

Addition:
To cover TGR Alice-like layouts and some more niche layouts that weren’t in the base kit, as well as giving more legend options. Basic 40s compatibility also included.

Novelties:
Made in collaboration with Sour, to add a bit more personality to your board.

International:
Your standard kit for European layouts - support for British, Finnish, Swedish, Norwegian, Danish and German keyboards.

Sakura:
Accent spacebars, for an extra splash of colour.

Collaborations:
RAMA WORKS x GMK Fuji
Anodised aluminium with enamel infill. All colours will be matched to the approved GMK samples. Both icons designed by my talented friend Sour.
