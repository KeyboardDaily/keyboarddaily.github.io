September 5th Update:
Cases and PCB’s are under production right now. We were moderately successful. About 80 orders, so to the few of you thanks for supporting myself and my vision for this 40% If there is one good thing to take home from this, it’s the feeling of envisioning and producing and making an idea become a product and a reality. Anyways, a rough estimate on production end - I’m going to say end of september - please, accept this with a grain of salt. It could come sooner, or it could come later.
As far as GMK1983 goes, still grinding on it in my spare time. Have been learning how to do my own 3D modelling and renders so that I can produce an excellent info package and illustrations for the next and final IC. I have no eta on this set launching at this time.
Hey Geekhacker’s.
My apologies for not frequenting this place as much as I probably should. However, I am here and looking to make a change to that.
This is a pet project I’ve been working on since roughly February of this year, the Mechmini 2.
I’m sure most have heard of it - but I know there is some who haven’t, and for that here are the details.
-QMK
-RGB SMD Underglow
-Ortho Plate
-Staggered Plate
-CNC Milled & Anodized aluminum high profile case with polycarbonate base to diffuse RGB underglow
-PCB is drilled to accept both staggered and ortho layouts.
The kit will ship with
-PCB
-Cherry PCB Genuine stabs
-1x Ortho plate
-1x Staggered plate
-Case
-Hardware for assembly
Actual photo of the case and plate and pcb, all together as one. Words can’t describe how elated I am.

Case was designed by /u/hineybush - I would like to thank Josh for his exceptional work. Here are the renders.

At the same time as this - I have been furiously working on my 1983 set, in GMK. I would like to put that here as well.

Any questions comments concerns, as always my doors are open.
