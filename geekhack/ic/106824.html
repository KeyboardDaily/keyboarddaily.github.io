Hi All
I’ve been around the Mechanical Keyboard community for some time, and wanted to put my foot in the door, with so many interest checks and group buys by first time runners coming around recently I was inspired to start my own, meet GMK Simpsons, I’ve been a long time fan of Simpsons and it’s been a huge influence on my life, as I was born in the same year that simpsons started, this set pays homage to two of my favorite Simpsons characters, Mr Burns and Krustin the Clown, the yellow is the same skin colour as mr burns while the blue is the same blue as Krustin The clown.
I picked GMK to be the manufacturer as they have a reputation for producingthe highest quality keycaps, and the profile as DSA because DSA has always been a profile that interested me and I’ve been wanting to try it out.
At the moment I’m still trying to decide what addition sets I should be including, this is just the base, and if it should be done in PBT.
I’m also trying to learn how to render keycaps, unfortunately there were a couple of issues, one of the keys went blue and I can’t change it back and one is black and blue and I can’t figure out why, for now just imagine that the keys are the same as the keys around them and that is what it’s supposed to look like.

If any vendors are interested in running this please PM me, otherwise I’ll do it as a closed run through paypal.
