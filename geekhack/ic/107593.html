Here is the IC for my latest set, GMK βeta.
In collaboration with Krelbit of Switchmod.net and Oblotzky, we have been able to translate one of my favorite Betamax cassette tape sleeves into a keyset, using stock GMK colors.

The Colors
The colors used will be L9, 2B, RO2, and V2. The legends will be CR, which is black.

The Kits
Our kit’s aren’t 100% finalized. We will have either 1 base and 1 alpha/accent add-on kit, or 2 base kits. Please fill out the form below to express your interest in either.

DO YOU LIKE JUSTSYSTEMS HIPRO? SO DO WE!
PLUS KRELBIT IS A HUGE WEEB AND WILL ONLY SELL SETS WITH SOME SORT OF JAPANESE-BASED INSPIRATION.
SO HERE’S THAT. LOOKS PRETTY SICK.

Regarding split/extra spacebars, OG Spacekeys will be running in a few weeks on my site, so you can get L9 spacebars from that.
The Dates
The group buy will run from August 21, 2020 to September 14, 2020.
The Vendors
US - Switchmod.net
EU - Oblotzky Industries
Canada - Deskhero.ca
SEA - iLumKB
Oceania - Daily Clack
China/International - zFrontier
Last but not least, I leave you with some board renders, on the Kei and No. 2.


For updates and discussion, please check the keyset section on the Switchmod.net Discord Server: discord.switchmod.net
