Hello & Welcome. - The Group-Buy for “StormTrooper White” HB60% Cerakote was a success!
We have also been busily preparing to ship out the Copper, Bronze & Aluminum spacebars from the GB-R2 last month. A few weeks ago, we had more sample colors for the HB60% sprayed up by our Cerakote partner, and would like to check if there is enough interest to support another Group Buy.

Courtesy of “Guts” From our discord channel
Case Specifications:

  • Base and Lid - V2 - Glass bead blasted & Cerakote

  • Sandwich mount with O-rings

  • 4 degree typing angle

  • Honeycomb Pattern machine inside and on the back face.

  • Bumper Feet (clear)

  • High quality in-house machining - made in Canada

  • Price: $290.00 USD

  • No PCB - Not Included - Works well with DZ60 Solder & Hot Swap
