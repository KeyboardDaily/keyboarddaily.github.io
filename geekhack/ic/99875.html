Hey everyone,
Welcome to the interest check for GMK Vaporwave
This set is inspired by glitchy aesthetics and holographic anime propaganda.
You can follow the development of this keycap set either on this post or over at naturalpack.org, whichever is easier for you.
Please leave a comment with your thoughts and suggestions for this set.
Newest updates will be towards the top of the post.
About GMK Vaporwave
This set comes dipped in lavender and an indigo purple, topped with neon pink and bright aqua legends. Base set will be an “all-in-one” style, so no need to buy multiple kits.
GMK Vaporwave will be available over at NovelKeys, MyKeyboard.eu, Daily Clack & zFrontier.
