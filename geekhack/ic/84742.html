Hey everyone
After completing one set of 50 orders for R1 and having some interest via PM for R2, I have discussed this with Greatfirewall (who’s organizing the manufacturing in and shipping from China).
This time we’re able to offer RGB underside LEDs (so maybe choose some clear acrylic!) and carbon fiber plates. However besides the increased prices of the higher end parts, Greatfirewall did not account for some fees on his side in R1 so the base prices of the kits have to go up.
Looking to deliver another 50 units.
New pricing:
New pricing (all in USD):
Base kit (with either HHKB or Normal plate): $99. Note, for R1 the HHKB kit was always additional on the base kit with normal plate, this is no longer the case. You can purchase EITHER the HHKB or Normal 40% top plate in the base kit.
Additional metal plate (HHKB or Normal): $20
Upgrade to black carbon fibre for the base kit: $25
Upgrade from black carbon fibre to other color (requires the upgrade to black carbon fibre): $5
Add another carbon fibre plate in any color or style: $30
Additional acrylic mid section (any color): $18
RGB Leds: FREE!
NOTE: Last time shipping from China was billed separately but now it’s included in the base kit price.
PRICES DO NOT INCLUDE PAYPAL FEES TO ME. 1.04x is the rate to use when calculating your own kit total. Prices do however include all fees to Greatfirewall (who’s using paypal which costs another 4%).
Since it took around 5 months last time from placing the order to shipping from North America, I do not want to list parcel pricing and will collect this when I receive the order from China. In R1 I collected this upfront and parcel prices increased a bit but I had enough to cover this (international pricing changes a lot more often). To international customers: you are responsible for your own import taxes based on the //value declared//. Insured or uninsured options are available to USA / International. Insured tends to be a lot cheaper but again, I’ll send out a survey after the parcels have shipped from China to determine shipping.
Example order: Base kit, in gold carbon.
Total before shipping from North America: $85+$30+$6, $121. $121 x 1.04 = $125.84

Now this seems very similar to the AMJ40 right? Here’s a statement from Greatfirewall on the matter:
Quote They copy almost all of the 40% layout include golbat and plank.
They made amj60 by copy gh60chn, made amj mini by copy kmac mini, made amj40 by copy other 40% kit,made amjpad by copy ghpad, they even copy a tkg-toolkit fireware tool from tkg.io.
All their products are copy others idea.
So if you don’t care about supporting designers you can get a similar kit for cheaper but otherwise for not too much more you can get the authentic product.
