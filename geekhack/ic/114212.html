Hello,
This is Kevin Lim from KLC.
Today, I drafted the next Geekhack IC for the Monte Bianco, a TKL keyboard designed by Syryan.
Syryan is a designer who has been in this field since the early 2010s. He introduced the EM series (e.g. the EM-7) and participated with Lin in a project to design the Dolphin.
Now, he is ready to say hello to the keyboard world with his new TKL project, Monte Bianco

Monte Bianco offers 3 colors
Render Image



Proto Sample Shot




Monte Bianco offers a double gasket-mounting method, for which individuals can choose their preferred hardness for the gaskets.



Also, we will also include switch dampeners in the package.
Mid switch dampener  goes between switch housings.
Lower switch dampener  goes under the switch bottom housing.
Both of which you can choose to apply for extra dampening and a different typing experience.

     Materials: 6061 Aluminum Case, Brass Weight
     Weight (approx.): Unbuilt, about 2.5kg
     Typing Angle: 9 degrees
     Front Height: ~19.8mm
     Plates: Aluminum, PC, Brass
     Price: USD 530
     Units: Unlimited
