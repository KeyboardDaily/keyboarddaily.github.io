Thoughts on MilkyWay
I understand that there has been a huge influx of sets under this new manufacturer. They have reached out to me and provided a sample of their keycaps and here are my thoughts:

  • Reverse dye-sub is done well, surprisingly

  • Legending is sharp

  • CHALKY (definitely one of the more chalky PBT in terms of feel)

  • Service-wise: VERY QUICK and responsive

  • Not a fan of the legends they used for previous sets, a possible US vendor is already receiving new samples of updated legends

  • SLIGHT issue with one key of a prototype sample, but have been reassured it is already fixed

  • Possible vendor will receive samples as well as me to ensure the quality is up-to-part for a group buy

  • Possible vendor’s new sample should improve thickness, line consistency, and better font

  • I WAS TOLD, THERE WILL ONLY BE 3-4 SETS RUNNING AT ONCE WITH A 3 MONTH LEAD TIME
    For more information, here is their discord: https://discord.gg/ecxzfnue5P
    Thoughts on Kitting

  • I believe a newer manufacturer should focus on less kits that appeal to the mass to get their names out there earlier

  • With the demand of 40s, orthos, and international language kitting not doing exceptional lately under GMK, running them under a new and baby manufacturer will not be considered

  • Makes it easier and simpler for the manufacturer to succeed

  • Reasons for Delay:*
    [Not a bad delay, don’t worry!]
    Main vendor and I want to receive samples in hand prior to GB date start to give reassurance to customers on the product/exact colors they will be receiving when they order
    The samples are made, but due to shipment times, we have decided to push the GB date back a tad after the holidays to make sure we can get them in hand
