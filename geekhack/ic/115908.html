High65

Hello everyone,
We are TD studio, and this is our 2nd board that we would like to share with you
High 65
First of all, we are greatly appreciated for your support on the Hi-finger 7.5(Extreme75) 300 units were sold out, which was far beyond our expectations and made us greatly encouraged. However, a number of people dm me that “this board is gorgeous, but I can’t get use to this layout.” Thus, we are ready to launch a new keyboard more common layout.

Inspiration：
The original intention of designing this board is that I really like the layout of the Filco FC660m.The layout is really comfortable, but I wanted a different typing feeling. Therefore, we try to make this kit variable, which means this board can meet different users, and you can experience three different mounts on it.
Specifications：
Case material: 6063 Aluminum
Typing angle: 6.5°
Mount: top/gasket/ Tray&leaf-spring
Colors: Black 3c,Gray 10c,Blue 654c,Off-white
Plate：
Top mount plate (6063 Aluminum)
Gasket mount plate(PC/POM)
Tray&leaf-spring mount plate (PC/POM)
Sale method:
Quantity：
Price：
Date：

Mounts:
1.Top mount:A traditional structure. Through excellent consistency, top mount is solid and stiff for those who want a more fuller sound.
2. Gasket mount: The most popular structure nowadays wont be absent. Gasket mount has an outstanding sound and soft touch. We will be integrating a similar design from Hi-finger75.
3.Tray&leaf-spring mount: The traditional tray mount may have some shortcomings, but in High-65, we creatively cut leaf springs at the screw holes on the PCB (refer to the PCB diagram) to make sure that the overall typing experience is comfortable, and do our best to achieve a balance between consistency and soft elasticity.

About PCB:

About appearance：
Our layout is very close to FC660m, but some details have been remodified in the bottom row.
We specially place the conventional caps indicator light on the left border for separate display and aesthetic, btw its more obvious to be noticed when typing.
