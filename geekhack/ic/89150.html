Hi Everyone, I’m here to introducing you The Peach by Revotech.
So the idea is a minimalist aluminium full size keyboard machining to perfection, these images post above is a un-finalized render but only small details will be changed, just tell me what you want to change. And here’s the process right now:

  • Making prototype: the prototype keyboard will ready in 2 or 3 days.
  • Making PCB
  • There will be a lot of colors option for anodizing from the manufacturer but maybe we will make it 4 or 5.
    They will come with few options:
  • Standard kit: Case and Aluminium plate.
  • Full kit: Case, plate and PCB.
    We are working on the price but it will be around 350$ for the case.

FAQs:
1. Who’s Revotech and why Triviumtek involved in this project?

  • Revotech is a new team in Vietnam, the team was founded by a group of Vietnamese pro-gamer and have a quite close relationship with Triviumtek. So The Peach will be manufactured in the same facility that making Triviumtek products and the GB will be hosted by us.

2. Do you have a PCB yet?

  • Yes, the PCB was developed and currently in testing phase now.

3. What would the MOQ be?

  • 10 and we’re good to go. But if we can reach 20, it will be easier for your wallet
