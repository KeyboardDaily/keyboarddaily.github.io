【IC】GMK RGBK Mod Pack - GB to start on Friday 7|17!

Hey there. So Ghostjuggernaught and I are thinking about running a RGBK mods group buy together. We are thinking about making this a simple no bull//// or frills set that will be comprehensive and offer as much compatibility as possible.
Here is a list of the keys we have in the set. Ghost and I have decided to just simplify things for now and we are going to get a mock up of just these keys. There are 24 keys in total, 8 control keys, 12 alt/alt gr keys, 7 shift keys, and 1 function key. Depending on the quote from GMK we may or may not add extra 1u blanks/window keys.

We will be using V4 (Blue - Alt keys), V1 (Red - Control keys), and N7 (Green - Shift keys and Function key) with CR for the Black text.
The interest check phase is planned to go until July 16th but is flexible depending on how quick communication between us and GMK is. The ordering period will be a month and is scheduled to start July 17th the day after the Interest check ends. The payment deadline will be approximately a month later around August 18th. We expect production to take 2-3 months. Then after that we will take between 2 and 4 weeks to ship out all orders. This is our skeleton schedule and nothing is set in stone.
When the group buy starts, we will have a google document to input orders. Orders will be invoiced at the end of the buy period. We would prefer you pay the invoice as soon as you receive it, but may give a one day buffer. Unpaid orders at the deadline will be dropped.
Shipping is as follows, all CONUS orders will be shipped via USPS flat rate. The number of sets you order will determine if it is a small ($6) or medium ($12) flat rate box. International orders will ship out only after ALL CONUS orders are fulfilled. Ghostjuggernaught and I have already looked into some sturdy corrugated cardboard boxes for international shipping. Shipping will also depend on the number of sets ordered since weight is the determining factor. International shipping should be around $15, but that is only a rough estimate.
