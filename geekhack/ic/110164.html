Hello all!
Welcome to the Interest Check for the Design03! Arctangent back with an adaption to my previous release of a sculpted stacked acrylic case, the Design02 (65%).
General Information:

  • Materials: Frosted/Matte Stacked Acrylic, 3mm thick layers, colors part of IC form
  • Mounting Method: Sandwich
  • Plate: 1.5mm thick, options/finishes part of IC form
  • Compatible PCBs: ProjectKeyboards, Switch Couture X Mechlovin Adelais, SneakBox Alice, and other similar
  • Typing angle: 6.5 degrees
  • Mounts: 1/4” Diameter solid brass standoffs, low profile crews for top mounts, standard profile for bottom mounts.
  • Weight built: 3.5 lbs / 1.6 kg
  • Pricing:*
  • Plates (Metals)*
    Vibration (Random Brushed) = -$5.00
    Sandblasted = +$0.00
    Brushed (Horizontal) = +$5.00
  • Add-ons*
    PCB: Expected to be +$50.00
    Plate Foam: +$15.00
