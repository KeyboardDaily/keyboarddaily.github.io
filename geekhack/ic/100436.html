First day (24-hours) group buy backer will be eligible for a First Love Keypora raffle by AlphaKeycaps. Yohans and I are working on the final mockup. Should be here anytime soon.
Much love, and see you all at launch <2

  • Mockup of Keypora by AlphaKeycaps*: Final draft is currently in the work. Should be here anytime soon

  • 6/28/2019*: Update deskmat and banderole box art (front and back).

  • 6/26/2019*: The following updates were made. Huge thanks to biip for all the suggestions on kit revision.

  • Revise novelties kit. Adding additional 1u R1 novelties and text novelties. Duplicate thorns novelties to 1.25U for consistency.

  • Change text + icon mods to icon mods + text mods bottom row. Update renders.

  • Change 1U R4 “Super” to “Code”. I think “Code” just looks nice and more balance given the small footprint of the 1U key.

  • Changing “Num Lock” to “Num” for 1 line consistency with icon mods.

  • Adding 1U R1 Fn and 1U R1 Code to base kit. This should cover split backspace TKL user and F13 key for TGR-Jane v2 CE.

  • Adding extra B to base kit. This should cover TGR-Alice user.

  • Revise on colors. I’ve decided to not go with the stock black CR from GMK and used a custom Pantone color instead. The stock CR is just too black to my liking and does not give off that muted effect that I was hoping to achieve. Physical color swatches already ordered. Update rendered color swatches.

  • Update on artisan collaboration. I am happy to announce that AlphaKeycaps and Rama will be collaborating on this project.

  • Update on target launch date and vendor. The set will launch on September at Canonkeys.com. Other proxy still TBD.

  • 4/28/2019*: Added poll for suggestion.

  • About*
    The inspiration of this set was from, well, my first love. I’ve known her since we were five, went to the same school and grew up together. We were crazy about each other at one point during high school, so we started dating. Senior year, college came around the corner and it just did not work out. I remember it was bittersweet, fun and heartbroken at the same time. So much time, so many memories and so many relationships have passed, but I still think of her from time to time. Her favorite color has always been purple, and for so long despite my original indifference to that color, it has become a part of me now. I hope this set will remind you of all the purest feelings and emotions you’ve ever had in your life! And yes, that include those nights getting wasted with your best buddy in the back of an old bar that smells like wet clothing with a big bottle of scotch trying to get over that one person you fall madly in love with!

  • Design*
    The main color theme of the set will be dark purple on black, with a tint of lilac purple. The set will use custom color on all three colors. The novelties pack was designed by our man biip. The aesthestic of his iconic approach is just so clean and sharp. The man is such a blessing to our community!
