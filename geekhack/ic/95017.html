Hi guys, ALF will be teaming up with zFrontier to bring you the ALF X3 Renova, a modern custom keyboard with a retro take.
Thick bezels, hefty blocks, oversized - this is the ALF X3. The blocky design is reminiscent of the classy and sexy look of the old keyboards from the past.
Features:

  1. Seamless design: the bottom housing is covered by the upper housing, no seams to be found on 4 sides;
  2. Two-piece top housing: the top housing features a two-piece design to introduce more colors and bring more flair to the classy look
  3. Low-key side brass inserts
  4. Multi-hex bottom weigh: hexagons are the most intriguing shape of the nature. Honeycombs, Tortoiseshell, Snowflakes, Cells, they all share this shape. And the straight line is the link to all hexagons, to all natural worlds. It is connecting everything just like the X3 is connecting Retro design to a modern keyboard.
  5. K-mini layout: 65% main body + 10 macros to the left
  6. RGB light strip
