Arch IC Layout Cloned?
Cipher=form of physical non-digital “encryption” therefore Ram can be lazy and reuse
The kitting is too barebones, why is this?
This stems from the alpha molds adding a sizeable amount to cost which means I can’t add way too many keys if I want to keep pricing reasonable.
What da Big Board
Join my discord, waitlist is almost full.
There’s no novelties?
I had a hard time finding novelty designs I would be happy with and as such decided to not include it,
I’ve left a spot in the IC form so that if you guys have designs you want to see we can possibly get a kit made!
What’s going on with the Mono legends being mixed?
Some keys such as ` , . just didn’t look good in mono so I chose to mix between the 2, there’s a spot in the IC form to make a definitive decision on whether we keep this or not.
