Hello! This is Trenton from Yoretech, and this is our first case and IC!

Many types of designer and custom cases like to add brass to the bottom for decoration and a bit of extra weight. I had an idea to make that material more prominent in the design, and clearly visible from the sides, front, and back. These thin bars provide a nice solid weight to the keyboard, and a bright color that can be used in synergy with all sorts of palettes and designs beyond the red shown here.

Each support bar also features three rubber support strips that provide a solid grip to whatever surface you set your board on, and equally distribute the weight of the board so that even with the extra weight, it poses no threat to the surface it’s placed on.

Thank you again for reading! As mentioned in the subject head, this is a standard 65% pcb compatible tray mounted case, and comes in a whole range of colors. We would love to put this case in front of you guys as soon as possible!
