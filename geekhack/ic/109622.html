Colors
The colors are all custom RAL colors, the red is a deeper red than anything GMK offers for stock colors, it’s definitely in between RO1 and RO2.
The white is a more pure white somewhere in between WS1 and WS2.
The dark grey is a color thats right in between CR and N9 its not as dark as CR and not as light as N9! It’s a very charcoal colored grey.
