  • supports various layouts (99pcs)

Renders


Prototypes


To achieve a purer and brighter texture, we used the push-plate method to develop the mold. This customized solution combines the advantages of both types of molding methods: active and passive force ejection. This allows us to eliminate thimble marks (visible when using ordinary molds) and also improves the finishing of the clean texture.
