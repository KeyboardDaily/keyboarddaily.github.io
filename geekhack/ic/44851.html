【IC】Custom MX|ALPS mini Keyboard (07-16 Update)

June 17, 2013 IC, 键盘 本文总阅读量次

类型: IC
作者: calavera
发布时间: 2013-06-17 05:33:01
更新时间: 2013-08-29 11:36:09
原链接: https://geekhack.org/index.php?topic=44851.0

Since there doesn’t seem to be enough interest for PCB/plate only, I’ve talked to GON and made a complete kit an option.
Note that to build this board (ALPS or MX) you’ll need to source your own switches and key caps.
PCB Specs:

  • Alps/MX hybrid - solder holes for both ALPS and MX

  • PS/2 NKRO (USB may or may not be an option)

  • Full LED support (full on/off, light up on press, light off on press, fading aka “breathing”..etc)

  • Dual bottom row: Winkeyless/* (1.5 mods and 7 space bar) or Winkey (1.25 mods and 6.25 space bar)

  • SMD soldering may be required
    Compatible cases:

  • LZ MX mini/SE aluminium

  • GON’s NS MX-mini case

  • GON’s NS Alps-mini case (if MOQ is reached)
    /For reference, here is a winkeyless layout:
