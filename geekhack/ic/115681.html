[IC] Krush65 PCB &RE6.5 by Nuxroskb
Features and the reason behind our product:
Today’s ever-expanding custom keyboard market offers a variety of budget options. This includes the KBD67 Lite, which is a really decent keyboard at its price point. However, it has yet shown its full potential currently for the following reasons:

  • Offer a rather stiff typing experience though being a gasket-mount board
  • Lack contemporary features such as flex-cut or rotary encoder
  • —->Therefore, Krush65 PCB aims to fully address these issues.

Krush65 PCB will feature the following:

  • Support for daughterboard
  • USB Type-C
  • Rotary encoder
  • Underglow RGB
  • 4 flex-cuts
  • Keymapping through VIA/QMK
  • Hotswap/solderable versions

Layout supported:
6.25u
7u
Split space

**Changes that the Krush65 PCB combo brings to improve the KBD67 Lite:
**- Complete redesign of the plate so the flex no longer centered in the alpha region, it can instead be felt on every spot of the keyboard

  • Swapping silicon to poron gasket
  • —> The combination of plate+PCB+Poron gasket will bring your KBD67 Lite to a whole new level.
