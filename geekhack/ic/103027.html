    Hey everyone! Over the past couple of months, I’ve been working on a fun, lighthearted set, and it’s finally time to see what others think about it.
    I’d like to present GMK Sloth!
    IC Form

    The inspiration comes from the animal that everyone knows and loves, the sloth. Sloths are known for their slowness due to their low metabolism and deliberate slow movements to compensate for it. The color of their fur is either brown, tan, white, or gray depending on their species, and at times, their coat can sometimes host a symbiotic green algae to camouflage them in the trees. They primarily eat leaves and climb up branches to stay off the ground to avoid predators. Kristen Bell also loves them.
    Lazy AF
    The “Lazy AF” kit is for those who have a common 60%, 65%, 75%, TKL, and 1800 layout. It also includes the F13 key for those who need it.

    Baby Sloth
    The “Baby Sloth” kit (40s) is for those who like smaller or more niche layouts.

    Branches
    The “Branches” kit includes 6u spacebars and accent spacebars.
