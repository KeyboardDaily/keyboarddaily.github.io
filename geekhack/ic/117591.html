Hello everyone! The Chieftain 42 is a keyboard case for the Corne keyboard, essentially the most popular custom split keyboard out there. This case was designed to be a hefty, luxurious, and elegant case that I hope peaks your interest! It also includes an optional tenting kit, and if you choose to forgo the kit, the mounting is discretely hidden away.
About me
Hi! I’m Ryan from Southern California and u/TimberForge on reddit. I actually started my journey in mechanical keyboards with the Corne first, which I learned about through r/mk on reddit. (First if you discount Razer keyboards haha) I’m an incoming engineering student at UCLA who likes to make random stuff in his free time and share it with others. This is my first group buy, but I’m confident I can make this work. I’m of course willing to spend lots of time on making sure that the end product is a great one.
Specifications
This is a CASE and PLATE set ONLY
Case Material: 6061 Aluminum, sand blasted & anodized
Plate Material: 5052 Aluminum, sand blasted & anodized, 1.5mm
Case Height: 21mm
Mount: O-Ring Sandwich Mount
Weight: ≈250g/0.55lb per half
Tenting Kit Optional
Price: TBD, ≈ $185?
Sound test coming soon. Join discord for updates ;) (On vacation rn)
Design Philosophy
There have been many Corne cases that go for a minimal profile, or cases that try striking a balance between a premium feel and a travel-friendly case. Instead of trying to be a jack-of-all-trades, master-of-none, this case instead goes all out into the hefty and premium route. I’m thinking that if we’re gonna to be spending a bunch of money for different keyboards, we might as well make them have very distinct features :P
Chieftain 42?
The Chieftain is a popular British tank, and the sloped sides and relatively high weight are shared characteristics of the tank and this keyboard case design. Plus it sounds cool ¯_(ツ)_/¯ The 42 of course is the number of keys on a corne. wow so creative xD
