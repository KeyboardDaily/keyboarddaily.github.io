PWade3 Productions presents:
DSS CMYPWade

“CMYPWade” is a name that’s been in my head for a long time. I first fell in love with the combo of cyan, magenta, yellow, and purple with Omniclectic’s CMYKeydelic sale. That love (and my CMYP collection) grew and I was eventually honored with the CMYPWade III Grimace by GAF. It’s a name I’ve wanted to turn into a keyset ever since and finally, inspiration struck. I’m proud to unveil DSS CMYPWade and by extension: the official launch of PWade3 Productions. As some of you may know, I’ve been designing keysets since 2018 with the launch of GMK Taro. I’ve been kicking around the idea of launching my own shop for a while and this felt like the perfect set to do it.
On with the show…
Kits

A large base kit featuring support for: TKL, 65%, 60%, basic 40%, ISO, and more!
I’ve also included a set of accent F-keys and arrows for those who want a little bit of pop and neutral alternates for those who want a slightly more subdued look.

An accent kit that emphasizes the chosen color of each row for that extra bit of pop.

A more “classic” modifier kit, one that both works with DSS CMYPWade but is also intended to share the love of CMYP with other sets such as DSS White on Black.

Need to fill out a fullsize, 1800 or just a standalone numpad? This is the kit for you.

Got a layout that requires some more niche spacebars? This will get you where you need to be.
Vendors
As announced above, I’m officially launching my own store, so I’ll be the US vendor for this set!
