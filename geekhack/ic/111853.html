Hello everyone!
Yasin Tahir here, some of you maybe know me as a YAS62 GB runner, some as a Y-CP Custom Sleeve GB Runner, and some not know me. Today I want to introduce you to my newest project, it’s Artisan Box and I called it “Historia”. I believe some of you who collect artisans had some history behind artisans you own. This box has a badge that attached using magnets and can act as feet so you can display it flat or angled, and will come with a sleeve to protect it during travel.
Details :

  • Aluminium 6061 Material
  • Sandblasting Finish
  • Multicolors Anodize
  • Stainless Steel Badge
  • Acrylic cover lid

Estimated price max $150 not include shipping (it’s not final also all production is in Indonesia so the price will be higher rather than I make it in China but I want full control on QC so I choose to make it here.)
I already made some prototypes for this artisan box and I quite happy with the result I get, just need some minor change and I made this IC to gather some visions from you to make this box perfect for everyone.
What needs to change on this recent proto? :

  1. Add feet bump on
  2. Fix The hole for the feet since it’s too loose for now and make the box not stable enough when use badge as a feet
  3. Maximum capacity of the artisans?

What do I need from this IC? :

  1. Colors options for the box
  2. Maximum capacity for the box
  3. Your feedback/suggestions
