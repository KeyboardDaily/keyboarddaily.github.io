GRAVIKEYS DESKMAT
Hello everyone! This is Pandalyssa from Zion Studios PH. I’m an Illustrator with penchant for cute stuffs so I incorporate my kawaii aesthetic art style in these deskmats. I created Gravikeys as a way of contributing in this community especially for my fellow mechanical keyboard enthusiasts. Your comments and suggestions will be very much appreciated.
Yukey, a space cutie keyboard enthusiast explores the vast expanse of keyboard universe. Her mission is to gather different colors of Gravikeys to complete her perfect build. Join Yukey’s sweet adventure as she traverse every sugary space of Gravikeys. Who can resist not wanting one of these adorable deskmats to brighten up your setup? Enjoy these sweet treats inspired color options that will perfectly match your keysets.
I decided to revise Gravikeys because I realized that I need to add more details & new colorways. I added more sparkles around, added stabilizers at the back of the astronaut and I modified the satellite too. I hope you guys love the new version of Gravikeys & participate the IC the second time around.
