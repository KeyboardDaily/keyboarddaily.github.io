Okay, so I know that ePBT was an extremely popular request when I initially launched the IC for GMK Fro.Yo.
After contacting KBDFans for inquiry on the possibility of ePBT manufacturing Fro.Yo, things looked like they could go that direction!
I received MOQ /# from Wei and we discussed the colors and Set Arrangements. But the main thing that kept us from going ePBT was there lack of Molds for the 40s Kit I have.
Extended 2048 had the similar issue of not being able to manufacture the initial 40s Kit Biip had arranged simply because of there lack of tooling to do so.
Specifically, they do not have molds for Row 3: 1.25u And 1.5u Keys.
Some may say this is a small detail but as an avid 40s user, I didnt want to do this Kit Halfway.
I asked Wei what kind of order quantities we would have to have to fund those 2 new key molds for ePBT, in hopes that it could at least be a structured possibility.
But after that inquiry Wei has gone silent on me and I dont think they are interested in manufacturing the set any longer.
Thats just the way it goes sometimes.
Maybe one day my friends!
