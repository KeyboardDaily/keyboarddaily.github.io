Our philosophy
Today we bring you the Daze65 65% gasket mounted keyboard. Daze means (noun) a state of stunned confusion or bewilderment. It is inspired by Japan, Japanese culture and oriental culture in general. We feel there is lacking amount of true Japanese beauty represented in this space so we bring you this keyboard today.
The layout of the Daze combines the functionality of a full size keyboard with the ergonomics and compactness of a 65% form factor. It is built to perform. The numpad Enter, Plus and 0 bring the benefit of having a numpad to a compact form factor while many dedicated layer keys can be used to simulate numpad number input. This increases user performance and productivity by up to 300%.

Innovation
We are expanding on the popular gasket mount design to bring you the most pleasant bouncy and soft typing experience
along with carefully thought out flex cuts. We are using Poron® gasket foam material for isolation to bring the most comfortable keystrokes.
The kit will come with 2 different case foams, PCB PE foam and plate foam for maximum acoustical pleasure and deep sound (thock) tuning.
For ease of assembly and to achieve the best aesthetic design while hiding the screws on the back case we have decided
to connect the top and bottom case using MAGNETS. We dont know how they work but we are confident that this is the best solution for this issue.
The back of the keyboard and sides are inspired by the great wave of Kanagawa. We can see beautiful curves which are formed on the back.
Our goals are to revolutionize the backsides of keyboards with intricate detail for most pleasant aesthetic experience.
The bottom is inspired by CW88 keyboard by Cruel World.
