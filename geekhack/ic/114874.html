Why did I choose Milkyway?
With longer wait times for manufacturers such as GMK,
which seem to increase every year, the time for change is desperately needed.
Milkway is, in my opinion, is one of the better suppliers at the current time-
with low lead times, affordable pricing and high standards.
Since they are relatively new, I know there will be scepticism on the validity of the claims
but I have heard nothing but appraisal for their previous sets - PBT [02/015] to be specific.
I firmly trust that they can deliver this set to a high grade and if you are still unsure,
please comment and I will do my best to answer any questions or concerns.
