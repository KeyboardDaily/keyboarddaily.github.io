Welcome back to SA Sunday Morning. It’s been a little while since this IC has seen any movement.
I’d been gone with another project and performing render services for this community but I thought I would take a break from that and put some time into this project.
Let’s see if we can bring this back from the dead.
For The Newcomers
SA Sunday Morning is a keycap set based on the color scheme of one of my favorite vinyl that I own and is named after one of the most significant songs in the album.

Announcements
Group Buy has been extended to January 16!
Hey there everyone, this set needs a bit of help. Due to the nature of SA it sure it can be a bit expensive. Intertwined with the holiday spending, I understand that not everyone may have been able to make financial room in December. With permission from the vendors, SA Sunday Morning is being extended 2 weeks until January 16.
