Hi guys, I’m catweewee. This is a little project I have been working on from July 2016 from the design stage until September when it started manufacturing. It is also the very first keyboard that I’ve designed and of course with much appreciated help from Yuktsi TGR, Juahenza and Pwner ION
THIS IS AN IC FOR R2
Now, some details about the SINGA and the concept.
SINGA is Lion in the malay language, which is where I’m from. Regal and magestic. Something what Id like SINGA to be

SINGA R1
More

Specifications:
Full Album: https://imgur.com/a/Nks3Z

  • The SINGA comes with a dual brass weight for heft. The LION brass weight is connected to an inner brass bar inside
  • Custom SINGA PCB by TGR (Winkeyless Bootmapper software)
  • Plates come in either brass or aluminium. Plates are all CNCed instead of laser cut, which makes it sturdier and gives a much cleaner cut
  • 8 Degree Angle
  • Approximately 2 KG weight without caps
  • Each individual SINGA is hand polished to ensure a better quality anode.

  • Layouts:*

SINGA R2
SINGA R2 will differ from R1 with the following:

  • SS Lion Weight, SS inner weight, Brass plates and Alu plates in the new colours

  • 6 colours: Burnt Orange, Sailor Navy, Champagne Gold, Jet Purple, Velvet (from r1) and Black (from r1)

  • WK and WKL options.

  • Limited to 55 units, FCFS Store Purchase. Once all 55 slots are taken and paid for, ordering will end and production will start.
