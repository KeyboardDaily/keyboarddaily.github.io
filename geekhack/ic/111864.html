-Group Buy for Milkyway Demon Girl is here-
An anime themed set inspired by a demon girl who fights for the survival of humankind.
-GB Dates: 10.04.21 to 11.05.21-
-Pricing-
North America
$90 USD - Demon Core (Base)
$28 USD - Demonic Transformation (Novelties)
$20 USD - Size Manipulation (Expansion)
$38 USD - Asanoha Metal Artisan
$20 USD - Deskmat (Pink or White)

-Colors-
196U | 4073U | Black 5U | 4206U | GMK WS1

-Kits-
This base kit has compatibility for
full-size, TKL, 75%, 65%, 60% and 40% boards along with
support for F13 boards and Alice layouts.

Novelties kit features adorably ferocious designs
made by Little Tokyo Keebs.

Expansion kit adds support for additional layouts,
and includes a set of bamboo green accent keys.

-Artisan-
Asanoha Metal Artisan by Bolsa Supply

-Deskmats-
Thank you for all those that voted for your favorite during the IC!
Presenting the winning two color options that will be available during GB.
