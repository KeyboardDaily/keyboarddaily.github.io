Hello Geekhack!
Misobee recently reached out to the LastPadawan team for our help with their interest check.


Following this interest check, the LastPadawan team will produce prototypes based on the most voted for designs in the collection.
Following prototype production and designer approval, we’ll move forward with a group buy.
Please note, we will most likely choose a maximum of 4-5 designs to move forwards with based on the results of the interest check. Of course if the interest check exceeds our expectations, we will revise our approach accordingly.
With that in mind, there are a total of 11 designs for us to share with you today.
The ‘Mono’ Collection consists of a Yin and Yang design featuring Kuro/Shiro inspired logos.
Included in the collection are the two featured Yin and Yang designs with Kuro/Shiro inspired logos and 9 additional complementary color offerings.
We begin with our two featured designs:
Presenting “Yin”
Featuring Kuro/Shiro inspired logo by Misobee

Presenting “Yang”
Featuring Kuro/Shiro inspired logo by Misobee

Presenting nine complementary color offerings
Featuring multiple colorways based on previously released GMK keycap color palettes
