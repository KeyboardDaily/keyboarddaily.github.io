Introducing the Deep Purple Switches from Keyboard.People, a new NA Vendor.
Were founded by a couple of hobbyists who have become deeply immersed in the keyboard scene over the past couple of years. So enough vague information about us and onto the switch at hand.

What are they? They are a full polycarbonate double-spring linear switch manufactured by Tecsee! The switch feel we were trying to obtain is moon-shoe-like, so stronger spring resistance for infrequent bottom outs and an extremely smooth housing.
Deep Purple Switch Info

  • Linear switch manufactured by Tecsee, makers of the Purple Panda and Blue Velvet switches.

  • Purple-dyed polycarbonate housing and stem, aiming to be near Pantone 274-C

  • 68g Gold plated double-length spring

  • Factory-lubed

  • MOQ of 50k.

  • Pricepoint to be near 0.65c but not locked down

  • What has been done so far?* Weve already received a small batch of uncolored samples from Tecsee, and they came out wonderful! We are both fans of linear switches and, although the market is already flooded with recolored JWKs and heavy hitters, we wanted to give this a shot with this manufacturer after being so impressed.

  • Sound and Feel?* Being an all polycarbonate switch, they are most similar in sound to the Water King switches. The double-length spring provides a more consistent feel throughout the press. There is very little noticeable scratch stock, but heavier lube will bring a deeper sound. The housing is very tight with minimal stem wobble that does not require filming.

  • Why?* We wanted a switch that straight from the manufacturer provided a great experience. Also… you know… because they are purple! They trigger that nostalgia reflex from our childhood gaming consoles and make for some seriously good barebones keyboard pics.

  • To Do:*
