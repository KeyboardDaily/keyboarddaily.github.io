Update 28/5-20
After just about 24 hours it’s became really clear what was wanted by the community. Therefore i will get to work ASAP on adding support for both HHKB and WKL, i will try my best to make sure both of these editions are added without having to much of an impact on the price, hopefully none (price will be anounced at a later stage). I will also see what i can do about adding support for more types of layouts on the plate, like Tsangan and a split spacebar.
Thanks alot for all the feedback - Keep it coming!
Pulse (60% Topmounted case)
Hello Geekhacker’s. My name is Tobias and i am an avid keyboard enthusiast like everyone else reading this thread. I am a sucker for good looking keyboard and prefer a somewhat basic style and decided to therefore spend some of my time during there uncertain times designing my own keyboard. After countless hours, long nights and many different styles and types of keyboards i’ve finally made a keyboard with a design i am proud of, the Pulse!

Specifications

  • 6° Typing angle

  • CNC milled and anodized aluminum case

  • Polished brass plate and weight

  • Top mounted plate

  • /*QMK and VIA compatible PCB

  • /*ai03 USB-C Unified Daughterboard

  • / = Not yet designed**
    Currently supports both standard ANSI and ISO layouts as shown below, if you want support for more layouts added feel free to leave a request in the form provided at the end of the post. If there are enough requests for said layout i will make sure its supported before launching the GB!

  • Renders*
    The case is still in an early development stage and the renders are not of the highest quality but i hope this helps to get a picture of what the final product will look like!


  • To-do list*

  • Interest check

  • Design a PCB with QMK, VIA support and ai03’s Daughterboard

  • Make modifications based on the feedback i get from the interest check

  • Order a prototype of the case and PCB

  • Decide a date and price for the GB
    The case is still in the earlier stages and there are still alot of things to be done. I have not decided any colors since i want the buyers to be able to have an impact on the product they are buying. The only color i will decide is for the Founder’s Edition. The top and bottom case will both be made of anodized aluminum and will be colormatched. If there is an interest for it the plate and weights can be made of aluminum instead of brass, i however prefer the look of brass so i set that as the standard option. The most important thing to me is that everyone who buys the keyboard is happy and satisfied with what they bought, therefore i am willing to make adjustments if there are enough people wanting that said change. This keyboard is inspired by just about every keyboard i have seen in the interest checks.
