Hi everyone!
I am a product designer looking to start my own brand of keyboards which focused on users who uses the computer a lot!
Many creators spend most of their time working in front of computers and keyboards are the main peripheral that connects you to the machine.
MUSO was created so I can bring the perks and qualities of the custom keyboards to a larger audience.
We aim to design long lasting premium mechanical keyboards with professional aesthetics for all types of users!
There is a Instagram page I created with the most recent updates but I would need to ask the moderators if I can share it here.
Discord server: https://discord.gg/C8ZcQw74SM
Creator 1.0
Please check out our first design the creator 1.0 keyboard!









The creator keyboards follows minimal yet distinctive aesthetics for different user bases.
Made entirely by CNC, the creator keyboard will be milled out from blocks of aluminum.
The keyboard is made with a two part construction which allows for a contrast of materials and different color combinations down the line.
It also features mounts that are compatible with 60% PCBs out in the market!
When it is launched there will be different options to choose from ranging from parts to DIY kits to a fully assembled keyboard!
