HB60 - HONEYBOARD60 BY MTBKEYS
We are proud to announce our very first 60% case design! It features a pretty simplistic, elegant glossy design with a honeycomb design in the inside which gives it a unique sound profile.
You will be able to choose from two tops. Standard or HHKB 7U for the same price.
Manufacturing will be done in-house in our shop here in Richmond, BC. Canada.

**SPECIFICATIONS
**
Case Angle: 4 Degrees.
Material : 6061 Aluminum Base, Plate, and High profile Standard or HHKB Top.
Plate Layout: ANSI/ISO Univeral
Price: $290.00 + Shipping for Basic colors - $310.00 + Shipping for premium colors.
Shipping: $25.00 US and Canada, $55.00 International.
Timeline: We have already had a successful GB run on reddit. We hope to run a Geekhack GB as soon as we reach 2 month account age (Oct 13) or we hear back from GH mods about vendor approval.
