Sumi is completed.
With the changes that are now applied to the Base kit, Sumi is completed. Because of the change in the vendor listing I were able to realise the last requests for the Set. This includes the addition of all hiragana double sublegends.
I didn’t felt right to me to just include some of the quaternary Keys, so I added all of them to make it a complete and more usefull Set.
The Hanko, shown on the Deskmats, did also change. It’s my signature and stands for “Virtual”. Just to give it some reference to me.
A minor change which will lead to better and faster colour checking phase is the change of the Pantone to RAL 3028. The new colour is pretty similar to the old one, it’s just a tiny bit darker. I think the colour fits the theme even better than the Pantone.
With that being said, I’m hoping to get the pricing from GMK within the next two weeks.
The Groupbuy of this Set will start on the 15th of July!
The Groupbuy Date had to be changed due to the NA Vendor change!
