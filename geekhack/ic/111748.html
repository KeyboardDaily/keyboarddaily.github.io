GROUP BUY THREAD IS LIVE!!!


The Evil Eye charm is known in Greek culture to help ward off negative energy from The Mati or The Evil Eye as it is more popularly known.
Being of Greek descent, I have grown up with the Evil Eye being a staple in my life.
I wanted to design this set as an ode to my upbringing and something that represents the significance of the Evil Eye.
