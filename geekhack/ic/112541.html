Hello Geekhack,
Our name is GoodGameKeyboards! Were very excited to share a keyboard weve been working on with you all. Having been in the keyboard community for a few years now, weve been able to experiment with a variety of different keyboards trying to find our endgame, as we all do, so we decided to build our own custom keyboard and hopefully, it can be an endgame for you. We genuinely appreciate the design and quality that goes into well-known keyboard creators in the community such as the Fox Labs, Rama, Keycult, Gray Studio, etc. We strive to continue the pattern of innovation and creativity that was established before us. After a lot of hard work, weve decided to create a keyboard that has multiple layouts which will give you the flexibility to customize it as you see fit. Were also focusing on delivering beautiful aesthetics along with a solid compact sound and pleasant typing feel. We hope you can join us in our goal to bring more unique keyboards into the community. We proudly present to you -Genesis-
