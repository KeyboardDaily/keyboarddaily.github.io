JER1800 quick introduction:
This is an OG-style customized keyboard kit. The features are as follows:

  1. The surface of the aluminum alloy case adopts the electrostatic powder coating process, and the powders specially customized basically restore the texture of the original version (G80-1800).
  2. Retro color matching. We refer to the original version (G80-1800) and the early cherry products NCR-3077 and NCR3007. We do color matching too many times!
  3. Continue to use the Plateless TOP structure same as JER80S, which may be a relatively rare structure, and we hope to bring you a different typing experience.
  4. The PCB solution still uses B series firmware.
  5. A custom aviation cable. (Interchangeable with JER80S data line)
  6. The brass weight adopts a special coating process to enhance the surface texture while effectively preventing fingerprints and oxidation.
  7. There are 4 standard indicator stickers, which can meet the needs of a variety of keycaps. (Other styles may be added later)
  8. ISO Return support, and 7U spacebar (left CTRL, ALT are WKL configuration) and 6.25X spaces (left CTRL, WIN, ALT are WK configuration columns), and the NUM area +- keys are split.

Group buying related and matters needing attention:

  1. All kits will be shipped out by the Deppon within 1 week after the sale is finished. (will refund if there are unresistible factors)
  2. The keyboard is not artwork, and there is no 100% perfect product. All goods have passed strict QC. But for extremely subtle flaws, it is regarded as normal. The purchase is considered agreeing with the term. A very small number of brass weights may have watermarks (inside) and small dots (inside) on the back.
  3. Please use a dry sponge to wipe away the dust and stains during use.
  4. Please be as careful as possible during the process of plugging and unplugging the keycaps, and prevent using metal key pullers to prevent scratching the coating (the safest way is to remove the keycaps after the PCB board is removed)
  5. The packaging and express delivery have been strictly tested. If there is still shipping damage, please contact me in time to deal with related matters.
  6. The kit does not include LED indicator beads, please purchase three 3mm LED beads by yourself.
