**/Update: 12/05/2019*
Hello everyone, I apologize for the lack of updates in the past couple of months. Ive had some roadblocks that I had some difficulty ironing out. I have also been dealing with some personal issues as well.
After giving it a lot of thought, I have decided to discontinue this IC for now and go a different direction with the set. The biggest reason for this is that the colorway I have chosen is much too similar to Kobayashis. I do not feel its right to proceed as is. (I also think itd be a challenge to reach MoQ, considering the number of responses and the amount GMK sets that are planned to drop in the upcoming months.) After playing with a few options, Ive just decided to drop the colorway and do something different with it.
I will share the new version in a different thread in the near future. For now, I am going to close this thread as I am not sure when this will be.
Lastly, I want to thank every single person who has expressed interest and has left feedback for this set. I will be taking everyones constructive criticism/feedback into consideration and implementing them wherever applicable in the new version. Again, I apologize to those who have expressed interest and have followed the IC.
