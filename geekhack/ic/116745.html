Hello Geekhack buddies!
6.xx is a placeholder name. In the meantime I want to share this project and gather some feedback. This has been another long term project that I am now ready to share.
*This is something I am very proud of. I hope you like it as much as my cat wants to steal it!
*

***IC FORM

Specs

  • 6 degree, 60% with xt column

  • Adjusted front height of 19.1mm (see below)

  • 7.8mm plate to top case clearance

  • WK and WKL options available

  • MX + Alps compat (with mx stabs)

  • Stock Alu 5052 plate with 1.2mm pcbs

  • Currently testing 1.6mm pcbs with ABS plates. PC plates are too dead sounding for this.

  • Internal brass weight/sheet. This is interchangeable with PC bottoms - reasoning below

  • External Stainless Steel weight - upcoming protos to have polished SS instead of brushed

  • Top mount standard, with optional gummy mount included. Uses 190x4mm orings

  • 1433g with current build (photo below)
    No foam. With this much surface area covered in brass, its not needed.
    The option of having a pc internal bottom was something I thought might be of interest. This piece replaces the internal brass
    I love how PC sounds, but hate touching it - or my cat scratching it. It is something I will test when I get new protos, and update here.
    Going forward I want to experiment with other materials in this fashion, so why not

  • Anodization options*
    I am testing another manufacturer. Each of the below are currently being tested with the anodizer, as there are several protos on the way.

  • I will remove what I do not like, or do not deem a high enough quality.*

  • Red (current proto)

  • Black

  • Navy

  • Blue Grey

  • Green

  • Silver

  • Purple/Lilac

  • Pink

  • Sekrit to be announced later on

  • Adjusted front height*
    Measured without feet as I am changing the feet being used. Measured from the inside edge of top case to external case floor.

  • PCB Support*
    Each unit comes with the necessary C3 Unified daughterboard + 150mm medusa cable.
    There are SMD LEDs on caps lock, and the 2 top left keys (acting as layer indicators)

  • Prototype images*
    This unit came with some particularly poor anodization - something I am working on to improve. Note - I take horrible photos
    GMK Red Devils

    Back (yikes it was a sunny day)

    Internal brass weight - can’t take a photo right now, heres a screenshot from a previous build stream. Yeah, that’s covering a large surface area.
    The gap is present on the divider between the XT columns and main alpha/mod cluster, to affect sound as little as possible.
    Note the USB port is also aligned along this divider.

  • Pricing / Vendors / Unit allocation*
    There will be a maximum of 100 units - this may be less. Price target $400 - it may be more but I will be trying to keep this as low as possible.
    Vendors? Too early to say. After I am happy with the prototype rounds, I will see.
    I will increase unit count if it seems as though this is warranted. Fear not.
