The Big Screen PBT

Inspired by eras without mobile phones and social media, The Big Screen PBTs ethos is about making the best experience with what we have. It features all things horror, giallo, skate culture and of course, the emergence of 3D media with classic red and cyan 3D glasses.
The Big Screen PBT is designed to be a functioning 3D keycap set with the use of red and cyan 3D glasses, and we intend to include a pair with every base kit.
