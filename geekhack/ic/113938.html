Natural Deskmat
Natural Deskmat is a mousepad designed to show the look of nature the forest and mountain.
Planned GB Starting Date: Dec 11th!
Prototype Pics
Prototype Pics Under Natural Light


A little bit about me.
I’m Jari and I’m a Finnish boy that likes designing with a simple style. I’m into Tech, Keyboards and Deskmats. I Wanted to bring out what I’ve got to show that I can bring some more nature out to your setups! I personally love nature and outdoors landscapes so I wanted to design this simple landscape.
Update [11/16/21]

Update [08/23/21]
