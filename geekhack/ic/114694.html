Royal Garden Deskmats

Hey everyone!
I present you the Royal Garden Deskmats.
These deskmats have been inspired by the videogame Hollow Knight, more specifically by one of the areas of the game, the Queen’s Gardens.
They feature the before and after of a greenhouse after everything went downhill.
