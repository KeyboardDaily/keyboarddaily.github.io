FAQ
So all positions mentioned in the specifications are supported with hotswap?
Yes, thanks to creative hotswap socket positioning by our PCB guy taylor.
Iso Support?
No, by nature of alice layout.
Won’t the sound be hollow?
After experience with the prototype, the board will not sound hollow when using the o-rings and acrylic bottom at normal typing distance. Using the POM plate will further mitigate this issue.
Can you further explain the mounting?
Of course - If you look at the photo of the plate, there are four holes, one in each corner. Those will be used to screw the plate into the case, and will be the only points of pressure on the plate. The plate will flex when the strips of metal connecting the hole to the main plate bend.
