GMK Orange Juice

Hi everyone, hope you are well. This is my first IC.
My inspiration for this set comes from my love of OJ and also a desire to build a fully orange keyboard.
My relationship with orange juice comes from a childhood memory of finishing my swimming lessons
(I wonder if that’s an Australian thing); and coming home to a hard earned glass of OJ.
I have searched high and low for the right keyset, but nowhere offers me a truly orange set,
with most of what is on the market being Orange-on-White, Orange-on-Black,
or only the mods being orange and everything in between.
So here I propose a truly
Orange-on-Orange
(with a bit o’ green)
set
(to go with your C3 tangerines…)

In addition to the base set, I will propose add-on kits such as different coloured modifiers and compatibility sets,
as well as novelties and possibly deskmats.
Ultimately, I want this set to be cheaper (GMK cheap? LOL) as well, so I want to restrict this set down to Latin alphas
and if there is no demand for different coloured modifiers, I would like the colour palette restricted too.
