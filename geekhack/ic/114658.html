Ciel
Ciel, a budget O-Ring gasket mount based on kkatanos opensource bakeneko60
Feel free to join my discord and fill out the IC form
IC Form
Discord

The Board
Based on the Bakeneko, much of the original design has been kept. Only changes have been the addition of an internal weight and a slight change in the back profile.
Since the original proto, an engraving has been added, bumpons have also been changed to custom silicone feet.

Layouts
Ciel supports normal 60% ansi as well as tsangan bottom row, split right shift, split backspace, and stepped caps lock.
The plate has been modified so that one should be able to use screw-in stabs, in my testing they have worked although it was a little finicky to get right

Misc
There will be extras going on sale at the time of GB, some of these, but not limited to are
Extra Fr4 plates, hotswap and solder PCBs, 50a and 30a o’rings, and daughterboards.

Colors
Burgundy, Blue Grey, Lilac, Black, E-White
Refer to pictures for colors rather than renders

Specifications

  • Internal Brass weight
  • 6061 Aluminum plate
  • PCB: Hotswap (Solder PCB avail for extras)
  • Cost: 180 for all colors except E-White, E-White will be 190
  • GB Date: Oct 7-Oct 21
