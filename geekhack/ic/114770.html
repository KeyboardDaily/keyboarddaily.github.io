Name: Symmetry 70
Goal: To bring out the most symmetrical design possible while using simple layouts
Layout: 70% (60% + Esc-F13)
Mount: Modified GEON F1-8x O-ring cord.
Typing Angle: 7 Degrees
Pricing: $410-450 (still looking into it for exact numbers)
Units: 300 + 60 extras (60 of each color total, this may increase at a later date depending on demand)
GB Date: Late October-Mid Nov
Lead Time: TBD
Front Height: 20mm from bottom to front edge, 24.3mm for effective front height
