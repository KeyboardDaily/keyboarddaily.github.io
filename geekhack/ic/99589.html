【IC】GMK Greypunk (yep, again)

Where to start?
Last year this keyset was started by Puddsy, if you want to check it out, take a look at the original IC thread.
Normally I can be pretty long winded, but there isn’t a whole lot to say here, so I’ll try to be brief (no one really likes reading crazy long first posts anyway, right?).
I really liked the set, but during the initial development a render was shown of all MG1 legends on N9, and I fell in love with that look. Puddsy said that he wasn’t going to run that kit, and that if people wanted it, they could buy it at OCo.
Well, OCo was sold out of that kit, so I started considering running just that add on kit after Greypunk dropped. Eventually Puddsy stopped working on the set in favor of another project and offered this one to anyone who wanted to take over. I saw it as a perfect opportunity, so I reached out and Puddsy turned the set over to me. Puddsy is no longer involved in the development of this set.
And so, here I am, rerunning the IC for GMK Greypunk.
I played around with it for a while, and felt that it would look nice as a more full featured set (and there were a few things I wanted to see added to accommodate my own boards).
Now I’m bringing it to you all, to show you where I’m at, and to ask for your input. We have a great community, and I’d like to involve everyone.
I’d like for this to stay a fairly simple set. I was never planning on adding ergo/ortho kits, novelties, hiragana, or any of the other crazy things other sets are adding.
I also understand that there are some big sets coming soon (Oblivion, Waves, Metropolis, etc.), so I’m not trying to rush this out. Lets give the wallets a break.
Colors are stock GMK colors N9 & MG1
So, please, take a look at the current state KLE below, fill out the IC form, and also leave some feedback here.
GMK Greypunk

GMK renders and community renders will come in the near future.
