SAKABATO
The Sakabatō (逆刃刀, Reverse-Blade Sword) is the last of the strange swords forged by master swordsmith Arai Shakkū. The Sakabatō is designed as a simple katana with its blade forged on the opposite side than normal, making it a sword ill-fit for killing. Its fittings are humble and simple, but vary upon depictions throughout.We want to make products with the most perfect angles as the master swordsmith did.
So CB:87//Sakabatō was born.
