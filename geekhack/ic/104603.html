❖Posh❖
GMK Posh’s Group Buy is now live! Please refer to the GB thread here
GMK Posh is inspired by the theme of “his and hers.” It offers a light cream base paired with navy blue and dusty rose pink.

Thank you for your support on GMK Posh. We will be launching on September 1st, so keep an eye out ♡
We made some small changes on the kits, such as changing Caps Lock to Lock, and adding an esc & numpad enter to the alt mods kit for more compatibilty.
❖Colours❖
Pink: RAL 3015
White: GMK CP
Navy: RAL 5022
❖Base Kit❖
Base Kit offers full size compatibility including 60% / 65% / 75% / TKL layouts, as well as numpad and basic ISO support.

❖Alt Alphas❖
Alternate Alphas Kit offers pink on white alphas, if navy on white isn’t your cup of tea.

❖Alt Mods❖
Alternate Mods Kit offers blue on pink mods. It almost has the same compatibility with the base kit mods.

❖Forties❖
40’s Kit includes split spacebars, as well as staggered and ortholinear layouts.

❖Spacebars❖
Spacebars kit is offered in additional colours and sizes.
