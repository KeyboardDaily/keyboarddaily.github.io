Introducing GMK Skyline

Where the idea came from:
Last year I was browsing reddit and stumbled on an amazing picture that inspired the colors and theme of this set. The image below has been my background since that day (July 12th, 2019).
Soon after I realized it would go great with an idea I was throwing around at the time for a colorway and asked the artist if I could use the picture, and they were glad to let me do so. Been waiting a while to release this but finally the day has come!
Theme:
Cars, More specifically Skylines, hence the name.
I plan on doing wheels, turbos, other car parts for the novelties.
I might try and get this art on a deskmat but need to ask permission to use it for actual merchandise first.

Thank you u/RaY29rus for this amazing picture and the permission to use it!
