GMK LIFTOFF
GB LINK
The Earth is the cradle of humanity, but mankind cannot stay in the cradle forever. ― Konstantin Tsiolkovsky
I’m excited to reveal the set I’ve been working on named GMK Liftoff. I have always been fascinated not only by space but the technology that helped us reach it. Going off of this idea, I decided to base my set off of the space shuttle. The colorway incorporates the colors of the shuttle itself as well as the external tank. I appreciate all the support I received while making this and am excited to see where it goes!
