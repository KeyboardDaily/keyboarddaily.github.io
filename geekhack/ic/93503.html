Didn’t think I’d be posting another thread with X60 in so soon, but here we are. I’ll keep it brief.
I will be doing a very small run of a revised version of the original X60 named X60 R. It keeps the original aesthetics of X60 V1, but has completely overhauled internal construction that addresses all issues from the previous version (noted below).




I’m expecting to receive the engraving for the weight by Sunday evening. I’ll then get the case quoted for production (will announce pricing once received), and start the buy (estimating late next week). Probably FCFS, but might do raffle if there is a lot of demand.
I may develop a USB-C QMK PCB if there is demand for a PCB to be included. Currently I won’t be providing one so there is freedom of choice.
Plate shown is a placeholder. There will be a universal plate available in different materials.
This is not X60 V2. That’ll be ran in the future, so don’t worry if you miss this one.
Case Information:

  • 8 Degrees Angle

  • Top Mounted Plate with Switch Opening

  • Compatible with any Standard 60% PCB

  • Choice of 3 Different Top Pieces

  • Specification:*

  • Black, Dark Gray, and Silver Anodizing

  • Brass Through Weight with Engraving

  • Aluminium, Brass or Stainless Steel Plate

  • 1 of 20

  • Price (excluding fees + shipping):*

  • Case (Top/Weight/Bottom) - $340

  • Plate - $30

  • PCB - $30

  • Change Log from V1 to R:*
    More

  • Fixed fitment issues with some PCBs around the USB cutout.

  • Lowered height of front from 19mm to 16mm.

  • Changed plate mounting spacing to prevent excessive flex on the sides.

  • Plate fitment to top piece will be tighter to decrease adjustment needed.

  • Changed inner corner sizing for the top piece & cap fitment to be tighter.

  • Fixed weight fitment.

  • Changed weight proportions to be more balanced.

  • Updated plate to not be ugly.

  • Fixed ISO Enter cutout.

  • Price/Shipping/Lead Time:*

  • Price TBA (See above).

  • DHL Express (1-3 days fully tracked) approx. $40 USA, $20 UK, $30 EU, $50 Asia.

  • Estimated May delivery.
    Thanks!
