Inspiration:
I hoped to capture the peacefulness that one feels when you gaze upon the beauty of nature.
Frankly, this style is quite common and often used as a wallpaper (Firewatch x /r/battlestations), however I wanted to do my own take, where the composition has the sky specifically covering the majority of canvas. I feel this would suit a keyboard desk setup more. Many other designs in this style tend to have a fair bit too much going on in the middle for my personal preference, thus I really wanted to strip it back and evoke that serenity. As such Ive avoided adding further elements to the sky, to avoid distracting the keyboard being the main feature of the set-up. There’s a beauty in simplicity.
Ive been recently exploring Nordic themed keycap concepts (Mischief & Northern) and this came about whilst around playing with deskmat concepts.
