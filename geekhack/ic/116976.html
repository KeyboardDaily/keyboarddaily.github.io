Hello Everyone
Today, I’d like to introduce you to my design PBT Nekoccino! As a coffee and keyboard enthusiast, I really wanted a way to bring the vibe of my daily coffee routine into an experience we can share with keyboards as well.
The morning sun pouring into my window as I dial in that perfect shot of espresso. My cat rubbing against my leg as I take that first sip to jumpstart my day.
Thats what this set is all about. A love for keyboards, coffee, and cats. ☕️
Inspiration
The colors and design for this set are based on the art on top of a cappuccino, as well as the colors of a well extracted Espresso. The orange, brown, and cream tones found throughout this perfectly balanced treat.

Kitting
We want this set to be sold in-stock and as an all-in-one kit! So, to appeal to as many people as possible, we aimed to include support for the following layouts: ergo, Alice/Arisu, and 60% to fullsize layouts, both WK and WKL.
