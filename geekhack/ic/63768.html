Allllrighty! Got some prices and have some updates.
I had a couple complaints about 60% users having to shell out for a full modifiers kit so I’ve decided to try and set this up with a Base Mods kit being the keys needs to complete a 60% board and then added a pack that finished out 100% kits. The price is the same ($60 for all or $40/$20 split up), just with a break for those who want this as 60% without paying for the extra keys. Also, I inverted Ergodox as I had several requests for that as well.
Quotes received include the cost of the custom legends rolled into the Core kit so that the novelties are affordable. This will be the most effective way to lower costs all around. I’m planning the MOQ of the core kit at 50 and will get some quotes for smaller quantities on some of the child packs.

::UPDATE 2:: (original post and first update at bottom)
Alright! Added homing set and a purple planetary pack. I also updated the colors a bit. After comparing several sets and using the Desk Authority guide, I’ve selected GTA as the grey and BO for the blue. I feel those colors will print closest to what I’m going for and the rendering should display them quite accurately.
Originally, I intended to use the Sun for the Escape key and the 9 planets would correspond with 1-9. I added a few more novelty keys though to improve customization.
Material - ABS
Colors - GTA | RDE | BO
Profile - DSA
So far, Deep Space includes:
1 Core Kit (alphas, GTA w/RDE legends) | 2 modifier options - Orion (RDE) & Polaris (BO) | Core Numpad | 2 Adder options - Orion & Polaris | Planetary Pack | Ergodox | ISO | Adapter Kit (Tsangan/Winkeyless) | Stripes | Extra Homing (Dvorak/Planck) | Extra Spacebar options - 6u . 6.25u, 6.5u | Planck | JD40
