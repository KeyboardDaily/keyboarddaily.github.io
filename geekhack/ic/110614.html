Farewell - Update 2021/03/15
Due to my changing views on IP-based keysets, as well as the several latest issues of other large IP-based sets, I will be shutting this one down (despite the fact it’s more inspired rather than IP based.)
My future keysets will be majority original ideas, and if they are based on an IP or use preexisting work, they will be licensed as to provide the original creators of the source material with income for their work. I have a big one of these in the works, being PBT DJ.
SA Magica has also been taking a lot of my time, but there was also a general lack of interest for this set (only around 45 IC responses as opposed to something like Magica’s 250+ responses.
Until next time, everyone.
If you want to keep up with upcoming projects (such as the Lotus, other keyboards, or PBT DJ, join the MisonoWorks Discord.
DSA Feline Knight is a set inspired by Ferris (or Felix) Argyle from Re:Zero.
Ferris, the knight of Crusch Karsten, is known as one Lugnica’s best healers.
Often teasing their guests, Ferris is a break Crusch’s more serious demeanor.
This set has been designed with not only Ferris’s typical outfit in mind, but also his general playfulness.
If you’d like to keep up with this set, feel free to join the MisonoWorks Discord!
Alternatively, you can follow the MisonoWorks Instagram, though this is more used for progress on my keyboards.
