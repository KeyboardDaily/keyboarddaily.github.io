Hi everyone!
Im currently back form my main job. It has been a long time since my last project and I have learnt a lot from it. Therefore in order to keep up with the demand and also the quality, I decide to run this project as a GB not “in-stock” like before anymore. Im very grateful to everyone that supported me in the last project!
This time I created 2 separate designs to give you a wider choice depending on your taste: The Coding series and Simplify Wave!
Coding is a design of mine for my best friend on his birthday. I want this deskmat to express his passion and at the same time be his motivation at work.
And Simplify Wave is a product that I wanted to give a more modern look to the wave pattern while remaining elegant and minimalistic elements.
I hope you can take time and give concurrent opinions as well as recommendations on these two designs. Like last time, I respect and take everyone’s opinion serious: https://forms.gle/aLtwDcy36zrsXcKX6
