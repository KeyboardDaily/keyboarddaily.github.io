Introducing Gradient keysets (Zen master, Paranoia, Dreamer), the most representative keysets from JianSheJu (JSJ).
ABOUT JianSheJu (JSJ)
Founded in Dec. 2013, Jiansheju(JSJ) has been focusing on designing and producing high-end keycaps. Jiansheju has various types of keycaps including laser-etched, dye-sub, resin and luminous keycaps. It is well-known for themed keysets such as Joker, EVA, Cardiogram, Overwatch and Gradient.
