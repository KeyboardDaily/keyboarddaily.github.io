The Set Name is Agent, Secret Agent.
The day I watched my favorite movie series for the 7th time I realized I had to make a keycap set out of the colors, so here we are:
This set is inspired by a suit from a golden movie of my favorite agent. Ever since I was a kid, I’ve been a big fan of agents.
I have my own collections of gadgets and exhibits and wanted to create my own keycap set together with this agent theme. I am so proud to share my progress with you today

FAQ

Q: Is this GMK Mercury?
A: No, the colors are slightly different, as well as the accents. I also talked with kekaiyo (Designer of GMK Mercury)
Q: Ok, but the Name is stolen from GMK Agent 01 !!???
A: Also no, plus I again talked with the designer. The colorway is quite different, and there are clearly too few agent sets in my opinion. In addition the GB of Agent 01 is already over.
Q: Is this a licensed set? Are there any intellectual property concerns?
A: The set does not contain any protected/ trademarked symbols, words or designs.
All designs were made by myself and nothing was simply stolen or copied. I took an inspiration for the colors from my favorite movie, that’s all
Q: Why do you have a blank Ergo kit?
A: A lot is preference in this hobby, plus with blanks you have a high compatibility/ need less keys and it looks clean
