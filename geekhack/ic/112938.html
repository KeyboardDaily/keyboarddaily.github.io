About
Command 65 was inspired by the classic Commodore 64 . We love the design and want to add some other elements, like toggle switches you can see from a control panel, Bluetooth , and a more general layout in modern times.

Command 65 is going to be a mold injected plastic keyboard project. Mounting type will be top mounted .
Renders (name plate design is not yet decided)


1st 3D Pinted Prototype (2021.6.29)

Toggle Switches
Three toggle switches will be in top right corner: Bluetooth switch, Win/Mac switch, and layer switch (not yet decided).
