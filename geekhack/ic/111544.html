Hi everyone! I’m happy to announce some deskmats I’ve been working on for a while!
More

The designs are mostly inspired by me not able to get my hands on GMK botanical deskmats (lol) but also designed around the upcoming IKKI68 Aurora in mind. The colorways are choosen such that it compliments the Mint and Devoted colorway of the IKKI68 but also versatile to use in other keycap set ups (eg. GMK Birch, GMK Tuzi, GMK Cojiro, Infinikey Vanir, etc.).
I had already run an IC before and based of the responses, I am happy to announce that designs are finalised! There had been feedbacks on creating an autumn-themed design as many people associate the word “Foliage” with “Fall”, which I had actually given it a shot coming up with something. But ultimately I wasn’t satisfied with what I ended up with hence the 3 designs will be the finalised “kit”. So this IC is serves as a gauge to the number of willing buyers, and of course, promote the deskmat!
Based on the amount of interest expressed, I will then be able to gauge the price to set at. In all honesty, I am not sure if the price would even budge, but hey every dollar counts! (Right now, the price is set at the standard deskmat price according to the regional vendors.)
