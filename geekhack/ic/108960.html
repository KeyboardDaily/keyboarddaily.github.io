ORI No.2

GENESIS

1.INSPIRATION
ORI GENESIS having been inspired by the design of the HHKB PRO HG, which is one of the most impressive I’ve ever seen, its impact is not trivial to the entire current keyboard design.
And that motivates me to make a design based on its impressive features, but at the same time will bring in the breath of ORI design.

2. THE “GENESIS”- https://www.ori-club.com/genesis
After completing my first groupbuy with the scheduled time (2 months). It marked something very meaningful to me, and this will be a design that transcends its own claim, it is a new start.
In order to do that, I will have to learn a lot from the previous buy group ORI 87. And especially my production has a lot of unnecessary problems, and this time I will try to do better. .

3. What’s different?
In a conventional seam-less design, you would use a screw to catch it from above, through the PCB. But this time, ORI GENESIS will be completely assembled without that.

4. Why alice layout?
Perfect twin balance layout!!

5. SPECIFICATIONS ( will update more)
CASE
Alice layout
12 assembly parts
8° typing angle
Polycarbonate case
Polished or sandblasted accent options
Standard plate is aluminum, sandblasted brass available as add-ons
Colour Anodize: Black/ Silver/ Burgundy
Isolated gasket sandwich mount
Flex-cut plate
Limited 50/50 piece
