Introduction
Hi, I’m Jarrod aka guypigeotto/#2428 on Discord (where I’m most active). I’ve been an active member of the Australian keyboard community for over two years now. I also moderate the Daily Clack discord server, and sometimes I ship your orders. I also really like beige and handarbeit.
Inspiration
GMK Handarbeige was inspired by three things: a stupid portmanteau, the classic elegance of KA1953, and the beauty of GMK Handarbeit+
With the popularity of excellent colourways like Red Alert, Olivetti and Tulip bringing a pop of colour to beige, it felt natural to bring the best that colour accents have to offer to beige. This set feels like a homage to the unity of beige and colour, a personal favorite of mine.
Colours and Kitting
GMK Handarbeige features all stock GMK colours, which will be listed below. It is a combination of the U9+L9 beige background, with the GMK Handarbeit+ array as legends. It also includes V4 front print.
