2. Specifications

  • Typing Angle: 8 degree

  • Colours:
    a. Entry Edition - Stacked frosted acrylic and riser w/ gold hardware.
    b. Premium - Stacked frosted acrylic w/ brushed stainless riser and black hardware.
    c. Founders Edition - Matte black acrylic w/ brass hardware and brass riser.

  • Plate Options: FR4 and POM

  • Plate Foam: ESD PE Foam

  • Mounting Style: Gasket Mount utilizing Poron Foam

  • PCB: Custom PCB created by Swiftrax
    /*Hotswap
    /*USB-C
    /*Underglow RGB
    /*ESD Protection

  • Weight: Cascade riser

  • Layout: Compact 1800

  • To bring a premium acrylic experience to the 1800 keyboard layout.
