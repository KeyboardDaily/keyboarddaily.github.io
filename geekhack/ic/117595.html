Type Box

TBX - 101800
INTRODUCTION
-
Hi everyone, please to meet all of you in this lovely community. Im Hymn, a designer and render artist for keyboards.
This is my first project that Ive started as a concept design back in DEC 2021 and to my surprise, it blew up on reddit and facebook.
And so here we are, making the Type Box into reality and I hope you all could join this journey as well.
I wouldnt have made it without the support from you guys.

  • Hymn -
    CONCEPT & DESIGN
  • Type Box is an accumulation of retro techs and gadgets back from 70s to the 90s.
    From keyboards to industrial machinery, and from game stations to command centers.
    Heavily inspired with little bits of every tech and gadgets I could find and remember.
    Focus
  • The main design and the focus is towards creativity workflow.
    Where artists/users can fully utilize the whole layout of the keyboard.
    Such as tool palettes to custom key remap for VFX artists or 3D artists.
    And to fully provide the full package with both function and design.
    FEATURES
  • 5 degrees typing angle
    40 degrees tilted F row
    2 rotary encoder on both sides
    3 LED lights indicator on left
    Top mount
    WK & WKL layout Alu & PC plate
    Both hotswap and solder PCB
    10 left macro keys + 1800 layout
    Aluminum top & bottom casing for now
    [ will consider other materials if theres demand. ]

QMK/VIA compatible
