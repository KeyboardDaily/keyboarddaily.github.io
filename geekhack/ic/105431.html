This is an IC for an aluminium ISO enter keycap.
There is room for discussion on my Discord Server: https://discord.gg/D8zCAMQ
Minor Updates about this interest check will be posted to the GeekHack thread and the Discord server. Major updates will be posted on reddit.com/r/MechMarket.

This Interest Check is for an Aluminium ISO Enter Keycap - this will be CNC machined with an anodised finished. The keycap is modelled from a GMK ISO Enter.
I have received a prototype (pictures below). Only issue is the stem is too loose.
This is my fault as I erred on the side of caution when designing since aluminium is not as forgiving as ABS on switch stems.
I have already sent off for a second prototype with dimensions that should work for this.
I have also increased the wall thickness to give the keycap some more heft.

The expected price for these is 40 to 50 USD, if interest is super high this could definitely be lower!
