Hi, everyone!
I am very excited to bring our latest project, Half-feather, to the Geekhack community.
The logo engraved on the upper case comes from the ancient Qin Variant of Seal Script which is the symbol of left-half of the word Feather written in Seal Script.
The design of the weight on the back is inspired from the art form of traditional Chinese paper cutting, which shows the contour of a vividly depicted butterfly.
Details
Case option: Full anodized aluminum
Angle: 6°
Plate option: anodized aluminum or multicolor gradient PVD brass
PCB: running through C³-LDN firmware (not open source this time) with diver (about the diver, there gonna be a English version of it, but the manual is still being translated at this moment. It will take some time.)
QMK firmware will be optional.
Mounting Method: Burger mount with O-rings (simplified gasket mount)
Others: 3mm&3.5mm silicone dampener (after testing I decide to remove the 3.5mm dampener between plate and PCB. and keep the 3mm dampener under the PCB )
Price range: around $300→from $220
Typing sound testing vidio: https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=IWFWmMir-IY&feature=youtu.be
