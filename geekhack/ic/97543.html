Hello All,
Staccato is a 60% hhkb/wkl layout board i’ve been designing for the better part of this year. The name “Staccato” refers to extremely short and isolated notes in the music world and the goal of these gaskets is to isolate the plate from the other parts of the case. Increasing the torque on the screws will increase the amount of sound transferred through the gaskets to the plate and will inherently change the sound. I will provide a video in the future to illustrate this a bit better. Currently I have nitrile gaskets (the same material as o-rings) but I have ordered a few different materials including foam to test. Upon reception of the initial prototype, I discovered a few errors in my design that have since been fixed. The blockers on the hhkb version were .5mm to big, the usb port was too small to support all cable types. The 2nd prototype has been ordered and I will report back with more photos once I have that in my hands.
Pricing - TBD depending on units sold (most likely below 300 usd excluding shipping)
Angle - The case is at a 6.66 degree angle
NE ISO AVAIL? - No I will not support ISO or any other layouts than what the plate shows (7u hhkb/wkl with split backspace, stepped caps, and split r shift support. If you MUST have an ISO plate, I will post a plate file here and you can get it cut with laserboost or lasergist
What colors will be available - None have been selected yet, but i will ask the factory for a list of all they offer (i just submitted a pantone to get my prototype)
What materials will you offer? - aluminum only for the case, brass only for the weight, and aluminum or brass for the plate. Possibly a copper plate at a higher cost
What pcb works with this? - any universal poker style pcb (gh60,dz60,panc60,b.face,etc)
I will update this with a google form to formally record questions and answers, but in the meantime please leave your comments and concerns below.
Thank you
