【IC】Kuro Shiro Deskmats

December 1, 2019 IC, 桌垫 本文总阅读量次

类型: IC
作者: ThePanduuh
发布时间: 2019-12-01 00:23:25
更新时间: 2019-12-01 00:23:25
原链接: https://geekhack.org/index.php?topic=103623.0

Hello!
First things first, I did ask gok [designer of Kuro Shiro] about doing this, and potentially making a group buy out of it. He was okay with it as long as I got KBDFans on board with it, since they were the vendor of the set. I am hoping to get Novelkeys to actually get the mats as they do a lot of them for a lot of keysets. I think it’s doable as long as I can get both vendors on board. Before I get there, I want to see how many people would legitimately be interested in this design.
As we all patiently wait our Kuro Shiro sets to arrive, and order more keysets, and more deskmats, I got thinking about a simple Kuro Shiro inspired deskmat. I quickly got to work in Photoshop mocking things up and posted in a Discord server and the man himself MiTo reached out to help me progress the design and get things set up properly (alignment of the characters). I can’t thank him enough for his help in getting it just right and worthy of printing.
A few quick design elements:

  • The RAMA caps for this set are opposite the deskmat, and putting shiro before kuro was not something I really wanted to do (Shiro Kuro just doesn’t seem the same).
  • If the right side was white background and black letter, the right side would get dirty from the mouse arm moving around.
  • MONOCHROME, Kuro Shiro, black white, literally all I want since my theme has been black and white monochrome.
    So here it is, the Kuro Shiro inspired deskmat.

    If you’re interested just comment here. Just seeing how many people would legitimately buy one of these. I’m not sure of any MOQ requirements or anything, but I’m not opposed to finding out from Novelkeys.
    Thanks for reading!
