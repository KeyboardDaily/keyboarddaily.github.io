Greetings Geekhack!
This is an interest check for the Sol Deskmat Series; a stellar backdrop to complement your mechanical input interface.

Featuring mandalas (circular abstract geometric patterns that symbolise the cosmos), this design is a tribute to the Sun and planets of our Solar System.
This depiction of the Heavens doesnt pretend to be scientifically accurate, but at least its the Heliocentric model (shout-out to my man Copernicus).
Ill leave any New Age metaphysical interpretations to those with broader minds than mine. I for one, simply just like anything related to Space. Space is cool.
