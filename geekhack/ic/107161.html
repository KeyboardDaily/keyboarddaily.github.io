Hey everyone! During my free time in quarantine, I decided to finish off an old project I started (and posted here) late October last year.
With that out of the way, I introduce you to the new and improved:
Infinikey Strawberry Lemonade
Information
To save you a paragraph worth of text, I’ll keep it simple.
This set is one based off one of, in my opinion, summers greatest drinks: Strawberry Lemonade.
Its colors are fruity and fun, copying after the flavors of its namesake.
Profile: Cherry
Sculpt: 1-1-2-3-4-4
Legends: Dye Sublimation
Plastic: Infinikey PBT
