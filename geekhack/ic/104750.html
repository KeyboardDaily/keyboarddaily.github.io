my first full tkl design - hence the very creative and unique name!
prototype first
the proto will be WKL and it will be mine
design is pretty much done at this point, just waiting for china to reopen and get back up to speed so i can get a proto ordered.
protos ordered and in. everything is great, I had to make two small changes to the case itself and I also modified the weight a bit to add some more screws (the weight screws are all internal).
I am planning on starting the group buy in mid/late June of this year (2020).
the PCB (h87c-hsp) prototypes are good to go.
gb info here: https://geekhack.org/index.php?topic=104750.msg2919393/#msg2919393
TLDR
Cost: $530-540 base cost depending on plate
Date: June 21, 2020
Time: 2 entry slots at 10AM EST and 8PM EST
My num one numpad will be offered alongside.

PCB will be h87c, an h87a with a USB Type C port. the hsp (hiney special project) edition.
gonna do burger mount of course, jane v2/koala plate mounting locations, but with a redesigned ish a bit plate
weight is two parts, brass and stainless
6 degree angle
gonna use torx screws


full run ETA, will do a proto run then then a public raffle/fcfs/vickrey sort of thing with like 30-40 units, no more than that likely. I do plan on doing multiple runs - goal is to do smaller but more frequent runs/buys, so drop new colors/etc every few months
