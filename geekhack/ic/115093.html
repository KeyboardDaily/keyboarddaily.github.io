Dear Community,
this is the interest check channel for the Edinburgh41, a 40% mechanical keyboard I designed in Edinburgh, Scotland. I have named the keyboard after this city in honour of the wonderful people who have directly and indirectly supported me on this journey. Please take a look at the credits section for details.

And now some facts:

  • Based on Reviung41 by gtips
  • 40% split unibody, column staggered
  • Analog thumbstick in the centre
  • Case
    o Gasket mount
    o Frosted and Clear Acrylic
    o Centred USB port
    o Symmetric design
    o High-profile
    o No angle
    o Maybe engraving, See IC form
  • Controller
    Pro Micro or equivalent (wireless not yet supported due to thumbstick custom code)
    o Not to be socketed
  • Software
    o QMK
  • PCB
    o Hotswap (Kailh/Gateron)
    o LED underglow + one LED on top
    o Colours white or black, tbd (as of 24.10.2021), see IC form
    o Has to be self-soldered, apart from bottom side LEDs
  • Plate
    o POM plate included in every order
    o Additional plate options tbd (as of 24.10.2021), see IC form
  • Screws
    o M3
    o Colour/Material tbd (as of 24.10.2021), see IC form
