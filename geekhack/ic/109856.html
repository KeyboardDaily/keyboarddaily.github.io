Greetings, we are TS Workshop, a group of enthusiasts from Vietnam.
We love them keebs and fortunately the stars have aligned such that we have the skills and the passion to come up with our own design.
Months in, we have finally gotten a satisfactory prototype and we would love to hear your thoughts about it.
Introducing - TS60
About TS60

  • 60% layout
  • Two piece aluminum case
  • INTEGRATED PLATE in top part - we are planning to go for 2 options: ANSI and HHKB
  • Compatible with DZ60 and its varieties
  • Machined brass weight
  • Accessories made with aluminum:
  • Binder
  • Feet - may have different angles, starting from 5 degrees.
  • Brass mid-body accent
  • Coloring options: anodization or paint.
