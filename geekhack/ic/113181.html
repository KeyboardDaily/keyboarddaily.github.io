Inspiration
The inspiration for this set comes from the current iteration of the Bloomberg Terminal Keyboard, the Starboard.
Intended to bring the terminal’s interface up to modern standards, the Starboard combined the classic color-coded keys of the financial world with the form factor that users were accustomed to at home.
If it reminds you of GMK White on Black but with accents, front print, and new numpad legends, that’s because it’s GMK White on Black with accents, front print, and new numpad legends.

Compatibility
The base kit includes compatibility for most common keyboards including 40%, 60%, 65%, 75%, TKL, 96-key, 1800, CP, and full-size. It also supports ANSI, ISO, Tsangan/WKL, HHKB, F13, and more. The spacebars kit increases your options to include split layouts and boards using 6u space.
