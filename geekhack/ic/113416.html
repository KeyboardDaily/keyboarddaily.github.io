-﴾﴾﴾- Change Log -﴿﴿﴿-
✵ Colours adjusted for sub legends:-
➢ - Katakana: Lightened
➢ - Hangul: Lightened
➢ - Cangjie: Orange to Blue
➢ - Cyrillic: Red to Orange
➢ - PentaHash: Raw with CR Legends
✵ Character set Updated to OCR-A:
OCR-A Was an optical character recognition font designed in the 60’s to be read by both humans and machines, it’s been used extensively in scifi and cyberpunk movies since its conception and is so fitting for the themes of this set.
https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/OCR-A
✵ Name & Branding:-
➢ - Dropped the Ulliam, because I’m not even sure why I thought that was a good idea
➢ - Updated the Branding to be more legible and taking further away from the Cybergoth/Tribal
✵ Kitting Changes:-
➢ - Novelty kit dropped, in favour of beefing up the Megacorp kit.
➢ - Mod Kit updates: 3 more keys added for 40’s Support, Terminal Iso added

More
➢ - Alpha kit updates: Coloured R1 Esc, Arrows, 6.25u & 7u Added to each Alpha Kit

More
➢ - Quadhash now PentaHash in Raw with all 5 language sets displayed on the face of a single keycap.
➢ - Latin Alpha Kit added (Tennetively)
✵ Deskmats:-
➢ - WWW Deskmat Dropped and 2 New Simpler mats have been added, I am still open to more options if you have any opinions please let me know on the form.
✵Misc:-
➢ I- Still waiting for samples from both manufacturers, unlike regular dye sublimation sampling I need to work with the manufacturer to ensure the Glow in the dark pigment is utilised correctly on top of getting the colours down, I am pushing the GB back till at least September I will have samples coming in the next week or so and will have a better idea of how close we are, I think my experience working through these kinks with Keyreative will greatly help reduce any issues, my sweat probably contains some traces of strontium at this point.

local vendors this August. The spiritual successor to Cyberspace, Inspired by all your favorite cyberpunk tropes with its own spin, welcome to the sprawl. There are
alphas in multitudes of languages and colours with a hefty modifier module equipped to kit out even the weirdest layouts.
Boost up your cyberwear with the megacorp multipack or launch headfirst into cyberspace with the jack-in novelty kit,
youll be sure to find something to cover your layout and all basked in a bath of strontium glow.
