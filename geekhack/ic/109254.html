Introducing the Function96v.2
GB NOV 20 - 29

IC Form: https://forms.gle/FsHJaG195R52AmG4A
Discord (Best Place for Updates): https://discord.gg/HaxhD7v

The Maximum Function Compact Keyboard made by an Engineer for Engineers, Accountants, and anyone who needs a Numpad

About the Function96 v.2:

  • Case and PCB are designed all by me (thanks to the Keyboard Atelier Discord)
  • Completely Screwless Exterior
  • Top/Burger Mount
  • Blockers between bottom mods and arrow keys to support non-visual finger navigation (the biggest issue with the 96% layout)
  • Added Blockers in the F Row for the same reason.
  • Added ISO and Stepped CapsLock Support
  • Added Support for 6.25u Spacebar as well as 2.25 - 1.25- 2.75 split space support
  • USB-C
  • QMK and VIA support
  • Added ESD Protection
  • Added an Aesthetic cut at the top
  • Added engraved JTM Logo on the bottom
  • Additional Renders and Details can be found in the Discord under /#v2-design-updating
  • Update 1: The board is not hotswap, but I am heavily considering offering personal mill-maxing soldering services so that those that are looking for the capability for hotswap aren’t left out!

Update 2: ISO and stepped capslock support added! Also trying out a new style of burger mount using the gaskets used on gasket mount boards.
