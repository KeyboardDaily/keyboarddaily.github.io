Hi everyone, I sincerely hope that everyone is doing great and that youre all safe at home!
Its already been one year since my first group buy started today so, I think its a good opportunity to make another one.
Let me introduce you to my new set being EnjoyPBT Spectrum : https://enjoymy.gallery

As you can all see, this set is a mix of white alphas with CMYK modifiers. This idea came from my love for from OG keysets + CMYK Add-on, something that you guys can recreate, this set is definetly a tribute to that iconic combination:

Okay, I heard you guys, you wanted more compatibility, full white modifiers etc, so here we are with new kits for the set !
The kits are now the following :

The base kit offers great compatibility by covering the standard ANSI layout, Tsangan, HHKB, and WKL.

I decided to add full white modifiers as you guys asked, aswell as alice support with an extra B in the kit.

  • Yes, this is better as an extra kit and I don’t want to have white modifiers in the base kit.
  • No 2.U shift ? No because no.
  • Will it be expensive ? No.

    You guys were right, the lack of orange spacebars was definetly something I could fix so I added them aswell as colored arrows and alice spacebars.

    Numpad support for the many people that messaged me about it, so here it is.

    Finally, the ISO Kit. I really didn’t want to put this kit in my set but the ISO community was so nice to me that I couldnt refuse.
    However I don’t offer Norde support at least for this round (unless something crazy happen).
    Color matching wont be an issue because these colors already exist, I chose them from stock GMK colors but this set will actually be Dyesub PBT and not Doubleshot ABS :
