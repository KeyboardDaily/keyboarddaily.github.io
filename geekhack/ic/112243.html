Changelog:
04/25/2021

More
Hey everyone!
This week’s update brings you a significant rework of the kitting, QOL changes, additionally some simple deskmat designs. (leave feedback ty <3)
Base kit changes:

  • Complete overhaul, removal of numpad to keep costs in bay.

  • Added the much requested 2.00, 2.25 and 2.75U spacebar options

  • Removed 2.00u Shift

  • Added 1.50u Super keys and an extra 1u Alt key
    /NEW/ Numpad kit:

  • Initially wanted to provide as big of a coverage as possible (with the current colour options) with the base kit, but with the introduction of the new accent colours, this change had to be made to keep costs on the ground.
    I know this change might be a bummer for some, but I hope the newly introduced colour option makes up for it!
    /NEW/ Accent kit:

  • This kit includes a much requested feature, that being a better representation of the green in the set.

  • Additionally, this kit provides further options for split spacebar users and some of you that use 6.00U spacebars.
    40s/Ortho kit:

  • Less accent options, but bigger coverage with the introduction of the R3 1.25U Commit, R3 1U Code and the R4 1U Send keys!

  • Removed R3 1U Tab key.

  • Added 2x1.75U Space keys to support JD40/45 variants.
    Novelty kit:

  • Added green ISO and ANSI enter novelties.
    New deskmat designs!

  • Added some simple mats! Leave your feedback on those!
    I realise some of these changes might introduce some unwelcome changes, but I do believe this direction is highly beneficial for the set in the future, making it more colorful, while hopefully making purchases feel better.
    It’s entirely possible I missed something, or made some mistakes, please do point them out for me! Thank you for the feedback, take care!
    04/29/2021
    More

Changed the colours of the set, added RAL colour samples. Updated the renders to reflect previous changes.
(Disclaimer: Colours are an approximation, the final product may have some differences!)

More

Files sent for quoting, kits finalised.
Colours
Colours chosen are: RAL9001, RAL1015, RAL4006 and RAL 6036


Proposed Kits
Core
The kit provides coverage for common layouts, including 60%, 65%, 75%, TKL, 660, Alice.

Novelty Kit
Flower shop and Paris themed novelties for everyone to enjoy.

Accent Kit
Magenta and green accents for common spacebars, ISO enter keys and arrow keys. Includes further options for split and 6U spacebar users.

40s/Ortho
Provides coverage for the most common small form factor layouts including Van variants, Candybar, Whimsy, JD40/45 and Ortho variants.

Numpad
Pretty self-explanatory, a simple numpad kit with accent options.
