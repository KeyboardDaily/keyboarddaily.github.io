GMK Diet Soda, MD

Inspiration
Why don’t you just give up the charade? You’ve been putting on airs for years pretending to be something you’re not. From the moment you were old enough to think, you’ve known you were different. Made out of different stuff. Uncommon stuff. REAL stuff.
The landscape is littered with pretenders, posers and meatbags who don’t know better than to ride shotgun with their daddy, Status Quo.
If you found yourself nodding to anything you just read, consider buying this Keyset. It’s because of a drink. We’re all because of something. The ball is in your court. Night Court. John Larroquette and the bald guy from Night Court. Pop.
Kit

That’s it, one big base kit. If you can’t afford it, too bad, this ain’t a charity sweaty.
