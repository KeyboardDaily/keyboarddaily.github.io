GMK Katakana BoW

Simple, Sleek, Fun
I saw Katakana with the rise of weeb sets and really liked the “angular” look of the characters and wanted a simple BoW version of it in Cherry Profile!
I also saw this as an opportunity to attempt to fix the small gripe I have with the top 3 key cluster of the nav keys (Print Screen, Scroll Lock and Pause)
Kits


Colors are GMK Stock WS1 and CR
My goal is to keep the kits small but pack as much usefulness into them so that anyone can buy any kit, even if not interested in the base kit.
Basekit was made to be smaller than usual to keep price low.
Kitting is not final and I welcome any feedback in the comments or on the IC form!

FAQ
Why no “insert some key here”?
To keep price at a minimum. If you really want the key, comment here or in the IC form and if enough people want it, I’ll add it!
Why no Kanji F-Row/ numbers?
I wanted to stick with katakana only (except for the halfwidth, fullwidth key), plus Japanese use Latin numbers and it fits with katakana.
Deskmats or Novelties?
I do not have either planned
Why new Pause, Print Screen, Scroll lock legends?
The og GMK ones don’t look good and don’t fit the katakana style which I really like, so I created new ones!
Molds?
Mito’s katakana and custom molds for the Half Width, Full Width and Pause, Print Screen and Scroll Lock
