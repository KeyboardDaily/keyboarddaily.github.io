RE:65
I. INTRO:DUCTION

The RE:65 is our 65% top-mount keyboard.
At re:search labs, our goal is to introduce a minimalist yet enticing board.
While allowing the board to remain brand-less, its distinct design will give it a recognizable character.
We also aim to give our customers the creative freedom to customize their own unique boards.
You know whats up. Milled metal. Anodized. Polished. Coated. Customizable.
Minimal. Not boring.
