About the creator/Ace 6.5

The Ace 6.5 is based on card tricks and magic, specifically the Aces. I have been doing magic and cardistry for 5-6 years and I wanted to introduce my hobby to this hobby in a unique way. I have always had a love for magic and cards since I was young, and now I want to share that love with the keyboard community and maybe teach some tricks along the way.
