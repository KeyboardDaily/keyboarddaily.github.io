[Album] http://imgur.com/a/5A3Mu
PietPtr and I are thinking of running a GB for a 58-key version of the Ergodox.
So basicly it is the ErgoDox without the 1u thumb cluster key and the bottom row. He has an album ready with how it should look since he has build one for himself already.
This group buy will be for a kit containing the following:

  • An acrylic case and plate
  • Nuts and bolts to secure the case
  • 2 Arduino Pro Micros, optionally with firmware already flashed on them.
  • Male and female headers for the Pro Micros
  • A cable to connect the two halves
  • Rubber feet
  • 100 Diodes
    What you’d need to buy yourself if you decide you want to build it on your own:
  • 58 Cherry MX compatible switches
  • Wires
  • Solder
    We will also be able to build the board for you, but that will be more expensive.
    Let us know what you think of this in the Google form at the top of the post, all feedback is appreciated.
    Cheers
