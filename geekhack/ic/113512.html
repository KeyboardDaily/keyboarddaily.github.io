Designed by Zhou from the DiMi Studio, the Colossus75 (Also known as Zhou75) custom keyboard kit is now in IC.
The special edition in collaboration with Glove Studio!!! Check out here! https://geekhack.org/index.php?topic=113608.0

Summary of Specs

  • 75% layout
  • Top mount or an unconventional gasket mount, your choice!
  • 7-degree typing angle
  • Seamless design
  • VIA/QMK compatible PCB with RGB lighting
  • Priced at $2xx
  • (1) Introduction*
  • About DiMi Studio*
    DiMi Studio is a relatively new designing team based in China, their previous works include Zhou65/Korno65 and Lumos Alice style keyboard. They are striving to bring a sweet point between decent design and more accessible pricing.
  • About ELBoard*
    We are a US based vendor specializing bringing brilliant design and products that were previously certain region only to the international market. We provide all-round service including IC/GB posting and the actual GB running. Please feel free to join our Discord to stay tuned for more products.
  • About Colossus75*
    The naming is suggested by ELBoards Discord members, and the inspiration is from the bulky appearance that reminds us of many titan figures in video games. What is interesting about this board is it allows for two distinct mounting style with a reasonable pricing.




  • The Mounting Style*
  • Option 1: Traditional Top Mount*
    To be honest, the inclusion of same old boring Top Mount is more of a safe move to hedge for the risk in option 2, which is rather experimental.
  • Option 2: (A cousin of??) Plateless Gasket Mount*
    Not sure what to call this mount yet, please feel free to suggest a better name for this mounting style. As shown in the graph below, the PCB is fixed in position by a 3.6mm poron plate foam on top and a 2mm gasket below the edges. With this mounting style, you can feel free to remove the plate to achieve a plateless build. And of course, if you prefer to have a plate in place, there is always room for it between the top case and plate foam.
  • Aesthetics*
    This kit features a sleek seamless design, which means no screw holes on the outside!! The internal screws will be M3, and the positions are shown here. 7 degrees typing angle.

  • PCB*
    Yes, hotswap version will be offered, and yes, RGB a thing. Also yes, VIA/QMK compatible. A flex cut as shown in the picture for a softer typing feel.
    More details to be finalized and updated.
  • Color Options*
    TBD at the moment. Lets hear out what colors you guys want the most!!
