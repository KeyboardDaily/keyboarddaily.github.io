Daedalus by Aether Hemera
Hello there. We are proud to present our first addition to this community. Inspired by the creativity of the craftsman from Greek mythology Daedalus.

Thank you for all your responses to the poll
We received far more responses than we expected and that allowed us to finalize a few decisions about what we will be offering with the board.
We have ordered a prototype and it should be here in a few weeks.
Specifications
Case:
-Aluminum 6063 case
-6.5 degree typing angle
-18mm front height
-Isolation Gasket Mount (We are testing between two choices, we’ll be able to better determine with a prototype)
We will be offering choice of HHKB blockers
Plate:
-ANSI (ISO if there is high demand)

  • Polished Bronze (Finishing may change)
  • We will be making the plate in blasted brass

Weight:
We are currently running a poll to chose between a few different weight designs of varying complexity. Please let us know what you think.
-Polished Bronze (Finishing may change)
-We will be doing the weight in blasted brass with the circular maze as that was the most popular choice. We also removed the Daedalus branding from the weight to give it a cleaner look. The bottom is coated in chrome for the reflective look.
PCB:
-Maarten Plain60-C Rev2 with USB-C daughterboard
Layouts(Plate):

We will be offering an ANSI Tsangan layout. Further layouts may be included if there is high demand.
Price:
-The price is tentatively set at $420
Colors:
The color selection for the group buy is as follows:

  • Black
  • Blue(naming still under review)
  • E-White (we quite liked this color once we started looking at it more)
    There are the renders with our updated colors are below.

Quantity:
We plan to run 100 boards and most likely FCFS
FAQ
New Studio:
We may be new to the development scene, but we are not new to keyboards. We are both avid keyboard enthusiasts and have been participants in this community for a while. We are working hard to ensure that this keyboard lives up to the great legacy that all the other creators on this platform have worked hard to build.
Updates:
We have the blue prototype in hand and are looking through it to make sure it fits our standards.
We ordered some 3D prints which led to us making some internal changes in the design to make the pieces fit better. Edits such adjusting the cutout on the weight for the JST cable and connector and the USB cutout to fit larger cables.
Next Steps:
We are planning on ordering prototypes of the other two colors and once we receive those we will be able to set a GB date.
We are currently looking at obtaining prototypes of the board manufactured we have been looking around and we should soon have more information with regards to that soon.
More

We are currently experimenting with a 3D printed prototype of our design so that we can finalize all the small details about our components. We will post updates on this when they are available.
We will continue to improve our renders and designs as we go. We plan to get some renders with key caps up soon.
After that we will move onto CNC’d prototypes and find a manufacturer we can work with for the GB.
During this period of time we will continue to accept community feedback and modify our design as we go. This is our first keyboard so the process will be a little slower, but we hope to have the GB up in the next few months.
Interest Check Disclaimer
All renders, designs, materials, colors and anything else on this IC is a work in progress and may not be representative of the final product. NOTHING IS FINAL until the GroupBuy goes live and until then we will continuously work to improve this design in as many ways as possible.
First Keyboard:
While we have been involved in other projects in this community as consumers before, this is our first time jumping in ourselves. We have a lot to learn throughout this process and we do not plan on cutting any corners. This is our initial design and we have many aspects that need to be tested and finalized before we can ship this product out to be enjoyed by all of you. Please stick by us and offer your advice as we prototype and adapt our keyboard into the best it can be.
Aether Hemera
