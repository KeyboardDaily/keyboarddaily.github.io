Features
6 Degree typing Angle
Front Height 21.5mm
Made from 6063 Alumium + Anodized/E-Coated
12/*4 Cluster + 5 Number Keys
STM32 based Hotswap PCB
Gasketmount Case
Brass/Alu/POM Plate
5 different Options for the bottom Row (See IC Form)
Large Brass Weight with optional Engravings relating to “Saeculum”
Estimated Weight 1.6KG unbuilt
Rotary Encoder

Case Foam

Colors

“When do you plan on showing a prototype?”
I aim to finish the prototype in January. This will be delayed further if I am not happy with the PCB thats on its way right now.
“When will the project move out of the IC stage?”
When I’m happy with the prototype, and I try to make it happen in 2022.
