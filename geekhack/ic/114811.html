GMK Orange Boi is co-designed by me and Typo.mk. This set is inspired by my orange cat Richard as well as orange cats everywhere (including the girl ones, despite the name of this set). One of the goals of this set is to raise money for cats in need! If GMK Orange Boi makes it through group buy,a portion of designer profits will be donated to The Odd Cat Sanctuary, a feline rescue organization that was started to help kittens and cats (as well as an occasional dog) with “challenges” or that have been neglected. Check out their website to learn more and to admire all of the adorable kitties that theyve rescued!
To follow progress on GMK Orange Boi, follow us on Instagram (Ty, me), and feel free to join my Discord server.

COLORS
We have confirmed with GMK that they match to Pantone plastic chips as well as RAL (for RAL they match to the book standard) and selected our colors accordingly.
Orange - Pantone PQ-1625C (Pantone plastic chip)
Pink - Pantone PQ-190C (Pantone plastic chip)
Cream - RAL 080 93 05
Pastel pink (UV print) - RAL 010 90 10
