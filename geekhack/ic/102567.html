Hey Everyone,
Currently I have 2 Exents and one of them has a crappy PCB with bad RGB LEDs and is in just generally bad condition. (Picked it up aftermarket) Thus I am looking for a replacement and there doesn’t seem to be any, as well as other boards that could use it as well.
I’m getting this IC started in it’s infancy and seeing how much interest there is in a Center USB, 65% Replacement PCB for boards such as:

  • Exent
  • Dollinger65?
  • Zypher?
  • Other high end 65% with center usb?
    I know that Zeal will not be making the 65 again, and some of the Zypher boards could already use a replacement. I also know that apparently the Zeal65 fits in the Dollinger, so there would potentially be some need there. Finally I know the Exent PCBs were kind of //// from the GB to begin with and they’re BMC which also sucks. This PCB would come with components already soldered, no SMD needed on your end.
    Again this is in it’s infancy so if you wanted to fill out the form and provide feedback below that would be fantastic. I know USB-C will fit the Exent and Zypher, but I’m not sure about the Dollinger.
    https://docs.google.com/forms/d/1OvJxbhvy-Aib5BNG30RsDwYz4JUJ7LJ3y7KDR2Wxkvw/edit
    Looking for RGB Underglow for the Exent, but understand not everyone wants that. I also understand that Per Key is basically out of the question unless you want to pay an exorbitant amount for a PCB like the Zeal65.
    Let me know if I missed anything but I feel there would be a decent amount of interest for additional/spare PCBs.
    EDIT: This will be an ATMega32u4 board
