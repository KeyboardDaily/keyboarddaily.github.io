Design
This board was designed with a space motif in mind, and so the overall design goal was to include stylistic callbacks to this theme in a way that was tasteful while also maintaining a sleek and elegant look overall.
In terms of sound and feel, we designed the Cosmo primarily to provide a soft and flexible typing experience, achieved by the leaf springs and the pcb and plate flex cuts.
We also wanted to play to the strengths of the naturally deeper tones of acrylic without the muddying of the low end; in other words, a deep, yet clean sound signature.
