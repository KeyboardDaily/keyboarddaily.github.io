A Titanium Esc Keycap
Inspiration
Inspired by Japanese innovation, the keycap with a singular dot is named kaizen, which in Japanese means improvement.
To me, kaizen represents a consistent improvement in a balanced life, that we should improve ourselves, but not to the point of ruin. This is meant to encourage kaizen.
The keycap is a dot with no edges, but instead has smooth inward slopes. I appreciate luxury, and when you feel the dot with your finger, it’s polished, and feels substantial. I didn’t want any edges on the actual face of the keycap because it would disrupt the smooth artistic design and manner, for which it was intended. The titanium is long-lasting, but also something the discerning public members can acquire.
This simple design, unobtrusive, yet elegant, will be the kind of reminder that you choose. The neutral colorways and minimalistic design allow this piece to be timeless and universal.
