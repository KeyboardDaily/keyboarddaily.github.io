The Deep Deskmat


Hello everyone!
The optical illusion deskmat is back and it’s almost time to join in. The Deep Deskmat group buy will begin March 8 to March 22!
After over 1,900 IC entries, the final colors that will be available during the GB are Black, White, Classic Beige, Gum, and Hackerman.
Last IC: https://www.reddit.com/r/MechanicalKeyboards/comments/ktscfw/ic_the_deep_deskmat/
**Vendors+Pricing:
**

  • US: TXKeyboards | 20usd
  • CA: ApexKeyboards | 29cad
  • EU: mykeyboard.eu | 25eur
  • AU/OCE: DailyClack | 30aud
  • SEA: Monokei | 20sgd
  • HK/CHINA: ThicThock | 20usd/140yuan
  • PH: Zion Studios PH | 25usd
    /*please note that this deskmat design will only be available from these official vendors

**Specifications:
**

  • 900 x 400 mm
  • 4 mm thick
  • Stitched edges
  • Renders: ( https://imgur.com/a/Xdeytd6)

**Colors:
**

  • Black (white on black)
  • White (dark on white)
  • Classic Beige (dark on beige)
  • Hackerman (green on black)
  • Gum (pink on lightblue)

Timeline:
**Group Buy: March 8th - March 22nd
**

  • Production: 30-40 Days
  • Shipping to Vendors: 4-5 Weeks (Depends on vendor location)
  • Fufillment: Early Summer
    Lastly, I just wanted to say thank you to everyone for the kind words and support! This is my first deskmat but hopefully it won’t be my last.
    Background on The Deep Deskmat:
    I came up with this idea while I was cleaning up my desk and thought to myself,
    wouldn’t it be fun if I could expand my desk without buying a brand new bigger one?
    This inspired me to design a fun visual illusion of “expanding” my desk to be deeper.
    I also recently got an white & black keycaps for my keyboard and wanted something
    to match the look. I would love to get your feedback on the design!
