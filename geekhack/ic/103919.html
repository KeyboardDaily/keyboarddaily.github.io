Please Consider DSA Take Out
Howdy! I’d like to drop a project I’ve been working on for the past few weeks, that I’m really excited to share!
Inspired by your typical take out bag, DSA Take Out aims to embrace the bootleg aesthetic created from cloned fonts and blurry single color illustrations.
This set if all goes as planned will be SP dyesub DSA. It uses SP WAN, and SP RBR for plastics, Pantone 1795 C and Pantone 440C for legends.

Charity? Yep! I plan on donating 25% of the profits to Meals on Wheels. Obviously, food related, this charity is extremely transparent and goes to a great cause.

UPDATE!
After mulling over this, and looking back on interest form, I’ve come to realize that this set just isn’t going to make high enough MOQ to justify having external vendors. However, I still want the set, and I know others are in the same boat. Because of this, I’m going to be running this on my website and fulfilling it myself at a lower MOQ. Still going to be SP. Still going to be the same design. I’m going to be contacting SP this week about quotes, and if all goes well I should be able to run this in either May or June.
New kits have been designed to reduce the amount of SKU’s, and maximize compatibility.
