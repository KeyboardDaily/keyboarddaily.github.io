Details****
Case

  • Exploded 65% with Macro Column
  • OLED Screen, capable of playing gifs in colour
  • Magnetic Badge
  • Poron Gasket Mount
  • ANSI/ISO
  • “Seamless”
  • 1.48kg~ built
  • 6 degree typing angle
  • 1.2mm Thick FR4 Plate
  • 21.5mm front height from top of lip to bottom without feet.

PCB

-1.2mm PCB thickness, will come with 0.4mm thick shims

  • USB Daughterboard (Pinout is different from UDB)
  • QMK/VIA
  • 0.96 OLED Module
  • Hotswap ONLY
  • Ultra-soft JST cable
  • 6.25u and 2.75u,1.25u,1.75u split spacebar support

Sale Info****
DISCLAIMER: ALL INFORMATION IS SUBJECT TO CHANGE.
Fulfilment
This will be fulfilled in a group buy.
Due to the amount of options regarding the parts of the aluminium case, ordering a flat number of each colour for each piece and packaging it would be a logistical nightmare.
That being said, the cases are already currently being machined, and the orders will be submitted to the factory for coating after the machining is completed. This way, the lead time is significantly reduced, with the customer only needing to wait for the surface treatment to be completed.
Pricing
400SGD~ for e-coated colours at MOQ of 30 sets total, regardless of colour, pricing will be adjusted accordingly to demand.
60SGD - Extra PCB package, includes PCB, OLED, daughterboard, ribbon cable and JST cable
Colours
Due to current developments with the anode factory, there will not be anodised colours in favour of E-coated colours instead for the time being.

  • Beige
  • Dark Green
  • Pink
  • Blue
  • Yellow
  • Lilac
  • Black
