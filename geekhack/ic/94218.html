Specification
Material: chrome tanned pull-up leather
Technique: hand cutting & hand stitch
Thread: polyester waxed thread
Made in Bandung, Indonesia
This new sample is in a darker color as requested.
The sample is for a 60% keyboard, but no worries other sizes are also doable because it is hand cut to perfection.
Possible sizes available are 60%,75% and TKL since i have keyboard with those sizes, other type might have to give me dimensions.
Price & Shipping
Starting at $49 for the 60% exclude shipping and paypal fees
1 sheet of leather could create around 6-8 cases (60%).
So technically if you want a different colour of leather (especially unique ones), either you try to find another person to join with the same colour or pay some extras to compensate us.
The cases will be shipped from Indonesia to everywhere in the world using DHL or EMS
