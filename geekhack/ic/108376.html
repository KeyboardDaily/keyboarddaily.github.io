Hello keyboard geeks, I’m Aziz from CLAP LAB. I have never imagined that one day I will be able to design my own keyboard and post on GH! Today I want to show you my 65% gasket mounted keyboard. As a 65 lover I got inspirations from keyboards such as: Rama m65, Iron165, Noah, Space65 and many others! Okay, enough of me talking, I want to show you some renders (please excuse my rendering skills )

  2. Gasket Mount

  4. PCB-QMK supported Hotswap and Solder
  5. USB Type C
