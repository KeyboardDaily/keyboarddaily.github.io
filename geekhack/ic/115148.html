    Mounting, Plates and PCB info
    Top mount using Tadpoles by Geon Works,
    both 50A and 80A hardness tadpoles will be included with the board.
    Plate materials offered: 1.5mm POM, 1.5mm Carbon Fiber and 1.5mm Aluminum.
    All plate files will be released to the public after GB has shipped.
    PCBs (As of 2/11/21): Both Soldered and Hotswap PCBs will be available during GB, Files for both hotswap and soldered PCBs will also be released after GB. Soldered will be the default and come with each board, Hotswap will be an add-on. There is no minimum order quantity for the hotswap pcb.

    Design Explanation
    This Keyboard is called Rotor, meaning: ‘to revolve or rotate’ in Latin.
    This can be seen in the design language of the board featuring rounded corners as well as a pen tray/area to roll balls.
    (Pen will not be included in the kit, 14mm metal ball will be.)


    Specifications
  • Centered USB C with C3 unified daughterboard
  • Front Height of 18.5mm (without feet)
  • Case and Weight Materials: Anodized aluminium case, polished internal stainless steel weight
  • Weight: ~2.4kg for the case + internal weight built
  • Case colors: Silver and Brown Grey (Pantone)
