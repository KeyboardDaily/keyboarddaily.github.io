Hira70
Hello, I am Saab and this is a very early IC for my first project that I have been working for well over a year.
Hira70 is a FRL TKL
The inspiration behind this project sparked during my time in Punjab, Hira in Punjabi is the translation for a Diamond or a Gem.
The weight is designed to remind the user of a diamond on the back of their keyboard.
Majority of the trial and error was done for the weight, in the beginning I was not able to find a manufacturer that would be willing to work with me for this geometrical design.
Some even said that its impossible, the weight I was after is supposed to be shiny, reflective and reflect light like a diamond would.

Prototype Pics:
These are prototypes and therefore they will have defects on the finishing.
This will not occur during production stage.
As for prototypes, I am very happy with how they turned out. Large amount of prototyping was done for the Weight.
I experimented with different finishes, started off with a simple plain polished Stainless Steel Weight. That was too plain in my opinion.
Then multiple prototypes were done for the diamond shaped brass weight. This was done using electroplating process as polishing this weight would be impossible.
I also plan on adding an option for Sand Blasted Weight for different aesthetics.




Polished Stainless Steel, Brass Electroplated, Brass Electroplated. in order from top to bottom
