Bling Switch Introduction
It is designed by Keyfirst and produced by HM. HM was found by one of Kailh’s engineer.
Adding shiny powder to the housing of the switch is not easy to achieve in the process. In order to achieve the best visual effect, it is also necessary to take into account the feel and quality of the switch, which is difficult for us in the first place.
To make the Bling switch from the visual, feel, and quality of the effect to achieve the best, we continue to choose new materials and use a new switch mold to try to make them, and finally achieve the Bling switch that meets our expectations.
The Bling switch has an excellent appearance, a lot of optional pressure grams, and amazing light transmittance. I believe our efforts will definitely make your keyboard shinier.
Product Detail
Linear Switch
Transparent housing with shiny powder
POM Stem
60 grams of Gold Plated Spring
5-pin PCB mount
3.8 MM TRAVEL
2.0±0.5mm Pre-travel
Press life: 6000w
Color: Bling pink, Bling blue, Bling purple, Bling green, Bling yellow, Bling purple
