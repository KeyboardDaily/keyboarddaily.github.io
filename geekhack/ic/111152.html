Mirror60
Mirror Polished Stainless Steel Base, Stacked Acrylic, Gasket Mounted 60% by ENG Studio
Group buy website: https://engstudio.co.uk

Introduction:
Welcome to the first project by ENG Studio - the Mirror60. This aims to provide a unique stacked acrylic 60% in multiple aspects, like the never seen before Onyx plate (micro carbon fiber filled nylon), or the mirror polished stainless steel base, while providing great compatibility with the inclusion of all three tops in each kit (standard, WKL, HHKB), and universal 60% PCB support.
Specs:
-7 or 8 degree typing angle (Both included as standard)
-Comes with all three of Standard, WKL and HHKB tops
-PLA or Onyx (Micro carbon fiber filled nylon) plate
-3mm thick mirror polished stainless steel base
-Stacked Acrylic construction
-Nickel plated brass standoffs and stainless steel screws
-Colour options: Clear and Black
-Standard 60% PCB compatible. PCB not included.
