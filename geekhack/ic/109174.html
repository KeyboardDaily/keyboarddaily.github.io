Inspiration
Inspired by Japanese innovation, the keycap with a singular dot is named kaizen, which in Japanese means improvement.
In the past, I struggled with becoming a better human being. I would always be selfish and only help others for my self-interest. There would be days where I’d break down because I hated myself at the time, and I wanted to change for my future. There’s always been this business side to me, this entrepreneurship side, and this glowing red urgency that I needed to have a successful life, someway somehow. And, the only time I experienced the feeling of scarcity in life was when I was trying to buy something, and I didn’t have the money, and from that day out, I’ve been the personal hype man to my own goals, and my life. To me, kaizen represents a consistent improvement in a balanced life, that we should improve ourselves, but not to the point it ruins ourselves.
