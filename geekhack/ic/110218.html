【IC】6060 - 60％ WKL gasket mount hybrid

Hello keyboard peeps, it has been several busy months designing the 6060. Now that the prototyping for the 6060 is complete, it is time for me to start the Interest Check for the keyboard, introducing the 6060 the first Gasket hybrid layout that can use all 60% plates and all left sided PCBs!.
Our philosophy behind this keyboard design was to provide everyone with the opportunity to get a gasket mount keyboard for a cheap price, however with a gasket mount comes a few issues, you must use the plate provided with the keyboard and that isn’t fun for people wanting to explore different plate options; to combat this issue we have come up with a design that allows the user to plate mount with removable M2 standoffs thus making it possible to fit any 60% plate and PCB.
The board has been through several iterations of prototypes and review units will be sent out in the following months.
I am sure at this point there is still some room for improvement. Please kindly let me know your thoughts if you will be interested in this, and your suggestions for the GB and the design. If you would like to chat in Discord, here is the link: https://discord.gg/Yrshqrs8ST

Materials
We’re using 2014 aerospace grade aluminium for this board, although its a little more expensive we chose this alu in particular as its the strongest out of the bunch and this will increase the amount of times you can rebuild your keeb (so the screw inserts don’t thread).
