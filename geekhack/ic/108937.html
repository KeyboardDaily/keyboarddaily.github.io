Introduction:
Hello fellow members of geekhack, I’ve been lurking here for a little bit and finally felt like creating my first set of keycaps. Some of you
may or may not have already seen an early rendition of what I have made on reddit that was back maybe a couple of months ago, and I
finally thought that it was time to create an interest check for it. By the title of the post most of you will already know what it is however
I want to introduce to you all ePbt Dreamscape.
Inspiration:
Dreamscape was originally derived and inspired from the BTS album cover Love yourself Answer. The colors often remind of a setting
sunset or a rising moon, with colored gradients stretching over the sky and I thought that maybe something like this would look cool
to other people than just me.

Why not GMK?:
For a set like this with 7 custom colors I would imagine it to have an insane cost, as GMK handerbait costing what it did with only base colors
and would not be economically sound in my opinion; however, if all of you really want this set to be GMK I try my best and get a quote, albeit
that some novelties will need to be reworked and some other small things in order to do so. Lastly I wanted to make sure that poor people like
me can still afford this set
