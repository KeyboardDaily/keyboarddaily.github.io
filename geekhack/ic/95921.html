You guys can see my Geekhack acc is totally new. But in mechkey community of Vietnam, my name is quite well-knowned between people.

  • Name of project: Stabwood Handcare (Stabilized Wood Handcare for Keyboard).
  • Material: Resin (high quality) and Stabilized Wood

    “Stabilized Wood” that I used in the product is calling “Nu Nghiến”
  • “Nu” is Vietnamese word (in English, it is “Burl” - big thanks to Keebmeupscotty for helping me find the right word), meaning is the damaged part on the body of a tree (cutting, scratching by animal and bug; lighting,…) and be healed through a long time by the tree itself.
    This part will consume the nutrition to recover and grow into a strange part on the body of the tree.
    Each part has the beautiful one-and-only natural grain and form.


  • “Nghiến” is Vietnamese name of the tree: Burretiodendron hsienmu (a.k.a Excentrodendron Tonkinese).
    Wiki: https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Burretiodendron_hsienmu
    This species of tree can be found in Vietnam and China. I am Vietnamese and we are here used this wood widely in house furniture and carving altar.

    Side-note: “Nu Nghiến” as Vietnam carpenter sometimes call it “Ngọc Nghiến” to dignify the gorgeous beauty of this tree grain. “Ngọc” in English is “Gem”.
    “Nu Nghiến” <=> The gem was natural made by the tree.
