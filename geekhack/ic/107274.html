Design Elements
Nasu uses Yuktsi’s ever so popular Alice layout. With that said it was designed as a more modern approach to the classic Korean keyboard design. It utilizes a seamless design thus ensuring a clean and simple look and simplifies the process of anodizing. Additionally the sides are inspired by the OTD Koala keyboard as they are my personal favorite type of keyboard sides. All these factors combined make for a minimalistic and elegant keyboard that reminds of a simpler time in keyboard design.
Specifications
Typing angle: 6°
Materials offered: Aluminium(6061),PVD coated brass(for the weight)
Plate materials: Aluminium, Brass, POM
Colors: Black,Silver,E-White,Deep Red,Navy Blue,Deep Purple
Front height: 19mm
Mounting: Gasket mounting using 16, 40 shore A silicone strips with adhesive backing
Connectivity: Custom PCB which uses ai03’s unified daughterboard (PCB designed by Yiancar), QMK will be supported
Weight: ~2,3kg
