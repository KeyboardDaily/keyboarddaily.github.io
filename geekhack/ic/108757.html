Holmen
The Holmen is a minimal 65% gasket mount keyboard made with productivity in mind.
Features Overview
 Typing Angle: 7 Degrees.
 Case Material: Aluminum Top and Bottom.
 Plate Materials: Aluminum, Brass, Carbon Fiber.
 Mounting: Gasket Mount.
 Front height ~20mm.
 Miscellaneous: Rotary Encoder, 3 Macro Keys, Removable Internal Weight.
 Colors: Black, Grey, White, Deep Blue(Navy Blue), Plum.
