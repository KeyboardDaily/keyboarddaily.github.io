SA Bliss is a pink and grey bedroom inspired set. It will be SA R3 uniform profile.
Manufacturer and possible vendor are still being arranged at this time.
❀ Announcements ❀
This set WILL be made by SP in ABS double-shot. Custom match pink order has already been placed (on 05.10). GB Start: July 01, 2019
Vendors
US: Dixie Mech
EU: My Keyboard
Asia/Oceania: Daily Clack
Prices

Numpad: 30$
Novelties: 50$
Spacebars: 30$
ISO: 25$
❀ 05.15.2019 - Artisan collaboration with Meika from @latrialum has been confirmed, others are in the works!
❀ 05.22.2019 - The group buy will start on July 01, 2019 on Dixie Mech. More details about other vendors and pricing will be announced soon. Artisan collaboration with @alphakeys has been confirmed as well!
❀ 05.29.2019 - EU proxy will be mykeyboard.eu and Asia/Oceania proxy will be Daily Clack! Pricing will be announced soon. Artisan collaboration with @keyforge has been confirmed!
❀ Palette ❀


While the color palette is decided, the exact shading might differ slightly due to manufacturer decisions.
The current shade of grey is SP ABS GC.
I am receiving another pink Pantone chip in several hours and will finalize the exact shade of pink then.
The grey color is SP ABS GC.
The pink color is Pantone 13-1406.
❀ Kits ❀
Any additional kits (ISO, ortho, ergo, etc.) will be created depending on interest.
Base Kit
Base kit compatibility covers 40% through TKL with some of the funky formats in between.
Ortholinear compatibility will also be included (added 05.02.2019).
65: 1.75 shift
hhkb: 1.75U ctrl, 1.5 mods, 1 mods
40: 1.25 tab, 1.75 enter, 1.75 shift, 1.25 shift, 1 mods
ortho: 1 tab, 1 shift, 1 pink enter, 1 grey enter
