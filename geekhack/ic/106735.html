Hello everyone! The last 4-5 months of hiatus on the set has been primarily due to an overload of work on my end, as I was working two jobs before COVID-19 struck, as well as building relationships and expanding horizons for the business. With a renewed vigor, Ports interest check will be resuming with a vast amount of changes and updates!
Infinikey Port
The Set
For those that have seen the previous IC thread from a wee while ago, Port’s colouring is inspired from a well-loved Australian dessert, the jelly slice, very specifically the port wine jelly slice. The trifecta of colours are layers of jelly topping, sweetened condensed milk, and crushed Arnott’s Biscuits. Aeroplane jelly is the definition of an Australian brand, as it has been owned and ran by the same family since 1927. As an Australian, it’s an honour to translate that feeling of nostalgia and home to a keyboard and to the global community.
/DISCLAIMER/ Infinikey Port is not affiliated with RAMA WORKS in any way, shape, or form.
The colours have changed over from RAL to Pantone with the change from GMK to Infinikey. They will stay very similar, but I feel that the new Pantone colours are richer and much closer to what I had envisioned.
A deal has been struck with TheKey.Company (TKC) to manufacture the kit through Infinikey, and thus will be changed over to PBT from GMK and ABS. There are a variety of reasons for this, and I will go into those below.
The first is the development of dye-subbing that the Infinikey factory has developed: it is now much easier to have reverse dye-sub with the PBT material, which was nigh-impossible when I had posted the thread for GMK Port last year. This allows for a near-infinite number of opportunities for keycap sets in a variety of colours alongside a shorter manufacturing period, lower MOQ requirements, and a lower cost to consumers for more keys. TKC has been incredibly accommodating with working the set in to their schedule, negotiating with their own worldwide proxies, and assisting with any questions Ive had.
Kits
Unlike the variety of kits that were originally declared under GMK, Infinikey Port has two kits: the core kit which will come with a couple novelties and the majority of keys most layouts will need, and the mega add-on kit, which provides more novelties and more niche keycaps that should fill just about any board. There will be no changes to these kits as they have been chosen to cover the largest field of layouts at the best price possible.
