We are in the planning stages of making some high end keyboard sleeves. We have the equipment to both dye sublimate or print direct to fabric, so can work in polyester, cotton and a lot of various fabric blends.
I am planning on making some designs that match popular key cap set (dulch, miami, ect), but we also can use your art/designs on the sleeves, additionally we are planning on having some standardized sizes (I have a Poker and Novatouch to work from initially) but we can size them to your particular keyboard.
What would you be willing to pay for sleeves like this? Any interest?
We can also do mousepads and wristpads and could match colors on keysets if there is any interest in those too.
