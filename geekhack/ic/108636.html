Updates /#2:
Variable Gasket Structure
There are 10 chips (A - J) in total that snap on to the bottom case. Chips B, D, G and J are optional, the rest must be installed. When all 10 chips are installed you get a firmer, less bouncy type feel. You can choose to remove chips B, D, G and J for a bouncier experience. Please refer to this image for more explanation.

Underglow Will Be Available
As suggested by IC reponse, we will add underglow for IKKI68 Aurora.

Finish
Frosted.
The overwhelming majority wanted a frosted finish. We are not ruling out clear finish in a future round, but for round 1 we’re going with all frosted
Default plate material
Aluminum. Every Ikki68 Aurora will ship with an Aluminum plate.
Carbon Fiber, Brass, Polycarbonate and POM plates can be purchased as extras.
PCB
Ikki68 Aurora will have 3 PCBs.
[Default] QMK hot swap PCB with multi-layout support.
QMK soldered PCB with multi-layout support.
Bluetooth soldered PCB with a fixed layout. The same one as Ikki68 with the possiblity of some updates.
Multi-layout support
Both soldered and hot swap QMK PCB will have support for:
Split Backspace
ISO enter
Split Left Shift
Split Right Shift
Split Spacebar
Stepped Caps Lock
Here’s a photo visualizing the options： https://i.imgur.com/umgeMX4.png
Brass weight
An addition by popular demand: you will be able to buy an 400g internal brass weight offered as an extra.
We’ll be having a design contest soon and the winning design will be engraved onto all the brass weights.
The Aurora weighs around 700 - 800 grams without any additional weight. With the weight it should weigh about 1100 - 1200g or 2.4 - 2.6lbs, just a touch lighter than the all aluminum Ikki68
Custom badges
All Ikki68 Aurora will ship with a default badge. You can buy additional custom badges as extras.
Just like Ikki68 you’ll be able to choose from a handful of designs and colors. These are what we call Community Badges. You can also have your own design custom made just for you. These are called Bespoke Badges.
The badges will be “hot swappable”. You will be able to change a badge without taking apart the case.
Ikki68 badges will be compatible with Ikki68 Aurora out of the box.
Ikki68 Aurora badges will be compatible with Ikki68 through the use of an adapter, sold separately.
Join our discord server to stay up to date with badges. You can submit a design to be used as a community badge. Use the commands !badges and !bespoke-badge in any channel to find more info.

A polycarbonate version of Ikki68 was THE most requested item in the original Ikki68 group buy. Due to various constraints it wasnt possible to do at that me, but now were very pleased to announce Ikki68 Aurora.
It will feature a gasket mount system utilizing 20 gaskets. The case will be made of injection molded polycarbonate to keep the costs down and achieve a fast manufacturing me. Were looking forward to receiving feedback from all the Ikki68 owners and hope to incorporate all the positive changes into the Aurora.

Additional Details
Just like the namesake, after each rebirth IKKI keyboards become better, stronger. Ikki68 Aurora features a gasket mount system due to popular request.
Polycarbonate has a high chance of cracking if you apply too much torque while tightening the case screws. This can also happen from repeated screwing and unscrewing. Thats why weve embedded copper sleeves inside the case for screws to thread on to, instead of the plastics shell itself. This will make the case more durable and less prone to cracking.
The default plate material will be either Aluminum or Polycarbonate. Please vote in the IC form for your favorite between the two. Additional plates in different materials will be available as add-ons.
Custom badges were hugely popular with Ikki68. Were pleased to announce that theyre making a come back in the Aurora. Each keyboard kit will come with a default badge, and you can buy additional badges as add-ons. There will be several designs to choose from. You can also send us your art work and have a one of a kind custom badge just for yourself!
We are currently weighing the pros and cons of keeping Ikki68 Aurora backwards compatible with the custom badges from the original Ikki68. On one hand we have a new mechanism in mind that will make the badges easier to swap out and machine, on the other hand this new mechanism will make the Ikki68 badges incompatible with Ikki68 Aurora.
We hope to produce the mold, iterate on the design based on testing and feedback and have some prototypes manufactured for streamers before launching the group buy. We will keep you updated on the progress via our discord or via email if you share your email address with us in the IC form. If youre interested in the Ikki68 Aurora, please fill out the IC form so we can take your feedback and ideas into account.
