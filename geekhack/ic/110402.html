Hello Everyone,
CaucasianWiz and I are excited to announce a project weve been working on for a little while now. Its a keycap set inspired by US Naval Aviation in the Pacific Theatre of World War 2. We tried to make it pay homage to its inspiration as much as possible while attempting to keep it within the appeals of a global community.
