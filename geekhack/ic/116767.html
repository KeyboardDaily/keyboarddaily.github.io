Hello keyboard friends, I am here today to show you a project that I am really looking forward to and wanted to do for a while, and am also looking forward to what you guys have to say about it.
Inspiration:
This is inspired by the Spanish DEC Space Invader keyboard, I have recently received one of my own and I have enjoyed it so far, and thought to bring some of it, specifically the Ejecutar key, to the MX scene to enjoy it on even more keyboards along with anybody else who would like to do the same.

Info:
I tried to get this as close as possible to the real key, but also have it be as compatible as possible with other keysets and keyboards, with this, I have decided to make it a r1 2u key, to replace a backspace or any other key where this will fit on your funky keyboards.
