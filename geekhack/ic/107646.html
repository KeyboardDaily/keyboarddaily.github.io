IMPORTANT1: Please note that although we are at the final testing phase, all things are still subject to change however unlikely, the final description and options will be given in the GB topic.
**Designs:**All related designs shown have been finalized and prototyping done,
unlikely to change changed and laughing at myself. but now itâ€™s really finalized.
Parts Included: Top & bottom frame, plate, PCB, back bar, type-C plate, underglow bar,
weight. [added some filling options]
Primary Material: Aluminum alloy (6063-T5)
Secondary Material: Polycarbonate for the underglow bar, (optional brass bar sold as add-on)
Brass for weight.
Titanium alloy(TC4) for type-C plate (the tiny red one in the first image) [may subject to change]
type-c bar switched to aluminum for it looks better and more uniform.
Color options: E-white, Black, Sand, Dark gold, Pink, Dark green, plus wildcard options (costs extra).
Firmware: QMK, VIA compatible.
Mounting: Top mount (possibly gummy compatible)
PCB Option:
1: Hotswap PCB with Kaih 1511 socket per-key-rgb lighting (reverse mount, north facing)
2: Solder PCB, single color per key led compatible. (south facing)
Plate Option:
1: 1.5mm aluminum plate for hotswap or solder version pcb.
2: 4.0mm aluminum plate for hotswap only.
3: two version(flex and very flex) of 1.5mm Carbon fiber plate for both pcb versions
(optional brass for add-on too, tbd)
