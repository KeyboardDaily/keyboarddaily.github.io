Cornelius
Gasket mounted 40% column staggered keyboard
Hello, everyone.
I’m foostan, let me introduce about the new own product “Cornelius”.
My main product are Corne Keyboard series those Open Source shared at https://github.com/foostan/crkbd.
Cornelius is a new keyboard designed based on the concept of the Corne Keyboard.

  • CONCEPT*

  • Compact with minimal number of keys required

  • Ergo-like layout to ease the burden of the body

  • Gasket mounted structure with a focus on the typing experience

  • DETAILS*

  • Typing angle: 6.5deg

  • Mounting type: Gasket mount

  • Materials: Anodized(or E-White) Aluminum Body, Anodized Aluminum(or Polycarbonate) Plate, E-Black Brass Weight, PORON Gasket and Foam

  • PCB: USB-C, hotswap or soldering PCB, configurable with QMK and VIA

  • COLORS*

  • Body: Black, Dark Gray or E-white

  • Plate: Black, Purple, Burgundy, Blue or Polycarbonate

  • RESULT of IC*
    Q: What color would you choose for the case? (Multiple Choice)
    A: E-White: 48.7%, Black: 43.6%, Dark Gray: 32.4%. The other answers are all less than 1%. As planned, the case in three colors: Black, Dark Gray, and E-White.
    Q: Which plates would you choose? (Multiple Choice)
    A: Anodized Aluminum Black: 50.2%, Polycarbonate: 36.0%, Anodized Aluminum Burgundy: 21.8%, Anodized Aluminum Purple: 16.7%, Anodized Aluminum Blue: 12.7%. The other answers are all less than 1%. As planned, the plate in four colors: Black, Purple, Burgundy, Blue or Polycarbonate.
    Q: Which PCB would you choose? (Multiple Choice)
    A: Hotswap PCB: 73.4%, Soldering PCB: 35.4%. The Hotswap PCB will be included by default as planned, and the Soldering PCB can be purchased as an optional extra.
    And other questions or requests
    Q: Brass plate maybe?
    A: Brass is not included in the options. I will release the plate data.
    Q: To be OSS
    A: It will not be released as open source. I will release the plate data, and may release the PCB as well.
    Q: Is it possible to use a keycap puller without scratching the case?
    A: No problem. There is a standard margin between the case and the keycap.
    Q: Rotary encoder support in the outer bottom corners.
    A: I do not intend to add a rotary encoder to this design.
    Q: Option for weight color
    A: I’m sorry. Weights are for E-Black only. Please understand that this is for risk hedging.
    Q: Please include VIA Configurator support.
    A: coming soon.
    Q: Is it an option just to buy the PCB and key-switch plate?
    A: The PCB and plate can be purchased as options.
