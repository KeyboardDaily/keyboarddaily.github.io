Hey Geekhack! baul here, finally bringing Safa to the public!

GB link
Safa is intended to be a lightweight, simplistic take on the TKL form factor (i.e. it’s just a rectangle), with my primary focus being feel and sound.
There’s no real backstory here, I wanted to make a TKL and now I’m here to share it with you!
شكراً
صفاء is Safain Arabic, meaning purity / serenity.

Specs

  • 5 degree, 88 key (F13) TKL.

  • Winkey and WKL

  • Adjusted front height of 18.5mm (excluding case feet - 3M 8mm bumpons)

  • Primarily top mount, secondary gummy worm

  • Fixed ANSI, ISO plates. Variable JIS Plate available (4.5u stabs are being worked on to possibly ship with this in time, but we still need a lot of testing. Prayge for it). Default plates will be Alu 5052 in case matching colors.

  • Additional polypropylene (PP) plates will be available. Specifically chosen for its more dampening effect over PC

  • Aluminum half plates are being worked on. I have tested fr4, it feels amazing but Alu will help mods. I will confirm this at a later date[/i]

  • Internal brass weight only - not exposed externally. I live in sandland, its hot, humid, and I never look at the bottom of the board. If its on my desk how can I? I wanted this to change the sound of the board, no other reason.

  • The weight and board overall were tuned to deliberately have reverberation throughout the case. No foam will be sold with this. If you want a quiet board, like my own preferential build, use PP plates with cherries or gat milk tops. Trust me

  • Color options*

  • 432c Dark Grey

  • 533c Navy

  • 188c Dark Red

  • E-White

  • Silver / clear coated ano

  • Raw (no ano)

A few QOL insights…
Brass weight coverage - made to cover as much of the alphas as possible. Being such a low profile board has its limitations, and this was as much coverage as I could reasonably achieve

Alignment pillars - a simple way to assemble the case and not worry about alignment. These cover both the X and Y axis’

USB tolerance - uh…yeah…kinda obvious. Also helpful for locating the port when not looking directly at it, as the entire ledge leads to this point.

The original Safa

The initial design from October 2020 was intended to have a simple external profile, which has carried over to v2. Each unit had 8mm uniform bezels and were all WKL units  I tried to make it work, but I was not happy with the aesthetic result. Note the engraving changes as well, you can see in the prototype photos noted above
***The following changes were made from v1 to v2

  • AFH reduced to 18.5-19mm from 20.5mm. Small, but significant change for comfort at such an angle
  • Side bezel reduction, likely what takes this from being an okay board to something that looks significantly sleeker
  • Engraving updates  rounded off, cuter font
  • Engraving location  moved to inner radii of Pause key
  • Weight rework  lower case height, therefore thinner weight. However, weight now covers more surface area in return, giving a more balanced tone across all alphas
  • USB rework: up 1mm on y axis. Now there is a groove on the back ledge to easily identify the location
  • Plate tolerances: much much tighter on north and south sides. Still room for wiggle, may tighten, but this will increase the risks of fitting
