MechWild here again with another Pro Micro based through hole kit, this time its a numpad. Our previous product, the Mercutio, was a 40% board which is intimidating to a lot of people because theres no numbers unless you make use of layers. So here you go, a little companion for your keyboard that does the numbers.
Renders

supplied by Reddit user Superalex051




The MurphPad is a numpad with a few extra features. There is a macro row above the typical numpad area as well as 2 keys adjacent to the numpad area. We are including a rotary encoder and OLED as well with this kit to squeeze in some extra functionality. And some of you might just want to use this as a macropad and weve thought of that too. The MurphPad will support an all 1u layout. If youve had your fill of knobs, you can use an MX switch in its place.
But wait, theres more! If that still isnt enough customizability, you can mirror the numpad layout.
