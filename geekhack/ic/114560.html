GMK Any Key
First things first, IC Form
Now that you’ve hopefully filled that out, I can answer “why?”

funny. Ok, so it’s not funny.
Besides trying to be a little silly, this keyset aims to add versatile modifier legends to navigation and macro clusters.
It has always been a pet peeve of mine when my keycaps don’t match their function, and I hope to ease that annoyance.
Additionally, if any other designer has a similar bug in their brain, this will create new molds if they don’t already exist.
Renders

This keyset uses stock GMK colors CR, WS1, N9, CP, and U9 to match popular BoW, WoB, Dolch, and Beige sets.
