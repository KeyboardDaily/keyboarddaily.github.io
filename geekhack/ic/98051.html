Hello everyone.
We are the DGX team, a mechanical keyboard maker from Vietnam.
This is our latest product: Titanium GR2 Plate: https://imgur.com/a/P3vEzJA
Some reasons why we choose titanium instead of other materials to make plate:

  1. Is is more flexible than aluminium and brass
    If you like the sound of brass plate, leave it as it is.
    If you want the plate to be harder and the sound to be more bassy, you can heat-anodize it.
  2. Color:
    By using different voltages, a wide range of unique colors can be produced.
    For example: We can make a Rainbow-colored logo like this:

    You can choose any logo, max size is 20x20 (mm).
  3. It is very strong and durable.
    We would appreciate hearing your opinions on this.
    If you have any questions, feel free to ask me.
