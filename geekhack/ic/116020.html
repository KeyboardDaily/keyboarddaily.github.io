Hello geekhack and welcome to our first IC

About Exit Plan
Exit plan is a Chiptune/dance album by Fearofdark that is made using tracker software. Some of you may know fearofdark from his song, Rolling down the street, in my katamari.
Inspiration
Having listened to this album many times, we felt a connection to it and were inspired to make this set to express the vibe of the music and album art. Going with JTK was an easy choice because of their triple shot keycaps to help us better express the feel of the album.
