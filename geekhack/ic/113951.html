Questions & Concerns Addressed
More

❀ Why aren’t your legends center-aligned?
✿ Because I do not like center-aligned legends.
❀ Why does your shift look like control?
✿ When I decided to take on this project, I decided very early on that I would be providing custom legends.
This includes icons. My goal was to establish a design language that remained cohesive, made sense, and
reflected my own self. These were my results. At the end of the day, they look nice and functionally remain the same.
❀ Why didn’t you provide text mod support for 40s?
✿ There are only so many kits that I can include with this set. As much as I would love to offer every kit possible,
that is just infeasible. Throughout this project, I have received an overwhelming amount of positive feedback
regarding my custom icons. As a result, I decided to offer icon mods over other options (blanks/text).
I apologize to those of you who are unhappy with this decision.
❀ Why doesn’t the QAZ kit support ‘à la mode’?
✿ I have found that the 1.25u P makes the ‘à la mode’ design look really ugly with a 6.25u Spacebar.
No matter how many solutions I tried, the overall design would have weird seams and/or
unbalanced flavors. While the diagonal pattern looks good with the split spacebars, I do not
think this justifies including an additional ≥9 keys to an already great looking kit.
❀ Can you make a kit without Strawberry/Vanilla/Chocolate? I only like the other flavors!!!
✿ No, that’s not Neapolitan.
