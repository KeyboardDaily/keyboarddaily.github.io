Assembly

The design is made up of 5 parts the top body, plate, bottom body, weight and, badge plate. when completely assembled only 4 screws are visible. the 4 screw holes line up perfectly with the 4 filleted corners to have a slightly industrial look to the design. The screws provide pressure to compress the o-rings slightly, the o-rings help to isolate the plate creating a different sound signature. The badge plate locks into the weight and is secured by grub screws instead of more traditional screws due to space constraints. The weight then screws into the bottom body again using grub screws once again. The 2 pegs at the back help for easier assembly for users.
