Inspiration

Hi I am super excited to present my fellow enthusiasts GMK Mika, my first keyset design inspired by my very own Shiba Inu Mika.
I totally stole her color theme for this project. Mika showed up in my life last year in June and has been a proud and stubborn member of the household since then.
This keyset is dedicated to my now best friend Mika and all of you dog lovers out there, I hope you like it!
