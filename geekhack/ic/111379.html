Hello, so this form is an interest check for the foliage deskmat. Pretty self explanatory.
The designs are mostly inspired by me not able to get my hands on GMK botanical deskmats (lol) but also designed around the upcoming IKKI68 Aurora in mind.
The colorways are choosen such that it compliments the Mint and Devoted colorway of the IKKI68 but also versatile to use in other set ups (eg. GMK Birch, GMK Tuzi, GMK Cojiro, Infinikey Vanir, etc.).
The centrepiece is a small reference to Singapore as it depicts Singapore’s national flower, the orchid, and a little red dot. This will be easily covered when your keyboard goes on top.

I am satisfactory with the design, hence unless there is a strong opinion against the design there won’t be any huge changes to it.
There is however a small divided opinion amongst my friends, some like it with the small detailed flower, some didn’t. Hence I am leaving this up to the community to vote.
Edit: I will be making some minimal changes to the design based on the early feedbacks I’ve gotten. Do stay tune to the updated design as I think it is an improvement from before.
