Hello, geekhack! This is JayP from Primatech proudly presenting our first keyboard, Lisa.
Lisa is a 65% alice-like top mount board made out of bent steel. The board is designed in collaboration with Smol from Void Case.
Details:

  • 2mm steel top and bottom

  • 1.5mm steel plate powdercoated in black, Black FR4 plate as an option

  • Powdercoated body, black or white

  • Top mount

  • 5 degree typing angle

  • Custom PCB with USB-C by Ergorius
