PLX
Gasket Mounted 60%
GROUP BUY THREAD

As our first keyboard design we wanted to do something sleek and minimal for our desk spaces. We’ve designed a compact board with a weighty feel and style that contrasts with its soft typing experience.
The PLX is comprised of a 3 layer construction. The top and middle cases are machined from solid billet 6063 aluminium and feature soft chamfered edges and modern rounded corners. The solid brass base weight is fastened through the plate and PCB to achieve an entirely screwless appearance.
Inside, your choice of plate material is mounted between gasket strips, with kerf cuts in the north and south ends of the plate. Each keystroke has a soft bottom out and quick return. Offered in winkey, winkeyless and HHKB designs our variable soldered layout will please any compact connoisseur.
TECHNICAL SPECS

Front Height: 20mm

Weight: 2.7kg built
Colours: anodised black | e-white | anodised red

**Top Case Options:**WK | WKL | HHKB
PCB: variable soldered layout | designed by 2Moons (Basekeys) | USB-C daughterboard | configurable with QMK/VIA | No LED support | Includes ESD Protection or fixed hotswap layout
Solderable PCB Layout Options: locked tsangan bottom row | split BS | split RS | ISO | stepped caps
Hotswap PCB Layout: locked tsangan bottom row | splits BS | split RS

PROTOTYPES
V2 Prototype
The V2 Prototypes feature subtle internal improvements which improve the building experience for the end user.
