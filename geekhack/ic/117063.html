Hello everyone, this is Hongoo from OneKey Studio.
This time we bring you our new 80%keyboard kit design- 流80-NAGARE80.
Inspiration
As a keyboard lover myself, I always wanted a case with a softer look. I did look into other designs with curves. Those are all great designs. Each one of them has its unique characteristic. However, I want a more reserved look, a delicate curvature with a powerful tension. So, I decided to bring this to life with other members of OneKey studio. Special thanks to friends who participated in my live stream. Your pieces of advice are very precious. ‘流’ is the Chinese character of Flow. Flow is not only the behavior of liquid but happens to other stuff in nature. I also get inspiration from the forms of wind and dunes.

Design

We made the width between the 60% zone and the functional zone the same as the width of the frame to get a tidier look for the working face. Bring out the theme by the wave light. The original design of the light was a wave cutout without the bars. To provide a digital feel and a more balanced look, we changed it to this look now. When you turn on the RGB, you get a wave. If you do not like the RGB, it becomes a wave hidden under the surface.

We wanted to give this design an accent, like a ripple on a calm surface or a breeze on a hot day. We put a slight curve on the bottom edge of the big rectangle instead of using an all-curved shape. To emphasize this curve, we used two-colored anodization to give it a highlight.

We worked hardest on the lines of the side profile. Side-profile is the most shown part of a design. After a lot of fine-tuning, we finally reached an agreement among all designers in our group. The straight line on the top is the start of all curves. The curve on the top part is the materialization of our understanding of the concept-NAGARE.
Concave shapes of the lower part offer a series of curves that supports the top curve. The diffused reflections of the anodized aluminum finish give the side profile a dynamic visual.


Parts and Structure
To reach the optimum even light, the soft plate of the logo light can affect the position of the slot on the PCB. (Subject to GB product)
The material of the PE sheet and the hardness of the tower gas silicon are in testing. (TBA in future updates)

Tower gas
Tower gas provides a softer response through the shape change of the tapered silicone pad and the plate. Compared to a gasket structure, tower gas has a more linear feel when tapped. Tower gas does not affect the installation of the gasket foam.
