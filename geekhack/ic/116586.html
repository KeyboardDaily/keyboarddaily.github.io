This is: artisan case  by sandy. After a few rounds of prototypes Ive near finalised a CNC stackable 6x6 case for artisans using a PMMA-like material. It can stack without having to store the lid aside.
I aim to provide a minimal yet quality feature-set, such as fillets, stackablity, and circular rings for holding caps while trying to keep costs to you low enough to buy multiple and carry it in stock.

In The Works
I am heavily researching injection mould based on feedback and popularity to decrease cost heavily. Slight changes were made for durability at the same time as refining quality standards with between two manufacturers for the CNC route.
FAQ
Feet?
There are no feet, with my current design it would impede stackability. There is minimal surface area on the bottom of the unit that will touch a table or other surface
Magents?
Currently the lid is laser cut. adding magnets would require I CNC the lid which would increase cost and change the profile of the unit, affordability is my main goal.
Max Artisan Height?
Currently the maximum artisan height is 21mm. In later revisions we may increase this to 27-30 if requested. I reached out to the tallest artisan maker I could think of and that height would accommodate theirs but it would make the tray very tall.
Discord?
I have a private discord right now that I am considering making a ping server but I do not have the bandwidth to handle moderation with the increasing influx of scammers and spammers.
