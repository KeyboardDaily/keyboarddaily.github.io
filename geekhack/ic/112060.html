Bag Information
I have previously worked on Keyboard bags for GMK Fuji and GMK Tuzi, I am here to introduce my newest bag, SA Dreameater.
This bag will come in the following sizing “fits up to”:

  • 40%: 10.2 x 3.5 x 1.5

  • 60%: 12 x 5 x 2

  • 65%: 13 x 5 x 2

  • 75%: 13 x 5.5 x 2

  • TKL: 14.5 5.5 x 2

  • 1800 Compact: 15.5 x 5.5 x 2

  • Fullsize: 17” x 5.5” x 2

  • Wide Bezel Addition: Add 1” top + bottom

  • Custom sizing also available.
    Bag Materials

  • Commercial Marine Vinyl - Thick and used for boat covers, treated to be fade resistant

  • Colors: Navy, Aqua, with Navy straps and navy interior.

  • NO ZIPPER Design - Feel safe that your keyboard will not be scratched or damaged placing it inside or removing it from the bag.

  • 1/4 inch premium foam padding

  • Waterproof Canvas Interior

  • Navy Canvas Strap with high quality silver metallic clasps

  • Cross Body and Top Strap Extensions available

  • Matching Attachable pouch and Standard Pouch available
