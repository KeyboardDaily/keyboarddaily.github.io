GMK Alexandria
Intro
There is no wealth like knowledge, no poverty like ignorance. Ali ibn Abi Talib
From us here at KeyLabs, an IC for a set weve been working on for some time now - GMK Alexandria.
This keyset takes its namesake from the Great Library of Alexandria - a tribute to the preservation of guiding principles like truth, knowledge, and wisdom.
The color palette evokes imagery of texts from a bygone era, with yellowed pages and worn leather bindings - yet standing the test of time.
Alexandria is an homage to great GMK sets of yore - and by that I mean actually fairly recently in the grand scheme of things, but lets go with it.
What we determine to be true evolves over time, and the human pursuit of knowledge is ongoing.
Alexandria will mirror that concept. Nothing you see here is final, this is an interest check after all.
