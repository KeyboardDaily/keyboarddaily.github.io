Hello everyone, I hope you’re all doing well!
I am very excited to finally bring this set to the public. I started working on this project back in July of 2019 and it has been quite the journey. For those of you who don’t know Moomin or Moomins is a show/book series that tells the adventures of the Moomin family and friends. For the keyset, I have mostly focused on the 90s TV series which takes place in the peaceful Moominvalley. I always loved how this series could make you feel so calm and tranquil, if you get the time it is a great watch.
It’s been a lot of work but this set will have full licensing!
EDIT: WE WILL HAVE A NORDE KIT

If you’re curious where the inspiration for the colors comes from, in the 90s show the main characters Moomintroll, Moomin Mama, Moomin Papa all had these nice soft whites with a drop of color and it was important for me to bring that to the keyset. Note: you can see more examples of this by Googling “Moomin 90s” or by checking out this opening song for the show HERE
