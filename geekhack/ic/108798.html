Hi everyone!
This is my first interest check and first design so any feedback is welcome. Anyways, onto the fun stuff.

I’d like to introduce you to what I call Let’s Tango! This board is a split ortholinear 40% board that uses the Let’s Split PCB.
I’ve been looking for a split planck style board with a nice case for a while and never found anything I liked so I decided to design one myself. This design was meant to be simple and cheap whilst also remaining high quality. It is an aluminium case with the following features:

  • Split ortho design using the Let’s Split PCB
  • 7 degree typing angle
  • Top mount design
  • Swappable plate
  • Swappable weight
    Currently, the case will only come in two colours three colours (Black, White and Silver), however, I’m open to more colours, if you’d prefer a different colour please let me know in the form below and if there’s enough interest I’ll see if I can make it happen.
    I’m looking to sell these as a kit with the PCB and everything needed to build the board (not including the switches and keycaps) however if there’s interest for just the case I can make arrangements for that too.

The kit will include (aluminium):

  • 2x Top cases
  • 2x Bases
  • 2x Plates
  • 2x Weights
  • 2x PCBs with all components to assemble
  • TRS cable & USB cable
  • Screws and rubber feet to assemble

Extras available include:

  • Weights - Brass , Aluminium, Polycarbonate (frosted or clear) For the first run, only brass will be available. See 28th December Update.
  • Plates - Brass , Aluminium, POM, Carbon Fibre, Polycarbonate For the first run, only brass will be available. See 28th December Update.

Here are some more pictures of the prototype: https://imgur.com/a/HoDK2hY
I am very open to change on this design and I’m happy to make alterations based on requests in this interest check. I have some further changes I’d like to make to the design now that I have a prototype. If you’d like something changed please do fill out the form and let me know and I’ll see what I can do.
Price: Aiming for (but hopefully lower) $180 USD for the kit + shipping with extras available (plates/weights) Unfortunately, I underestimated what each unit would cost to have in-stock, along with the price of raw materials increasing and currencies shifting. I’m going to wait for all the units to come in before having a set price.
If there is enough interest in this design, this will be sold as an in stock itemfrom Sydney Australia to allow for fast shipping times so you can get your product ASAP.

20th of November 2020
Hey Everyone,
Thanks everyone for the support so far, I’ve had a lot of suggestions on what to change/include so here’s what I’ve changed:

  • I’ve decided to swap the PCB to the Vitamins Included PCB by Duckle29, I’ve been working closely with him on getting this board ready and working. I recently got a prototype made of the revision 2.1 board and it is fantastic. This run will be the first time this revision board has been made so I’m looking forward to it. Changing the PCB to this board adds hotswap functionality, USB-C, backlit LEDs and it’s all pre assembled. I’ve been playing with it for a while and really like it and hope you all will too. Since it’ll be the first print run of this version, I’ll be looking to offer the PCB separately as well.
  • Added tenting holes in the base plate and I’m waiting on a few stand off samples to test out, then I’ll look to put together kits for tenting as an optional extra
  • Added pinstripe diffuse - this is going to be a thin piece of frosted plastic to diffuse the backlight from the PCB which will be an optional extra
  • Altered the weight design to add a bit of flare rather than the original - check out the pictures to see two different designs I’m deciding on and give me your opinion
  • Ran a few renders with different chamfer sizes to compare the differences - check out the pictures to see what you prefer
  • Increased the size (and shape of the cable outputs) of the case to allow the new PCB to fit
  • Added alignment poles, adjusted screw positions to hide screws and added rubber feet guides
    I’m super happy with the new design and will be interested in hearing your thoughts.
    So, from here, I’ll be ordering a new prototype design and will get a few colour samples from them, after that I’ll send out a new google form for you all to fill out for what colour you want, this will allow me to place an order that will hopefully get everyone the colour they prefer. Then i’ll be placing the big order!!
    Thanks everyone, if you’ve got any questions feel free to reach out to me
