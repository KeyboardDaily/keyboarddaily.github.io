IC form here https://forms.gle/Yw4EFGFNd6f6sVFv5
About The Switch
T1s are tactile switches with a 67g spring and a clear housing made from Nylon PA and a POM stem. Gold plated spring and contacts. PCB mount.
I have secured a price of USD $0.50 per switch with a 25,000 MOQ. At GB the price will be charge in GBP.
The group buy for this IC will be run through my store ( www.opticboards.com). Switches will be sold in packs of 120 (Currently £49.35 GBP). I offer flat rate shipping. £3.50 UK, £5.50 EU, £7.50 Rest of World.
