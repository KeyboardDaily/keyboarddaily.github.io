Hey everyone!
This is Moyu.Studio, re-introducing the Queen Switch interest check. We’re based in China and this is our second switch group buy. Our first was the Momei switch, which was run in China and Europe.

The Switch Colors: The housing will be a semi transparent red, with a black POM stem. The colors are inspired by the Queen position on a chess board.

Switch Details:
Linear switch
Manufactured by JWK
Top Housing: Polycarbonate (PC)
Stem: POM
Bottom Housing: Nylon
PCB Mount/5-Pin
No Factory Lube
Spring: A unique, 60g, three section spring
Details on the spring: Building on the popular two-stage springs, this is an entirely new type of spring, never before seen in a switch. This spring provides a small change in pressure during the press and a strong rebound. It’s a wonderful, unique touch.

We are currently working with the manufacturer on getting samples prior to the Group Buy launch. Please fill out the IC Form so that we can get more feedback on the Queen Switch prior to the Group Buy.
