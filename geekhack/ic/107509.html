This set was inspired by the band, Roselia. Based on the series BanG Dream! A media franchise that has an anime, concerts, music and a mobile rhythm game. Roselia is known for their gothic inspired designs, their colorful characters and their powerful music. The color scheme is based on the logo’s gradient and the band’s official color. The colors have been matched physically with a RAL book with official merch.

Who is Roselia?

Reference


Color Matched
Colors: CR Black, RAL 280 20 30, RAL 300 80 15
We did not reference the band outfits because in the game and at lives, they change every year. The main inspiration came from the logo and these colors are referenced from Bandori Fandom. These colors were used as a starting point and have been color matched with various official merch with our RAL Design book.




Kits
Kits are finalized.
The kits are designed to fit most 60, 65, 70, 75, 660, TKL, 96, 1800, Full-size keyboards in ANSI, tsangan/WKL, ISO, HHKB, Alice and more.
Note: we are reading your responses and are making adjustments to kits based on feedback. A new form will be made to vote on specific changes will come in the future.
The novelties are based on each member of Roselia. All novelties and logos were designed by ItszBrian.

Clarifications
-All novelties and the deskmat were created by hand.
-The guitar and bass resemble the instruments made by ESP Guitars but they are NOT scaled down versions (the actual scale models would not fit GMK’s requirements anyway). You can say they were chibi-ed in order to see the details on the keycaps.
-The rose actually took the longest time to create as it’s a critical part in representing the keyset and the one you see here was hand drawn to avoid any designs used by Bushiroad.
-I have purchased a license for the font French Script and modified it to create the Devoted logo
-It is highly unlikely an official license from Bushiroad would happen anyway since they directly produce merch themselves
I hope this answers all questions about potential copyright issues.
