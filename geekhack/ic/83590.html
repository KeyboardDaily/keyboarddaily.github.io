【IC】Revo & Dr.D Sandwich TKL keyboard

Hi,everyone:
I am Raymond from Revo Design. Today i am gonna show you our lastest custom Tenkeyless keyboard.
This is a Custom keyboard designed by keyboard enthusiast Dr.D and produced by Revo Design.
Less is more. Thats what Dr.D wants for his keyboard. After using other sandwich keyboards,he want to do something different.With no metal mat on the bottom,this keyboard weights over 2 kg. You will feel like typing on something that can not be moved.
Simple,elegant,something you will love for a long time. Thats how i feel about this board,and i hope you guys can enjoy it as well.
Lets see some pictures of the sample we made:



Features:
Aluminum 6063t
CNC made cases
Anode
7 degree slope
Ansi and Iso
Fully programmable
Full LED backlighting
The kit will include:
Upper case(wk or wkl)
Middle case
Lower case
Plate
PCB
Logo D(made of alu and anode)
Screws and rubber mat
Color option: black,silver,red,blue,gray,pink
Price: Around $260 usd (shipping and fees not included)
GB time: I am hoping we can run this one really quick.
This will run on EC and GH at the same time.
To be determined:
Shipping cost.
Payment method(I really don’t know much about this,if anyone can help with that,please let me know.That will be much appreciated!)
So what do you think,guys? leave a message or PM me,i’ll get back to you as soon as i saw them
Thank you.
I see you all interested in the Logo D…..For people who don’t like it, i’ll make a version of no logo on the top.
Update: The D is inspired by Initial D. About the PCB,we use a M87. It’s programmable via bootmapper. The middle layer is made of anodized alu,so you can chose from the color we provided.
I prefer with no back lighting on the PCB. They only look good in the pictures.
