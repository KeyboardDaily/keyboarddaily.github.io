:)Hello, World

Hello everyone
I’m back again,
after a period of hard work Tyche75 this work is about to meet with you!

The name Tyche
comes from the Greek goddess of fate,
who is said to throw good luck and bad luck at people. Like fate,
Tyche is pronounced close to the sound of keyboard tapping. So we decided to name it Tyche75





The whole design uses the most suitable 7° cut for big data. And keep parallel lines as much as possible.
This will make the sensory overall unity stronger. The head retains the horizontal cut, and the three-dimensional angle is also vertical sense.
But the trouble with such a design is that it brings a lot of processing difficulties.
So after the first draft and again revised the internal structure, and finally achieved the initial desired design characteristics.
And in terms of overall weighting, it will form a more stable knocking feel, but of course you can just put it up on the table.


In addition, the design of the nameplate,
the author wanted to be more in line with the theme of fate,
drew an artistic illustration, and produced in the form of PCB,
the bottom part of the nameplate to make the whole design looks more interesting.




I have started to enter the production phase
Expected to meet you in 45 days
I have prepared 4 colors
Next is the detailed configuration
Finally, thank you for your comments on the last IC

Basic Info.

  • Layout. 75%
  • Color: Aluminum with anti-oxidation surface treatment
  • Material. Top and bottom case aluminum, logo is Brass
  • Degree: 7°.
  • Structure: no plate spring washers
  • PCB: Hot-swap and C-type PCB (including LED part)
  • Plates：1.5mm PC
  • Weight: Nickel-cobalt alloy
  • Logo：Brass
  • Spacer: 3mm poron
  • Will use Type-C
  • Supports Bluetooth
