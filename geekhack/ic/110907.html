We are thrilled to announce one of our very first keysets, Tundra, which we have been working on for a while now!
This set was inspired by tundra biomes, notably the Tarfala Valley in Sweden, where you can find white mountaintops, goldish and dark brown soil, and vivid green plants rich in chlorophyll.
The release features two sets: A base set with ANSI, ISO, numpad, and a deep gold anodized aluminum artisanal keycap, which will also be available separately.
We decided to go with the SA profile, as it’s a smooth, robust, rounded profile, perfect for the vision we set out for this release.

About us:

We are a small company composed of keyboard enthusiasts and folks who work in industrial design.
Our goal is to experiment with various manufacturing techniques on different types of Aluminium, PBT/ABS plastic sublimation and dyeing procedures in order to bring unique,
one-of-a-kind keycap ideas to life. The brand aesthetic we are looking to build is of functionality, minimalism and designs narrated by a strong foundation of thought and story.
