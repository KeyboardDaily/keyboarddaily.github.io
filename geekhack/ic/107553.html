【IC】Haku - Top mount 65％

My first 65% design - well, not really the first, but the first to leave the drafts. Hopefully no more death threats avail…
Some relevant info to start:
Top mount (with support for burger mount, if that’s what you’re in to)
Alu case and plate, brass weight (case ano colors: silver or black; plate red ano)
7 degree angle
WKL only
QMK compat pcb
Wanted to do 20 units, 10 of each color (maybe this is a bit too ambitious considering it’s a bit “different”, in terms of layout)
Price: $375 - $450
Wide price range, but it was just a guess based on proto cost. I’ve yet to get the quotes because I was waiting to see if anyone would even be willing to try it out.
Here’s the layout I went with:

Basically arrived at this layout because I’ve been a fan of what matrix did on the 1.2og since I saw it and wanted it in a 65% form factor.
Eventually came across the ca66, but it released a bit before my entrance to the community and mechmarket is, well, yeah…
Also, wasn’t the biggest fan of its design language and I dislike when arrow keys aren’t aligned with the bottom row.
Messed around for a bit after doing some research and ended up with a uniqey c70-esque layout, with the obvious omission to accommodate the led bar.
Opted for 7U spacebar instead of 6U. Sacrificed a bit of bottom row symmetry but I like what I’ve come up with.
Apologies in advance for potato phone photos:

Two changes made from the proto. Removed the middle brass accent. Was annoying, no longer a fan. Changed weight engraving to something simple, to match PCB decoration.
Basic renders of colors since I got the proto in purple (originally wanted to offer purple but decided to move away from it):

In hindsight, maybe my first attempted addition to the design community should have been something more conventional, but I guess it’s too late now…
Generally, tried to keep things simple in terms of quantity and color offerings since I’m just one guy trying to do this for the first time.
Hopefully this can go well because I have some other designs I’d like to get out there as well.
My Phone produces garbage tier photos, so I’m working on getting better images from more angles of the proto.
