Hello and welcome!
This keycap set is designed around a famous photograph taken of the Earth titled, “The Blue Marble”. While the original photographer is unknown, it is still, “one of the most widely distributed photographic images in existence.” [1]. When viewed at a distance, the Earth is said to have the appearance of a marble.

Quote **Original NASA Caption, Saturday December 23, 1972:**View of the Earth as seen by the Apollo 17 crew traveling toward the moon. This translunar coast photograph extends from the Mediterranean Sea area to the Antarctica south polar ice cap. This is the first time the Apollo trajectory made it possible to photograph the south polar ice cap. Note the heavy cloud cover in the southern hemisphere. Almost the entire coastline of Africa is clearly visible. The Arabian Peninsula can be seen at the northeastern edge of Africa. The large island off the coast of Africa is the Malagasy Republic. The Asian mainland is on the horizon toward the northeast.

FAQ
Q: Where will this be dropped?
A: I am not entirely sure yet. But there is a good chance it will be on Massdrop or PMK, unless someone can help me organize it.
Q: Why did you choose the sculptured SA profile?
A: Sculptured SA (1-1-2-3-4-3) rows have different angles that make it very similar to the earth in many respects. While subtle, the shifts in angles are make it similar to hills. Plus the tall SA keys have a bit of a rounded bulge. Therefore I found it quite appropriate for this keycap set. (Plus SA is awesome )
Q: Why does/doesn’thave this key? [pic]
A: I am a usually a full keyboard user. All of these child kits have been developed with my own personal research and might not be perfect. I would not doubt that some kits could potentially be optimized better. If you have any suggestions I would love to hear from you!
Q: What color chips are being used in this set?
A: Two standard ABS colors (BDH & WFK), and two that will be matched with a specific Pantone color.
&
Q: How would I cover a…?
A: Each keyboard can vary greatly. But I have tried to accommodate as many different layouts as possible.
Full Size keyboard: Base/Alphas + Modifiers + TKL Kit + pH Tester
ISO keyboards: Base/Alphas + Modifiers + ISO / UK Kit (+ TKL Kit if TKL or larger, & + pH Tester if you have a Numpad)
Standard TKL: Base/Alphas + Modifiers + TKL Kit
Winkeyless keyboard: Base/Alphas + Mods + TKL Kit + Tsangan + Spacebars
75% Keyboards: Base/Alphas + Modifiers + TKL Kit + Tsangan
Infinity 60% Hacker/Standard: Base/Alphas + Modifiers
Standard 60% (ANSI): Base/Alphas + Modifiers
Standard 60% (ISO): Base/Alphas + Modifiers (Depending on layout, ISO / UK Kit may also be necessary)
Whitefox, Neutrino, RedScarf68: Base/Alphas + Modifiers + TKL Kit + Tsangan + Spacebars
Leopold FC660M, Clueboard: Base/Alphas + Modifiers + TKL Kit + (Tsangan OR Novelties)
JD40: Base/Alphas + 40’s Kit + Spacebars
Planck or Preonic: Planck Kit
ErgoDox: Base/Alphas + ErgoDox Kit
HHKB-like keyboard (Duck Viper, Duck Eagle, some GH60 layouts, some KMAC Happy layouts): Base/Alphas+ Modifiers + Tsangan
Q: Is this set compatible with Logitech G710 or Corsair Strafe?
A: No, not entirely sorry. There is no 6.5u spacebar included in this kit. You will need to find one or use your own.
Q: I have a suggestion. Where should I go to ask it?
A: Comment below and ask away! This GH thread is the ideal place to ask questions regarding The Blue Marble.
