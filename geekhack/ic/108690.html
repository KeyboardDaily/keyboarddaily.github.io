Hi guys, this is CMM.Studio Freather again. It is the third time greeting you guys this week, and we are dropping one last IC for the week.
As many of the community member wondered, what is the keyboard on the Rifen JTK NightSakuras IC renders, and how to get it. Well, after a long wait, we are happy to release the official IC for Fuji65 for international GB.
But hold on a second first, let me introduce NightSakura. NightSakura is one of our studio members, and he is active under same name in Chinese Keyboard community. He owns the right for this kit. It is not included in our studios line up.
The original concept behind the kit is pretty simple. Nightsakura has always love the Japanese element, and felt perhaps putting it on the keyboard would look lit. Therefore, a Japanese themed Fuji65 was born.
On the design side, the general theme is simplicity, with sakura flower badge changeable in character and flower. (Cuz his name is sakura!)
The backside consists of inert method weight. Kudos to TGR! To accompany the name of the kit, we designed a Mountain Fuji shaped back plate to fit the keyboard scheme.
