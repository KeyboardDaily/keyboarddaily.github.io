[Droplet]
A TKL Keyboard.
Hi, everyone!
I am Bahm, a keyboard enthusiast like you guys.
I have run several keyboard GBs in Korea, and now I am going to run my keyboard GB globally.
However, as I think many of you guys don’t know about me, let me introduce my works first.
BTW, I am running CNC machining shop by myself.
It’s a small shop but good enough to make high quality keyboards!
[Dolch TKL]
Dolch TKL is designed by a Korean, who’s nickname is Heok.
But this keyboard is made in my shop. Yeah, I made it.

[Bahmbi (night rain) Keyboard]
This is the first keyboard which is designed and made by myself.
The concept of this keyboard was ‘rain’ , so the logo on the right side is made with a concept of ‘raining’.
The name Bahmbi comes from my nickname ‘Bahm’, which also means night, and ‘Bi’ which means ‘rain’ in Korean.
This keyboard has a top mounted plate and has ‘Aluminum’ and ‘PC’ version.
I applied ‘diamond cuttings’ to the edge of the keyboards. Beautiful…imo.



[Jetstorm]
Jetstorm was my second keyboard which I designed and made.
This was the first keyboard in Korea which electrophosis was applied.
Duck0113 has supplied PCBs for this keyboard.


[Evelyn]
This is my most recent keyboard with ergo layout.

Now let me introduce my new keyboard ‘Droplet’







And I also made a proto.
Although this proto is not finished yet.
Anodizing and polishing will be done next week.




[Specification]

  • Typing angle : 7 degrees
  • Top mounted
  • PCB not decided yet, but this keyboard will support any B type 87 layout PCB(recommend A87.b)
  • Brass weight attached to the bottom surface of the bottom housing
  • SS weight attached on the upper surface of the bottom housing
  • There will be an engraving on the top of the arrow keys. BUT NOT DECIDED YET. Forget the engraving on the proto pics.
  • WK and WKL
  • Black, dark gray and silver
  • Plate with Aluminum, Stainless Steel and Polycarbonate (PC)
  • Estimated Price : 500,000 KRW (So let me see how the USD-KRW currency rate will be in near future) excluding shipping fee.
  • Keyboard with weights, plate, PCB and carrying bag will be included.
  • GB will start at Jan. 2021.
  • GB will be done in my Shopify site
