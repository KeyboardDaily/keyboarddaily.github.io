GMK Moonlight

The inspiration for this set came from my love for the great outdoors, specifically the ambience from the moon. This set uses a dark blue-grey to represent the cool color of the night, and a beige-gold to signify the yellow hue the moon gives off on a night in the wilderness.
The novelties have been catered to create a visual representation of the outdoors, with rocky mountains, toasty campfires, as well as camping!
Groupbuy beginning on May 21st!

Colors

This set uses a RAL Classic and RAL Design color, one of which has already been color matched (RAL 1002, GMK Rudy Accent).
Proposed Kits

GMK Moonlight has 4 kits, a base kit with full coverage, an alternate alpha kit, a novelty kit and a spacebar kit.
