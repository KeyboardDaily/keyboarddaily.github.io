It’s almost my first post here, though I’ve been a keyboard freak for about two years mainly on r/mk as u/okaboni.
Here I introduce a new keyset plan,
GMK Sakura Retro

Living in Japan, I really love to see pale pink cherry blossoms (sakura) and some fresh green leaves together on their tree. Combined with OG Cherry and GMK Classic Retro’s colorway, these moderate colors express peaceful mood in spring. Antique pink like GMK 3C is called “sakura-iro”, color of cherry blossom in Japan, so this pink is featured this time.
Colors
Colors are all GMK stock colors (L9 beige, U9 grey, 3C pink and AE green) to keep MOQ and prices low.


The pics were taken under cloudy sky and an incandescent lamp respectively.
Keys (v1.1)
Base kit

Supports standard ANSI, WKL, HHKB, 65%, 75%, 1800 and ISO UK for now.
Hiragana kit

Japanese sublegends kit which supports both ANSI and ISO.
