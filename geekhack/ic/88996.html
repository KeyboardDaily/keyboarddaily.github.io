Updated: 2/27 - Switch to Pantone C colors, got chips, scrapped novelties
Pantone color chips:

Hi!
**Disclaimer:**I am a complete noob and I have actually very little knowledge of how to properly run a GB for a keyset. I’m not a designer and I’m not even familiar with all the different layouts of keyboards. With that being said, I will take all the suggestions, critique, and help I can get to make this keyset a reality!
Inspiration comes from easter eggs. I’m a big fan of pastel colors, and thought these colors would make a lovely keyset. This has been stewing in my mind for awhile, and I wanted to get this IC out on Easter, but didn’t make it in time. I have a bunch of other things on my plate but I thought the least I could do is get an IC started.
I wanted to use 4 pastel colors: blue, purple, pink, and yellow (the colors in the GMK Pastel ‘logo’) but I can’t use a light color on another light color because the contrast is not enough to distinguish the letters. So I’ve opted for a pastel yellow + blue and then the pink and purple are darker tones. I understand this means using all custom colors and yes, I know that will mean at least a higher MOQ. But I believe!
Without further ado, I present..

Here’s a graphic I made of the keys that will be included in this set. The colors are darker in this graphic (most notable the pink) but I will try and update this with more accurate colors soon.
The background is an image that my artist friend Christy (BeKyoot) made (I’ve already asked and gotten her permission!) and you can find more of her cute artwork on her website and instagram.

And here’s a preliminary render done by thesiscamper! I plan to get more renders once the colors are 100% decided upon.

I expect the colors to be light so closer to the render than the graphic that I made.
I believe this supports 1800, 75%, WKL, ISO, and HHKB (in addition to the standard full keyset). Please let me know if I’m missing keys for those kits! I kinda just gathered the extra keys/kits from looking at other GMK sets and so I might be missing/have extra of something.
I want to run this via GMK, Cherry profile, but I suppose that’s not entirely set in stone just yet! I don’t think I’d go for SA but I suppose DSA might be a possibility.
Oh, and yes, I know apolotary ran an IC for pastel GMK but it had very different colors and I have already reached out and asked if me running this IC would be okay! So yes, I made sure I wasn’t stepping on any toes before posting this
Please feel free to share your thoughts and comments, and of course, if you’d be interested in a set like this.
Cheers,
Tiny
