Hello it’s me jebbra again
Quite some people are asking about the continuity of Mondrian keyset and at the same time I wanna expand Mondrian so it can be used on another layout.
Brief Introduction
I created this keyset to overcome the infinite layout of custom keyboardespecially ortholinearsby using a thematic blank keys using coordinated accents.
The inspiration came from one of the most famous painting of humankind: Composition with Red, Blue and Yellow by Piet Mondrian.
Base Kit

Nothing is changed in Base Kit, I think it is already a solid kit
Now there are another 2 kits that I wanna add to this round: Dox to cover Ergodox and another similar layout (Broadwalk, Unigo66) and Stagger to cover standard keyboard.
Dox Kit

Because this kit intended to be used with Base Kit, the accent provided in this kit is quite minimal to press the price.
Stagger Kit

Personally I still want to cull the accent spacebars because they’re already available on Originative and I believe their presence in this kit is adding a quite significant price hike.
Pricing
I haven’t ask any quot to GMK for this round but last round was about 70 or around $90 for the base kit at 150 MOQ. Nowadays GMK quote is different, raised about 10-20% iirc. So take this info with a grain of salt.
Disclaimer
Of course the future of all the kits are depending on market interest. Round 1 is already hard enough so Round 2 will only happen if the demand really there (or there are vendors that brave enough to cover them). So please, fill the IC form to accurately gauge our interest
