Inspired by the Saiyan warriors of the show and manga Dragon Ball, GMK Saiyan encapsulates Goku and Gohans dōgi uniforms and comes in two accent colors: yellow and black, for the days when you dont feel like going super. Included in the novelty kit are all 7 dragon balls for your number or function row. With this set on your favorite board youll be able to kick ass and still have Shenron on call in case you need your wishes granted.
This set will be double-shot ABS by GMK. Due to the necessity of using a custom color for blue, I am currently planning on using Massdrop as the vendor for this set. I’d very much like this set to reach MOQ and that is the most likely way it can happen. Additionally, they may be able to help in the case there are any licensing issues with FUNimation.

Uses a custom blue color on the modifiers (Pantone 2766 C). I will be getting color chips of this soon.
