Hello everyone！This is TT from OwLab.
Today we are introducing a new keyboard designed by my designer friend Nomi and me.
The origin of this keyboard is to commemorate the music band -OWL which I set up and music I loved.
We started to have this design idea in May 2020. After four months of development and modification, we set up a group buy in China in September 2020.
With good feedbacks, we decide to bring it to all friends in the customized mechanical keyboard communities around the world!

Colors:

  • Anodized: Black, Gray, Purplish Blue, LNY Red and LNY Green

  • Electrophoresis: E-White and LNY E-Yellow
    Specs:

  • PCB: supports QMK + VIA; equipped with ESD Protection; you can choose from one solder PCB or a hotswapped PCB in your package.

  • Materials: 6063 Aluminum and Brass

  • The Structure: Gasket-mounted and leaf spring plate

  • Typing Angle: 6 degrees

  • Front Height: 17mm.

  • Weight: 1.4 kg (built w/ alu plate)

  • Plate Thickness: 1.5mm

  • Knob Materials: Aluminum

  • Layout:
    Hotswappable:

    Solder:

  • Foam/Pad:
    A silicone PCB mute pad, a PORON PCB foam and a case foam come with the package for no additional charge;
    Don’t get confused, you don’t need any of those to build the Voice65%, all optional preference choices.


  • Plates:
    Full aluminum plate and a PE plate.

  • Weight:
    An external brass weight with PVD coating (Sand-blasted, not mirror finish) and Anti-fingerprint coating.

  • LNY* series come with a brass weight with a sand-blasted and black-coated PVD finish.
    Characteristics:

  • With such extension parts surrounding the plate, the plate will not directly contacting the case.

  • PCB, USB daughter board, RGB daughter board are designed to be separated to solve the problem of USB port moving up and down when installing the gasket-mount system.

  • Default function of the knob is controlling the light bar. The function of the knob can also be set in VIA as:
    · Adjusting the volume of computer
    · Adjusting the brightness of the switch RGB lights
    · Adjusting the brightness of RGB light on the daughter board
    6 functions of your choice by following the guide

  • Per-switch RGB and light bar are separately controlled. We have provided various light modes.
    Kit includes:

  • Case: one alu plate+one FREE PE plate, top case, middle case, bottom case and brass weight.

  • PCB: switch PCB, USB-C daughter board(installed), RGB daughter board(installed) and JST cable.

  • Other parts: silicone pad, knob(installed), fastener of the knob, Bottom EVA foam(0.5mm thickness as a part of ESD protection), PORON® gasket x 16, screws(installed), silicone light diffusion strip(installed), and light-covering EVA foam(installed).
