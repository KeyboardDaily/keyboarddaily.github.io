IC feedback form

The focus of this specific IC is on kitting. I’d like to get feedback on this now, so I can make several changes. I am waiting for SP to come back to me specifically with certain icon samples.
You know what 9009 is, and I wanted a basic, boring beige DCS set. I’ve been lazy since October, but now I want to push ahead. There’s the inspiration. Red alert’s color scheme, nor sleeper, were for me. This set uses Signature Plastics’ classic WYSE fontface, making for once damn clean look :nut: wait this isn’t discord…
WHY???

DCS does have much thinner plastic, and whilst this is (incorrectly) believed to be a detriment to quality caps, I have yet to find people who say they hate how it feels and sounds. Think of it as a more ‘chirpy’ cap, which simultaneously allows you to really feel the switch more. Simple physics, thin cap = less force dampening
