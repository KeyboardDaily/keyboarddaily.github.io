In the future I’m planning to release many other products and make acrylic really affordable for everyone.
Inspiration
The Bongo Box is an acrylic artisan box inspired by the original bongocat.
Before making this project, i started by contacting all related people to bongo the artisan Maker hello caps, the designer of the bongo case for keyboard and the creator of the original meme (StrayRogue) of the bongo cat. They all agreed to let me run this design.
IC FORM https://forms.gle/SCigbGmCBuVZRUqb7
The Product
At the moment the prototype has 7 layers, but realized that some SA caps won’t fit since the gap
from bottom to top is not big enough.
So I duplicated the middle layer (in the end it doesn’t change anything), you can look at the renders below.
