Hey everyone! We are thrilled to introduce our interest check for Chronos, a stylish and secure artisan case born out of our need for minimalistic design and obsessive attention to detail. Chronos is intended to enhance your workspace and display up to 40 of your most prized artisan keycaps.
Case Features

  • Displays up to 40 artisans.

  • Machined 6063 aluminum.

  • Fixed Anti-UV Polycarbonate Window

  • Unique magnetic closure.

  • Stem slot cell design.

  • Non-slip bumpons.

  • Dampening pads.

  • E-Coating or anodized finish.

  • Case Specifications*

  • Cell Size: 20 x 20 x 21mm (up to SA Profile)

  • Cell Wall Size: 5mm

  • Case Dimensions Standard: 185.5 x 120.5 x 29mm

  • Case Dimensions VSM: 190.5 x 124.5 x 29mm/*
    /*VSM = screw mount acrylic window version

  • General Information*

  • Pricing: Approx. $150 USD

  • Lead Time: Approx. 60 Days

  • Pre-Sale expected to start Mid-December

Interest Check Page, Gallery & Form
We would really appreciate it if you could spare a bit of your time to fill out our interest check form. Your valuable feedback will help us fine tune our design and allow you to be part of our creative process. We look forward to hearing your thoughts, and appreciate your time.
Updates:
1/5/21 - Thank you all for your amazing feedback so far. I just wanted to chime in with the current status

  • We have decided to upgrade the window to Anti-UV Polycarbonate instead of acrylic.
  • We are working on a stand, however we are not sure certain if the stand will be ready for the pre-sale. If not, it will be available as an add-on while the pre-sale is in production. The current stand design features an 11degree angle, however if you have suggestions on different tilt angles, please let us know
  • If you’ve filled out the IC form, you will receive an exclusive buy link before the public pre-sale. We want to be certain that those who have contributed with feedback have a chance at purchasing the case. We are very close to announcing the pre-sale, so please look out for that update shortly.
    Thanks!
