About
This set came to me as an idea playing on of my mobile games Azur Lane. The game is an anime based game with ships as anime characters fighting against an foreign / alien type of ships called sirens. One of my favorite characters in the game was Ayanami. The character is been known as the “Demon God” in the game and referenced as such in her own quote lines. The color scheme and novelties is based off the character to give notice White, Navy, and Red look using RAL. You can find images and references below and hope everyone enjoys the set!
