Note on colors: all renders are matched to physical color samples  see the photos in the drop-down below.
The color of the mods especially is extremely sensitive to light temperature. We tried our best to portray them in a neutral lighting condition.
Due to this, slight changes to the Pantones / RAL colors may be made during the color-matching process in order to deliver the set which you expect!
