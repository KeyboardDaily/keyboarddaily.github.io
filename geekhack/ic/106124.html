[IC] JTK AZURE

Hi, I’m relatively new to this community and I would like to share with you guys this colour set i have in mind.
I present to you JTK Azure.
JTK Azure was an idea I had after chancing upon RAL5009(Azure Blue), hence the naming of the set.
I decided to pair it with RAL9001(Cream) as the two main colours of the kit.
Furthermore adding PANTONE WARM RED C accents could add some interesting contrast to the largely Azure and Cream kit.
