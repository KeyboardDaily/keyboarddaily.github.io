GMK GameTime

GMK GameTime was inspired by the Lakers Jersey during the 2020 seasons:
Purple on Yellow: Original Colors.
Yellow on Black: Worn during playoffs and finals.
Color used in this set: GMK DY(Purple), GMK CV(Yellow), GMK CR (Black)

Kits
Note: Lightbase is Purple on Yellow. Please check out board renders for a closer look




Novelties and Deskmat notes:

Eura, the designer of novelties and deskmats for this set, suggested and I agreed that all of the profits
I earn from Novelties and Deskmats will be donated to one of these organizations below:
-BAJI (Black Alliance for Just Immigration)
-NAACP (National Association for the Advancement of Colored People)
-Alzheimer Association
-Wounded Warrior Project
-Aid Still Required
Eura will determine which organization above to donate the money to.
Im glad that through GMK GameTime, we can support people who are in need,
especially for what we all have been going through in 2020.
