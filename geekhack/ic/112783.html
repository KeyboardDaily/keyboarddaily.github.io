1. Details

  • 65%
  • Isolation gasket mount with leaf spring plate
  • 6.5° typing angle
  • 18mm front height (excluding front chamfer)
  • Footprint = 340mm wide x 136.5mm deep
  • Weight is between 1.8kg and 2.5kg depending on configuration
  • Arrow cluster
  • Rotary encoder
  • ANSI and ISO plate options (6.25u and 7u)
  • USB-C daughter board
  • Aluminium and Polycarbonate Case Options
  • Various Weight Options
  • Custom Knob
  • Expected Q3 Group Buy Date

2. Status
Update 16.05.2021: Lot’s of great feedback from the first IC form so far! I am currently working on other layouts for the PCB, as well as the plate design. Flex cuts are next on the agenda!
R1 prototypes have been received and are in the process of testing. I am happy with the Polycarbonate, Black and E-white but the teal didn’t come out as expected and will, therefore, be replaced with a Dark Navy. Some dimensions needing minor adjustments, this will be done in the coming days. This includes adjusting leaf plate design slightly and fixing the gap between keycaps and the case.
R2 prototypes will then be ordered to confirm that I am happy with fitment and with the colour outcome. R1 custom knobs will be ordered at this time too, along with deskmat prototypes.
Update 01.06.2021: I’m very excited to announce that Mekibo is on board to be the US vendor for Chapter 1 Group Buy!
Changes for R2 prototypes are going smoothly. I have also been working on redesigning the bottom engraving, and will release it once finalized!

Along with this, I will be offering a deskmat GB alongside Chapter 1 which is inspired by one of the many beautiful frames in the comic!
