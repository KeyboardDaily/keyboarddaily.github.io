Nemui - A 65% With a cute sleepy themed blocker.
Fill out the GOOGLE FORM here
GO TO THE GB POST

Now with wrist rest!

Inspiration
I wanted to make a 65% for myself to enjoy. However, working on case design while also working a full-time job led to me only being able to sit down and work on the case while completely sleep deprived.
This lust for sleep was the biggest inspiration for the board: cozy, sleepy vibes.
CASE SPECS

  • Colors: Black, Blue, E-White, Purple, Wine Red
  • Layout: 65% with a top right blocker
  • Typing angle: 7°
  • Width: 315.75mm
  • Height: 18.33mm~ (front), 30.34mm~ (back)
  • Weight: Approximately 1.5kg, depending on configuration
  • Plate mounting style: Top+leaf spring mount
  • Availability: 250 units MAX

WRIST REST SPECS
-Walnut wood with a white enamel infill and a clear varnish finish.
-Still deciding number of units avail for the wrist rest.
-Will be raffle format for this.

PCB INFO
The PCB for this kit was designed by KnoblesseOblige
ARM MCU, STM32 based
QMK/VIA compatibility
ESD protection
USB-C Interface
No hotswap avail
Note: Underglow is not supported as all units will be aluminum
The plate and pcb support a fixed layout for simplicity on design.
See below for all potential layouts.

Current plate materials include:
Anodized Aluminum
Sandblasted Brass
Sandblasted Polycarbonate
Plate files will be released once the GB is completed for anyone who wants other materials, but if you have a burning desire for a specific material – please fill out the google form.

PROTOTYPES/PRIVATE RUN
The 4th and final run of prototypes are functional! I decided to change the mounting style from top mount to top + leaf spring mount for the internals of the case. The result was the sound profile of top mount with quite a bit of flex at the same time, the spacebar feels really nice.
I also ran a private run with 9 other members in the community, the images below has the lastest prototype (purple) and all the other boards are from the private run.
