DSA Forested Nights is a set made mostly for 60% and similar boards with compatibility for Full Size, TKL, and various others through compatibility packs.
This set started as a personal fantasy inspired by a mix of the DCS Midnight keyset and the colors from a piece of pixel art I have been working on and off on for the past month or so. I decided that since I had no way of getting a set like this on my own, I would try and get a group buy going.
The set will be doubleshot ABS seeming how I have no way of getting PBT caps with the font being lighter than the base :/
Font will be the SP standard Gorton Modified, it is the cleanest font for keycaps in my personal opinion and I think it will look fantastic on this set.
As for the color of text, the plastic I am going with is a darker shade than what is found on DCS Midnight. It leans more towards green than blue, which can be seen in this picture here (The left color chip is what was used in DCS Midnight)
Link to color difference: https://imgur.com/a/6uBNk
I am still working on designing the novelty keycaps for this set. They will be a 1u key meant to replace the escape key in the same offset tealish color as the font and enter key. Control, Windows, Alt, and Function key, will likely consist of the tree pictured above, A mountain, and a moon, all with a circle bordering them too keep some level of continuity, but this is subject to change.
Do you want this set in DSA or SA?: http://www.strawpoll.me/13861432 Set will be in DSA.
Strawpoll for layout compatibility: http://www.strawpoll.me/13861425
