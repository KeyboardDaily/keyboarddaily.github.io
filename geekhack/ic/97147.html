About me
Hi Geekhackers. This is INETT STUDIO. We are a relatively new keyboard design studio founded in March 2018. The QQ group id (if you know what it is) is 528509432. This is my second IC after Triangle 103. The Triangle 103 GB, unfortunately, only happened in China due to the 2 months limit of new accounts on GH. Hereare some photos from the Chinese GB if you are interested. Now we bring our newest design to you.



Cross 70%
Cross 70% is a board features with a special semi-embedded top&bottom structure and a unique 70% layout. With different color combinations, the semi-embedded structure can offer numerous possibilities from the top and side views. On the top right corner is a nameplate similar to Triangle 103. I really appreciate Pakay and JJ ‘s help.
