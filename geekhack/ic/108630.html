Hello GeekHack
This is Patrick at NEO Keys (instagram: neokeys, GIVE AWAY will be done through instagram; Discord https://discord.gg/2MCKpQQ). I’m here to present you our first design Palette G67. The project started back in June and has gone through multiple stages of material test, design and samples and we’re quite happy with what we have so far! And we’d love to hear some feedback and input from the community before release.


Case Features

  • Acrylic sandwich structure, with fully isolated, gasket mounted switch plate. Switch plate is supported by 6 silicone and 6 EVA gaskets and completely hidden from sides of the case.
  • Laser cut acrylic case plates
  • Switch plate options: Carbon Fiber, Brass
  • 6.5 degree typing angle
  • 18 pre-set case color schemes and more to come..
  • Fully customizable case color. 20 colors for each of 11 acrylic layers, total over 409,600,000,000,000 possible combinations. And more to come..
  • Countersink holes only. All mounting screws will be flush with case surface.
    PCB Features
  • QMK programmable
  • Hotswap and soldered(ISO, 7U spacebar, split BS compatible)
  • Per-switch SMD RGB light
  • RGB blacklight
  • Center mounted USB-C port
    About the group buy
  • What’s included in the kit?
    Kit comes with 11 layers of acrylic (7 layers of case body, 4 layers feet), PCB, switch plate, silicone and EVA gaskets (with spare), titanium coated M2 screws, brass posts, allen wrenches, USB-C cable, a microfabric cloth
  • How much?
    Kit in pre-set color scheme with carbon fiber plate - $140
    Full custom color scheme +$10
    Brass plate +$10
    Shipping is not included. Expected to be $50 for US and Europe. Board will be shipped from Shenzhen China.
  • How to place order?
    Custom features are far too complicated to be handled through order forms, so we set up a website for this group buy, link will be posted in the groupbuy thread.
    And here is a preview of color customization interface. Sorry for not having a real time rendering page. When GB starts each day I will run a few renders requested by customers and post on IG.
  • MinOQ and MaxOQ?
    Groupbuy will be on when order quantity hits 100. Maximum orders will be capped at 200.
    First 50 orders will be shipped with a free acrylic lube station in random color.
  • When?
    Groupbuy will start within a week or two depending on IC result on Oct. 23 Friday (as my account was registered on Aug. 21 and only 2 months old account is allowed to post in GB) and open for orders for 1-2 weeks depending on order numbers.
    Lead time should be somewhere around 60-90 days.

    How does the gasket structure work?
    Here are some photos to explain the gasket structure.
    Plate locates between second and fifth layer, supported by 12 silicone and EVA gaskets which are taped to the plate itself. The plate is completely hidden from all 4 sides of the case, giving the user the freedom to customize case color scheme.


    Dimensions of the case and typing angles?
    Here are some drawings and numbers. Opening for USB-C cable is 12mm wide.

    By using 3 or all 4 layers of feet, you can have 5.3 or 6.9 typing angle.

    Weight?
    Complete board with brass plate weight - 810g
    Copmlete board with carbon fiber plate weight - 630g
