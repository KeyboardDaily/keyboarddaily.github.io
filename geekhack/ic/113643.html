Hello everyone!
It’s my first post here and I am thrilled to introduce you the Corgi65, the butt keyboard!










Corgi65 specs:
Top case options: Option 66: Badge + WKL / Option 67: No badge + WKL / Option 68: No Badge
Materials: Aluminum top & bottom case
Typing angle: 4 degree
Mounting method: Top mount
Plate: Silver aluminum, black aluminum, polycarbonate or Brass (purchased separately)
Case colors: E-white, Black, Grey, Silver, Blue, Green & Pink
Badge colors: Black, Silver, Grey, Green, Blue or Pink
Weight colors: same as badge or Green, Blue, Silver & Pink
Note: PCB is not included in the kit, can be on request
Compatible PCB: DZ65 soldering(for all 66, 67, 68); DZ65 V3 hot swap(for 68); CZ67(for 68); CZ68(for 68) and more
What is in the kit?
1 x Aluminum top & bottom case
1 x Weight
1 x Plate - silver or black aluminum, polycarbonate or brass purchased separately
1 x 3.5mm poron foam
1 x Bottom foam
