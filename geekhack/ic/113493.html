GMK Cascadia

IC Form Here
Themed around winter in the Cascade Mountains of the PNW, Cascadia aims to be a clean, minimal set,
offering simplistic and straight forward kitting that provides the best possible balance between support and value.
Colors
GMK Cascadia uses entirely GMK stock colors, with WS2 on 2B mods, N9 on WS2 Alphas, and WS2 on TU2 mods.
The use of only stock colors does two important things – it helps to keep the cost of the set as low as possible, and also avoids the sometimes complex and slow process of color matching.
By using only stock colors for this set I’m hoping to help combat some of the recent sentiment about longer lead times and high cost often associated with GMK sets.
Kits
Base Kit

The Base Kit provides support for all common layouts (60, 65, 75, TKL, 1800, Full Size, etc), as well as offering 3 key 40s support and an Alice B
Spacebars

The Spacebars Kit offers greater support for Alice, 40s, Ortholinear, and other less common layouts
Novelties

The Novelties Kit has been kept on the smaller side in order to maintain affordability.
Novelty designs by SpikedSynapse
