FAQ’s:
More

Q: Why make a large set? Why not just break it up into single colors?
A: Organization and communication ease. It is far easier to invoice/pack orders in terms of complete sets of 16 keys than breaking it down to each individual colorway. Mistakes can be minimized using this method, and MOQ’s are easier to obtain when each order can count across the board and not simply to one colorway set. This also allows for a lower MOQ overall and will thus ensure that the entire profile set will be made.
Q: Can I buy more than one set?
A: Yes. You may purchase as many sets as you like. Remember that US shipping will be included in the price of each set, and only a $10 charge per order (not set) for shipping International regardless of the amount of keycap sets purchased.
