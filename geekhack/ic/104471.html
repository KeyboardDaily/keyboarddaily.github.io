Meet Slice
60 Percent Split Keyboard by 2Moons
The Alice has been split.

Affordable and Beautiful
Designed to be elegant, yet cost effective. This is a true DIY keyboard but uses nearly all SMD components. Even the RGB version which includes dual OLED displays and is based on the Corne, uses mainly surface mount components. Initially in acrylic and stainless steel, the curves give it style, the build makes it a challenge, and the parts make it affordable for anyone. Though not ground breaking initially, upgrades have been made. The non-RGB version is simple and easy to build.
