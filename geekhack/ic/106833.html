【IC】GMK Lasaka

Hi guys!
I’ve not really been active as a creator, but here today I present you with my first key set that I hope to get in production. I call it Lasaka. Lasaka is a combination of the words Laser and Osaka. I took inspiration for this key set from the two key sets which make this name. GMK Laser was such an amazing set, and I had a lot of inspiration from that, but the main thing this evolved from was my original set, Osaka, which didn’t pass an IC back in May of last year. I’ve put a lot of time and effort into perfecting the colourway, and I hope that with all your help and support we can get this key set launched next year! I would love to hear back from you guys on what you think about the colours and your general feedback on the set.
P.S. For you weebs out there, I am thinking about adding Japanese subs to this set, but wanted to get some feedback first before I did that. Might do a similar thing to GMK Laser and have a separate kit for that. Let me know if you guys want something like that, and also don’t forget to let me know what other kits y’all might be interested in.
Allons~y
After some deliberation and feedback, I think we are going to go ahead with cream coloured legends. More feedback is welcome and appreciated!
