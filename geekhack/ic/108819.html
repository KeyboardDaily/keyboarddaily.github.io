The Brick 65





Above image is a PC Brick version. You can put ARGB LED Strips below that PC Brick.
Imagine light coming up from bottom and you put your lego figures on that!

You can check the grooves below the PC brick.

The render on the above image is the initial design of The Brick 65(typing angle with 6 degrees).
Honestly speaking, I like this design much more.
But…if keycaps are put into the keyboard,
the keycaps may hinder the brick. And it will not look so good.
So I raised the brick upper surface 10mm.
But still I am interested on the initial design, and may develop further with a bit higher typing angle.

Various bricks I am gonna make.
I Will provide variety of bricks,
including bricks for artisan keycaps,
bricks for lego figures, tray bricks,
and a brick with function keys and knob(if I can find a PCB supplier to make a PCB which supports all that functions) etc.
If you have a good idea for bricks, please visit my discord server and let me know.

Above pics shows The Brick 65 with a transparent PC brick for artisan keycaps
and a half sized brick.
[Specification]
 Typing angle: 6.5 degrees
 Material

  • Upper case: Alu 6063 or 6061 (Not fixed yet)
  • Bottom case: Alu 6063 or 6061 (Not fixed yet)
  • Brick: Alu 6063 or 6061 / Polycarbonate / Wood / and other materials !
     65 Layout
     Will support 2 X ARGB connectors (which you can see on the motherboards) on PCB
  • There are grooves below the brick for ARGB LED Strip installation
     Will provide various bricks !
     Aluminum plate. Will support stepped caps/split backspace/1.75u and 2.75u right shift, ANSI/ISO, Screw-In-Stabs
     Burger mounted on upper(top) case
     PCB - Currently On-going
  • Supports ANSI/ISO
  • Soldered or Hotswap NOT fixed yet
  • Screw-In-Stabs
  • Supports 2X ARGB Connectors for ARGB LED Strips
  • Will be Powered by QMK
  • Will support VIA Configurator
  • USB-C on a daughter board
