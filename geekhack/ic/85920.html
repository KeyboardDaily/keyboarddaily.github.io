Project is on hold indefinitely (probably never happening :c)
Hey there everyone! I’d like to introduce you to an idea I’ve had for a while. I call it, GMK Carrier at Sea.
This set is a combination of two of my favourite colours, blue and grey. But I was also inspired by the image of a military aircraft carrier at sea, just like the one below.

The colours used are 2B for the grey, TU1 for the blue and CV for the yellow. At the moment, the set should cover most staggered layouts available. There are no plans for ergodox, planck, or other ergonomic keyboard support for the moment, but if the interest is there it can be added as an addon kit, same for an international kit. Novelties might also be added in the future, in the base set or as an addon, depending on the interest and the number of novelties.

Keep in mind that the current render is imperfect, notably when it comes to the icons on the enter and shift keys. It is an early render and should be replaced by a higher quality render in the future. If you believe other keys should be included in the base set, feel free to leave a comment.
As always, constructive feedback in appreciated. Thank you for looking at my project.
