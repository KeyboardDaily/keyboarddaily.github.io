GMK Vintage/Modern Cherry Logo Novelties Pack

Update
Final kit render. Color scheme: 1) Vintage, 2) Black on White/CR on WS1 (GMK Black on White,etc.), 3) White on Black/WS1 on CR (GMK Minimal, etc.) and 4) CR on U9 (GMK 9009, GMK Classic Retro, etc.)

  • Vintage

  • White on Black/WS1 on CR (GMK White on Black,etc.)

  • Black on White/CR on WS1 (GMK Minimal, etc.)

  • CR on U9 (GMK 9009, GMK Classic Retro, etc.)

  • 11-13-19:* After some initial thoughts, I am planning on running this with GMK first. Will be a great addon pack for GMK 9009/classic beige retro GMK set with U9 base color.

  • 11-14-19:* Add LED to R1 logo.

  • Preface*

  • Xin chào,*
    This is the Interest Check for the Vintage/Modern Cherry logo addon kit. I always dig the vintage look of the cherry logo. After some thoughts, I think I might just run this with GMK first. The goal is to pair this a versatile, cross-compatible vintage Cherry add on kit that you can pair with your favorite GMK 9009s or pretty much any retro vintage GMK beige set out there.
