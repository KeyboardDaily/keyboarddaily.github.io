Introduction

Coming up with a board name was hefty. As I look around my house, I’ve decided to name it my plant, which is a Cactus Angel Wing. So then it gave me an idea as a board name and would confidently attract the board name to the audience. I’m a plant lover, surely being one, contributed something to the hobby. )
About Max

Hiya there keyboard enjoyers! My name is Max, the Founder of max works. Or you can call me Asti as my other name. And I started getting interested through the hobby beginning of the pandemic 2020. I’m based in the Philippines. I’m glad I explored this area of the hobby as I met new friends, and decided to start a group buy myself. Cactus will be my first ever project and surely there will be many more to come like Shiba TKL! (partnerships too)
In Depth of Cactus

The board was designed by hali/#4045, Cactus layout is an XT - FRL - WKL - TKL board.
Material of the board is 6063 Aluminum. And both brass and aluminum will be sandblasted
Winkeyless option only
There will be 4 colour options: Lilac , Pink , Green and Silver.
INTL Price: $325 + shipping
EU Price (With VAT shown upon checkout) $375 + shipping
It’ll consist of 78 keys, with an option of split backspace and split right shift
Top mount
6° Typing angle
Cactus Inclusions

The inclusions are:
1x Custom Carrying Cactus Case
1x Cactus Unit (Color of your choice)
1x Solder PCB with ANSI/ISO Layout Designed by RMI-KB
1x Daughterboard made by Croktopus that follows ai03 standard
1x Aluminum Plate
4x Bumpons
1x Hi-Chew Candy
Hex Screws
Internal and External Weight



Side Profile, PCB, and Mounting points
