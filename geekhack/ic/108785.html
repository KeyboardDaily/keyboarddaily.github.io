    Hello keebs, i’m Geon
    I am very excited to introduce my first foreign market product, Vanquish65.
    I run a CNC factory in Korea and now I’m producing F1-8X keyboards and selling them.
    The Vanquish65 is my second keyboard in a new attempt, and will show our direction and philosophy.
    It designed as a 65% tray-mounted keyboard with a torsion beam structure.
    I’m sure, it will eliminate prejudice against tray mounts.
    Sorry, but it is difficult for me to communicate in English, so please use the link below for quick reply.
    Discord - https://discord.gg/Znt2Fdn
    Instagram - https://instagram.com/geonworks

    The housing is single-structured with the material A6061-T651

    The Vanquish65 is a 65% keyboard with supporting two types of CAPS LOCK, BACKSPACE and SPACE BAR (6.25U and 7U).

    PCB will be manufactured exclusively.

    A total of five pcb brackets are used and a variety of materials are available as options to change strength.

    The section was enlarged so that it could be seen directly rather than explained in words.

    I changed the exterior design by the opinions from the discord. Because I am focusing always more on basic things than the exterior design.




    It is the keyboard that focuses on usability, therefore excessive logos and names of keyboards would be not printed or engraved.

    Screws are invisible from the outside.

Vanquish65 Specification :
Housing Material : Aluminium A6061-T651
Weight Material : Aluminium / Stainless / Brass
PCB Mount Material : Alumnium A5052-H32, A6061-T6, TBD
Plate Material : Aluminium and Brass and FR4
Mount Type : Torsion Beam Structure Tray Mount
Angle: 7.0 Degrees
PCB: QMK Compatible, Solderable
Color : Black, Gray, Navy, Burgundy and White
Price: Under $300 without Ship,VAT,White color
Quantity: 300~500 units
