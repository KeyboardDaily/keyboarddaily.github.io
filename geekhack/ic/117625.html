Hello friends!
It’s our first time posting here in Geekhack so would appreciate any advice that is given and please be gentle with us
ktechs is a Singaporean-based keyboard mods/accessory vendor looking to bring in an interesting proposition for all things mechanical keyboards.
We’re taking our first foray into custom mechanical keyboard switches with the S1 switch.
S1 aims to lower the clackish sound profile while providing a loud, satisfying bottom-out experience with a long pole stem.
We are utilizing a HPE Top housing, a proprietary blend by Tecsee that seeks to better UHMWPE.
The bottom housing utilizes another custom blend from Tecsee and the material it’s based on is unreleased at the moment.
Disclaimer: 26/6/2022 - Post date: I would like to say that we’re not really active in Geekhack but we’ve found that some of the material blends and decisions made are really similar to that of the Honeydew IC that’s currently ongoing. I’ve reached out to them and clarified that we’re not looking to replicate their work/work based off their project ideas as we’ve started worktesting/discussions and sampling in the Apr/May window.
We’ve already jumped over to the samples release stage with the focus being placed on the handfeel as well as the sound and performance of the switch and currently have these samples on sale through our online store:
https://ktechs.store/products/ktechs-s1-switches
Do note that our current selling price is low at 0.7SGD at the moment to facilitate sample release into the market
Our intended GB Pricing is estimated to be 0.8-0.9SGD per unit to be confirmed based on Tecsee’s quotation. Est 0.6 to 0.65USD
Through the release, we are collating feedback from our discord server while requesting sound samples and thoughts/opinions on these switches to improve the switch’s performance.
25/5/2022 - Increasing the amount of lubricant placed on the switch by the factory. This is basically due to the usage of POM as a material.

  • We’re considering changing the springs over to Gold Plated 2 stage springs from Stainless steel 2 stage springs to bring down the spring ping from feedback.
