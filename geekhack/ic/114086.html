**Dino104
** IC Form ﻿You might be asking, why full-size? In the current state of the hobby, there is a lack of full-sized boards, and the idea behind the Dino104 was to help fill that void a bit.
The Dino104 features a seamless, wide-bezeled design that gives the board a large presence. Dino features designs inspired by the Google dinosaur game on the top bezel as well as on the back of the case. Case designed by nFaiz with the help of ChickenMan. PCB designed by Olivia
