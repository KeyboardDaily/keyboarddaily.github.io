The proposed Zippered Hoodie (model/# SS4500Z) is by a local shop called Independent Trading Company ( http://www.independenttradingco.com/collections/mens1-all/products/mens-midweight-zip-hooded-sweatshirt).
“Our not-so-standard Men’s zip hooded sweatshirt is made with soft, midweight fleece and generous fit for comfort. Features jersey lined hood, heavy gauge drawcord with metal eyelets, /#5 metal kissing zipper and 1x1 ribbing at cuffs and waistband.” Construction is 80% cotton and 20% poly, so there will be some shrinkage. Of note though is that the fit on these is oversize, so you should be good with your standard size - it will be a loose fit.
The design:
I am going to keep this simple and to the point. I will be doing the hoodies in black with a logo on the front left and back.
