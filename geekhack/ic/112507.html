Hello everyone, this is the interest check for GMK Night Tare.
Night Terror + Nightmare: Night Tare
IC FORM
COLORS
The set uses the best of both worlds, blue calming alphas in order to create a more calming feeling from nightmares, and a dark navy mods to symbolize darkness and nights.
The set uses RAL 5002 for the alphas, Pantone 282 C for mods, with Pantone PQ - Green 0921C and Pantone 2708C for accents.

Novelties:
These will be UV printed, and yes, that’s a front print novelty.
There’s a lot of symbolism in the novelties so here’s some background on them:
More

  • Demons are very apparent in both sleep paralysis and nightmares (Demon novelty)
  • A lot of nightmares are about death (skull novelty)
  • Tired eye, well, from staying up due to nightmares and sleep paralysis
  • I did a lot of research about nightmares etc, and found out that there are sigils (symbols) to prevent things from happening, therefore I thought it’d be a nice idea to have a sigil that prevents nightmares (Symbol novelty)
  • The wires represent not being able to move, being stuck in one place, going back to that feeling you get when you experience sleep paralysis where you’re not able to move.
