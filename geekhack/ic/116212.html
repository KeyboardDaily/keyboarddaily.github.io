La+ (Laplus) - 65% Gasket Mount Keyboard
Designed by Mecha Maker, in collaboration with AEBoards and Keyboard Treehouse
IC Form here: https://forms.gle/ks73KM7GdDBBFcDc6

Photos: La+ Prototype 2
About me:
Hi all, my name is Javan and I’m the designer of the La+ Keyboard. I’m new to Geekhack but have been running keyboard building and switch modding services as Mecha Maker since 2020 while studying to be an industrial designer. During this time, I set out on a journey to design my own keyboard to get more familiar with CAD, experience every step of taking an idea through to a concept and manufacture, and well, because I really wanted to make something of my own!
The La+:

  • Semi-seamless two-tone design with curved front lip
  • 7.0° typing angle
  • 6063 aluminium top and bottom case
  • 5052 aluminium plate, white POM plate being considered
  • Sandwich poron gasket mount (gasket / plate / gasket)
  • AEBoards feet
  • Compatible with Constellation PCB
  • Blockerless 65% layout. Stepped caps and split backspace supported
