Q. Where’s the f13 key?
A. Top right of the base kit.
Q. Replace base kit mods with N9?
A. Originally I wanted to make the set with N9/black mods. This idea got some criticism for copying other sets, thus swapping the colors. I’ve seen the set with N9 mods and it doesn’t feel like the Hanok I designed anymore nor the vision I have for this set. I will not be swapping the colors at this point in the IC nor adding a 2nd base kit with N9 mods.
Q. Mono Hangul alpha?
A. Will not be adding due to sales from other sets.
Q. What is the estimated price for the sets and deskmat?
A. I still have no idea but it’ll be in the price range of other kits of the same size. Pricing was summitted on the date listed on the change log. Hopefully pricing will be announced soon.
Q. Dark deskmat?
A. Yes
Q. Rama cap?
A. I need the GB date to be announced in order to work with rama. Hoping for rama cap tho… Thinking of a black roof tile design for the rama currently.
Q. Separate Hangul from Latin and make an additional kit?
A. No
Q. Ral 1015?
A. After looking at RAL color ring, 1015 was a little too red and went with 2028, after looking at colors for a long time.
Q. Why the change of vendors?
A. Unfortunately GMK stopped shipping to new vendors and most of my vendors need to pay double shipping. Thus the change of vendors sadly.
