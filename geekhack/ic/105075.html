MAK WongBox
Introducing the MAK WongBox, a budget friendly artisan case. The WongBox is a 7x7 case with an acrylic lid that can be had in a plethora of colours. When we say budget, we mean it; this one won’t hurt your wallet. We are aiming for $30-35! Oh and did we mention this GB will be counted in weeks and not months?
We have developed a high quality 3D printing production process for the WongBox and we are excited to share our first prototypes with you.


Full Album - https://imgur.com/gallery/1lAPFMo
Why did we choose 3D printing as our production means? That’s easy: it’s cheap, durable, fast and looks great. On top of that you can choose pretty much any colour you want to show off your collection! For our first print run, we would like to hear from you on what colours you would like to be available. Once we have delivered the first run, we plan to open this up and have them available to purchase at any time with additional and custom colours available. This is the first product line from MAK and you can expect some more exciting things down the line!
Product Specs

  • 7x7 artisan case

  • 17cm x 17cm x 2.1cm

  • 3D printed base, acrylic lid

  • Standard colours: white, black, grey, purple, blue, pink, red

  • Premium colours: Carbon Dust, Stone White, Blazing Copper, ZealTM Blurple

  • Lid colours: clear, smoked

  • Estimated Pricing*

  • $35: Standard colour + lid

  • $50: Premium colour + lid

  • $13: Desk display stand

  • $10: Extra lid

  • ~$15: Shipping to Canada and USA (other destinations will need to be calculated)

  • Improvements from IC Feedback*

  • Increased max artisan height from 1.6cm to 1.9cm

  • Increased the size of the finger slots to make the lid easier to remove

  • Created the display stand!
