As a continuation of my previous work from https://geekhack.org/index.php?topic=113053.0:
I have been funding new production runs of Elmo’s QMK-compatible modern drop-in replacement PCB for the AEK II / AEKII ( https://github.com/kb-elmo/aek2_usb). I now have a limited production run of 25 GREEN PCBs in-hand , plus a few leftover BLUE PCBs from last time.
Both runs (run 1/blue and run 2/green) PCBs are lead-free RoHS compliant, HASL finish.
Boards have been manufactured by JLCPCB, the same company Elmo used for the original production run. Boards are being sold at-cost, plus actual shipping. If you want a full kit, the pricing for those is based on actual cost of the components, plus $10 for my time counting all the components, flashing the microcontroller, etc.
The boards are IN HOUSE AND READY TO SHIP!
