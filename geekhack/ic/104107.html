【IC】SP DCS Alps Polycarbonate blanks

January 4, 2020 IC, 键帽, SP 本文总阅读量次

类型: IC
作者: helborne
发布时间: 2020-01-04 20:17:19
更新时间: 2020-01-04 20:17:19
原链接: https://geekhack.org/index.php?topic=104107.0

Put short, we all know finding keycaps for Alps switches (and Matias) is a bit of a pain. Fortunately one of my favorite profile caps from SP, DCS, can be made for alps switches. I like the MX mount clear PC ones so much, I wanted to order a bunch of 1u caps for Alps. I figured that some of you would be interested as well.
For those of you unfamiliar, documentation for DCS can be found here: https://pimpmykeyboard.com/key-cap-family-specs/
I’ve attached the colors that SP has sent me via email. I’ve also been told that aside from clear, they can also press pink and purple.
My intent is to order at least 100 of rows 1-5 each, with each row having its own price break. Each color/row combination of course begins another price break. I’m not looking to profit from this at all. Any price breaks will be passed on to everyone that joins. This is would be my first group buy, so any advice is definitely appreciated.
Thanks for looking
