Hello all,
I’m zekkin – I’ve been around for, wow, about six years now, with only the last two being spent diving into keyboard design. Until now I’ve mostly kept to posting my work on the MechKeys Discord server (pretty much exclusively in /#kb-kustoms), my Instagram, and in various smaller Discord servers amongst close friends. I have a few keyboards that are in the wild, none being from a public group buy, at least in the sense that it was posted here, on Geekhack.
During those two years I only ever designed things with sandwich or top mount and wanted to try something different. I was not too pleased with the general public’s aversion to reveal the “secrets” of the fabled OTD 356mini (despite there being very high resolution images of the board available), so I decided to make something that should, in theory, replicate the mounting style, but mixed with my own aesthetic flavor.
Anyway, here’s Ogre.
