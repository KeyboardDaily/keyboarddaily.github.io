Hi everyone!
I am a fan of Mass Effect and I just had this little idea (inspired from Matt3o’s recent designs in IC), so I thought I’d just try and put them up to see if it’ll look nice as keysets.
But shortly after I’ve done my draft, I’m beginning to doubt my design would make any sense, still I’d like to see the feedbacks from you guys.
I had some time and I just did this little design.
Admittedly, it seem similar to Matt3o’s “Midnight” set and boost’s “Cheap TKL”, but I really like the dark and bright legend contrast and I wanted it to look more or less like the N7 armor.
Here’s the draft of my design so far.
